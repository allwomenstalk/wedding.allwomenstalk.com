# 4 Ways to Shine with Wedding Chandeliers ...

There are so many ways to **shine** on your wedding day or ways to let your wedding day shine! The bride and groom don't need any extra help shining on their own; their love, smiles, and happy glow make it a very happy day no matter what happens. But perhaps you want to **light the way** for your guests and make everything else sparkle...with thanks to[The Favour Shoppe Blog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://thefavourshoppe.blogspot.com/2011/02/tip-from-pros_23.html)for inspiration, here are 4 ways your day can shine with **wedding** **chandeliers**...

## 4. A Glowing Ceremony...

![A Glowing Ceremony...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/chandelierceremony_420x630.jpg)

Photo: [La Vie Photography](https://laviephoto.com/) via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/02/10/washington-wedding-by-la-vie-photography/)

Every wedding day has a story and shines on it's own with the love of the two people getting married. The **love** and **warmth** of the couple, the bride and her father, the guests, the **candles** and the stunning **chandeliers** shine in this beautiful ceremony. You have to check out the rest of the amazing details of their ceremony and reception, the bride is the owner of [Hobson House Design](https://web.archive.org/web/http://hobsonhousedesign.squarespace.com/)and created their invitation suite, cake topper, and much more from her inspiration and favorite movie [Big Fish](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0319061/)!

## 3. Rustic Elegance...

![Rustic Elegance...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/barnchandelier_420x560.jpg)

Photo: [LBPS Events](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.lbpsevents.com/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/02/04/santa-barbara-wedding-by-joy-de-vivre-mark-brooke-photographers/)

I love the rustic, country feel of a **barn wedding**. They are becoming very popular venues, and with this gorgeous **rustic elegance** wedding reception I can see why. I love the contrast of the family style tables and the country look of the barn with the touch of unexpected **sparkle** from the **chandeliers**. It's city chic ballroom meets simple backyard wedding.

## 2. Hanging on a Tree...

![Hanging on a Tree...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/treechandelier_421x633.jpg)

Photo: [Docuvitae](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.docuvitae.com/) via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/02/17/elegant-california-wedding-by-docuvitae/)

Another wedding with a story I am happy to share! The bride and groom are very family oriented and since she was a little girl the beautiful bride always imagined getting married at her** parent's home**. With 275 guests and a mom who knows a little something about holding events, the **backyard** was transformed into a **whimsical** ceremony and reception area. The bride's mom, Stacey Winkler, hung **lovely chandeliers** from their **trees** and used her own vintage candelabras as centerpieces. Visit [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault/image/143216) to see photos of the bride's dad, [Henry Winkler](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001857/), who is **the Fonz** from[Happy Days](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0070992/)!

## 1. Under a Tent...

![Under a Tent...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/tentchandelier_420x280.jpg)

Photo: [A Charleston Bride](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.acharlestonbride.com/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/02/10/southern-wedding-celebration-from-a-charleston-bride/)

Is it the lovely **fabric**, how a **chandelier** fits so perfectly, or maybe that a chandelier isn't decor you usually see in a **tent** that makes me love this trend so much! There is just something so dreamy and romantic about having a chandelier as part of your** tent reception decor**. Together with the fabric it softens the look and gives it an elegant glow.

**Are you using chandeliers to help your wedding day sparkle and shine? **
