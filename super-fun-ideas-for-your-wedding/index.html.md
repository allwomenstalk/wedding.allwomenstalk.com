# 9 Super Fun Ideas for Your Wedding ...

There are so many fun ideas for your wedding! It's important to make time for fun because the whole planning part is sure to stress you out. These fun ideas for your wedding let you, your groom, and your guests have a blast. Having a fun wedding will definitely start off a happy marriage. Check them out and let me know which ones you'll be incorporating into your wedding!

## 1. Pre-wedding Field Day

![Pre-wedding Field Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/u3/hs/0d1unwqy_600x400.jpg)

A pre-wedding field day is the perfect way to get your groomsmen and bridesmaids together! Everyone can get acquainted with one another while playing fun games. It's also perfect for a joint couples shower. It's basically just a perfect way to get everyone together before the big wedding day. This is just one of the fun ideas for your wedding!

## 2. Dj Song Request on Rsvp Cards

![Dj Song Request on Rsvp Cards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/om/e4/zl/zuwv8gzy_570x376.jpg)

When you send out your RSVP cards, add a line where people can request a song. It's an awesome way to ensure that there'll be lots of dancing going on! All you gotta do is play the music your guests want to hear. Plus, it'll be super funny to read all the requests when you get the RSVP cards back.

## 3. "my Two Cents" Coasters

!["my Two Cents" Coasters](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ag/h1/zz/eoc4wtva_600x450.jpg)

This is such a cute idea! Serve your drinks on "my two cents" coasters. Your guests can leave their advice for the new bride and groom. It'll be a cute keepsake, to see all the advice your friends and family have to offer. They can also serve as cute little reminders to keep your marriage running.

## 4. Love Story Aisle

![Love Story Aisle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vp/w9/ad/yx7gc4gm_600x407.jpg)

This is beautiful! Use your aisle to incorporate your love story. Use pictures of your first date, first memories together, etc. Guests can even write sweet messages on it while they wait for the ceremony to start. It'll be so sweet to walk down the aisle and see the pictures/messages that remind you why you're getting married today.

## 5. Personalized Converse for Reception

![Personalized Converse for Reception](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gd/nl/yp/01zt7qr3_400x266.jpg)

When it's time to party at your reception, rock these "Bride" converse! Your hubby can rock the "Groom" ones! They'll look adorable and make for some great photos. Isn't this awesome?!

## 6. Candy Bar Instead of Wedding Favors

![Candy Bar Instead of Wedding Favors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ra/f7/qs/knm2ebai_600x396.jpg)

Instead of giving out wedding favors, have a table full of yummy treats. Your guests can pick their favorite ones to take home with them. After all that dancing, your guests are sure to work up a sweet tooth!

## 7. Colorful Bridesmaids Dresses

![Colorful Bridesmaids Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o8/hs/ov/v7o5dj2y_600x399.jpg)

Sometimes an array of colors is prettier than just choosing one! As long as the colors still mesh, you'll all look fabulous in photos. Color is never a bad thing! Which colors would you love?

## 8. Decorate Bathroom Doors

![Decorate Bathroom Doors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d5/nz/zy/a3w54r1w_600x338.jpg)

This is too cute! Decorate the bathroom doors with childhood photos of you and the groom. It'll be a cute touch and get guests talking! Plus, when do people ever turn down the opportunity to look at funny baby pictures?

## 9. Transformer Wedding Dress

![Transformer Wedding Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t6/l9/yc/0e6rcrvh_600x450.jpg)

After you say your "I Dos," it's time to party! With a transformer wedding dress, you go from ceremony to reception in no time. Isn't it amazing?

Well, there you have it! These are just 9 out of SO many fun ideas for your wedding. Which one did you like the best? What other ideas do you have?
