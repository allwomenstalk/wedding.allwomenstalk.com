# 3 Hot Color Palette Trends for Your 2011 Wedding ...

From **weddings**, celebrities, and fashion to television, sports, and entertainment, trends come and go. So when the time comes to start planning your wedding, you can follow **trends** like these **3 hot color palettes for your 2011 wedding**, but remember to start **planning from your heart** and it will be the best wedding ever...

## 3. Pink and Gray Are Here to Stay...

![Pink and Gray Are Here to Stay...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/pinkandgray_404x461.jpg)

Photo: [jacinandpat.blogspot.com](https://goo.gl/ZvcIV6)

Did you hear that [honeysuckle pink](https://www.pantone.com/articles) was just named by [Pantone paints](https://www.pantone.com/articles) as the **color of 2011**? If you like to follow **wedding** and fashion **trends** then pair your stunning **honeysuckle pink** with tones of **grays** and **silvers**. I love the contrast of the **pink peonies** and pink ranunculus with the **silver brunia berries **and fuzzy gray dusty miller leaves. Will your groom wear a [gray suit](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-hot-looks-for-every-groom-to-rock-in-style/)for your pink and gray wedding?

## 2. Green & Orange All Year Long...

![Green & Orange All Year Long...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/orangeandgreen1_400x267.jpg)

Photo: [JLDesigns.blogspot.com](http://jldesigns.blogspot.com/2010/10/photo-shoot-for-southern-weddings.html)

When you think of **green and orange** together you may think of [fall wedding colors](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ideas-for-a-fall-wedding/); looking at this **beautiful vintage bouquet** I see a **wedding** for **all seasons**. I love the **earthy tones **that the **green succulents** bring to this floral bouquet of peach parrot tulips and [blush and coral poppies](http://www.flowers-cs.com/poppy.html). **Have you found your 2011 wedding colors? **

## 1. Aquamarine & Gold Glitter in the Sun...

![Aquamarine & Gold Glitter in the Sun...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/aquaandgold_408x272.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

I love the way **golden yellow flowers** shine against **rich color tones** like these beautiful bridesmaids' dresses in **aqua** and gray. [Spring and Summer weddings](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-dresses-for-every-spring-2011-bridesmaid/) will glow naturally with this gorgeous palette, but bring this [color combination](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.weddingbycolor.com/blog/) to your fall and winter weddings for an amazing **pop of color** contrasting the fall foliage and winter snow.
