# 5 Bridal Looks Right from the Golden Globes ...

**Celebrity fashion trends** sometimes inspire our every day looks like[Michelle Williams](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-adorable-michelle-williams-red-carpet-looks/) cute pixie cut or [Reese Witherspoon's](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-reese-witherspoon-street-style-inspirations/) chic street style. They can also inspire us for one very special look as well. Find your **wedding day inspiration** from these **5 Bridal Looks **right from the** Golden Globe red carpet! **

## 5. A Hippie Garden Gown...

![A Hippie Garden Gown...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/michellewilliams_405x601.jpg)

Photo:[Awards.OMG.Yahoo.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://awards.omg.yahoo.com/photos;_ylt=AvckHddjR7_1DcJeT3rau9WZwBx)

[Michelle Williams](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0931329/) has always been fashion forward and though some may not love this look for the **red carpet**, it's a fabulous gown for a **hippie chic bride**. I love the **daisies** and the flow of the [Valentino](https://www.valentino.com/en-us/) gown. Find a matching [hippie head band](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-vintage-inspired-wedding-looks/), send out [daisy invitations](https://goo.gl/ZmrVll) for your **garden wedding**, and don your groom with a [delicate daisy boutonniere](https://www.stadiumflowers.com/?iteID=2200). Are there **daisy** **details** in your wedding?

## 4. One Shoulder Glamour...

![One Shoulder Glamour...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/nicolekidman_410x614.jpg)

Photo: [Awards.omg.yahoo.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://awards.omg.yahoo.com/photos;_ylt=AvckHddjR7_1DcJeT3rau9WZwBx)

There are no [accessories](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-lovely-bridal-accessories-in-hot-metallic-colors/://) needed for this **glitz** and **glamour** look because the beautiful [Prada](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.prada.com/) gown shines in the spotlight on it's own. How adorable is the **bow** on the waistline! I love the one shoulder look, [Nicole Kidman](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000173/) is glowing in this dress. The [perfect high heels](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-high-heels-to-spice-up-your-wedding/) are the only thing she needs to complete this stunning bridal look.

## 3. Grey Ruffles...

![Grey Ruffles...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/juliebowen_408x614.jpg)

Photos: [Awards.omg.yahoo.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://awards.omg.yahoo.com/photos;_ylt=AvckHddjR7_1DcJeT3rau9WZwBx)

Though not every **bride** wants to wear **white** she still wants to have that **bridal look** as she walks down the aisle. This gorgeous **ruffled** [Tadashi Shoji](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.tadashicollection.com/#) gown will show off your **unique style** as it did for [Julie Bowen](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0100866/). I love the **grey** and hope to see more bridal dresses in this beautiful color. Are you wearing a unique [bridal gown](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-real-brides-that-will-catch-your-eye/)?

## 2. Simple Elegance...

![Simple Elegance...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/carrieunderwood_410x584.jpg)

Photo: [Awards.omg.yahoo.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://awards.omg.yahoo.com/photos;_ylt=AvckHddjR7_1DcJeT3rau9WZwBx)

When you look like [Carrie Underwood](http://www.carrieunderwoodofficial.com/us/home) everything on you looks **simply elegant**! Her complete look speaks **bridal**, from **hair** to **bag**! I love that many [actresses](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-celebrity-couples-who-recently-said-yes/) went without bold jewelry pieces, there is none needed for this elegant[Badgley Mischka](https://www.badgleymischka.com/) gown. I love her [metallic clutch](https://goo.gl/5Uxbhl), it's very **trendy** for this [seasons bridal look](https://www.onewed.com/blog/hot-hue-for-2011-brides-and-bridesmaids-shimmery-champagne-1981976/).

## 1. Shimmering Bride...

![Shimmering Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/jenniferlopez_407x626.jpg)

Photo: [Awards.omg.yahoo.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://awards.omg.yahoo.com/photos;_ylt=AvckHddjR7_1DcJeT3rau9WZwBx)

[Jennifer Lopez](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-most-insanely-insured-celebrity-body-parts/) always knows how to **make a statement** and if you're that kind of **bride** than take notes from the pro! I don't know which I love more, her [Zuhair Murad](https://www.zuhairmurad.com/en/home) bridal like gown or her **shimmering crystal shaw**! Go all out from head to toe in matching **crystal accessories** to show off your **diva**, "I'm the bride", look as you make sure to turn heads your way!

Are you **inspired** by any **red carpet looks** for your **wedding day**?
