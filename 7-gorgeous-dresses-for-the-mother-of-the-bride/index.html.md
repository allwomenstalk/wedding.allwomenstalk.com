# 7 Gorgeous Dresses for the Mother of the Bride ...

While all eyes are usually on the bride, it’s just as important for the mother of the bride to be looking her best. Timeless, elegant designs are always a winner. Are you about to become a mother of the bride? Why not try one of the following dresses on for size?

## 1. Alberta Ferreti Draped Silk-chiffon Gown

![Alberta Ferreti Draped Silk-chiffon Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xb/bs/hliwvb7x54c8f361773ec145018006_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $3165.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/rchJJ3)
This gown is a show-stopper. The silk-chiffon is the most beautiful rose colour. I love the draped, Grecian-style design. Keep the accessories to a minimum as you don’t want to attract too much attention away from the bride!

## 2. Oscar De La Renta Cotton and Silk-blend Twill Dress

![Oscar De La Renta Cotton and Silk-blend Twill Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/ma/so59lmil54c8f362a5185697959433_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1690.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/QEtJZQ)
I think that this dress would be perfect for any mother of the bride. It is made from a wonderful combination of cotton and silk-blend twill. The design is timeless and could suit a variety of occasions. Finish the outfit off by pairing it with neutral coloured pumps and a cocktail ring.

## 3. Oscar De La Renta Kelly Boucle-tweed Dress

![Oscar De La Renta Kelly Boucle-tweed Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wr/lo/hnf7btgs54c8f363a805f015327439_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1690.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/RpgZOg)
This tweed dress is perfect for those looking for something a little left of centre. The multi-coloured tweed is both a classic yet unexpected design feature. The tweed material is sophisticated, while the frayed hems add a touch of casual-cool. You will definitely get your money’s worth from this dress as you will want to wear it over and over again!

## 4. Issa Long-sleeved Silk-jersey Dress

![Issa Long-sleeved Silk-jersey Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y8/cu/rnvrsenz54c8f364b9040912001842_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $535.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/vFJ84x)
The deep blue colour of this dress is what initially caught my attention. The long-sleeved dress is made from luxurious silk-jersey. The warp around self-tie waist is a wonderful design feature. The long sleeves would also make it perfect to wear for an autumn or winter wedding. This dress is all about understated style.

## 5. Temperley London Camilla Silk-crepe Dress

![Temperley London Camilla Silk-crepe Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jy/j1/aqwlgxft54c8f365ad2a1616821019_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $950.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/D1Leja)
The zig zag inserts on this dress are such an eye-catching design feature. The grey and black colour combination is perfect for a mother of the bride who wants a dress that is elegant and timeless. The gathered sleeves also make for quite a flattering silhouette. Team this dress with some statement gold jewellery and patent black accessories.

## 6. Oscar De La Renta Paneled Tweed Dress

![Oscar De La Renta Paneled Tweed Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d4/an/o3x2jbi354c8f36695abf740020232_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1690.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/2aX30F)
This dress is just so sophisticated! The tweed panels add a twist to an otherwise simple navy dress. I love the sleek, lean silhouette of this dress. It’s perfect paired with both sky high pumps or demure flats. Finish the outfit off with some eye-catching jewellery and a beaded clutch.

## 7. Lela Rose Pleated Cotton-twill Dress

![Lela Rose Pleated Cotton-twill Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yw/p3/nz88ls6454c8f367b5dc8085929451_333x500.jpg)

**Price:** $1095.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/AyJMQ4)
The sea green colour of this dress is absolutely amazing. It would be perfect to wear all year round. This flattering dress features a cowl neck and asymmetric pleats on the skirt. You can keep accessories to a minimum, or go all out – the choice is up to you!
