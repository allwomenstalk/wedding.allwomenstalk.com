# 7 Things to do at a Bachelorette Party ...

When it comes to your bachelorette party, there is like a million of things you shouldn’t do. Like getting totally wasted, doing things you might regret later, things that might end up on the Internet, things that might earn you a night in the “big house” and so on and on and on. Okay, I totally agree with all that because a bachelorette party is something the bride would want to remember, not wish she could forget. So, what should you do? Well, here are some bachelorette party do’s to help you get started:

## 1. Have a Nice Meal

![Have a Nice Meal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/kc/h9w00ey054c8f9abf0623315371417_500x333.jpg)

Image source: [data.whicdn.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://data.whicdn.com/images/16332392/tumblr_lt9xiqWjl91r0nxjjo1_500_large.jpg)

Pick the bride’s favorite restaurant and book a table there because that’s the best way to start a successful bachelorette party. You can opt to prepare dinner yourself or have the food catered, in case you will be having a short get-together or you don’t think the attendants are willing to sacrifice a lot of cash. A meal is a definitely bachelorette party do as it will enable everybody to have a chat with the bride and fill up their stomachs and prevent getting drunk later on.

## 2. Naughty Activity

![Naughty Activity](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/ob/ftjdjdvt54c8f9acd583e667329695_500x322.jpg)

Image source: [data.whicdn.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://data.whicdn.com/images/16332392/tumblr_lt9xiqWjl91r0nxjjo1_500_large.jpg)

Yup, you’re all allowed to get a bit naughty so relax. Next bachelorette party do is organizing a fun, a bit naughty activity like going to a strip club, ordering a stripper or taking a brief course in striptease, burlesque, Tantra or something like that. Now this all depends on your bride because some people are shyer than the others and, if you do something that would embarrass her, she might even get really mad at you.

## 3. Pajama Party

![Pajama Party](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v5/tk/mp07oshq54c8f9adab327391001801_500x333.jpg)

Image source: [data.whicdn.com](http://data.whicdn.com/images/12431409/229417_174327815958289_100001432161814_452415_547106_n_large.jpg)

Some people like it wild while some prefer to keep it casual so, if the bride-to be is one of those casual people whose idea of a good time rarely includes fancy dinners and clubbing, organize a teeny pajama party. Remember how we all used to love them back when we were just kids? Remember how we used to share secrets and play truth or dare or other potentially embarrassing games? Well, I’f you are one of her childhood friends, reminding her how you used to have such a good time for all those years will definitely be well-received.

## 4. Tropical Weekend

![Tropical Weekend](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/nh/wkjs59n054c8f9aea11f9298276970_500x311.jpg)

Image source: [data.whicdn.com](http://data.whicdn.com/images/14980203/tumblr_lrtl2tB8Ea1r3r4uoo1_500_large.jpg)

Well, if money is no problem then you must organize a fabulous tropical weekend or a trip to a popular spa. Massages, tropical cocktails, handsome waiters and chilling on the beach or next to the swimming pool is just what your dear friend needs to relax and stop stressing about the wedding. If money is an issue but not a very serious one, you might want to ask around and see if anybody owns a beach house you could rent or borrow. But what about a road trip? As long as you know where you’re going, how to behave, how to get there and who to avoid, a road trip is a bachelorette party do.

## 5. A Dream Came True

[Image source:](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/7-things-to-do-at-a-bachelorette-party/519x338/)[cache2.allpostersimages.com](https://goo.gl/s2zfVr)

If you’re living in a big city, you’ll definitely manage to find somebody your BFF/bride-to-be adores. It can be a local DJ, famous chef you’ll hire to cook for you or give you all a few basic cooking lessons, a celebrity impersonator that could turn your night into a dream come true you’ll definitely want to have on tape. All you need to do is use your imagination and think long and hard about it. You’re not a maid of honor for no reason hun, you know her best and that great idea on how to surprise her will soon come to you.

## 6. Vintage Theme

![Vintage Theme](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cd/sc/dknr6iep54c8f9af92330187707396_500x338.jpg)

Image source: [data.whicdn.com](http://data.whicdn.com/images/15160781/costume+party_large.jpg)

Now, the point of this one is not to look sexy but to get the most hideous dresses you can find and get the bride and the whole bridal party to wear them. It won’t be easy, especially if you will be going out but, if they have any love for costumes and Halloween, they will definitely accept to do it. Be very careful, though, as this is a bachelorette party do only if everybody (especially the bride) says yes to the idea. If not, you can do the usual sexy, vintage theme.

## 7. Party Crashing

[Image source:](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/7-things-to-do-at-a-bachelorette-party/fotolia_18780801_subscription_xl/)[ifguide.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.ifguide.com/files/Fotolia_18780801_Subscription_XL.jpg)

If the bride-to-be thinks having fun without her fiancée would be wrong, you could organize a bachelor and bachelorette party all in one or make a deal with the guys to crash your party at one point. This would probably help your bride relax, have fun and drink because she knows her fiancé is there too. Having the guys over for a drink or two or having the diner alone and then inviting them to go hit the club all together is good because you don’t have to worry about girls drinking too much or the possibility of anything bad happening. The guys are there to make sure you are all safe and sound and nobody will even dare to abuse the fact that you’ve all had one glass of champagne too much.
