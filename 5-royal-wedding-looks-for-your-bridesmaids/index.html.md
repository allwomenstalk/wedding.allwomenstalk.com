# 5 Royal Wedding Looks for Your Bridesmaids ...

We may not know what the royal wedding colors are, but we can still have fun inspiring you with **bridesmaid dresses** that the **Royal Wedding Party** may wear. We do know that the bridesmaids should look very **formal** and will probably wear **sleeves** or straps of some kind. Perhaps one of these lovely gowns will be the look for the ladies in the royal party...

## 5. Royal Gloves...

![Royal Gloves...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/bridesmaid2_423x563.jpg)

Photo: [DavidsBridal](https://goo.gl/fWyXp6)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/956/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Davids-Bridal?image_id=17920&gallery_id=956)

I know most of these looks don't have straps or sleeves but I loved the **formal look** of the gowns, especially this beautiful dress from [Davids Bridal](https://goo.gl/HHvGux). I can picture the **royal bridesmaids** wearing **gloves** with their pretty dresses, which will probably be in a much lighter color than black.

## 4. Royal Lace...

![Royal Lace...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/bm4_427x569.jpg)

Photo: [Jordan](https://web.archive.org/web/https://goo.gl/JVUBsC)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/793/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Jordan-Fashions?image_id=15743&gallery_id=793)

This stunning **lace bridesmaid dress** from [Jordan fashions](https://web.archive.org/web/https://goo.gl/E89Mrk)will be the perfect match for [Kate Middleton's](https://www.officialroyalwedding2011.org/)ladies if her wedding gown has lace as well. I guess we'll all just have to wait until April 29th to find out!

## 3. Pockets...

![Pockets...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/bluebridesmaid_427x569.jpg)

Photo: [2beBride](http://2bebride.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/760/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Kathy-Ireland-Weddings-by-2be-Bride)

Another reason I love all of these dresses for the royal party is because of the **modern** look and details that Kate and William will probably replicate. While still having a **formal** and traditional feel to honor the royal family, these fun **pockets **on this [Kathy Ireland](http://2bebride.com/hallchoose.html?collection=kathy%20ireland%20by%202be%20-%20Party)dress will give the royal ladies that modern look they love!

## 2. Pretty Bows...

![Pretty Bows...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/bm3_427x569.jpg)

Photo: [Mori Lee](https://www.morilee.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/834/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Mori-Lee-Bridesmaids)

This gorgeous **copper** **colored** bridesmaid dress from [Mori Lee](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridesmaids/bridesmaids/297)reminds me of the Kate Middleton **nail polish** that [Butter London](http://www.butterlondon.ca/drupal/)came out with called ["No More Waity Katie"](http://www.butterlondon.ca/drupal/lacquers/no-more-waity-katie) ! I love the color and the adorable bow on the bodice, maybe this will be the dress.

## 1. Modern & Sophisticated...

![Modern & Sophisticated...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/bm5_429x571.jpg)

Photo: [After Six](https://goo.gl/VQqdlL) via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/734/fashiontrends/Bridal-Accessories/After-Six)

If your **royal ladies** are looking for a **modern** and **sophisticated** look too than this is the bridesmaid dress for them. I love the neckline and the pretty bow on the shoulder; this is a style I haven't seen before and now I want to see more! This amazing dress from the [After Six](https://goo.gl/8DYqEm)line from [Dessy](https://goo.gl/shFA1v)will look beautiful on so many girls who have different personalities, shapes, and sizes.

**Have you found the modern yet formal look for your bridesmaids?**
