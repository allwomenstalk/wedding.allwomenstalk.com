# 5 Colorful Bridesmaid Looks from the Oscars ...

I was in love with all of the beautiful dresses on the **Oscar **red carpet last night and **Anne Hathaway's** performance as co-host! She really showed us that she is more than just the girl next door. So instead of showing off all of the bridal looks you could steal from the night, here are 5 colorful **Oscar dress inspirations** for your **bridesmaids**....what do you think?

## 5. Color Me Red...

![Color Me Red...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/annehathaway1_414x644.jpg)

[Anne Hathaway's](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0004266/)complete look is stunning. I wonder if her glow came from being co-host of the [83rd Academy Awards](https://www.oscars.org/oscars)or because she was wearing a vintage [Valentino](https://www.valentino.com/en-us/)**red hot gown**? Everything from her **simple but elegant updo** and** red lip**, to her formal gown and sparkly necklace, can be beautiful inspiration for your [bridesmaids](https://goo.gl/JgmLfC).

## 4. Lovely Lace & Lavender...

**Romantic**, sweet, and delicate; a perfect gown for [Mila Kunis](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005109/)from[Elie Saab](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.eliesaab.com/site.html). I love how **soft** the lovely **lace** and **lavender** color are, this would be a wonderful garden wedding inspiration for your [bridesmaids](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-colorful-bridesmaid-dresses-for-spring-summer/). Could you see your lovely ladies in a lace gown?

## 3. A Million Dollar Look...

![A Million Dollar Look...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/amyadams1_407x644.jpg)

[Amy Adams](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0010736/)looked like a million dollars, and cost that much too! Adams borrowed beautiful **jewels** from [Cartier](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.cartier.com/)that cost **$1.35 million dollars** to keep her look sparkling from head to toe! I loved her amazing** glittering navy gown** from [L'Wren Scott](http://www.lwrenscott.com/). Check out [BagBorroworSteal](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.bagborroworsteal.com/jewelry/fine-jewelry?ref=n2_e2-8_d2-4)to** rent jewelry** for your wonderful **bridesmaids** as they walk down the aisle in this glimmering style!

## 2. Bright & Bold...

![Bright & Bold...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/jenniferhudson2_407x599.jpg)

[Jennifer Hudson](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1617685/)has been through so much in her life and still manages to put on a beautiful smile while looking hot, hot, hot in this **coral** [Versace](https://www.versace.com/gb/en-gb/home/)gown. Her **glamorous** look; hair, jewelry, and bold gown remind me of the classy **old Hollywood** style. She rocks it well and so can your [bridesmaids](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.watters.com/BrowseByColor/WattersWatters/Spice/5218/).

## 1. The Beauty of the Night...

![The Beauty of the Night...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/natalieportman1_403x606.jpg)

[Natalie Portman](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000204/)stole my heart a long time ago when I first saw her in the movie ["The Professional",](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0110413/)she was only 13 years old! Does she not look even more stunning as a mother-to-be? I love the **color**, **draping**, and off-the-shoulder look of her [Rodarte](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.rodarte.net/)gown, a beautiful style for your **pregnant bridesmaids**. Even her** side-swept hair** is lovely!

**Will you find inspiration from the red carpet for your bridesmaids?**
