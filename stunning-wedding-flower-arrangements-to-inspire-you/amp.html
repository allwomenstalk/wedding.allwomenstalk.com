Frequently Asked Questions

What are some outdoor wedding flower ideas?
Oh, you can totally go with a mix of wildflowers and greenery for that natural vibe. I love the idea of using sunflowers or lavender, too. Aren’t they just perfect for that open-air feeling?
Any thoughts on peach, pink, and white flower combos?
Honestly, this combo is just dreamy. It's soft, romantic, and works perfectly for any season. You can mix roses, peonies, and maybe a splash of eucalyptus. So elegant, right?
How can I make my wedding flowers stunning?
You know, it's all about creativity and personal touch! Play with colors, add unique elements like feathers or berries, and work with a florist who gets your vision. It's your day and it should reflect your style perfectly.
What if I want to use red flowers in my wedding?
Red is just classic! It’s bold and really makes a statement. You could go with classic red roses or mix them with greenery or even some gold accents. Definitely lots of wow factor in that!
Any advice on using unique flowers like blue orchids?
Oh, blue orchids are just mesmerizing! They add such a modern and exotic touch to your arrangements. Maybe use them as centerpieces to really show them off. They’d definitely stand out!
