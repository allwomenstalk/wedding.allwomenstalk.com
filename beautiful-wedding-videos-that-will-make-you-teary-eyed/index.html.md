# 7 Beautiful Wedding Videos That Will Make You Teary Eyed ...

I love watching beautiful wedding videos; I mean really, who doesn’t? You get to see two people come together and give perfect toasts and see beautiful dresses and tons of tears and smiles. I love it - it’s such a warm and happy moment! It’s incredible to catch it all on video. Here are 7 beautiful wedding videos that will make you teary eyed (or just really, really smiley)!

## 1. Victoria & Jason by Lightfield Lewis

This is one of the most beautiful wedding videos I’ve ever seen. If I could afford this team for my wedding, I would totally do it. The music, the style, everything is just wonderful. I love when wedding videos tell a story, when it’s more than just a little image of the two lovebirds standing still and getting their picture taken. That’s what you want in a video, a story!

## 2. Travis & Briana Broome's Wedding by Sweet Tea Videography

This is such a sweet wedding video! I love that they wrote their own vows - it's such a lovely personal touch, and the vows themselves are magical and romantic. My favorite part is when she's reading his letter before the ceremony. Right in the feels!

## 3. Clinton & Callum

To anyone who thinks marriage can only be between one man and one woman, and that anything other than hetero love is "real," I invite you to watch this video. If it made the guests cry, it’ll make you cry too - and maybe, just maybe you'll have a change of heart, and see that love can cross all sorts of boundaries.

## 4. Benji & Zoe by Untitled Film Works

What I love about this video is that we get to see the progression of the wedding. It’s like a mini movie (it is 12 minutes long!), a documentary of love. That’s why I love wedding videos, but that’s also why I love videos that have more of a docu-feel. The words that are said at that moment are the most true they’ll ever be because they come from the most loving, honest, and uninhibited place, and that is beautiful.

## 5. Beautiful in White

Oh the tears. When the groom cries, honestly, I’m done. I think secretly, it’s every girl's dream that her groom will shed a tear or two when she’s standing up there with him. What makes this video so lovely is the genuine love you can feel coming off the screen.

## 6. Joe & Sarah by Paperback Weddings

This is a wonderfully shot video. I love the style of it; soft, sweet, a little like a music video. Such a perfect vibe for a wedding video! What I love about watching wedding videos is that you get to see the cool little ideas and gifts that are given. It’s all the small touches that were imagined up by the bride and groom. It’s nice to see the humour and the laughs that come through such a joyous occasion, because it’s such a wonderful reason to celebrate – love.

## 7. A Beautiful Wedding in Paris

For anyone who's ever dreamt of a wedding in Paris, for all the romance, you need to watch this video. It's so full of light, laughter, family, friends, and reflection, it’s just lovely! And the music is perfect, too, don't you think?

Wedding videos are now almost as popular as wedding photos, and after watching these incredible videos it’s not hard to see why! Videos capture all the tiny things you never thought possible, and you get to see how everyone interacted, how your event played out, and revisit that day forever. They say that you barely remember your wedding because it goes by so fast; luckily, we have videos to help us remember! So tell me, what are some of your favourite wedding videos?
