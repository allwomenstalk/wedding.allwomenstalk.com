# 5 High Heels to Spice up Your Wedding ...

Shopping for my wedding shoes was one of the hardest **accessories** to find. **Wedding Shoes** have become **a big deal** and not just another overlooked wedding detail that no one remembers. Here are **5 high heels to spice up your wedding** that will leave all the ladies wondering where you bought them...

## 5. Add a Little Sparkle...

![Add a Little Sparkle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/glittershoes_404x303.jpg)

**Photo**: [Zappos.com](https://goo.gl/bH3C3D)

**Price**: $200.00

Feel like a princess or a Hollywood goddess in these **sparkling high heels** that will keep you **glowing** all through your **big day**. I loved my wedding shoes, but now I wish I had found something like these [Badgley Mischka's](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.badgleymischka.com/lifestyle/footwear/)! Perfect for a **wedding** at the [Plaza Hotel](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theplaza.com/events/weddings/) or one with a [Hollywood movie theme](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-wedding-themes/). **Could you see yourself in a pair of hot glittering heels like these? **

## 4. Something Blue...

![Something Blue...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/blueheel_404x303.jpg)

**Photo**: [Zappos.com](https://goo.gl/TQJg2D)

**Price**: $61.00

Many **brides** like to keep [old traditions](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-cultural-wedding-traditions-for-any-bride-and-groom/) and these sexy heels from [Jessica Simpson](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.jessicasimpsoncollection.com/) can help you with your **something blue tradition**. I love the something old, something new, something borrowed, and something blue [tradition](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-interesting-wedding-traditions/); it's one of my favorites. **Are these blue heels something new for your wedding traditions?**

I've seen these **unique heart high heels **all over the [wedding blogs](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.mydreamwedding.ca/2928/real-wedding-janet-matts-wedding-with-red-hearts/) and think they are just **darling**. I wonder who started this **gorgeous trend **of wearing [Vivienne Westwood](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.viviennewestwood.co.uk/shop/womens-accessories/shoes/) heels on her big day? Forget the heart candy for your [Valentine's Day wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-romantic-ideas-for-a-valentines-day-wedding/), all you need are these **wonderful heart high heels**!

## 2. Fun with Stripes...

**Photo**: [Zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com/anne-klein-eli?oosRedirected=true)

**Price**: $79.95

Can a girl fall in love with a pair of **high heels**? I'm not a big [shoe girl](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-tips-on-buying-fabulous-shoes/)...more of a bag girl, but I love every **detail** about these [Anne Klein](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.anneklein.com/Shoes/90263428,default,sc.html?utm_source=affiliate&utm_medium=partnerize&utm_campaign=10l1110-viglink) heels. The **stripes** on the inside, the little striped **rosette** on the **open toe**, and the **black** **and** **white** trim of the outside; just so **feminine**.** Are you walking on the wild side with your wedding day heels? **
