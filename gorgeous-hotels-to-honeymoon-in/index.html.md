# 9 Gorgeous Hotels to Honeymoon in ...

Now that the wedding is over, it’s time to relax in some of the most luxurious honeymoon hotels the world has to offer! Nowadays there are lots of couples who register their honeymoon, which if you ask me is pretty good thing to request for a wedding! So let’s take a look at 9 gorgeous honeymoon hotels to celebrate your nuptials in!

## 1. The Sarann, Thailand

![The Sarann, Thailand](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Qk/YG/IAXZIGxu3X3rhFydApxA2jyk_600x400.jpg)

[thesarann.com](https://goo.gl/fp6q4Z)

OMG is this one of the most gorgeous honeymoon hotels or what? Seriously. With infinity pools, gorgeous architecture, luscious greenery and beaches and skies so blue you could swear they were connected, you can’t go wrong. Oh, then there’s the spas and the great food, and beautiful sunsets too.

## 2. Protea Hotel Kruger Gate, South Africa

_ proteahotels.com_

I’ve always wanted to go to Africa, and I have a friend now in South Africa to can attest to just how gorgeous it is. So now that I have a second-hand account of how lovely it is there, I can safely say that this place is one of the best honeymoon hotels money can buy. Actually, once you get there you’ll realize that the price is actually quite reasonable. Imagine a gorgeous hotel that blends in with the wilderness of Africa, that’s literally what this place is like.

## 3. Santorini Kastelli Resort, Greece

![Santorini Kastelli Resort, Greece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Pc/rl/BwxEFMjj8oNAVrx6ln9AHPoy_600x388.jpg)

[kastelliresort.com](https://www.kastelliresort.com/)

One of the best honeymoon hotels resides in Greece, and not just anywhere in Greece, Santorini to be exact. Santorini is one of the most stunning and gorgeous islands in the world, if you’ve always wanted to go to Greece, this place might be it for you. With its white buildings, breathtaking views, and so much natural light you don’t even know where to look, this place is a stunning honeymoon destination!

## 4. Daydream Island Resort & Spa, Australia

![Daydream Island Resort & Spa, Australia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uA/YG/MHyTb5nCNM2lRSEBVHLXn0Yo_600x338.jpg)

[daydreamisland.com](https://www.daydreamisland.com/)

Anywhere in Australia is gorgeous no? So without a doubt one of the most gorgeous honeymoon hotels in the world has to reside in one of the prettiest island countries in the world! Almost every room has an ocean view, every room is gorgeous, and every sunset is stunning. You and your other half will love it here, guaranteed.

## 5. Baros, Maldives

![Baros, Maldives](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eT/iA/inHNOB2rYEnnnNFUJ9Y0t8Yb_600x407.jpg)

[baros.com](https://www.baros.com/)

You know those honeymoon hotels that come up right away as soon as you Google ‘Honeymoon’? This place is one of those destinations. It’s gorgeous and no words are needed. Unless of course, beautiful blue beaches on a private island with great food and privacy mean nothing to you.

## 6. San Pietro Di Positano, Italy

![San Pietro Di Positano, Italy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/Ea/Rw/NuNyhnVj2gvIPPJNiNSeuNTm_550x350.jpg)

[relaischateaux.com](https://www.relaischateaux.com/us/italy/sanpietro-campania-positano)

Positano is a place of dreams. It’s one of those destinations that you can’t even begin to imagine exists. It’s one of the most exclusive, and probably one of the most exotic honeymoon hotels you can ever come across. Situated on a gorgeous cliff, overlooking the ocean and mountains and rolling green hills, you will feel like you’re in heaven.

## 7. Ponta Dos Ganchos, Brazil

_ pontadosganchos.com.br_

Brazil! Hands down, one of the most beautiful honeymoon hotels on the planet, you can’t get much more perfect than this place. There’s a reason half the world's supermodels come from Brazil you know; there’s something in the water there. Plus, you’re going to have such an incredible time; you’ll look even more like the gorgeous girl you are in your guy’s eyes. He married a hottie!

## 8. Shangri-la Hotel Paris, Paris

![Shangri-la Hotel Paris, Paris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/yH/Ls5YoLRjNCbcHh2jigiLEzvN_600x450.jpg)

[shangri-la.com](https://goo.gl/H2ruoL)

You want luxury? Paris is your place to go. One of the most glamourous honeymoon hotels in the world, you can’t help but feel like royalty when you’re here. Take a look around, take in the beauty of this hotel, and book it today for your honeymoon!

## 9. Taj Lake Palace, India

![Taj Lake Palace, India](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xq/kN/sYS1KXeQKTOf0Rpf0xCwiYKu_550x365.jpg)

[tajhotels.com](https://www.tajhotels.com/en-in/taj/taj-lake-palace-udaipur/)

Mystery, opulence, and elegance - honeymoon hotels don’t get any much more grand than this. It has everything you could ever want: glorious dining; beautiful landscapes; stunning architecture; and the chance to immerse yourself in one of the richest cultures on the planet.

So there you have it ladies! A list of some of the most beautiful hotels in the world to go honeymooning in! Some of them are a little bit opulent, some of them are perfect for your wallet. So tell me, where do you want to go for your honeymoon?
