# 4 Sexy Hairstyles to Create on Your Wedding Day ...

Many **brides** go back and forth about whether they should wear a sophisticated updo or **leave their hair down** to create a look with** sexy waves** or **curls.** To help you figure out your big **bridal hair** question here are 4 sexy celebrity styles to inspire you to **leave your hair down** and go **natural**...

## 4. Side Swept...

![Side Swept...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/rosariodawson_410x342.jpg)

Photo:[BellaSugar](https://www.popsugar.com/beauty/Rosario-Dawson-Wears-J-Mendel-SAG-Awards-2011-13510950)

[Rosario Dawson](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0206257/)doesn't need to try hard to pull off this **simple** and **sexy side swept** look and neither will you! With a [curling iron](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.ehow.com/how_5082393_create-loose-curls-curling-iron.html), some [Tresemme flawless curls mousse](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.tresemme.com/Products/Flawless-Curls/Flawless-Curls-Extra-Hold-Mousse/), and a beautiful [white magnolia hair clip](https://goo.gl/aN24DO)your **bridal look** will be complete. Are you leaving your hair down for the big day?

## 3. Wavy Bed Head...

![Wavy Bed Head...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/lea_michele__403x597.jpg)

Photo:[Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/2011-sag-awards-which-of-these)

With a [birdcage veil](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-birdcage-veils-to-rock-for-your-wedding/), **red lips** and a **red rose bouquet**, I can see a **bride** turning this wavy bed head look into a hollywood goddess style that would turn the groom's head in an instant! [Lea Michele](https://www.popsugar.com/beauty/How-Get-Lea-Michele-Wavy-SAG-Awards-Hair-13569179)looks amazing and she know's it! This is a look to be confident with ladies, so **rock it down that aisle**!

## 2. Soft & Sweet...

![Soft & Sweet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/hilary_swank_hair__403x597.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/2011-sag-awards-which-of-these)

This **sweet** and **soft** look on[Hilary Swank](https://www.popsugar.com/beauty/How-Get-Hilary-Swank-SAG-Awards-Hairstyle-13559135)looks fabulous for the red carpet. But why not put a[hippie chic headband](https://goo.gl/YF1e0Z)on, either on top or in front on your forehead, and you'll be one [70's loving](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-vintage-inspired-wedding-looks/), peace sign waving **bride! **I would love to wear a **hippie look** for every day style!

## 1. This Girl's Got Curls...

![This Girl's Got Curls...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/mia_kunis_403x597.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/2011-sag-awards-which-of-these)

**Best Dressed** [Mila Kunis](https://www.popsugar.com/beauty/How-Get-Mila-Kunis-2011-SAG-Awards-Hair-Makeup-Look-13569014)may have also had the **best hair** of the night that would look **gorgeous** on your **wedding day**! Just picture her amazing [Alexander McQueen](https://goo.gl/W9GeyG) gown as an ivory [Vera Wang](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.verawang.com/wedding/bridal-collections/)and you are ready to walk down the aisle.
