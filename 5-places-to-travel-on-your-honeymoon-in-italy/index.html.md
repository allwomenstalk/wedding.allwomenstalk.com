# 5 Places to Travel on Your Honeymoon in Italy ...

**Italy** has so much to offer for an **amazing honeymoon experience**; one every couple would enjoy. To find the historic, romantic, country, city, and sands of Italy follow this guide to find out if one of these **5 places is yours to travel on your Honeymoon in Italy**...

## 5. Find Your Romantic Side...

![Find Your Romantic Side...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/gondolavenice_401x321.jpg)

Photo: [YourWeddingAbroad.net](http://www.yourweddingabroad.net/destination-weddings-on-a-boat/)

[Venice, Italy](https://www.travelchannel.com/destinations/italy/venice) is the** floating city of love**. Board a gondola for a **romantic ride** on your **honeymoon** while you are serenaded through the beautiful city. Because **Venice** is a **popular** and expensive city for [tourists](https://allwomenstalk.com/top-10-travel-booking-websites/) you may want to only **plan a day trip** and then travel on over to [Tuscany](http://www.destination360.com/europe/italy/tuscany) for some wine. Are you planning a **romantic honeymoon**?

## 4. History on Every Corner...

![History on Every Corner...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/roman-colosseum-exterior_400x288.jpg)

Photo: [GoItaly.About.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://goitaly.about.com/od/italypictures/ig/rome-pictures/index.htm)

[Rome, Italy](https://www.travelchannel.com/destinations/italy/rome) is a great place for the **history loving couple** who plans to do a lot of walking to enjoy the [famous historic sites](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.travelchannel.com/Places_Trips/Destinations/Europe/Italy/Rome/Attractions/ci.Recommended_Tours.index) on their **honeymoon**. You'll want to visit the [Colosseum](https://www.italyguides.it/en/lazio/rome/ancient-rome/colosseum), [the Pantheon](https://www.italyguides.it/en/lazio/rome/ancient-rome/pantheon), the [Vatican](https://www.italyguides.it/en/lazio/rome/the-vatican), [the Trevi Fountain](https://www.italyguides.it/en/lazio/rome/squares-and-fountains/trevi-fountain), and the many **statues** and **museums** around this [breathtaking city](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-most-romantic-destinations-for-your-honeymoon/).

## 3. More than Just Pizza...

![More than Just Pizza...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/Sicily_411x251.jpg)

Photo: [Tourist4life.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.tourist4life.com/sicily/)

[Sicily, Italy](https://web.archive.org/web/http://sicilyguide.com/) is rich in [culture](https://web.archive.org/web/http://sicilyguide.com/what-to-do/theaters/), **history**, **villages**, and [beautiful beaches](https://web.archive.org/web/http://sicilyguide.com/what-to-do/sicilian-beaches/). Travel to **Agrigento, Sicily** to discover the [Valley of the Temples](https://www.italyguides.it/en/sicily/agrigento/valley-of-the-temples) where you'll find ancient Greek ruins. But also remember, this is **Italy** where they have amazing, fresh ingredients...so don't forget to [try a slice of pizza](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.my-pizza.it/text/AL%20NORD-EST/bin/list.html)! Will you look for [food & wine](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-tips-for-choosing-good-wine/) on your **honeymoon**?

## 2. Beautiful Beaches...

![Beautiful Beaches...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/naplesitaly_408x306.jpg)

Photo: [ItalyGuide.com](http://www.italyguide.com/)

[Naples, Italy](https://www.travelchannel.com/destinations/italy) is another hot spot for a **honeymoon** full of culture, history and **beautiful seaside adventures**. Did you know that there is an [amusement park](https://www.edenlandia.it/) in Naples? One of the most popular spots in **Naples** is the island of [Capri](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.capri.com/en/beaches), where you can swim, [explore caves and sea coves](https://www.capri.net/en/s/la-grotta-azzurra), shop, enjoy the nightlife, and learn about the [history](https://www.capri.net/en/s/historic-center-of-anacapri).

## 1. The Country Side of Life...

![The Country Side of Life...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/tuscany_408x270.jpg)

Photo: [ItalianVisits.com](http://www.italianvisits.com/)

Tuscany, Italy is for the wine lovers and **country side honeymooners**. Take a [cooking class](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.cookingintuscany.net/) in [Florence](http://www.florenceinitaly.com/) where you'll learn to **make pasta **from scratch, travel to [Chianti, Tuscany](https://www.chianti.com/wine/chianti-winetasting.html) to a culture known for their **wine**, and enjoy the hill sides and hospitality of the kind village folk.

**Where will your honeymoon in Italy take you? **
