# Who Gets Married 38 Times? This Couple Did!

Can you imagine attending 38 weddings in 90 days? Now can you even imagine it being the same couple getting married 38 times? That's exactly what Cheetah Platt and Rhian Woodyard did. The couple from Los Angeles had been ordained, which made it perfectly legal for them to perform their nuptials all around the globe. Naturally they took amazing pictures of their ceremonies but because they are a couple of acrobats, they were wedding photos with a difference.
