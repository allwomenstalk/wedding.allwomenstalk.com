# 7 Wedding Blogs to Follow on Social Media Networks ...

Make planning easier with these great wedding blogs to follow on social media networks. By following them on Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram, you can get a head's up on new trends, giveaways, and even see previews from gown runway shows. I wish Instagram was around when I was planning my own wedding, I love seeing gorgeous bouquets from planners who are putting them together for a bride. Get more ideas for your big day with these wedding blogs to follow on social media networks now and forever...

## 1. Style Me Pretty...

They are one of the most well known wedding blogs to follow on social media networks. Style Me Pretty features inspirational shoots, DIYs, vendor features, and hometown love. The cool thing about following them on social media networks, especially Instagram, is that you get a little behind the scenes look at what they do. On their Facebook page you’ll find little snippets of what goes up on their site, so if you don’t have time to read the full post you can just get a little sneak peek on there.

## 2. Utterly Engaged...

If you love BHLDN gowns then you should definitely check out Utterly Engaged on Instagram since they just posted a few photos from the BHLDN’s fashion show! This is why it’s so helpful to follow wedding blogs on social media. If you are just at the beginning of gown shopping and aren’t sure what you want, what could be better than seeing a preview of gowns soon to come out?! Utterly Engaged is more than just a blog though, they are an online magazine that features everything wedding related, including tips from experts.

## 3. Lovely Little Details...

I love Jacin from Lovely Little Details, she’s always posting gorgeous floral bouquets that she put together herself! She pretty much does it all: wedding planning, floral arrangements, [table decor](https://diy.allwomenstalk.com/coffee-table-decor-ideas-that-dont-require-a-home-stylist/), everything! Check out her Pinterest boards for all things wedding inspiration; she even has boards for her wedding couples and West Elm boards from a few tablescapes she helped put together. Her fashion style is pretty gorgeous too, so be sure to follow her on Instagram as well!

## 4. The Perfect Palette

If you are looking for colorful wedding inspiration, you will definitely find it on The Perfect Palette’s Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and Instagram pages! On her Pinterest page she has boards separated by color, so if you are planning a Mint wedding or a Navy Blue and Fuchsia wedding, chances are she has what you’re looking for. Plus if you are waiting to see what color trends come out next, follow her on Instagram or Facebook so you can get her palettes in your news feed.

## 5. Southern Weddings Magazine...

If you are a Southern bride or even if you’re not, you will love Southern Weddings Magazine's Instagram and Facebook feeds. Now with video on Instagram, SW magazine is taking advantage of really showing you a behind the scenes look at who they are and what they are doing. You’ll also find adorable phrases of the day, like this one: “Sure as squirrels live in trees, I’m for you and you're for me.” Wouldn't this be adorable to say in your [rustic wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/country-rustic-wedding-theme-ideas/) vows?!

## 6. Wedding Chicks...

If you are looking for a certain vendor, DIY designs, or wedding goodies to buy, Wedding Chicks is the wedding blog to follow on social media networks. Every day on their Instagram feed they feature a wedding vendor; whether it’s for hair accessories, personalized totes, or even personalized rubber stamps, they have it all. If you’re a DIY bride they have a few great boards on their Pinterest page featuring tutorials and free printables!

## 7. Martha Stewart Weddings

If you love all things Martha Stewart and are planning a wedding, then be sure to follow along with her editors on Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter, and Instagram. Guess what? They may even share your wedding photo on Instagram when you use the hash tag #MarthaWeddingCrashers so they can re-post it! How amazing would that be to see your wedding day on Martha Stewart Weddings' Instagram feed?! What I love most from their Facebook page right now is that they shared in the love of the big equality wedding news from California and posted eight of their favorite same sex wedding couples! Hooray for MSW!

What wedding blogs do you follow on social media networks? Are there any wedding blogs you follow that I haven’t mentioned?
