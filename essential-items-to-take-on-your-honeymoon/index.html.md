# 6 Essential Honeymoon Items: What to Bring on Honeymoon for Her

While exploring [J.Crew](https://www.jcrew.com/) recently, I realized they have an incredible selection of honeymoon-ready essentials. They consistently offer stylish and comfortable pieces that are perfect for a post-wedding getaway. Whether you are heading to a tropical beach resort or exploring a historic European city, putting together a thoughtful collection of honeymoon items is a must for your trip. Figure out what to bring on honeymoon in advance so you can spend less time stressing over outfits and more time enjoying your getaway. Here are six honeymoon essentials for her that combine timeless style with effortless comfort.

Before packing your suitcase, keep these quick travel tips in mind:

- Choose versatile pieces that easily transition from daytime sightseeing to evening dinners.

- Opt for breathable, lightweight fabrics like cotton, linen, and soft straw weaves.

- Pack neutral footwear like [simple sandals](#3) that pair seamlessly with multiple outfits.

## 1. A Honeymoon Hat

![Honeymoon Hat](https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/05/hat_404x404.bmp)

When you hop off the plane and head to the sunny sands of your [honeymoon destination](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/honeymoon-destinations/), you will want a wide-brimmed straw hat to shade you from the bright sun. Since your skin might not be ready for intense rays just yet, a stylish sun hat is the perfect way to look chic while protecting your face and shoulders from sun damage during long beach afternoons.

## 2. A Great Bag

![A Great Bag](https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/05/bag_404x404.bmp)

For your honeymoon, you will need a versatile bag to carry all your daily essentials down to the shore or out for an afternoon of sightseeing. I love the organic texture and rich tones of a handwoven straw hobo bag. It is exactly the kind of practical yet charming accessory I wish I had brought when we went on our honeymoon to Naples, Florida!

## 3. A Simple Sandal

![A Simple Sandal](https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/05/sandal_404x404.bmp)

Even though you will likely want to go barefoot once your toes hit the warm sand, you still need a simple yet cute pair of sandals for walking around town. You probably don't want to rely solely on bulky sneakers or heavy shoes, as most of your resort wardrobe will consist of light dresses, breezy shorts, and swimwear. A lovely strappy leather sandal from [J.Crew](https://www.jcrew.com/) or [Nordstrom](https://www.nordstrom.com/) will perfectly match your relaxed, carefree vibe.

## 4. Shorts to Relax In

![Shorts to Relax In](https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/05/shorts_404x404.bmp)

When it is warm and sunny, your top priority is to relax and enjoy a worry-free trip. This is why your wardrobe should include comfortable casual staples like broken-in boyfriend chino shorts. They are fun, breathable, and cut at just the right length to keep you feeling cool and relaxed all day long whether you are lounging by the pool or browsing local markets.

| Essential Item | Best For | Packing Tip |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wide-Brim Straw Hat | Sun protection & beach style | Pack with rolled clothing inside crown |
| Woven Hobo Bag | Beach days & town excursions | Use as carry-on to save suitcase space |
| Leather Sandals | Daytime strolling & dinner | Choose neutral shades to match any outfit |
| Chino Shorts | Relaxed lounging & sightseeing | Pick breathable cotton with a relaxed fit |
| Tank Swimsuit | Resort pool & ocean swimming | Look for supportive lining and full coverage |
| Summer Printed Dress | Romantic dinners & evening strolls | Choose lightweight fabrics that resist wrinkles |

## 5. The Perfect Swimsuit

![The Perfect Swimsuit](https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/05/swimsuit_404x404.bmp)

You only get one chance to celebrate this wonderful occasion, so finding a flattering swimsuit that makes you feel genuinely confident is essential. We all have features we love to highlight and areas we prefer to cover. A well-designed tank suit is a classic option because it offers gentle bust support while providing smooth coverage for your stomach, creating an elegant silhouette that transitions beautifully under a [summer dress](#6) or lightweight cover-up.

## 6. A Summer Dress

![A Summer Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/honeymoondress_404x404.jpg)

When you and your sweetheart are heading out to a romantic dinner for two, you will want to look polished without feeling overly formal. A bright, bold print summer dress is the ideal piece for those special nights out. If you are still putting together your trip itinerary, you might find these [tips for planning your honeymoon](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-planning-your-honeymoon/) incredibly helpful as you finalize your travel schedule.
