# 5 Adorable Ring Bearers and Their Cute Suits ...

**Little boys in suits** or **tuxedos** are just so **handsome** with their slicked back hair and their little ties. As the **ring bearer** walks down the aisle they look so proud and honored to help out for your big day. If you're considering whether you should have that special kid hold your ring pillow let these **5 adorable ring bearers in their cute little suits **persuade your answer!

## 5. Little Boys do Silly Things...

![Little Boys do Silly Things...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/ringbearer_407x271.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

They're **adorable** but can be unpredictable sometimes, which makes for [funny photos](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault) like this one! **Little boys** like to get messy, get into trouble, and [make everyone laugh](https://allwomenstalk.com/). I love the [yellow bow ties](https://goo.gl/E2c0jd) and the [cool mustaches](https://goo.gl/AjaAQy)! These boys are just too cool and aren't afraid to show off! **How many cool little boy ring bearers are in your wedding? **

## 4. They Don't Mind Wearing Pink...

![They Don't Mind Wearing Pink...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/pinkboy_400x600.jpg)

**Ring Bearers** will wear what you put them in because [boys](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-reasons-to-have-kids-in-your-wedding/) aren't as picky about **clothes** like little girls! This little boy is **so proud** to be responsible for the **rings**, how cute is he holding them up in his hand. His [pink tie](https://www.boyssuitsandtuxedos.com/boys-ties-boys-bowties/fuschia_tie_for_boys.html) looks so cute with the [gray suit](https://goo.gl/7OrdFN)and[little chuck taylors](https://goo.gl/Yy5I5w)! **What colors will your ring bearer wear? **

Hopefully he made his way down the aisle in his [handsome dress shirt](https://goo.gl/Xu0742) and [pants](https://goo.gl/7oKBas). **Was your ring bearer nervous on your big day? **

## 2. They Love the Attention...

![They Love the Attention...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/ceremony-flower-girl-ring-bearer_402x305.jpg)

Photo: [ChicagoIllinoisWeddingPhotography.com](https://www.chicagoillinoisweddingphotography.com/tag/gorgeous/)

On your wedding day it will help the shy kids if you have more than one child walking down the aisle for your ceremony. **Kids** like to hang out and play together and **little ring bearers** love the **attention** they get from the cute [flower girls](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-adorable-flower-girls-and-their-pretty-dresses/)! This charming little boy seems shy at first but as soon as the flower girl helps out he warms right up to the camera. I love his [tuxedo](https://web.archive.org/web/http://kidsformal.com/boys-shaw-lapel-tuxedo-p/146fg.htm) and his [shiny little dress shoes](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.dsw.com/?referer=shoes.com)!
