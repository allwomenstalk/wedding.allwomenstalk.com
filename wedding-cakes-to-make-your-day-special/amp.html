Frequently Asked Questions

What are some popular wedding cake flavors for 2024?
Classic flavors like vanilla, chocolate, and red velvet remain popular, along with modern favorites like lemon raspberry and salted caramel.
How far in advance should I order my wedding cake?
It's best to order your wedding cake at least 3-6 months before the big day to ensure availability and customization.
Can I customize the design of my wedding cake?
Absolutely! Most bakers offer full customization to match your theme, colors, and personal style.
How many tiers should my wedding cake have?
The number of tiers depends on your guest count and style; typically, 2-4 tiers suffice for most weddings.
Are there gluten-free or vegan wedding cake options?
Yes, many bakers provide gluten-free, vegan, or other dietary-friendly options without sacrificing taste.
What is the average cost of a wedding cake?
Prices vary widely, but most wedding cakes range from $3 to $10 per slice depending on design complexity.
How do I keep my wedding cake fresh on the day?
Keep the cake in a cool, dry place and avoid direct sunlight until serving time.
Can I taste the cake before ordering?
Many bakeries offer tasting sessions to help you choose your favorite flavors and fillings.
What are some trendy wedding cake decorations?
Fresh flowers, metallic accents, hand-painted details, and minimalist designs are very trendy right now.
How do I transport my wedding cake safely?
Use a sturdy box, keep the cake chilled if possible, and have a designated person handle transportation carefully.
