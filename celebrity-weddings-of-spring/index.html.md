# 5 Celebrity Weddings of Spring 2011 ...

Who doesn't love a beautiful **celebrity wedding**? I know I'm a sucker for finding out about the gown the bride wore, who was in their bridal party, and all the gorgeous details from their big day. Some celebs get married and you're not surprised at how fast they end up [spliting](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-celebrity-couples-who-raced-up-the-aisle/). But just for one moment, one special day, you would like to think they can have it all...and live happily ever after...

## 5. A Country Celebration....

![A Country Celebration....](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/Miranda-Lambert-Wedding-Dress_465x576.jpg)

Photo: [Since1910](http://www.since1910.com/engagement-ring-blog/) via [UsMagazine](https://www.usmagazine.com/stylish/news/see-miranda-lamberts-wedding-dress-2011185/)

[Miranda Lambert](https://www.mirandalambert.com/)and [Blake Shelton's](https://www.blakeshelton.com/)rustic, **country wedding **is one of my favorite celebrity weddings this year! She wore her **mother's gown** for the wedding ceremony saying it would give her good luck since her parents have been married 33 years....aww! How much do you love that he wore a suit jacket and jeans...country hot!

## 4. Aqua & Yellow...

![Aqua & Yellow...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/Sara-Rue-Wedding-Pictures_468x468.jpg)

Photos: [PopSugar](https://www.popsugar.com/)

I have been a fan of[Sara Rue's](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0749081/)since she was in the teen show["Popular"](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0202748/)and now I can't believe how much weight she's lost with [Jenny Craig](https://www.jennycraig.com/)! How much do you love her wedding colors, aqua and yellow. Her stunning dress is from [Amsale](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.amsale.com/)and she even wore **something blue **heels by Aaron Smith.

## 3. Sister, Sister...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/05/celebwedding_464x480.bmp)

Photos: [CelebrityWeddingBuzz](https://web.archive.org/web/https://maintenance.theknot.com/)

We loved watching [Tamera Mowry](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005255/)and her twin sister, [Tia](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0610298/)on the hit show,["Sister, Sister"](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0108927/) and now we watch as they grow up, get married, and have babies! First it was Tia who got hitched but now she's a** pregnant bridesmaid **in her sister Tamera's gorgeous wedding. How adorable is their little dog as **ring bearer **and check out groom [Adam Housley's](https://www.foxnews.com/person/personalities)**converse sneakers**!

## 2. Kings of Leon...

![Kings of Leon...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/caleb-and-lily-wedding_445x615.jpg)

Photo: [CelebrityWeddingBuzz](https://web.archive.org/web/https://maintenance.theknot.com/)

A rock star and a[Victoria Secret's](https://web.archive.org/web/http://vsallaccess.victoriassecret.com/2011/04/25/sexy-little-bride-in-the-big-apple/)model....a [celebrity wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/biggest-celebrity-weddings/)doesn't get any cooler then this! [Kings of Leon](http://kingsofleon.com/)leader singer Caleb Followill married Lily Aldridge, one of Victoria's angels, in a beautiful garden wedding on May 12. The rocker wore a **[Gucci](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.gucci.com/us/home)**suit as the bride walked down the aisle in [Vera Wang](https://www.verawang.com/).

## 1. Third Times the Charm...

![Third Times the Charm...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/michael-buble-wedding-pictures-05_458x685.jpg)

Photo: [JustJared](https://web.archive.org/web/http://justjared.buzznet.com/)

Some [couples](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-celebrity-couples-who-recently-said-yes/)celebrate their **wedding day **with one small, intimate ceremony and sometimes later with a second, much larger reception with out of town friends and family members. But [Michael Buble](https://www.michaelbuble.com/higher)and wife [Luisana Lopilato](https://luisanalopilato.com/?v=7516fd43adaa)were not only married twice, but three times! Their first wedding day was a small civil ceremony in March, then later in April they partied with 300 guests in Buenos Aires (where the bride is from), and now on May 20th they danced with another 350 guests in Buble's hometown in Canada!
