# 5 Unique Things for Your Bridesmaids to Carry ...

Maybe you're a **non-traditional** **couple** or maybe you just want something other than flowers for your **bridesmaids**. Your wedding should be personalized with your **style**, so dare to be different with these **5 unique things for your bridesmaids to carry down the aisle...**

## 5. Walk by Candlelight...

![Walk by Candlelight...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/masonjar1_404x269.jpg)

Photo: [GlamourandGraceWeddings.blogspot.com](http://glamourandgraceweddings.blogspot.com/)

For your outdoor, [rustic wedding](https://web.archive.org/web/http://everylastdetailblog.com/real-wedding/real-rustic-florida-wedding/#)these candlelight [mason jars](https://goo.gl/sLNiX2)are the perfect touch to set the **romantic** mood. For all those [bridesmaids](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-dresses-for-every-spring-2011-bridesmaid/)with allergies, show your **bride** this cool idea for her big day! **Are your ladies carrying something unique? **

## 4. Get Carried Away...

![Get Carried Away...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/Bridesmaids-and-balloons_400x267.jpg)

Photo: [AislinnEvents.com](https://aislinnevents.com/tag/real-weddings/)

**Celebrate** your big day with [bright balloons](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.wishspecialevents.com/blog/2009/07/wedding-balloon-ideas/)as you put a **smile on your bridesmaids** and all of your **guests**! Bring the **trend** from the [engagement shoot](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-sexy-engagement-shoots-to-love/)right into your wedding day with **balloons** of fun random colors or ones that match your wedding decor. **Will this trend fit into your wedding theme?**

## 3. No Fuss with a Clutch...

![No Fuss with a Clutch...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/cluthes_403x258.jpg)

Photo: [Simplyinspireddesigns.blogspot.com](http://simplyinspireddesigns.blogspot.com/)

A **clutch** is a great [bridesmaid gift idea](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-ideal-bridal-party-gifts/) as well as something cool for your **ladies to carry down the aisle**. I just love how **classy** and timeless these **bridesmaids** look with their [clutch](https://goo.gl/kCBQHY), black dresses, and beautiful pearls. **Are your bridesmaids getting a fancy gift? **

## 2. Your Biggest Wedding Fans...

![Your Biggest Wedding Fans...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/fans_405x270.jpg)

Photo: [CarmenWeddings.co.uk/blog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.carmenweddings.co.uk/blog/)

How adorable are these **bridesmaids** hiding their faces with their beautiful[chinese fans](https://goo.gl/skEqh7)! **Keep your girls cool** for your [summer wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-beach-weddings-to-inspire-your-summer-wedding/) with this idea as they** fan themselves** on that hot August day. I love the little touch of flowers on their wrist; a great idea for **brides** who want to have a floral wedding but have **creative ideas** as well.

## 1. Feel like a Kid Again...

![Feel like a Kid Again...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/pinwheel_406x277.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/Rule42](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Plan a [carnival theme](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/07/15/carnival-themed-wedding-inspiration/) as your guests eat popcorn and cotton candy, play fun games for big prizes and skip down the aisle with your **bridesmaids** carrying hot pink [pinwheel bouquets](https://www.marthastewart.com/272283/paper-pinwheels). **Everyone will feel like** **a kid again** and will never forget the [memories](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-wedding-themes/)they made at your [awesome offbeat wedding](https://web.archive.org/web/https://goo.gl/PK2BcL).

**Do you have a unique item for your bridesmaids to carry? **
