# 8 Ideas for an Emerald Themed Wedding ...

Pantone has done it again with their pick for color of the year, sending brides rushing to find Emerald Themed Wedding ideas for their big day. It's a rich jewel tone that will look gorgeous for a Winter or Spring wedding with succulent bouquets and sparkling green wedding shoes. So tell us, are you planning an Emerald Themed Wedding this year or next?

## 1. Emerald Wedding Invitation...

_ You're planning a wedding with a striking, rich color, so why not use it in every detail possible? Start by sharing a sneak peek of your Emerald Themed Wedding with your guests with a gorgeous, bold invitation like this one by weddingpaperdivas.com_.

## 2. Emerald Engagement Ring...

![Emerald Engagement Ring...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/2_emerald-engagement-ring_398x235.jpg)

Dare to be different when considering what your engagement ring will look like. If you really love green, go all out and rock a stone that will match your Emerald Themed Wedding. With a ring like this one, everyone will be sure to grab your hand for a look when you tell them you're engaged!

If you are a one of a kind bride who is already rocking an emerald engagement ring, complete your look from head to toe with these gorgeous heels for your Emerald Themed Wedding. Shop at heels.com_ for these and many other shoes for your big day.

## 6. Emerald Wedding Cake...

![Emerald Wedding Cake...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/6_emerald-wedding-cake_600x900.jpg)

Wow, what a pretty cake, it looks too pretty to eat! From your ring to your dress and now an amazing Emerald Themed Wedding cake, your guests will be head over heels in love with your entire wedding day. I love the mix of the soft, white ruffles with the emerald fondant, a great combination of colors for a winter wedding.

## 7. Emerald Wedding Decor...

![Emerald Wedding Decor...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/7_emerald-wedding-decor_399x600.jpg)

I've always loved the idea of using wine bottles for table numbers, especially when you can get double use out of them as favors for your guests. Simple little details like green wine bottle decor for your Emerald Themed Wedding will make a big impact when you are already wearing green shoes and a green dress. You don't need to go over the top on your decor when you are planning a wedding with a strong, rich color like emerald green.

## 8. Emerald Wedding Bouquet...

![Emerald Wedding Bouquet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/8_emerald-wedding-bouquet_400x600.jpg)

I am obsessed with succulent wedding bouquets and love the subtle hint of greenery in this one. A green bridal bouquet combined with softer colors will compliment your Emerald Themed Wedding dress perfectly. If you are wearing a white gown like the bride pictured here, consider an all green bouquet filled with succulents, green cymbidium orchids, and green amaranthus.

When planning a [wedding theme](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/country-rustic-wedding-theme-ideas/) around a color, especially one as rich as emerald, you want to remember that less is more. If you are wearing a sparkling green wedding dress for your Emerald Wedding Theme, you may want to wear subtler shoes. Has the color of 2013 inspired your big day?
