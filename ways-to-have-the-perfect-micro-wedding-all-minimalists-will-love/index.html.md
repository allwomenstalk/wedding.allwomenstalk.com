# Minimalist Loving Tips to Have the Perfect Micro\-Wedding ...

When I got married, I had a really, tiny wedding. I loved it! I thought it was intimate, beautiful and awesome. If you are thinking of having a tiny wedding, it's cool! These ideas will totally help you plan. Promise!

## 1. Keep the Décor Simple

Really, the decor isn't the focus!

## 2. Don't Get Caught in the Details

It's a small wedding, there are LOTS of details, but don't get too caught up!

## 3. Consider the Seating

The seating will be beautiful, no matter how many people come.

## 4. And the Location

The location is most important. Small wedding means you can have it anywhere!

## 5. Table Décor Can Be Elaborate

Not as many tables to consider!

## 6. You Can Add Your Own Flare to It

![You Can Add Your Own Flare to It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m1/ti/pu4lecng5737d7fc7b53e103401077_600x600.jpg)

And it'll still be affordable because there aren't so many guests!

## 7. Remember to Keep the Cake Smaller

![Remember to Keep the Cake Smaller](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yf/gs/qth24gv65737d7ee4cbaf466115777_600x600.jpg)

We still had a three tier for thirty-five people, but it was worth it.

## 8. ...or You Can Go with Cupcakes

![...or You Can Go with Cupcakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yt/mc/kuj68tue5737d7b329770278524610_469x469.jpg)

Or ... cupcakes are an option.

## 9. If Your Wedding is Really Small …

![If Your Wedding is Really Small …](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/ku/k0if3k6x5737d6df85146598206892_600x600.jpg)

Why not go with one table?

## 10. You Can Be More Carefree in Planning

![You Can Be More Carefree in Planning](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oy/q7/ztopnef65737d7a01fb5d653031186_500x500.jpg)

Don't sweat the small stuff love! It's not a two-hundred person wedding.

## 11. It's All about You, as a Couple

![It's All about You, as a Couple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r9/zh/swbl0n285737d792afe5e370284940_500x500.jpg)

No matter what your style is, remember, your wedding is about you.

## 12. Better Music Choices!

![Better Music Choices!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/c1/vs2vptei5737d8118cb95176012041_500x500.jpg)
