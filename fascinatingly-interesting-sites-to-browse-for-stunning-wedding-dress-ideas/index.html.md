# 8 Fascinatingly Interesting Sites to Browse for Stunning Wedding Dress Ideas ...

Browsing for Wedding Dress Ideas is the first step of finding that ideal wedding gown and although some brides insist on the fact that they’ve tried only one or two dresses before they’ve found the perfect one, I believe that being prepared and having a few wedding dress ideas is not a shabby plan either. Sure, trying on wedding dresses is fun, but I the process is waaay less complicated if you know what you want. Don’t you agree? Well, take a look at these amazing websites right here and you’ll find many fabulous wedding dress ideas to help you get started:

## 1. Maggie Sottero

**Website:** [maggiesottero.com](https://www.maggiesottero.com/)
Luxurious and not a bit over the top – any Maggie Sottero dress will make you look like a princess! This website is a must-visit if you’re looking for wedding dress ideas to get you in that bridal-mood and, after checking out everything this wonderful website has to offer, you’ll definitely feel the need to start trying on bridal gowns like there's no tomorrow.

## 2. Demetrios

**Website:** [demetriosbride.com](https://www.demetrios.com/)
If your dream dress should be sexy, elegant and rich in embellishments, I’d strongly advise you to have high hopes in Demetrios dresses! They are a bit over-the-top but hey, if that’s what the bride wants than that’s what the bride should have! Right? Take a look even if you’re not a fan of crystal-embellished gowns because you’ll definitely need all the wedding dress ideas you can find.

## 3. Cymbeline

**Website:** [cymbelineusa.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.cymbelineusa.com/)
Looking for something glam, classy but low-key, something whimsical or maybe something totally traditional? Well, Cybeline Paris is the next place to look for wedding dress ideas and I simply must add that I’m sure you’ll soon find your favorites right here.

## 4. Pronovias

**Website:** [pronovias.us](https://www.pronovias.com/)
Ah, Pronovias! I might as well change my name to match the brand because I literally feel like they are finding their wedding dress ideas in my dreams and turning them into reality! Most Pronovias dresses are chic, totally feminine and interestingly low in embellishments (especially when compared to Demetrios gowns) which gives them a sort of a classic, everlasting beauty every bride-to-be instantly recognizes instantly.

## 5. David’s Bridal

**Website:** [davidsbridal.com](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
David’s Bridal is an online shop you can use even if your plan isn’t to re-stock on fresh wedding dress ideas but to buy a dress instead! In fact, if you’re looking for an affordable wedding gown (or wedding accessories), I’d definitely suggest checking out sale and outlet sections because you’ll find many interesting, extremely budget-friendly solutions.

## 6. Pnina Tornai

**Website:** [pninatornai.com](https://www.pninatornai.com/)
Famous for her sexy yet glamorous dresses, Pnina Tornai is a designer whose dresses you simply must check out if you want a lace-up corset gown that’s going to make your bust look like it never looked before! Crystals, see-through lace, ruffles, tiers, layers and layers or tulle – her dresses might be over-the-top for some, but they will definitely make sure that you’re the star of the show!

## 7. Kleinfeld

**Website:** [kleinfeldbridal.com](https://www.kleinfeldbridal.com/)
If you’re looking forward to taking a peek at a few new fabulous Bagdley Mischka, Lanvin or Lazaro styles, definitely visit the most popular bridal salon ever – Kleinfeld! You’ll get to choose between dozens of well and not so well known designers and hundreds of gorgeous bridal gowns! Now, if that's not enough to get your creative juices flowing, I don’t know what is!

## 8. Priscilla of Boston

**Website:** [priscillaofboston.com](https://beta.davidsbridal.com/)
Dresses you’re see here are the perfect mix of modern and extravagant, classic and chic and I’m sure you’ll find at least one gown that’s gorgeous enough to end up on your “maybe” list. Definitely a good site to browse for wedding dress ideas, so don’t make your final choice before you’re finished with your research!

How many websites did you have to check before you’ve realized that you’ll need to get married every year in order to use all those lovely wedding dress ideas you have? LOL! Most of my wedding dress ideas are actually Pronovias dresses but since I have 10 long comfy months until the actual big day, I still can’t guarantee my initial choice will be my final one as well.
