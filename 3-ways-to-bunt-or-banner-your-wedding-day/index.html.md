# 3 Ways to Bunt or Banner Your Wedding Day ...

Whether you call it "**bunting**" or you're looking for a wedding "**banner**", this **DIY wedding style** is a big trend that is catching on everywhere. And who doesn't like to have a cute, colorful banner full of flags to make things more bright and delightful! Here are 3 ways to bunt or banner your wedding day...

## 3. Pies & a Sweets Table...

![Pies & a Sweets Table...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/op/l1/ndsdw32p54c8f9db49719983053843_600x800.jpg)

Photos: [NessaKPhotography](http://nessakphotography.com/blog/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/02/08/austin-wedding-by-nessa-k-photography/)

First I have to tell you that I was inspired by the beautiful **bunting** over at a wedding posted on[Love and Lavender](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.loveandlavender.com/2011/03/gielie-and-ilanas-wedding/comment-page-1/), you have to see this adorable wedding! And now on to this wedding with **pies**, **a sweets table**, and a lovely bride and groom! They covered all details of their wedding with fun bunting and **banners**, I just love the pie! **Bunting **can take your wedding from drab to fab with little work, that's what's so great about it!

## 2. Laterns & Bunting...

![Laterns & Bunting...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xi/gh/h9asc39m54c8f9dce5eae235650876_600x800.jpg)

Photos: [SutherlandKovachStudio](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.sutherlandkovach.com/index2.php?v=v1)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/04/01/new-zealand-wedding-with-colorful-style/)

The woods, the **colorful bunting**, the dog; this wedding is just too cute! I love the **whimsical** feel of this beautiful outdoor New Zealand wedding, which took place at the bride's parents home at [Brick Bay](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.brickbay.co.nz/). With the bunting, the colorful matching **laterns** add fun and **cheerful details** to the outdoor setting.

## 1. Twine and Love...

![Twine and Love...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tp/lg/jy0rm9sf54c8f9de90404535075600_600x800.jpg)

Photos: [ErinHeartsCourt](http://erinheartscourt.com/blog/) via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/09/23/colorado-wedding-by-erin-hearts-court/)

There are so many wonderful details in all three of these beautiful weddings, and so much more than just **bunting**. Like this lovely couple who had several [bunting flags](https://goo.gl/zAVFXE)indoors and out as well as fun red and white **twine **that tied their [invitations](https://design-circus.com/)together. I love the photo of them kissing underneath the umbrella with the [love bunting sign](https://goo.gl/ZC2Yg5)...so romantic!

**Have you found a love for bunting and banners to detail your wedding day?**
