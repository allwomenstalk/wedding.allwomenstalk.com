# 4 Sweet & Simple Wedding Bouquets ...

Your **wedding bouquet** speaks a lot about who you are. It gives your guests a preview of what your wedding reception will look like and sets the tone of your wedding atmosphere. If you're an easy going, no frills **bride** you may find your wedding flowers among these **4 sweet & simple wedding bouquets...**

## 4. Sweet & Pink...

![Sweet & Pink...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/pinkroses_408x614.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/tag/bouquets)

**Simple**, **Sweet** and **Stunning**. Beautiful **pink roses **and a few accent pieces of [dusty miller](https://www.gardenguides.com/124154-information-dusty-miller-plants.html) and you have one remarkable bridal bouquet. You don't need ten different flowers to make a **statement**. Find your [favorite flower](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-best-flowers-for-your-wedding/) and turn it into a **full bouquet**.

## 3. Tropical Paradise...

![Tropical Paradise...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/orchids_401x600.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/tag/bouquets)

A** simple bouquet** of one flower doesn't have to be dull or boring, it can be amazing and **tropical** like these [white Phalaenopsis orchids](https://rhondapattonweddings.typepad.com/my_weblog/2009/07/pretty-phalenopsis.html). I love these **flowers** for a [summer beach wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-beach-weddings-to-inspire-your-summer-wedding/). Who knew that a simple bouquet could be so **breathtaking**!

## 2. Every Bride's Favorite Flower...

![Every Bride's Favorite Flower...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/whitepeony_400x266.jpg)

Photo: [BellaFiori-events.blogspot.com](http://bellafiori-events.blogspot.com/)

**Peonies** are a lovely flower that every **bride** adores. They can be [bold and bright](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-hot-color-palette-trends-for-your-2011-wedding/) like the pink ones in the top photo or they can be sweet and simple like this **white peony bouquet**. I love the touch of [sparkling rhinestones](https://goo.gl/z7qYlq) on the **bouquet handle**, it gives the bride a touch of **glamour** for her no frills bouquet!

## 1. The Most Simple Are the Most Sweet...

![The Most Simple Are the Most Sweet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/babysbreathe_407x609.jpg)

Photo: [HollyChappleFlowers.typepad.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://hollychappleflowers.typepad.com/thefullbouquet/)

**Delicate**, **Romantic**, and **Simple**. I love the look of a **baby's breath wedding bouquet**. They have been mostly used in bouquets as **fillers** and accents, but in recent years baby's breath has become the** show stopping** full bouquet. They are lovely for all [weddings](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-unique-wedding-bouquets-that-will-last-forever/) but perfect for outdoor, **garden** **weddings**.
