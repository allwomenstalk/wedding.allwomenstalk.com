# 5 Movies about Love Weddings & Marriage I Cant Wait to See!

This month there are three **movies** coming out about **love, weddings, and marriage** that I can't wait to see and two others coming out later this year about the same topic. These movies may all have weddings as a theme, but they are all very different. Some are very funny, while others are more romantic, and one or two seem to be a little dramatic. Which one will you head to the theater for?

## 5. Something Borrowed...

![Something Borrowed...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/something-borrowed-movie-poster_427x623.jpg)

Was there ever that guy who you really liked, fell head over heels for, but never told him about it? Well in [Something Borrowed](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0491152/), [Ginnifer Goodwin's](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0329481/)character Rachel, always had a crush on Dex ([Colin Egglesfield](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1148573/), from All My Children). But now Dex is marrying Rachel's best friend Darcy ([Kate Hudson](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005028/)), and when she realizes how much she loves him things start to unravel! [John Krasinski](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1024677/)plays Ethan, who is friends with both ladies. I wonder if Darcy will fall for him! Sounds like a big love triangle.

## 4. Jumping the Broom...

![Jumping the Broom...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/jumping-the-broom-movie-poster-_426x632.jpg)

We have all had those moments when we weren't sure how the in-laws would turn out and if our mother and mother-in-law would get along! [Jumping the Broom](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1640484/)brings those elements and cultural traditions to life in this **dramatic comedy** starring [Paula Patton](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1745736/)as the bride, [Laz Alonso](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0022306/)as the groom, [Angela Bassett](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000291/)as the uptown and uptight mother of the bride, and [Loretta Devine](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0222643/)as the downtown, wise cracking mother of the groom. If these two families can get along, I'm sure yours can too!

## 3. Bridesmaids....

![Bridesmaids....](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/bridesmaids-movie-poster_420x624.jpg)

This is a movie to take your ladies to and laugh all day long about the **funny** things these crazy, wacky **bridesmaids** get themselves into! With a cast full of funny actresses, [Bridesmaids](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1478338/)is sure to be a big hit at the box office. I love [Maya Rudolph](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0748973/)from SNL, she plays the unknowing bride, while [Kristen Wiig](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1325419/), also an [SNL](https://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live)alum, plays her maid of honor.

## 2. Love, Wedding, Marriage...

![Love, Wedding, Marriage...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/love-wedding-marriage-movie-poster_430x637.jpg)

[Mandy Moore](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0601553/)always plays the sweetheart girl who we can relate to and connect with. In [Love, Wedding, Marriage](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1436559/), she plays Eva, a recently married marriage counselor who is trying to fix her parents' marriage while ignoring the problems of her own. [Kellan Lutz](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1553725/), from [Twilight](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1099212/), plays her new husband, who may have a few dirty secrets up his sexy sleeves! Weddings may be picture perfect, but no one said marriage ever was.

## 1. Twilight: Breaking Dawn...

![Twilight: Breaking Dawn...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/twilight_432x290.jpg)

I may not be a crazy fanatic like some people, but I do love **Twilight** and the romance of Edward and Bella. And what better way to pull me in even more than to celebrate their love with a **wedding**! In [Twilight: Breaking Dawn](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1324999/) Bella and Edward get married, go on a honeymoon, and well you know what comes after that, right! I don't want to tell too many secrets and things I wish I hadn't found out yet, so take the dramatic ride on November 18th for a movie that never has enough twists and turns!

**Do you have a favorite among these five movies?**
