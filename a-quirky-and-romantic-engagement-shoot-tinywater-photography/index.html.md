# A Quirky and Romantic Engagement Shoot: TinyWater Photography ...

There are so many ideas you can create for your** engagement shoot** with props, locations, attire, and even pets! But sometimes the best idea is to just **bring yourselves**; bring your **romantic**, **quirky**, **unique** personalities and the rest will come together naturally. All the reason why I fell in love with this **shoot** from [TinyWater Photography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/engagement/modern-baker-beach-engagement-session/?subid1=20220717-2344-28b6-a2ef-6d69c03616e5)!

## 6. Snuggling on the Beach...

![Snuggling on the Beach...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-026_420x281.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220717-2344-221e-84f9-22d3cc3a2a95)

Megan and Kelvin were in **San Francisco** visiting family members and decided to take this oppurtunity to take some amazing photos at [Baker Beach](https://www.nps.gov/prsf/planyourvisit/baker-beach.htm)for their **engagement session**. Caroline from [TinyWater](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/engagement/modern-baker-beach-engagement-session/?subid1=20220718-0014-1243-960a-2ade1bf0e2c2) knew that this was the perfect place for this **quirky** but **romantic** couple who had lived in the bay area but have since moved to New York.

## 5. Jumping for Joy...

![Jumping for Joy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-004_420x281.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220717-2344-34a8-9462-a2983c05f757)

Sometimes you may need to **warm up** at the idea of** taking photos** and taking them with someone you may not know. **Dance around**,** jump in the air**, and just wiggle around to get those nervous jitters out of your system! They may have serious jobs, Kelvin's a neuroscience researcher and Megan's a soon to be family doctor, but they sure know how to **shake it out** and** jump for joy**!

## 4. Being Silly...

![Being Silly...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-002_420x92.jpg)

![Being Silly...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-017_420x92.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220717-2344-3664-beb0-0c2820ab3903)

I love these sets of photos and how the **photographers** at [TinyWater](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220718-0014-1415-8130-8667d467936c) displayed them on their blog like a photobooth film strip. In the first set Kelvin is showing off his** martial arts skills**! How much do you love the second set here with their **adorable funny faces**! Be silly like them and get out of your comfort zone when taking your** engagement pictures**. Funny is always better than stuffy!

## 3. Just Chillin'...

![Just Chillin'...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-010_420x281.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220718-0014-2738-bcd9-c86a8cecbc1e)

**Explore** your neighborhood or maybe suggest a** favorite spot** from your first date to your photographer for your shoot. If you're not sure where to go let your photographer know what you're thinking for theme or details. I love seeing **architectural shots** like this one, sometimes all you need is a** cool spot** with a **doorway**!

## 2. Go Climb a Tree...

![Go Climb a Tree...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-020_420x630.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220718-0014-30b4-8837-489b0148280d)

How amazing is [Baker Beach](https://www.parksconservancy.org/parks/baker-beach), again so perfect for Megan and Kelvin who love the **outdoors**. Theres a **beach**, a **grove full of trees**, and so many elements that are great for **couples** who just want to **relax, **take photos and **sit in a tree**! There is no need to dress up in costume, bring props, or figure things out when you have a gorgeous location like this one.

## 1. Peace & Love...

![Peace & Love...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/modern-san-francisco-engagement-034_420x630.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220718-0014-336a-be72-65dab0ec37ca)

When there is **love**, beautiful **smiles**, and **couples** like these two you know the **photographer** and the bride and groom are going to have an amazing time! **Congratulations** to Megan and Kelvin, we can't wait to see photos of your June 11th **wedding** at the[Academy of Sciences](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.calacademy.org/visit/plan_an_event/wedding/).

**Special Thanks to Caroline** @ [TinyWaterPhotography](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/?subid1=20220719-0040-201e-9ce8-3a29f1105d9d)!
