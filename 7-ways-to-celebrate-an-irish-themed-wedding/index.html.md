# 7 Ways to Celebrate an Irish Themed Wedding ...

**St. Patty's Day** is March 17th and it gives us every excuse to party and celebrate so why not celebrate your wedding day with the** luck of the Irish**! There are so many ways you can go about decorating and accessorizing your **Irish theme**. You can go all out from top to bottom or give little hints of the holiday and heritage you love. Inspired by Nan and Dan @ [Dancy Days](http://dancydaysny.blogspot.com/)for their heritage and month long celebration...here are 7 ways to celebrate your **Irish Themed Wedding...**

## 7. Green Bridesmaids...

![Green Bridesmaids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/32230907529341CARMEL_535x714.jpg)

Photo: [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/831/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Siri)

This adorable **bridesmaid dress** is just the most beautiful color of **green**. It's so perfect for your **Irish Themed Wedding** that the name of the green color is **Ireland**! The bow on the shoulder is such a lovely touch on this [Siri](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.siriinc.com/br-9341.html)cocktail dress. There's even hidden side pockets! What a fun dress.

## 6. An Irish Gift...

![An Irish Gift...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/il_570xN_224228599_400x400.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/Vsc83](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

When I saw this unique **Dublin map locket** I knew I had to include it in my round-up. This would be a wonderful **gift** for the **mother of the bride** with a photo of the new couple inside. Or let your sweetheart know that you would like this as a **bridal gift** to celebrate your **heritage**!

## 5. A Green Wedding Dress...

![A Green Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/emerald-green-wedding-dress-02_410x260.jpg)

Photo: [Susan Dean](https://susandeanphoto.com/)via [GreenWeddingShoes](https://web.archive.org/web/https://greenweddingshoes.com/wedding-fashion-inspiration-from-the-red-carpet-the-golden-globes/)

Who says the **bride** has to wear white? I love the **emerald color** of the bride's dress; she rocks it well! Let your guests know how happy you are about **St. Patrick's Day** and make everyone green with envy when they see how gorgeous you are!

## 4. Irish Cupcakes...

![Irish Cupcakes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/St_-P-cupcakes_400x300.jpg)

Photo: [MyBakingAddiction](https://www.mybakingaddiction.com/happy-st-patricks-day/)

**Green icing**, **green cupcake wrappers**, and little **green shamrocks**, I wonder what you might be celebrating! Maybe you don't want to go all out with a green wedding dress or green bridesmaids but would like to show a little **Irish love**. With these little **shamrock cupcakes** you'll have just the right amount of love shown at your wedding reception for **St. Patty's Day**!

## 3. Save the Date...

![Save the Date...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/il_570xN_213785616_401x361.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/AngelEllie](https://goo.gl/5VTQ6X)

If you're going to plan an **Irish Themed Wedding** let your guests know all about your idea with the cool **magnet save the date**. You can order a set of 20 for $37.00 and **customize **the top photo with a shamrock, an Irish Claddagh, or many other designs.

## 2. Irish Cake Topper...

![Irish Cake Topper...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/il_570xN_197061820_411x397.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/RedLightStudio](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

How cute is this **Irish** themed **cake topper**! I really love how creative artists are when couples request a special order like this. The **groom's** little **kilt** just makes me smile every time I see it, you just know the bride and groom were so happy when they saw this in the mail!

## 1. Tradition...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/03/irish1_412x275.bmp)

Photo: [Luster Studios](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.lusterstudios.com/)via [IntimateWeddings](https://www.intimateweddings.com/blog/real-weddings-christine-tims-celtic-wedding-in-nc/)

Are you a fan of **St. Patrick's Day** just because you get to wear a cute green shamrock shirt and drink beer all day? Or do you love celebrating your **Irish heritage to carry on traditions and history? I love the beautiful** celtic dress this bride is wearing as well as the **traditional kilt** her groom has on. Will your spouse walk down the aisle in attire that represents **customs** of their family's culture?
