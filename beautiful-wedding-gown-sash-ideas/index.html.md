# 9 Beautiful Wedding Gown Sash Ideas ...

While you can never go wrong with a classic white dress, tying on a pretty wedding gown sash can add the perfect extra touch of color, sparkle and personality to your already stunning ensemble. Thinking of wearing a wedding gown sash, but not quite sure which style is right for you? Check out these nine ideas for a quick glimpse at just a handful of the many gorgeous designs available.

## 1. Bright & Bold

![Bright & Bold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/1_bright-bold_600x398.jpg)

Bold and sassy, a wedding gown sash with colorful floral details is just right for the bride who wants to add a colorful and non-traditional element of fun to her look. I think a sash like this would be a great choice for a chic outdoor summer celebration. It would also look every bit as eye-catching in photos as in person.

## 2. Classic White

![Classic White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/2_classic-white_600x399.jpg)

Like the idea of wearing a sash, but prefer one that’s a bit more classic and less trendy? A white floral sash adds a more understated, romantic vibe that’s not the least bit overpowering. Of course, some wedding gowns even come with a pretty sash already attached, so be sure to consider these styles along with tie-on options when shopping.

## 3. Colorful Rosettes

![Colorful Rosettes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/3_colorful-rosettes_600x400.jpg)

There’s nothing like a whimsical sash featuring a set of multicolored rosettes to help dress up a simple wedding gown. Prefer to skip the sash and focus more attention on your hair? These types of rosettes would also be a beautiful addition to a headband or fascinator.

## 4. Something Blue

Still on the hunt for your something blue? Consider a simple ribbon sash in a shade that complements your color scheme. If that seems too basic, you can also look for one featuring additional lovely details like rosettes or feathers. Tying the bow off to the side in front also adds an extra touch of sweetness.

## 5. Elegant Crystals

![Elegant Crystals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/6_bejeweled_600x400.jpg)

Brooches have recently become a popular alternative to traditional flowers in bridal bouquets, and affixing a crystal one to a sash can easily add an instant dose of glamour. Whether the sash already comes with a brooch attached or you have the option of adding your own, get creative and choose whichever combination of colors and designs best suits your personal style and wedding theme.

## 6. Bejeweled

When your dress needs a little extra bling, a bejeweled sash certainly does the trick. Looking for one that includes crystals? Beads? Pearls? With so many stunning possibilities, the end result is entirely up to you!

## 7. Peacock Feathers

![Peacock Feathers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/7_peacock-feathers_600x450.jpg)

A popular design motif at weddings throughout the past several years, peacock feathers give your sash a classy, vintage feel. Best of all, they can easily be paired with other types of feathers in various shades, along with beading, rhinestones and more.

## 8. Intricate Beading

![Intricate Beading](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/8_intricate-beading_600x449.jpg)

Speaking of beading, why not choose a sash that uses it to create an intricate and truly unique design? A sash like this strikes me as being ideal for a beautiful outdoor southwestern wedding. Do you agree? Where could you see it being worn?

## 9. Chic Drama

![Chic Drama](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/07/9_chic-drama_600x517.jpg)

Complete with sparkling crystals, an ebony sash adds the ultimate touch of drama to your day. Do you like this sash, or would you pick one in a cheerier shade?

As you can see, sashes come in all sorts of designs and sizes, making it easy and fun to find the one you love best! Are you planning to walk down the aisle wearing a wedding gown sash? What type of style will you choose?
