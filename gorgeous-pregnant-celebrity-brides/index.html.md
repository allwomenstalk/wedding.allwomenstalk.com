# 7 Gorgeous Pregnant Celebrity Brides ...

Sometimes brides get engaged and have a little surprise before they walk down the aisle and in many cases they like to keep it a secret if they can. Some of these **pregnant celebrity brides** embraced their bump while others tried to find a dress that would cover up their impending baby news. Here are** 7 Gorgeous Pregnant Celebrity Brides** who were glowing as mommies-to-be...

## 1. Drew Barrymore...

![Drew Barrymore...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/07/163396292701270973_hlye00DJ_c_434x316.jpg)

Source: [Reel Life with Jane](https://www.reellifewithjane.com/)

I have been a fan of Drew Barrymore's since she was in E.T. and have watched each and every one of her movies since, so I was thrilled to find out she was going to be a **pregnant celebrity bride**! How gorgeous does she look in these photos in her Chanel gown designed by Karl Lagerfeld. I can't wait to see how adorable her and husband Will Kopelman's baby is, rumor has it they are expecting a little girl!

## 2. Bethenny Frankel...

_ Source: Yahoo_

When Bethenny joined the Real Housewives of New York she immediately became my favorite housewife and I couldn't have been happier when she got her own show and said 'I Do' to Jason Hoppy. Her wedding day may have been crazy with her going to the bathroom in a trash can, but she glowed as she walked down the aisle in her belly hugging gown. Bethenny was one **pregnant celebrity bride** who was proud to show off her little bump.

## 3. Julianna Margulies...

![Julianna Margulies...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/07/julianna-margulies_432x432.jpg)

Soure: [Instyle](https://www.instyle.com/)

Can you believe that the beautiful Julianna Margulies was 41 when she was a **pregnant celebrity bride**! In November of 2007 she married Keith Lieberthal and in January of 2008 they welcomed son Kieran Lindsay Lieberthal, who is now four years old. Julianna wore a romantic Narciso Rodriguez empire gown while her groom wore a Giorgio Armani tuxedo.

## 4. Alicia Keys...

_ Source: [LilSugar](https://www.popsugar.com/family/)

Could any other **pregnant celebrity bride** be sexier than Alicia Keys! She looks like a goddess in her one-shoulder Vera Wang gown and her diamond headpiece. Her groom, Swizz Beatz, looks handsome and compliments her in his white tuxedo. If you haven't yet seen a photo of their adorable son Egypt Daoud Dean, who was born just three months after mommy and daddy wed, be sure to follow Alicia on twitter_and [facebook](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)to see him!

## 5. Lily Allen...

![Lily Allen...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/07/lily-allen-wedding-gown_432x456.jpg)

Source: [PopSugar](https://www.popsugar.com/)

It's hard to believe that after all the ups and downs in her career and personal life that British singer Lily Allen was a **pregnant celebrity bride** and is now a mom. Hopefully motherhood and marriage have changed her for the better because it has been confirmed that she and husband Sam Cooper are expecting their second child. I do have to say, she looks very sweet and elegant in her lace wedding gown by French designer Delphine Manivet.

## 6. Kendra Wilkinson-Baskett...

_ Source: CelebrityBrideGuide_

Who would have ever thought that wild child, playboy bunny Kendra Wilkinson would be a **pregnant celebrity bride** one day! She was only three months pregnant when she married football player Hank Baskett on the lawn of the playboy mansion in a stunning ball gown by R. Mine Bridal Couture. They did mention her pregnancy before their big day, but she also had her dress taken out some to give her growing baby some room!

## 7. Ashlee Simpson...

![Ashlee Simpson...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/07/ashleesimpson_432x486.jpg)

Source: [Celebuzz](https://www.celebuzz.com/)

At the time of their nuptials, Ashlee Simpon was only 3 months pregnant and hardly showing so they decided not to come out with the news until after they were married. Sadly this **pregnant celebrity bride's** marriage has since ended in divorce, which makes me wonder if the little bun in the oven was the whole reason they said 'I Do'.

Whether you embrace your bump in a tight, belly hugging gown or you decide to hide it in a more flowing dress on your big day, remember to embrace this new life together as soon to be husband and wife. Some of these **pregnant celebrity brides** may have hidden their growing little ones in their wedding photos, but in their hearts they were glowing and proud. In the past it may have been a touchy subject, but times are different so be inspired by these gorgeous **pregnant celebrity brides** and show off your baby bump on your big day and share the good news with your guests. Just be certain that you are getting married for love and not because you got pregnant.
