# This Bride Was Surprised with a Flash Mob Wedding at the Mall! Watch It Happen! ...

After being engaged for nearly five years, Christina McGinnis and Brian Green tied the knot this Christmas! They had been delaying the wedding planning because it just became too stressful and expensive. "It took away the purpose and excitement of being married," Green told NBC 11 in Atlanta. "The wedding is just the ceremony. The marriage is what's important, so I just wanted to focus on the marriage." So true!

Brian decided to surprise his bride-to-be with a spontaneous wedding...at the mall! Christina had gotten in her wedding dress because she believed she was going to help out a photographer buddy with a bridal project. Turns out, she was getting ready for own wedding!
