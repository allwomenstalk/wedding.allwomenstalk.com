# 5 Reasons to Have Your Dog in Your Wedding!

Our **dogs** are **like family** and always greet us when we come home from a long day. They always want to make us happy, so lets make them **happy** and include them in one of the **biggest days **of our lives! **Here are 5 Reasons to have your Dog in your Wedding! **

## 5. They Look Handsome in a Tie...

![They Look Handsome in a Tie...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/dogtie_410x274.jpg)

Photo:[StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

I don't know whether it's the **cute little tie** or the **cute puppy dog face** and big brown eyes looking at me that melts my heart! No need for a little boy** ring bearer** when you have a cutie pie puppy at home. Put a [tie on your dog](https://goo.gl/fCyj3d) as he **walks down the aisle **with pride carrying the [ring pillow](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-handmade-wedding-items-to-buy-on-etsy/) on his back! **Did you dress up your dog for your big day?**

## 4. They'll Make the Stress Go Away...

![They'll Make the Stress Go Away...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/doggies_404x269.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

**Brides and Grooms** have a lot going on for their big day and we forget to just let someone else handle the **stress** for us. So when your flower girl gets cranky, or your aunt Susie gets drunk, turn to your [favorite little companion](https://allwomenstalk.com/30-pictures-of-the-cutest-dogs-ever/)for a **big wet kiss!** Our [dogs](https://www.adoptapet.com/) are great at **cheering us up;** they know when we're upset and are always right by our side to **make it all better**. **Will your dog be there to cheer you up on your wedding day?**

## 3. They Look Good in a Tux...

![They Look Good in a Tux...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/dogtux_403x309.jpg)

Photo:[StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

Need a **best man** for your [wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-cozy-winter-weddings-to-heat-up-your-fire/)? Why not put **man's best friend** in a [tuxedo](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.doggieclothesline.com/product/dog-tuxedo-navy) and let him share the spotlight with you! He's been there for you every time you needed him so give him the attention he deserves as all your **guests** line up to **take a picture with him. I just love how serious the **dog in the photo** looks; he knows he has a big job** to do!

## 2. The Kids Will Love Them...

![The Kids Will Love Them...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/flowergirldog_406x270.jpg)

Photo: [InspiredByThis.com](https://www.inspiredbythis.com/)

Keep your **dog** and **wedding kids** **busy** as they **play together during your wedding. When you take group photos you may need someone to** watch your dog, so after your [adorable flower girl](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-adorable-flower-girls-and-their-pretty-dresses/)walks him [down the aisle](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theweddingdog.com/about_us.htm) she can keep your puppy company and stay out of trouble! **Did your dog cause any wedding day problems?**

## 1. Dogs Love Taking Photos...

![Dogs Love Taking Photos...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/cutedog_400x300.jpg)

Photo:[OneReputation.com](https://aislefilesblog.com/)

The [camera loves them](https://allwomenstalk.com/15-adorable-celebrity-dogs/) and they love the camera! **Dogs at weddings **make for great **photo memories**. How adorable is this little dog with the beautiful **bride and groom**!! Don't let your [dogs](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theweddingdog.com/about_us.htm) sit at home during your big day. They don't have to walk down the aisle to be a part of your favorite moments. Capture the **friendship** you share with your pet and cherish it forever.
