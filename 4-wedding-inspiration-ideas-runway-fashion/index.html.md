# 4 Wedding Inspiration Ideas: Runway Fashion ...

Sometimes we see the same **wedding ideas** over and over again; balloons, mustache sticks, and even candy [dessert bars](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/bar-desserts-everyone-loves/). All are good and fun, but maybe you want to find new and** fresh ideas**. Take a look at these 4 wedding inspiration ideas from the** fashion runway** and you may just turn your head to the catwalk for your **wedding decor**!

## 4. Shine & Sparkle...

[Rachel Zoe](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.rachelzoe.com/welcome) has given the [fashion](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/)world a preview of her new line and now you can be inspired to let your wedding **shine** and **sparkle** with this fun and flirty one shoulder **blue squin dress**! Do you think your **bridesmaids** would wear something like this!? If not, cover your reception with [crystal beads](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.save-on-crafts.com/crystalgarland.html), send out [chandelier invitations](https://goo.gl/tuP1H2), and find the perfect pair of[sparkly blue heels](https://web.archive.org/web/https://us.christianlouboutin.com/samira-strass-100mm.html) to get your **blue sparkle** **inspiration** started!

## 3. Summer Splash...

I just adore this** color combination** of pinks, orange, and black on this **bright** and **playful** dress by [Prada](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.prada.com/en). It doesn't have to be just the colors or style of something on the **runway** that inspires you, look at the **texture**, the **movement**, the **fabric**, and the complete look. You can build a **summer theme** full of bright orange [gerbera daisies](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-best-flowers-for-your-wedding/), pink ribbons, and a[signature coral colored cocktail](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.marthastewart.com/photogallery/tropical-cocktails?#slide_10)right from this yummy dress!

## 2. Purple Swirls...

You should know by now that my new favorite wedding color is **purple**, but I don't love this [Marc Jacobs](https://www.marcjacobs.com/?utm_source=43737&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=pepperjam&utm_content=8-11214&affiliate_id=43737&clickId=4032649374)look just for that reason alone. Look at those **stripes** and **patterns**, what a great shirt for your wedding **inspiration**! You don't see too much of stripes and patterns in the **wedding** world, some but not many. Take a closer look at the **bride** in the lower right and you'll see **swirls** on her beautiful gown; I would love to see more [dresses](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/9-vera-wang-gowns-from-her-new-white-collection/)and weddings like this!

## 1. Bohemian Chic...

The colors, texture, leather belt, flow of the fabric, and the style of her hair just screams **bohemian chic** to me! [Alexander Mcqueen's](https://goo.gl/W9GeyG)dress has inspired a fall, succulent, outdoor, orange calla lily, leather trunk wedding that I would love to see come to life. How brilliant would your **bridesmaids** look wearing a beautiful [floral dress](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-dresses-for-every-spring-2011-bridesmaid/)as you walk down the aisle in a flowy, [bohemian gown](https://www.onewed.com/wedding-dresses/)with a gorgeous [fern crown](https://goo.gl/BPsC23)on your head!

**Did you find your wedding inspiration on the fashion runway?**
