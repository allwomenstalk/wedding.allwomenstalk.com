# 3 Wedding Inspiration Boards to Celebrate the Color of 2011 ...

**Honeysuckle Pink** is the **hot color of 2011**, now this doesn't mean that every detail of your wedding has to be pink. To inspire the little details and accents of your 2011 wedding here are **3 wedding inspiration boards to celebrate the color of the year**!

## 3. Every Little Detail Counts...

Photos: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

There are so many beautiful ways to mix in touches of this **year's color**, [honeysuckle pink](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-hot-color-palette-trends-for-your-2011-wedding/), into the** little details** of your wedding day. I love the **bridesmaids** in this **inspiration board** and the gorgeous [pink dresses](https://goo.gl/eT1TII) against the pop of [white anemones](http://www.google.com/imghp?hl=en) they're carrying. Add a **touch of pink **into your [invitation](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.zazzle.co.uk/hot_pink_and_blue_wildflowers_wedding_invitation-161615484686114821) along with other colors of blue and purple or have a [fun signature pink drink](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.hostessblog.com/2009/10/pink-your-drink-cocktail-recipes/) during cocktail hour. **Where will you include honeysuckle pink into your little details? **

## 2. Pretty in Pink...

Photos: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

**Brides** don't be afraid to add **pretty pink **details to your **wedding attire **like the [hot pink shoes](https://goo.gl/XRvGNm) and [crinoline](http://www.sterlingandpearls.com/petticoat_crinoline.htm) in this inspiration board. If you've already found your wedding shoes, maybe your [flower girl](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-adorable-flower-girls-and-their-pretty-dresses/)will look just as **pretty in pink** as this adorable little girl! I love the [pink lighting](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.luminousdesigns.com/lighting.htm) for the **wedding reception**, what a great way to add your **wedding color** into your big day.

## 1. Decor, Favors, and Much More...

Photos: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

How much do you love the **wedding palette of **pink**, **orange**, and **yellow **with** **the [pretty paper lanterns](https://goo.gl/f6Jypo) **; such beautiful colors for a **spring** or **summer wedding**. If you like pink but don't want to go crazy with the decor surprise your guests with **tasty favors **like [pink strawberry jam](https://www.intimateweddings.com/blog/how-to-make-strawberry-jam-hot-damn-this-is-good-jam/) or [pink lollipops](https://goo.gl/Kt0NUg). Let pink find it's way into your wedding in the **small details** like your [place cards](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-unique-place-card-ideas-for-your-wedding/), your groom's adorable [pink bow tie](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.thetiebar.com/categoryPages/Purple_Pink_Bow_Ties.asp) or in your fun candy dessert bar.

**Have you discovered honeysuckle pink for your 2011 wedding?**
