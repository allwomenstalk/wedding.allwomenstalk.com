# 5 Fun Ways to Celebrate Your Nerdy Wedding ...

We all probably have a little nerdy side to our personalities; who doesn't love a good book? But sometimes there are people who like to go a little extreme; they dress up as their favorite characters, go to conventions, and even celebrate their big day** nerdy style**! For all you nerds and geeks out there, this **wedding** post is for you!

## 5. I Heart Robots...

![I Heart Robots...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/robotinvite_413x569.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/BonMoment](https://goo.gl/ZTolBR)

Okay, whether you're a **nerd** or not you have to admit how cute these **robots** are! I just love the way their arms go up to the top of the **invitation** to hold the heart in the middle! It's a great invitation to send to your guests to show your love for robots and let your geeky personality shine.

## 4. Lego Land...

![Lego Land...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/legocufflink_418x371.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/Crimsonking](https://goo.gl/um8ciu)

Is he not the cutest little **groom**! I just want to hold him and carry his little **lego **body around all day! If your husband-to-be is a lego fanatic these **lego groom cufflinks **are the perfect gift for him to open on the day of your wedding. Who needs formal cufflinks when you have these fun little guys!

## 3. Book Worm...

![Book Worm...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/ringpillow_417x278.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/TheDaintyWoods](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I love every detail about this **book ring pillow**! This is such a great piece for teachers, writers, and nerdy book readers! The seller customizes the order to your wedding style and colors, whether it's rustic, vintage, or modern. It's a new and **creative** idea that I haven't seen before.

## 2. Math Geek Favor...

![Math Geek Favor...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/math_412x494.jpg)

Photo: [TheFavourshoppe.Weddingstar.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://thefavourshoppe.weddingstar.com:443/product/error)

For the accountants, math professors, mathematicians, and **math geeks** every where! This diamond shaped **calculator** is a practical and adorable favor for your guests. I love the "We Add Up" title! Now when your friends and family use their calculator to do their taxes they'll remember your **nerdy wedding day**!

## 1. Star Wars Fan...

![Star Wars Fan...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/starwarstopper_414x311.jpg)

Photo: [Esty.com/shop/PeanutButterBandit](https://goo.gl/m0QC3t)

Is it the buns on the side of the bride's head or just the look of this **nerdy wedding cake topper** that gives you a hint of what **movie** it's from? If you guessed[Star Wars](https://www.starwars.com/films), episodes IV, V, and VI, then you are correct! The bride is **Princess Leia** and the groom is** Han Solo** and they are the perfect match for your **Star** **Wars, nerd themed wedding**!

**What makes you and your wedding nerdy?**
