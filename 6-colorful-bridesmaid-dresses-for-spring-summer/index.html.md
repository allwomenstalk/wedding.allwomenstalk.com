# 6 Colorful Bridesmaid Dresses for Spring & Summer ...

The sun is coming back to the Northeast next week...50 degrees is a good winter day around here! So to celebrate the warm weather and **Spring** and **Summer** **weddings** check out these **bright** and **colorful** **bridesmaid dresses** that your ladies will love to dance around in...

## 6. Yellow as the Sun...

![Yellow as the Sun...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/davidsbridal2_417x557.jpg)

Photo: [Fashionablebride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening)
Dress: [DavidsBridal](https://goo.gl/C5wFon)

Have you ever seen a bridesmaid dress as cute as this one! I just adore the **yellow lace** and haven't seen a [lace bridesmaid dress](https://goo.gl/C5wFon)before, if you know of one point me in the right direction. Everything about this dress is just so adorable; the **sweetheart neckline**, the tea length, and the **floral accents** on the waistband. Cute, cute, cute!

## 5. Slip into Sangria...

![Slip into Sangria...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/jennyyoon_417x557.jpg)

Photo: [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/931/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Jenny-Yoo)
Dress: [Jenny Yoo](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.jennyyoo.com/products/crinkle_chiffon01)

This beautiful **bright** color reminds me of the **color of the year**, [honeysuckle pink](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-wedding-inspiration-boards-to-celebrate-the-color-of-2011/), but over at [Jenny Yoo](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.jennyyoo.com/products/splash)they call it **Sangria**! It's a fun, flirty dress perfect for warm weather. An orange and pink[pinwheel bouquet](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-unique-things-for-your-bridesmaids-to-carry/)would complete this **summer fun** look.

## 4. Put the Lime in...

![Put the Lime in...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/aria1_417x557.jpg)

Photo: [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening)
Dress: [Aria](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.ariadress.com/Desciptions/Desc103KC.htm)

Sounds like we're making a drink here with Sangria and limes; lemons are up next! This **lime** **dress** is so **fresh**, a great color I would love to see more of. [Aria](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.ariadress.com/DressThumbnails.htm)was smart when they paired it with a lovely pink clutch.** Pink** and **lime** will compliment each other perfectly for your [colorful wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-ways-to-mix-up-the-colors-for-your-wedding/).

## 3. Lemon Zest...

![Lemon Zest...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/morilee_417x557.jpg)

Photo: [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/833/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening/Angelina-Faccenda-by-Mori-Lee)
Dress: [MoriLee](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridesmaids/angelina_faccenda_bridesmaids)

I love the beautiful **ruffles** on the bodice and the **vintage look** of the skirt on this **lemon **[Mori Lee](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridesmaids/angelina_faccenda_bridesmaids/20205)bridesmaid dress. Pair this dress with a[mason jar bouquet](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.thebridescafe.com/?tag=Mason%20Jar%20Bouquets) and you have yourself a true **vintage wedding**!

## 2. Red Hot...

![Red Hot...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/morilee3_417x557.jpg)

Photo: [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening)
Dress: [MoriLee](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridesmaids/angelina_faccenda_bridesmaids)

Your **bridesmaids** will certainly be on the dance floor in this **red hot** [Mori Lee](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridesmaids/angelina_faccenda_bridesmaids/20206)silky taffeta dress! I love the gold heels. This palette is just the right color combination for your [Mexican inspired wedding fiesta](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-wedding-decor-ideas-kissing-in-the-kitchen/)! And just think, you already have your [sangria](https://www.spain-recipes.com/sangriarecipe.html)ready!

## 1. Purple Plums...

![Purple Plums...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/jennyyoo_417x557.jpg)

Photo: [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/fashiontrends/Bridesmaid-Evening)
Dress: [Jenny Yoo](https://www.jennyyoo.com/)

I just love the **pleating** on this stunning purple cocktail **bridesmaid dress**. The **plum purple** color would look amazing with yellow calla lilies. Keep this **colorful wedding theme** going from head to toe when you find yellow heels to match her bright bouquet!
