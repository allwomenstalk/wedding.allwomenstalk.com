# 7 Fun and Creative Ways to Ask a Friend to Be Your Bridesmaid ...

Would you like to know some fun and creative ways to ask a friend to be your bridesmaid? These are some ideas that can help you out. Of course you can go the normal, boring route and just ask her straight out. But if you want something a little more memorable, these are some ways to ask a friend to be your bridesmaid that fit the bill.

## 1. Play a Scavenger Hunt Game

This is on of the fun and creative ways to ask a friend to be your bridesmaid. Plan a scavenger hunt game for her. Send her on a hunt to find little clues that will bring her closer to finding the prize. For the prize, give her something such as a beautifully decorated box with the big question inside or maybe the wedding jewelry she would wear to accompany the question. This is a very unique way to ask this special question.

## 2. Propose to Her

This is another fun way to ask a friend to be your bridesmaid. Pop the question about being your bridesmaid with a special gift for her. You might have a box of candy instead of a box with a ring. You might give her a piece of costume jewelry. It doesn’t matter what you choose to give as your small gift, it is the fun way you are asking.

## 3. Send an Invitation

This idea is a thoughtful way to ask her to be your bridesmaid. It has fun and flair without putting her on the spot. She can take some time to think about if she would like to be your bridesmaid or not. You can ask her to RSVP or just chat with her later about it. If she decides to say yes, that invitation will be a nice keepsake as well.

## 4. Give Her a Care Package

Who doesn’t love a care package? It is filled with all kinds of little trinkets and goodies to enjoy. You could send your possible bridesmaid a care package filled with all of her favorites along with the question. You could also give it to her in person and tell her that it’s a care package filled with her favorites because she is one of your favorite people, which is why you would love for her to be your bridesmaid. This is a very sweet way to ask a friend to be your bridesmaid.

## 5. Give Her a Keepsake Box

You could give her a keepsake box when you ask her to be your bridesmaid. A keepsake box is the perfect choice to accompany your question. It provides a wonderful storage place for all the memorable keepsakes from your wedding. The keepsake box itself is a keepsake. This is a very touching way to ask this question.

## 6. Write a Poem

You could ask this question through writing a poem. You can go several different ways with this idea. You could write a very humorous poem that still gets the question relayed. You could also write a poem that reflects on your friendship and why you would like her to be your bridesmaid. Whichever way you decide to go with this idea, it is definitely creative.

## 7. Give Her a Preview

This is a way to give her a preview of your wedding and the benefits of being a bridesmaid. You could include things like the nail polish you would like her to wear or the jewelry you have chosen. You could enclose a picture of the shoe choice for your bridesmaids or a swatch of the material of the dress she would be wearing. This will add some excitement to the question. She can see what lovely things she will get to enjoy as your bridesmaid.

These are 7 creative ways to ask a friend to be your bridesmaid. How did you ask your bridesmaids this special question? Share your stories!
