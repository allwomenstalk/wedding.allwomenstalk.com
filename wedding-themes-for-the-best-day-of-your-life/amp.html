3 Vintage Wedding

Via Mariage vintage | Cherry Plum
This means muted and pale colors with a touch of elegance.

***

This means muted and pale colors with a touch of elegance. Think soft pinks, delicate ivories, and subtle greens that evoke a sense of timeless beauty. Decorative elements like lace, antique frames, and classic floral arrangements create an atmosphere of understated sophistication. The goal is to transport your guests back in time to a period of grace and charm, where every detail reflects a bygone era's romantic allure. Opt for vintage-inspired attire, retro tableware, and heirloom accessories to complete the look. This theme effortlessly blends the old with the new, offering a nostalgic yet contemporary celebration.

4 Great Gatsby Wedding

Via Great Gatsby Inspired Celebration
Go to town with feathers, pearls and sparkles.

***

Create an atmosphere of roaring twenties opulence with gold accents and art deco designs. Incorporate vintage furniture, such as ornate chaise lounges and gilded mirrors, to enhance the setting. Use old-fashioned fonts on your invitations and signage to truly embrace the Gatsby vibe. Encourage guests to dress in flapper dresses and dapper suits to fully immerse in the theme. For entertainment, consider hiring a jazz band to serenade the night away and don’t forget the Champagne tower - an absolute must for a Gatsby gala.

12 Modern Music Themed Wedding

Via frostedpetticoatblog.com
Choose a style of music or your favorite artist.

***

A Modern Music Themed Wedding is a great way to celebrate the best day of your life. It allows you to express your personality and style through the music you choose. Whether you’re a fan of classic rock, jazz, hip-hop, or any other genre, the possibilities are endless.

When it comes to planning a modern music themed wedding, there are a few key elements to consider. First, decide on the type of music you want to feature. This could be a combination of different genres, or one style in particular. Consider the atmosphere you want to create and the mood you want to set. You can also choose music that has special meaning to you and your partner.

Once you’ve decided on the music, you can start to plan the details of your wedding. Think about how you want to incorporate the music into the ceremony, reception, and other wedding activities. You may want to have a live band or DJ, or incorporate a sound system to play recorded music. You can also use the music to create a unique atmosphere in your venue, such as using lighting or visuals to enhance the experience.

When it comes to the decorations, you can use music-themed items to create a fun and festive atmosphere.

29 Pure Romance Themed Wedding

Via 5 Hottest Wedding Trends
Cram your wedding with everything that spells romance.

***

Romance themed weddings are becoming increasingly popular, and for good reason. From the decorations to the cake, the entire day can be filled with romantic touches that will make the day extra special. For a pure romance themed wedding, choose a venue with a beautiful view, such as a beach or garden. Incorporate romantic elements such as soft lighting, candles, and rose petals throughout the venue. When it comes to the wedding dress, opt for a classic, elegant look with a touch of romance, such as a lace or tulle gown. For the bridesmaids, choose pastel colors that will complement the romantic theme. For the wedding cake, choose a design that is simple yet elegant, and top it off with a romantic topper. Finally, for the music, choose romantic songs that will set the mood for the entire day. With these elements, you are sure to have a romantic and unforgettable wedding.
