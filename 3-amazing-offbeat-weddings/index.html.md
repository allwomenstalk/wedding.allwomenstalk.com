# 3 Amazing Offbeat Weddings ...

Throw your wedding tradition handbook out the window and go **offbeat** for your theme, attire, and everything in between. Relax and let things flow. **Wedding planning** can be stressful enough without having to worry about keeping up with traditions. Go offbeat for your big day like these **3 amazing** **offbeat weddings** and never look back!

## 3. Low Key Carnival...

![Low Key Carnival...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/og/wd/v2hrbyed54c8ecec71ffa998978182_400x400.jpg)

Photos: [Flickr.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.flickr.com/photos/24957349@N08/sets/72157625559532923/)

Something **borrowed** and something **handmade**. This was the **creative** **concept** for this fun, ["raddest wedding ever",](https://offbeatbride.com/seattle-vegan-wedding/)**carnival** **invitation**,' do it our way' celebration. When you have a** budget of $6,000** you make the best of it and always remember that it's not about the decor, cake, or bridal party; it's about **love**. Liz and Rev were **original** and **true to themselves**. Some people may not have liked their[quirky ceremony](http://www.rebeccabolte.com/?page_id=17), their non-traditional vows, or their vegan donut wedding cake and that's OK! [Be happy](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-love-being-a-tattooed-bride/) and remember everyone will follow when they see the** true love** you share.

## 2. A Folk Festival Celebration...

![A Folk Festival Celebration...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/dg/vn43fo2g54c8ecedad7d5668713012_400x400.jpg)

Photos:[Flickr.com](https://www.flickr.com/photos/mikfrawley/)

When you're an **offbeat**, non-traditional [couple](https://www.flickr.com/photos/mikfrawley/5342165991/in/set-72157625791255094/)you **celebrate** your wedding day in the only fashion you know. There can only be[great things](https://web.archive.org/web/http://kariz-matik.com/sunshine-coast-weddings/unique-wedding-woodford-folk-festival/)when you plan your [wedding ceremony](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ways-to-have-fun-on-your-wedding-day/)at the local [folk festival](https://woodfordfolkfestival.com/)and put on an** acrobatic performance** instead of dancing the waltz for your first dance! I love their **balloon altar** and their **old world folk wedding attire. The location and dress doesn't make you a **bride**, the **feeling in your heart** and the excitement of planning this life long journey** makes you a **bride**.

## 1. A Tent in the Woods...

![A Tent in the Woods...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/pt/p27kz4uf54c8ecef0e821686556053_400x400.jpg)

Photos: [Flickr.com](https://www.flickr.com/photos/46959657@N03/sets/72157625098344447/)

If you really love the **outdoors** don't just plan a[backyard wedding](https://offbeatbride.com/monday-montage-outdoor-woods-weddings/), go to the [forest](https://sherwoodforest.org.uk/)and set up a **colorful** **tent** like this **hippie**, **woodland** loving **couple from Nottinghamshire, England. Not only did they set up a tent for their **wedding reception**, but they also set up a **tent** for their[first night as newlyweds](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.paulsaxby.co.uk/)! You can be **offbeat** and **trendy too** like Lydia and Nathan did with their yummy **vegan cupcakes**, adorable cake topper and DIY wedding sign. Being **offbeat** doesn't mean trashy, it just means that your **more** **colorful than most [brides and grooms](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-vintage-inspired-wedding-looks/)!
