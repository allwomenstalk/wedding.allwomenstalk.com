# 8 Bridesmaid Summer Dresses Your Wedding Party Will Love ...

If you’re on the hunt for the perfect bridesmaid summer dresses, I’m here to help you on your search. With your big day coming up, you shouldn’t have to drive yourself into a fashion frenzy over wedding party wear. Because let’s face it: you have invitations to prepare, a venue to book, and a groom who needs a trip to bridal party boot camp. Here are 8 bridesmaid dress options that’ll make your wedding even sweeter. So, sit back, shop these looks, and watch your stress melt away!

## 1. Some Enchanted Afternoon Dress

![Some Enchanted Afternoon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/06/1_some-enchanted-afternoon-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $164.99 at [modcloth.com](https://goo.gl/YhiY0n)
This frock’s gorgeous blue hue is perfect for any summer wedding partygoer. Crafted from crinkled silk, accents like a V-cut back and silvery belt make the dress all the more sophisticated. I’d pair it with silver kitten heels for a look that’s totally on point.

## 2. Donna Morgan Satin & Chiffon V-Neck Dress

![Donna Morgan Satin & Chiffon V-Neck Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/06/2_donna-morgan-satin-chiffon-v-neck-dress_350x408.jpg)

**Price:** $158.00 at [zappos.com](https://www.zappos.com/donna-morgan-satin-chiffon-v-neck-dress?oosRedirected=true)
This summer dress’ empire waist will give your bridesmaids breathing room. And the light chiffon will keep them cool during those sultry summer days. I also love the sturdy straps and just-right cut, which makes it work for the busty gals out there.

## 3. Flounced Open Back Dress

_ **Price:** $22.80 at forever21.com_
I’ll be the first to admit that I love a good bargain. So why make your bridesmaids fork out a chunk of change for a dress they’d probably only wear once? This dress is feminine, flirty, and vintage-chic-perfect for a summer wedding on the sand. And best of all, it won’t break the bank.

## 4. Ruched Halter Neck Special Occasion Dress

_ **Price:** $29.80 at forever21.com_
They’ll be pretty in purple with this trendy ruched dress. When you look for summer bridesmaid dresses, it helps to go for something effortless yet elegant. Not only will it save your guests time and money, but you won’t have to turn into a Bridezilla over ripped tulle or a torn flower-adorned strap.

## 5. Lucienne One-Shoulder Silk-Chiffon Dress

![Lucienne One-Shoulder Silk-Chiffon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/06/5_lucienne-one-shoulder-silk-chiffon-dress_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $250 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/q2pSQm)
Achieving classic glam is simple with this bridesmaid summer dress. The one-shoulder strap is a throwback to the days when Grecian goddesses ruled the land. I’d happily don this anywhere from a wedding to a cocktail party –and not many seasoned bridesmaids could say that about their old dresses!

## 6. Soiree in Santorini Dress

![Soiree in Santorini Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/06/6_soiree-in-santorini-dress_350x500.jpg)

**Price:** $197.99 at [modcloth.com](http://www.modcloth.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you’re planning to wed by the sea, this mermaid-esque summer bridesmaid dress will definitely make waves. The strapless, ruffled creation is charmingly girly –something that would work well with a youthful wedding party.

## 7. Twobirds Tea Length Convertible Dress

![Twobirds Tea Length Convertible Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/06/2birds_347x683.jpg)

**Price:** $298.00 at [shopbop.com](http://www.shopbop.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
With over 15 ways to wear it, this is one bridesmaid summer dress any lady will love. The lengthy straps can twist any which way and the stretchy jersey material will fit nearly every gal in your party.

## 8. Draped Detailing Dress

![Draped Detailing Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/06/8_draped-detailing-dress_350x489.jpg)

**Price:** $34.99 at [shop.mango.com](http://shop.mango.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Your ladies will look red hot in this sultry summer bridesmaid dress. The cut and draped fit are refined yet sexy and the price is so right! I especially love how easy it is to dress down for the office, ensuring that its wearer will get her money’s worth and then some!
