# 5 Creative Ideas for an Easter Holiday Wedding ...

Yesterday over at one of my new favorite blogs, [Express-O](https://goo.gl/9keeUR), my friend Diana posted the cutest little [grass egg shell centerpieces](https://www.momtastic.com/family/161136-diy-holiday-wheat-grass-eggs/). With that, one great idea always leads to another. That's when I thought of **wedding ideas** inspired by the colorful** Easter holiday**; full of eggs, bunnies, and colorful nests!

## 5. Invitation Seals...

![Invitation Seals...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/rabbitseals_417x288.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/Whimsywhimsical](https://goo.gl/hlOl94)

I just want to take this little love bunny home with me! Send these adorable** rabbit seals** out to your guests and they will be delighted to say yes to your **Easter/Spring wedding** celebration.

## 4. Blue Candy Nest...

Whether your wedding day is around the **Easter holiday** or in **Spring**, these lovely **yarn nests** candy bowls will be a wonderful sweets table decoration. To add a little **fuzzy** look to your nest, just as Martha did, use [mohair yarn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.marthastewart.com/272442/candy-nests?amp%3Bbacktourl=%2Fphotogallery%2Feaster-crafts-and-treats&backto=true)to get this fun look!

## 3. Paper Bunnies...

![Paper Bunnies...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/cupcaketoppers_416x278.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/BugandBoo](https://goo.gl/W16jlV)

[Peter Cottontail](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0249577/) is hopping down the rabbit trail and wants to celebrate your wedding day with these cute **bunny cupcake toppers**! The little kids at your wedding will love these, and they will probably put a smile on all of the adults faces as well.

## 2. Cake Topper...

![Cake Topper...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/rabbits_417x313.jpg)

Photo: [Esty.com/shop/Kikuike](https://goo.gl/giBkHJ)

Our one year anniversary is at the end of this month and this post is actually the perfect celebration. Why you ask, because our anniversary falls on **Easter**! I would have loved to put this bride and groom **rabbit cake topper** on our wedding cake if it were this April 24th! How amazing are these little details like his tie and her rose bouquet!

## 1. Egg Shell Candles...

![Egg Shell Candles...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/eggvotive_414x517.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewart](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.marthastewart.com/photogallery/easter-crafts-and-treats#slide_13)

This **Easter DIY craft** takes the cake for most creative. These **egg shell votives** will bring Spring and Easter right into the centerpieces of your wedding tables. They can also double as **favors** your guests will love.

**Does your wedding fall around Easter or any other decorative holiday?**
