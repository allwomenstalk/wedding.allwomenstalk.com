# 3 Wedding Decor Ideas: Kissing in the Kitchen ...

Our personalities, likes, and dislikes help to **inspire** us in the **kitchen** and our **homes**. Now that you're planning a **wedding** and you need ideas let your home and **kitchen decor** inspire your wedding details like you never thought it could! Whip up some flavor with these **3 Wedding Decor Ideas: Kissing in the Kitchen...**

## 3. Copper Rustic Glow...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Anthropologie](https://goo.gl/seih3R)

Do you have [copper pots and pans](https://goo.gl/ri03cV)hanging from your pot rack and a **metallic** [KitchenAid](https://goo.gl/RN9vIM) that matches your [flatware](https://goo.gl/wgO53h)? Than you may like a [rustic chic theme](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-wedding-themes-inspired-by-paper-goods/)for your **wedding** with a **barn reception**, [gold jewelry](http://www.anthropologie.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), and glowing **centerpieces** or a[mason jar chandelier](http://karapaslaydesigns.blogspot.com/2010/03/diy-mason-jar-chandelier.html). Does your** kitchen decor** inspire you?

## 2. Mexican Flair...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Anthropologie](https://goo.gl/seih3R)

Your **kitchen** and home has been inspired by your favorite vacation to [Mexico](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.visitmexico.com/wb2/Visitmexico/Visi_Home); the **colors**, **patterns**, and **textures** make you smile. This[colorful feeling](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-ways-to-mix-up-the-colors-for-your-wedding/)warms your soul and will warm your wedding too. With [orange invitations](https://goo.gl/m0jkYG), a[signature sangria drink](https://web.archive.org/web/http://eleganteventdesign.com/tantalizing-thrusday-sangria-anyone), and a **bright table setting** your guests won't want to wait to book their trip to celebrate [Cinco de Mayo](https://web.archive.org/web/http://gomexico.about.com/od/festivalsholidays/p/cinco_de_mayo.htm)!

## 1. Vintage Love...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Anthropologie](https://goo.gl/seih3R)

White [dinnerware](https://goo.gl/xEv1VD), adorable little [teapots](https://goo.gl/MlKAcH), and a [fridge](https://web.archive.org/web/http://bigchillfridge.com/site/fridges)from the **1950s** fill your kitchen with **vintage love. **Since you already know where all the [vintage thrift stores](https://web.archive.org/web/http://vintagethriftshop.org/)and hot spots are you'll be able to find your [vintage wedding decor](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-vintage-inspired-wedding-looks/)in no time! Search for lovely [monogrammed teacups](https://goo.gl/jq56p8)for **favors**, dress your **bridesmaids** in[vintage dresses](https://goo.gl/01B7TU), and take [polaroid pictures](http://survey-smiles.com/) for your **guestbook**.

**Have you found the perfect wedding inspiration in your kitchen? **
