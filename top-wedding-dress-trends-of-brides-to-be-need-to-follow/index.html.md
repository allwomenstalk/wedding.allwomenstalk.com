# Top Wedding Dress Trends of 2019 Brides to Be Need to Follow ...

If you are getting married this year, then firstly, congratulations, and secondly, what the hell are you going to wear!? The stress of picking a wedding dress is something that all brides feel, and it can be even harder at the start of a new year when you don’t know what might and might not be in vogue for the months ahead! You want to achieve that balance of being in style but also timeless, but that definitely is achievable! Here are the top **wedding dress trends for 2019**!

## 1. Minimalism

![Dress, Photograph, Clothing, Shoulder, Wedding dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/qj/zm9hnhen5c524730e35c2684346744_600x750.jpg)

Long gone are the days of big puffy sleeves and frills for miles, 2019 is very much about sleek and minimalistic wedding dresses. Carrying on from the kind of look that Meghan Markle started off with her own wedding dress last year, the emphasis is more about your own natural beauty than the complications and over the top details of a the dress.

## 2. Halterneck

![Gown, Wedding dress, Clothing, Dress, Fashion model,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nv/iv/efo1hbfs5c52474ec20b7806482090_600x659.jpg)

You can expect to see a lot of halterneck style wedding dresses on the racks this year. Another look that was chosen by Megan, the like of Stella McCartney, Ronald Joyce and Justin Alexander are all designers who are looking to favour the flatting halterneck look inn 2019. For a wedding dress it provides the perfect balance of sophistication, fashion and reserve!

## 3. Gold and Metallic

![Dress, Clothing, Gown, Wedding dress, Bridal party dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yu/xp/v23dqas95c524768a4573913731715_600x750.jpg)

If you are looking to wear a wedding dress that isn’t the traditional white, you will find that most of the major options this year are gold and other metallic tones. This might seem over the top and in your face when you first imagine it, but the tones are very subtle, and they can make for a really beautiful and classy dress that feels traditional whilst also having that important touch of modernity and nonconformity.

## 4. Blue

![Gown, Wedding dress, Dress, Bride, Photograph,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/re/r2np62vt5c524781c4da0798918813_600x750.jpg)

Something old, something new, something borrowed and a wedding dress that is blue! Vera Wang is leading the way in 2019 with light blue tone dresses that look absolutely amazing. You can be sure that this designer look is going to filter down to the high street soon and there will be blue gowns available at all price points!

## 5. Pockets

![Gown, Wedding dress, Clothing, Dress, Bridal party dress,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/hf/xpnj9adg5c5247a6be44a374286708_600x750.jpg)

Wedding dresses with pockets started to garner some big attention last year but the trend is going to go viral in 2019. No bride wants to carry a bag on her wedding day but bemoans that she has nowhere to carry anything. Pockets in a wedding dress are genius.
