# 7 Beautiful Destinations for Your Australian Honeymoon ...

**Australia is a beautiful country with so much to do and see. A country filled with amazing wildlife, National Parks, historic land, breathtaking rainforest, and white sands and coral reef **honeymooners** would love to dive right into. Explore these 7 Beautiful Destinations** for your **Australian Honeymoon** and you may never want to leave mate!

## 7. Snorkel in Queensland...

![Snorkel in Queensland...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/Cairns_403x263.jpg)

Photo: [Cairns.com.au](https://www.cairnspost.com.au/nocookies?a=A.flavipes)

Did you catch any of [Oprah's visit to Australia](https://www.oprah.com/index.html)? She may not have snorkeled, but she showed us the best way one should **travel** to the [Great Barrier Reef](https://greatbarrierreef.org/)in [Queensland](https://www.australia.com/en/404.html), Aussie. Experience the **reef** from above and below as you take a [helicopter ride](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.cairns-heliscenic.com.au/scenic_flights.html) before you **swim** with the **fish**, **sea turtles**, and through [shipwrecks](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.gbrmpa.gov.au/onboard/home/high_standards/responsible_reef_practices/shipwrecks/historic_shipwreck_special_access_areas)and [underwater tunnels](https://www.homestead.com/). Will you make **memories** of a lifetime on your** honeymoon** in [Australia](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-animals-from-australia-id-love-to-see/)?

## 6. A Spiritual Wonder in the North...

![A Spiritual Wonder in the North...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/ayers-rock-pictures-3_400x263.jpg)

Photo: [Outback-Australia-travel-secrets.com](https://www.outback-australia-travel-secrets.com/)

Explore nature, **history**, and the first natives of the [Northern Territory](https://www.australia.com/en/404.html)at the [Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park](https://parksaustralia.gov.au/uluru/index.html). Feel the **spirits** of the [Anangu people](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.travel2-outback.com/guide_summary.php?guide_alias=anangu_waai)from millions of years ago as their living ancestors guide you through this untouched land. The is the one place on your [honeymoon](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-tips-for-a-smooth-honeymoon/)where you'll want to watch the **sunset** as the colors of **Uluru** take your breath away.

## 5. Ride a Camel in Western Aussie...

![Ride a Camel in Western Aussie...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/cablebeach_400x300.jpg)

Photo: [AmazingAustralia.com.au](http://www.amazingaustralia.com.au/index.html)

I've heard of riding horseback on the [beach](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-beach-bag-must-haves/), but have you ever thought about **riding a camel** on[Cable Beach](https://www.westernaustralia.com/en/Pages/Destination.aspx?n=Cable_Beach&pid=9005311)on your **Aussie honeymoon**! As you walk on the **white sands** and dip your toes into the [Indian ocean](https://www.thoughtco.com/indian-ocean-seas-1435186)don't forget to **travel** to the [Golden Outback](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.australiasgoldenoutback.com/en/Pages/Home.aspx) for a true **Australian adventure**. Where will your **honeymoon travels** take you?

## 4. Visit Kangaroo Island in the South...

![Visit Kangaroo Island in the South...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/kangas_410x308.jpg)

Photo: [SouthAustralia.co.nz](https://southaustralia.com/campaigns/sometimes-i-wonder)

No need to visit the zoo on your **honeymoon** to [Kangaroo Island](https://www.tourkangarooisland.com.au/) in [South Australia](https://www.australia.com/en/404.html). Capture amazing photos of **wallabies**, penguins, **fur seals**, sea lions, koalas, and **kangaroos **right on the landscape in front of you! There is so much to do on this little island; [climb the sand dunes](https://kioutdooraction.com.au/), kayak with the [dolphins](https://web.archive.org/web/http://cruisingkangarooisland.vpweb.com.au/default.html), and find your way through [limestone caves](https://www.tourkangarooisland.com.au/). Are you looking for an **exciting honeymoon** or something more **relaxed**?

## 3. To the Top in New South Wales...

Be king and queen of your** Australian honeymoon** when you [BridgeClimb](https://www.bridgeclimb.com/) the famous [Sydney Harbour Bridge](https://www.cityofsydney.nsw.gov.au/history)that even [Oprah and Gale](https://www.oprah.com/own-oprahshow/sydney-harbor-bridge-climb-mishap-video)conquered! Discover the rest of **New South Wales** along your honeymoon as you visit the **crocodiles** at the [Australia Reptile Park](https://www.reptilepark.com.au/), listen, watch, and learn at [Sydney's Opera House](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.sydneyoperahouse.com/homepage.aspx), take a tour of the places tourist don't usually see with [Sydney Uncovered](https://web.archive.org/web/http://sydneyuncovered.com.au/sydney-walking-tours/), and visit [Olivia Newton John's spa retreat](https://www.gaiaretreat.com.au/)!

## 2. All aboard in Victoria...

![All aboard in Victoria...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/Puffing-Billy-Steam-Railway_405x303.jpg)

Photo: [Melbourne.diarystar.com.au](https://web.archive.org/web/http://melbourne.diarystar.com.au/)

Take the **ride** of your life and **honeymoon** as you travel along [mountain tracks](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dw61Z72zczc)that were built in the 1900s on the [Puffing Billy](https://puffingbilly.com.au/?id=home). Go through forest, farmland and fern gullies on this one of a kind **steam train** outside **Melbourne, Australia** in [Victoria](https://www.australia.com/en/404.html). In this **Aussie** state you can** hike or bike** in the [mountains](https://www.mthotham.com.au/)or watch the **cutest penguins** walk home at sunset on the **beaches** of [Phillip Island](https://www.oprah.com/own-oprahshow/explore-phillip-island-video).

## 1. Cruise the River in Tasmania...

![Cruise the River in Tasmania...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/lady-launceston-cruising-the-cataract-gorge_404x278.jpg)

Photo: [Tamarrivercruises.com.au](https://web.archive.org/web/http://tamarrivercruises.com.au/index.html)

[Tasmania](https://www.australia.com/en/404.html)has more to offer a **newlywed couple** on their **honeymoon** than a [little marsupial](https://www.australiazoo.com.au/wildlife/our-animals/?mammal=tasmanian_devils&animal=tasmanian_devil)named after the devil himself! [Cruise](https://web.archive.org/web/http://tamarrivercruises.com.au/index.html)through the Cataract Gorge, glide along the [Hollybank treetops](https://web.archive.org/web/http://treetopsadventure.com.au/index.html), or **relax** and **drink wine** at the [vineyards](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.marionsvineyard.com/index.html). Make sure to do something you'll both enjoy and don't forget to just take it all in.

**What will you discover on your honeymoon in Australia?**
