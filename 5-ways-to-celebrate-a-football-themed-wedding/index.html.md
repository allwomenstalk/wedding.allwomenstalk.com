# 5 Ways to Celebrate a Football Themed Wedding ...

In honor of the[XLV Super Bowl](https://goo.gl/FisDJZ), the[Pittsburgh Steelers](https://www.steelers.com/), and the [Green Bay Packers](https://www.packers.com/)let's celebrate the only way we know...**wedding style**! No matter what team you favor, a **football themed wedding** can be fun, tasteful, and creative. Go all out with these **5 ways to celebrate a football themed wedding** and start the** tailgate party** right here...

## 5. A Touchdown Cake Topper...

![A Touchdown Cake Topper...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/football_400x480.jpg)

Photo: [JustCakeToppers](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.justcaketoppers.com/store/Wedding_Cake_Toppers/Humorous/Playful_Football_Couple.htm)

Whether you're **celebrating** your favorite [NFL](https://goo.gl/aavVQ5)team, your[college football team](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.seatonshootsblog.com/its-football-time-in-tennessee-bridal-portrait-in-orange-and-white/), or your **fiance's profession**, this **cake topper** will show off your** fun personality **along with your sports** **theme wedding. You don't have to go overboard and create a football explosion. Celebrate with **little details** like a [cool save the date](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-creative-ways-to-save-the-date/), [football engagement photos](http://sassaniphotography.blogspot.com/2010/07/kim-marco-dkr-memorial-stadium.html), or **football table names** and your **guests** will appreciate your** subtle decor**.

## 4. Football Favors...

![Football Favors...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/footballfavor_404x270.jpg)

Photo: [Flickr](https://www.flickr.com/photos/95822817@N00/3202881440/)

Give your **guests** a** football treat** to go home with when you buy these awesome [personalized M&Ms](http://redirect.viglink.com/?key=a7330d1bba7bc7d258ecc1463a1b25b1&u=http://www.mymms.com/NFL/Default.aspx)! My husband has always been a [Steelers fan](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-reasons-why-our-wedding-rocked/)and I knew these **football** [M&Ms](http://redirect.viglink.com/?key=a7330d1bba7bc7d258ecc1463a1b25b1&u=http://www.mymms.com/) would be the perfect **favor** to give our wedding guests, even if most of them were [Philadelphia Eagles fans](https://www.philadelphiaeagles.com/)! Are you celebrating your **favorite football team** during your **wedding**?

## 3. Show off Your Colors...

![Show off Your Colors...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/greenbaygarter_408x307.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy](https://goo.gl/yoyGM2)

Your husband-to-be is a **football fanatic** and you want to give him something special for your **football theme wedding**. Surprise your[lucky groom](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-classy-boudoir-photos-to-surprise-your-groom-with/)during your** garter toss** with this [Green Bay Packers** **](https://www.packers.com/)**team spirit**[wedding garter](https://goo.gl/yoyGM2)or in the **team colors** of your choice! Do you have a garter or maybe a pair of heels in your **favorite team's colors**?

## 2. Let Them Eat Cake...

![Let Them Eat Cake...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/greenbay_400x300.jpg)

Photo: [Flickr](https://www.flickr.com/photos/58879686@N00/3812117260)

Another great way to** surprise your guests** and your groom is with an [amazing grooms cake](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-ideas-for-an-owl-love-you-wedding/) like this **Green Bay Packers cheese cake**! Green Bay fans love to wear their[cheese heads](https://web.archive.org/web/http://store.thepackerman.com/cheeseheadgear.html) for the big game; now bring the **big game** right to your **wedding**! What does your **grooms cake** look like?

## 1. A Football Favorite...

![A Football Favorite...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/mascot_400x267.jpg)

Photo: [Flickr](https://www.flickr.com/photos/bkgrl_16/2779213047/sizes/m/in/photostream/)

When you go to a **football game** you remember the team, the stadium, the score, and the cool **mascot**! How unforgettable would your [wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-ways-to-be-inspired-by-wedding-balloons/)be when the [Steelers](https://www.steelers.com/)mascot[Steely McBeam](https://www.wtae.com/sports)walks into your** wedding reception**! Or maybe you could hold your wedding right on[Heinz field](https://acrisurestadium.com/private-events/)so Steely wouldn't have far to go!

**What details are you planning for your Football Theme Wedding?**
