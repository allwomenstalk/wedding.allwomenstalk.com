# 5 Ways to Display Succulents at Your Wedding ...

**Succulents** have become very **popular** for every type of **wedding**, there are no rules when it comes to **displaying** these **beautiful plants**. Let your imagination get **creative** with these eye catching wonders. Here are 5 ways you can **display succulents** at your **wedding**...

## 5. A Breathtaking Bouquet...

![A Breathtaking Bouquet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/bouquetsucculent_409x273.jpg)

Photo: [StephanieWilliamsPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://stephaniewilliamsphotography.com/blog/2010/03/02/erica-abrahm-wedding/)

**Succulents** can complement your **wedding decor** in many ways, whether it's [rustic](https://goo.gl/eZAa34), country, classic, [vintage](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-vintage-inspired-wedding-looks/), romantic, etc. No matter your **theme**, a **succulent bouquet** like this one just takes my breath away. I love how the **twine** completes the look. They are beautiful **on their own** or with a few **flowers mixed in**. How will your **succulent** **plants** be **displayed**?

## 4. Beachy Keen...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/01/beachbout_408x275.bmp)

Photo: [Ruffledblog](https://ruffledblog.com/a-vintage-hamptons-wedding/)

Would you have thought to display **succulents** with your** beach wedding decor**? Me either! I love how the** soft green** color blends with the orange and whites of the groom's **gorgeous beach boutonniere**. The contrasting colors catch your eye so you see everything in the [boutonniere](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-boutonnieres-for-your-groom-to-proudly-wear/) and not just one piece of it. I bet you are now thinking of adding these **pretty plants** into your **seaside wedding details**!

## 3. Share Your Succulents...

![Share Your Succulents...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/succulentfavor_408x306.jpg)

Photo:[OCEventStudio](https://web.archive.org/web/https://oceventstudio.wordpress.com/2010/07/05/beautiful-eco-friendly-succulent-wedding-designs-by-krista-jon/)

If you love **succulents** and[grow them in your garden](https://www.thespruce.com/grow-healthy-succulent-plants-1902806), why not share that love with your **guests**. It's a[simple favor](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-diy-wedding-favors/)with a thoughtful idea behind it. Not to mention they make for a **beautiful** **place setting**. I love the** rich colors** of the **orange fabric** against the** green plant**. Are you **growing succulents** for your wedding?

## 2. Have Your Cake & Eat It Too...

![Have Your Cake & Eat It Too...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/succulentcake_410x306.jpg)

Photo: [PrettyChicky](https://web.archive.org/web/http://prettychicky.com/2806/inspiration/succulent-wedding-decor)

Another wonderful idea for displaying your **lovely plants** and something that I haven't seen before. Include your **wedding cake** among the[rustic decor details](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-inspiring-ideas-from-martha-stewart-weddings/)that you have **planned** and you will have a complete look for your **succulent wedding! I love how they are displayed on the **entire cake** and not just as a [cake topper](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-awesome-wedding-cake-toppers/). What does your [rustic wedding cake](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/rustic-wedding-cakes-to-complement-your-theme/)** look like?

## 1. Succulent Love...

![Succulent Love...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/succulenthair_408x612.jpg)

Photo: [OCEventStudio](https://web.archive.org/web/https://oceventstudio.wordpress.com/2010/07/05/beautiful-eco-friendly-succulent-wedding-designs-by-krista-jon/)

When I saw this **stunning bride** I was just amazed at such a **unique** idea for **displaying succulents**. She **loves** them so much that she wanted them every where on her wedding day, even in her **hair**! She is radiant and her green hairpiece is glowing against her beautiful brown hair. Would you wear **succulents** in your [hair](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-bridal-hair-accessories-to-love/)?
