# 5 Etsy Wedding Finds for the Non\-DIY Bride ...

There are so many **DIY wedding posts** and **blogs** that make all of those adorable **projects** look so easy. If you have tried to sew a million little **hearts**, cut out tons of flowers, or glued together tiny buttons and have just failed with no win, don't fear you're in luck. Here are 5 **Etsy** Finds for the **Non-DIY Bride**...

## 5. I Heart Paper Garlands...

![I Heart Paper Garlands...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/heartgarland_411x322.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/PaperPolaroid](https://goo.gl/ootlws)
**Price**: $18.00

**Paper** from books, newspapers, and scrapbooking have shown up all over the wedding blogs in form of [bouquets](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-unique-wedding-bouquets-that-will-last-forever/), centerpieces, **banners**, and even for cake toppers. You may be crafty and can pull out those **creative** skills for those [projects](https://www.100layercake.com/blog/2010/11/23/diy-silhouette-drink-escort-cards/), but if you're like me, a **non-DIY bride**, then head on over to [Etsy](https://goo.gl/2ENGX2)for this beautiful** paper heart garland** that would look romantic and sweet over your **dessert table**.

## 4. Hearts Full of Yarn...

![Hearts Full of Yarn...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/heartwreath_411x548.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/hemccoy](https://goo.gl/ECybA8)
**Price**: $32.00

I saw these[DIY yarn wreaths](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.blogher.com/diy-yarn-wreath-felt-flowers)on every home and garden blog over the holidays and really wanted to make one for our front door, but never did! We get so busy, **brides** especially, and there just isn't enough time for all of the ideas we want to create. Don't let time run out before your big day, instead of DIY all of your **wedding details** check out [Etsy](https://goo.gl/woZfsA)and other stores for **premade crafts** to lighten your heavy bridal load.

## 3. Top off Your Cake...

![Top off Your Cake...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/cupcaketoppers_410x307.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/TheBestPaperSupplies](https://goo.gl/mxOGYa)
**Price**: $8.50

Sometimes it's the** simple little details** that make your wedding pop like these adorable LOVE** cupcake toppers** from [Etsy](https://goo.gl/mxOGYa). These look like they would be simple to make but maybe you're having 200 guests, so that means you would need to make 200 little cupcake toppers! That might be a [project](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/diy-wedding-ideas-on-a-budget-guest-blogger-poppies-sunshine/)you may want to skip to let someone else handle the **stress** of it.

## 2. Hearts in Your Hair...

![Hearts in Your Hair...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/heartband_409x407.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/uffdadesigns](https://goo.gl/YzjA7b)
**Price**: $18.00

Many brides may find it **too expensive** to purchase a beautiful bridal headband or **too difficult** to make their own. There are stunning big floral headbands, glitter and crystal headbands, and metallic ones that just seem overpriced or tough to make. Find something more low key like this adorable [heart felt band](https://goo.gl/yMP2lL)that would **save** you **time** and **money** from making your own or buying something too expensive. You don't need glitter to be bridal!

## 1. I Adore This Pillow...

![I Adore This Pillow...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/heartringpillow_411x528.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/LilliansGarden](https://goo.gl/8AjK1N)
**Price**: $38.00

This little** heart ring pillow** is just the sweetest thing I've seen and how cute would the ring bearer look as he carries this down the aisle! Ring pillows are another big DIY project that many brides are creating, but when you see **pillows** like this one on [Etsy](https://goo.gl/SXc5ty)you can't help but to be a **Non-DIY bride**!
