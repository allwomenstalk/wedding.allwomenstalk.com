# 7 Ways to Be a Happier Bride ...

Would you love to know some ways to be a happier bride? Of course you would! No one wants to turn into a snarling and scary bridezilla. These are some helpful ways to be a happier bride that you can consider.

## 1. Focus on the End Result

Sometimes it can be very easy to get completely wrapped up in the wedding plans and lose focus of what comes after the wedding. One of the ways to be a happier bride is to keep this in perspective. Remember that your wedding is one day but your marriage is forever. Make your coming marriage the main point of focus. When you do this, your stress level will be greatly decreased.

## 2. Delegate

One very wise bit of advice to follow is to delegate. You cannot possibly orchestrate your entire wedding alone, especially if it is a large one. Allow people to help you. Hire help where you need it. This allows you to focus on yourself on that special day.

## 3. Decide to Have a Calm Attitude

You know, having a calm attitude comes easily to some people. For others, it does not. Regardless, you can still make the choice to have a calm attitude. Decide to approach your wedding without allowing pressure to weigh you down. Your wedding is supposed to be an enjoyable event. Don’t let stress rob you of that.

## 4. Take Care of Yourself

It is easy to neglect your basic needs when you're so focused on the wedding. Nevertheless, it is still important to make sure that you eat right and get enough sleep as well as making sure all of your other needs are met. If you don’t take care of yourself, you put yourself at risk of getting sick. That is something you absolutely do not want on your wedding day. Be careful to set limits on how much you devote to your wedding and make sure that you are not neglecting your basic needs.

## 5. Don’t Stop Dating

Don’t ever stop dating. You should date all of your lives, even years after you are married. As for now, you should not allow your wedding plans to totally take over your relationship. Of course you are going to need to discuss things about the wedding, but make sure that you set some times aside to just enjoy each other’s company. After all, that is how you ended up headed for marriage in the first place.

## 6. Do Something Just for Yourself

Wedding planning and preparation is stressful. Do some small things along the way just for yourself. Schedule a pedicure. Call up your girlfriends and meet after work for some fun. Cuddle up with a good book and a hot cup of tea. Whatever refreshes your soul, do it.

## 7. Practice Gratitude

In the midst of stress, it is easy to forget how many things are going right. You begin focusing on all of the complications that can pop up in wedding planning. Take some measures to make sure you don’t get overwhelmed. Stop and think of all the good things that are happening to you. You are planning a beautiful wedding with the man of your dreams. Cherish that blessing.

These are some things you can do be a happier bride. What about you? Have you found it difficult to remain happy in the chaos of wedding planning?
