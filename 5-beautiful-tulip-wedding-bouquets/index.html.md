# 5 Beautiful Tulip Wedding Bouquets ...

Everyone has been thinking and dreaming of **Spring**, especially since last Friday when we hit a record here in the Northeast of 70 degrees! Mother nature is giving us little hints of the warm weather with beautiful sunny skies, so hopefully we'll see **tulips**, roses, daisies, and other **blossoms** pop up soon. In the mean time find your Spring inspiration in these **5 beautiful tulip wedding bouquets**...

## 5. Soft & Dreamy...

![Soft & Dreamy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/elegant-bouquet2_400x499.jpg)

Photo: [JL Designs](https://web.archive.org/web/http://ww38.jldesignsandevents.com/)via [Bollea](https://web.archive.org/web/https://perfectdomain.com/domain/bollea.com)

This lovely wedding bouquet is made of[blush parrot tulips](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.hollandbulbfarms.com/itemdesc.asp?amp;cat=TULIPPARROT&ic=19238&item=Libretto-Parrot-Tulips)and [ivory astilbe](https://www.thespruce.com/growing-astible-plants-1402833). I just adore the texture of the **astilbe**, lately I have seen magical bouquets made of [pink astilbe](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/10/06/palm-springs-wedding-from-carter-cook-and-the-floral-lab/). The colors and soft feel of this bouquet are so **dreamy**, does it not make you want a garden full of parrot tulips and ivory astilbe!

## 4. Succulent Love...

![Succulent Love...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/bouquet_407x509.jpg)

Photo: [Martha Stewart](https://www.marthastewart.com/7900176/tulip-wedding-bouquets)

You should know by now that I have an obsession with [succulents](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-display-succulents-at-your-wedding/), and this detailed bouquet just makes my love for them grow! The **colors**, the **texture**, the combination of plants and flowers, and the **arrangement** is genius. I love the way each plant and flower is represented on their own but together they make a beautiful** rustic bouquet** of succulents, **tulips**, spotted phalaenopsis orchids, columbine, and pink and white ranunculus.

## 3. Bright & Beautiful...

![Bright & Beautiful...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/tulipleaves_412x275.jpg)

Photo: [Cori Cook](http://www.coricook.net/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

I love to see bouquets mixed with **leaves**, plants, and even stems. These gorgeous **pink tulips** are given some contrast with **aspidistra leaves**; which are commonly used at the bottom of wedding bouquets as a [collar](https://web.archive.org/web/https://moderndaycollective.com/). Something so [simple](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-sweet-simple-wedding-bouquets/), yet so bright and beautiful.

## 2. Pretty in Pink...

![Pretty in Pink...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/pinktulips_406x508.jpg)

Photo: [Martha Stewart](https://www.marthastewart.com/7900176/tulip-wedding-bouquets)
This bride's favorite color must be **pink**! Another stunning bouquet with **astilbe**, these are the wonderful pink ones I was talking about earlier. Ranunculuses, sweet peas, and [rosario tulips](https://www.backyardgardener.com/plantname/)make up the rest of this stunning bouquet. It's amazing to see different flowers and textures in similar colors. Have you found an array of flowers in your** favorite color**?

## 1. Show Stopper...

![Show Stopper...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/yelllowandorange_416x624.jpg)

Photo: [AGardenParty](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.gardenpartysisters.com/) via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/01/19/south-jersey-summer-wedding-from-a-garden-party/)

Don't you just love the **colors** of this show stopping bouquet! **Orange** mini gerber daisies, hot pink spray roses, **yellow tulips**, orange calla lilies, protea, and **pink** dahlias are brought together in this **outstanding bouquet**. I can't believe I found a bouquet with all of my favorite flowers! You must visit the link above to see the rest of the floral arrangements and cake at this fabulous Summer wedding.
