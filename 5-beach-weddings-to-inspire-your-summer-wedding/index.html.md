# 5 Beach Wedding Ideas to Inspire Your Summer Wedding ...

**Beach weddings** are very popular, either at home or far away for a **destination** **wedding**. However, some brides and grooms can't afford to plan a beach wedding but would like to create the feel of one for their sizzling summer wedding. Look through these** 5 beach weddings to inspire your summer wedding **and bring your guests' imaginations right onto the **boardwalk! **

## 5. Wish upon a StarFish...

![Wish upon a StarFish...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/starfishcards_409x409.jpg)

Photo: [Brides.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-flowers/editorial-pick/2009/05/blooms-by-the-beach)

Bring the feel and smell of the **beach** right to your **guests** as they walk through your reception doors. These beautiful [starfish](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=shorechic.com) can double as a **seating card** and [favor](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-unique-wedding-favors/) for your guests to take home. Every time they see it they will remember the **amazing summer wedding **and feel like they're at the beach again! **What do your beach escort cards look like? **

## 4. Something for Their Feet...

![Something for Their Feet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/flipflops_400x266.jpg)

Photo: [ElizabethAnneDesigns.com](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/)

**Beach + Flip-Flops= Summer!** Make your guests feel like they're** walking on the boardwalk **as they slip off their dress shoes and [relax their feet](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-hottest-havaianas-flip-flops/) into the creative favor idea you thought of! Don't forget to find [flip-flops](https://goo.gl/8lyUg8) that match your **wedding colors** and get multiple sizes. **What beach idea did you create for your summer wedding? **

## 3. Beach Bouquet...

![Beach Bouquet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/beachbouquet_400x600.jpg)

Photo: [WeddingChicks.com](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/nautical-romance-wedding-ideas-at-the-beach-in-the-off-season-l-13972-l-41.html)

Plan an **elegant summer wedding** as you walk down the aisle holding this gorgeous [sea shell bouquet](https://www.marthastewart.com/7853663/shell-bouquet). Mix it up with some greens and moss for an earthy look. I love the look of the **bouquet** with her beautiful lace wedding gown. With this** elegant beach look** your guests will feel like they're on the sands of the [Hamptons](https://www.orbitz.com/?brandcid=domain.away.com)! What [beach](https://web.archive.org/web/https://allwomenstalk.com/7-reasons-to-have-a-beach-wedding/2/) atmosphere will **inspire** your **summer wedding**?

## 2. Beachy Keen Invitations...

![Beachy Keen Invitations...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/beachinvitation_402x555.jpg)

Photo: [Brendasweddingblog.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.brendasweddingblog.com/blogs/2009/7/20/beach-inspired-wedding-invitations.html)

Give your guests a little hint of the places you will take them during your **beach inspired wedding**. Send a [seahorse save the date](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.shophandmade.com/Item?Item1=H35Y0Q6), or a creative [sand dollar invitation box](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.passionpaper.com/nautical_sanddollar.html). They will be intrigued to see your summer wedding when they see the[fun invitation](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-gorgeous-wedding-invitations/) and will send in their yes reply right away! **Did you send out beach invitations for your summer wedding?**

## 1. We All Scream for Ice Cream...

![We All Scream for Ice Cream...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/eat-drink-chic-ice-cream_403x387.jpg)

Photo: [Polkadotbride.com](https://polkadotwedding.com/)

**Vanilla**, **Strawberry**, or maybe even **Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough!** What** ice cream** flavor reminds you of that wonderful **vacation** on the **beach** last summer? [Ice Cream](https://web.archive.org/web/https://thecrepescafe.com/7-awesome-ice-cream-flavors/) is a [summer treat](https://www.marbleslab.com/catering/) that everyone remembers so give your guests something they'll never forget about your boardwalk summer wedding. Include all their **favorite toppings** like cherries, jimmies, nuts, and whipped topping to take them back to that special summer place! **Will there be a [ice cream](http://www.eatdrinkchic.com/post.cfm/diy-ice-cream-parlour-make-your-own-sundaes-buffet-concept-for-wedding-or-party) or dessert bar at your wedding?**

**Are you heading to the beach for your wedding? **
