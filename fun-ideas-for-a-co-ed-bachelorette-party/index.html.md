# 8 Fun Ideas for a Co\-Ed Bachelorette Party ...

Not every bride and groom is looking for bachelor and bachelorette parties that feature half naked dancers, so why not consider ideas for a co-ed bachelorette party? Many couples are starting to combine their girls' and guys' nights out to celebrate together. If this sounds like you then continue reading for 8 Fun Ideas for a Co-Ed Bachelorette Party...

## 1. A Day at the Beach...

Here's one of the best ideas for a co-ed bachelorette party. Plan a co-ed party on the beach with a day filled with volleyball and surfing, and end the night with a bonfire and drinking if the laws of the town allow for it! It's the perfect way to have a co-ed party yet still have fun individually when you chat with your girls while the guys are busy throwing the ball around.

## 2. Sight-Seeing...

This is a great co-ed bachelorette party idea for a destination wedding or for when you have a lot of out of town guests. See what the town has to offer, explore places you've never been to before, show your guests famous landmarks, and end the night with dinner and drinks.

## 3. Skiing or Snow Tubing...

If you're planning a winter wedding or are big winter sports enthusiasts, head to the slopes for your co-ed bachelorette party. There's something for everyone at a ski lodge party, whether you want to ski, go tubing, snowboarding, or even stay inside to enjoy the spa or hot tub!

## 4. Play the Slots...

Are you close to a casino, or want to get really adventurous and take a trip to Vegas? Why not make your co-ed bachelorette party one to really remember when you play the slots and win big! At some casinos you can also find great places to dance and listen to live music. So put on your favorite party dress and play your luck at the casino for a night of gambling and dancing.

## 5. Take Me out to the Ball Game...

If you're both big sports fanatics, football, baseball, hockey, etc., consider going to a ball game for your co-ed bachelorette party. Sports games are a great way for both gals and guys to have fun together and not feel like this is something that is just for one person or the other. Root on your favorite team, have a few cold ones, and find out about the chance to get a group photo with the players or mascot to really complete your celebration.

## 6. Wine Tasting...

Is your big day being held at a local vineyard? Why not call your venue and see if you can host your co-ed bachelorette party there? Who knows, they might give you a discount! Sip on some wine, give your party guests a sneak peek of the vineyard, and experience the fun of what it takes to make the wine you've been tasting all day long.

## 7. Camping...or Glamping!

Your girls might not be into camping, but they may be into glamping for your co-ed bachelorette party! Glamping is where your ladies can enjoy some of the comforts of home while still being out in the wilderness with the guys. So go hiking, fishing, tell ghost stories while making s'mores, and sleep on a comfortable bed that's inside your tent!

## 8. Music Festival...

Don't just go to one concert, make a weekend out of it and go to a music festival for your co-ed bachelorette party. There are music fests like Bonnaroo and Coachella that go on for a few days and also feature other activities for your group to enjoy. What music fest would you like to go to for your co-ed bachelorette party?

Whether you are an active or a low-key couple, there are plenty of co-ed bachelorette party ideas you can enjoy together. From a wine tasting weekend to a day at the ballpark, there is something for everyone. Share these co-ed bachelorette party ideas with your friends and see which ones they prefer and think would be a good idea for your group. Remember the only bad idea for a co-ed bachelorette party is not having a designated driver or calling a cab to take everyone home. Are you having a co-ed celebration?
