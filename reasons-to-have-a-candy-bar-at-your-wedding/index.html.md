# 7 Reasons to Have a Candy Bar at Your Wedding ...

Having a candy bar at your wedding is a wonderful idea for several reasons. Have you heard of the candy bar craze that is becoming quite popular with weddings? A candy bar is when you basically have a buffet set up that is completely made of different candies. Let me tell you some reasons why having a candy bar at your wedding is a good idea.

## 1. It is Trendy

![It is Trendy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xd/up/eq/n4elo1v4_600x399.jpg)

Having a candy bar at your wedding is trendy and different. It is a relatively new trend and you can have a part in setting it. Most brides want to have their own unique flair in their wedding and this is a way to do it. It isn’t a normal choice in food for wedding receptions. You can do this at many different types of weddings, too. It can go with a menu based reception or a much smaller affair.

## 2. It is a Huge Crowd Pleaser

![It is a Huge Crowd Pleaser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qv/ib/hi/taj96epv_600x401.jpg)

Seriously, who doesn’t love candy? I don’t know anyone who doesn’t have a favorite candy that makes them happy. Choosing to have a candy bar is a huge crowd pleaser. You can have many different kinds of candy displayed so there is sure to be something for everyone. Candy makes people happy and that is all there is to it.

## 3. It is Memorable

![It is Memorable](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k4/jj/na/xr6q6dzq_576x384.jpg)

A candy bar is memorable. It isn’t something every couple chooses to have at their reception. It is something that people will remember about your wedding. They are likely to tell their friends and family about it and even suggest it for other weddings that they have a hand in. Everyone wants their wedding to be fondly remembered and a candy bar can help you to accomplish that.

## 4. It is Very Economical

![It is Very Economical](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/wa/vs/m7m3wtix_600x400.jpg)

A candy bar is economical. Candy is really pretty cheap. Even if you figure that every guest takes one candy bar off of the candy bar, you are still looking at a pretty low expense. It is a great way to add some fun to your wedding for a small cost. Weddings are expensive; it only makes sense to save money where you can.

## 5. It is Festive & Fun

![It is Festive & Fun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/tc/wy/kix0lqpi_600x398.jpg)

A candy bar is festive and fun. You can do so many different things to arrange a candy bar artfully. It can be arranged with a fancy flair or in a casual array. It is completely up to you. Any way that you go, you have added an air of festivity and fun to your wedding.

## 6. It Keeps the Kids Happy

Kids love candy. It is practically the language of children, right? It will help to keep them happy at your wedding, too. Even if they don’t like the smoked salmon you are serving, you can bet that a bag of M&Ms is right up their alley. Everybody wins.

## 7. You Can Get Creative with It

![You Can Get Creative with It](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vi/vq/rc/id65itl3_600x401.jpg)

You can get very creative when you decide to have a candy bar at your wedding. You can arrange it with all of your candy separate or all mixed up and fun. There are a lot of different ways that you can choose to display the candy at a candy bar. It is up to you and there is no wrong way to do it. Go with whatever makes you feel best.

There are so many reasons to have a candy bar at your wedding. Have you ever been at a wedding with a candy bar? What do you think of the idea?
