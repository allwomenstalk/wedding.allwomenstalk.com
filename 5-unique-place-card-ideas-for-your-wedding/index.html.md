# 5 Unique Place Card Ideas for Your Wedding ...

Every** bride and groom** wants their wedding to be **unique** in **decor**, **attire**, **favors**, and **details**. You don't want to look like you're trying to pull off someone else's wedding you attended two months ago! To help you find that unique idea here are **6 unique place card ideas for your wedding! **

## 5. Something Good to Eat...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2010/12/cookieplacecard_402x265.png)

Photo: [PreparetoWed.com/blog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://preparetowed.com/blog/2010/12/seating-card-cookies-cookie-creatives-by-jennifer.html/comment-page-1#comment-4836)

When I saw this **adorable cookie** I knew I had to share it and do a post about **wedding place cards**! Many couples have [delicious cookie favors](https://web.archive.org/web/https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ultimate-cookie-recipes/2/) but I haven't seen a [cookie place card](https://web.archive.org/web/http://preparetowed.com/vendors/NH/Favors/Cookie-Creatives-by-Jennifer.aspx) like this one. I just love it, don't you? Remind your guests not to eat it before they find their table!** Did you have cookies at your wedding?**

******4. Go Green...**

![Something Good to Eat...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/plantable-seed-place-card-500_400x400.jpg)

Photo: [Beau-coup.com](https://goo.gl/I1GV9z)

Would you believe that this** place card is plantable**? Dig a hole in the dirt, put the place card in, water, and soon you shall have a little garden of beautiful wildflowers! Each **seating card** is made of [biodegradable paper](https://greenpaperproducts.com/) that is embedded with **wildflower seeds**. You get a **place card**, a **favor**, and [no waste](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-reasons-to-use-eco-friendly-products/)! **What are your green wedding ideas? **

## 3. Winter Wonderland...

![Winter Wonderland...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/winterplacecard_411x329.jpg)

Photo: [Projectweddingfavors.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.projectweddingfavors.com/category/place-card-holders-and-frames.html)

**Christmas** is this week but if you're planning a **winter wedding for 2011** surprise your guests with this lovely **place card**. I love place cards that **double as a favor**, no need to buy two wedding items when you can save on your budget. These [magical snowflake ornaments](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.projectweddingfavors.com/product/snow-flurry-flocked-glass-ornament-place.html)will bring your [winter wonderland theme](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-cozy-winter-weddings-to-heat-up-your-fire/) to life as they **light up** your tables. **Are you using ornaments in your winter wedding?**

## 2. Head to the Beach...

![Head to the Beach...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/BeachChairFrame_408x326.jpg)

Photo: [MomentsofElegance.com](http://www.eweddingfavors.com/)

I love **beach chairs** and this one is perfect for a **place card** or a picture frame favor at your [beach theme wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-beach-weddings-to-inspire-your-summer-wedding/). Every time your guests see them in their home they will be reminded of the **beautiful summer wedding **you planned. These **fun beach chair** place cards will look perfect next to your [seashell centerpieces](https://www.marthastewart.com/7961134/nonfloral-centerpieces). **What ideas do you have for your beach wedding?**

## 1. Light the Way...

![Light the Way...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/luminaryplacecardvotives_405x303.jpg)

Photo: [MomentsofElegance.com](http://www.eweddingfavors.com/)

**Elegant**, **Classic**, and **Romantic**. Set the **mood** for your **romantic wedding** with these beautiful **candle place cards**. Everyone loves [candles](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-gorgeous-candles/) and now they will have their very own **personalized** one! I love the glow of the candle through the[vellum paper](https://www.papersource.com/gift-wrap/wrapping-paper). Have your guests put them at the center of the table where you have placed **rose petals **or **ribbon** and you have one easy made centerpiece!
