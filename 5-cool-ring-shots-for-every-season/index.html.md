# 5 Cool Ring Shots for Every Season ...

Thanks to the[photography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.weddingbeepro.com/2008/12/08/how-to-take-amazing-engagement-and-wedding-ring-photos/)and **wedding blogs** no one takes a photo of their **wedding rings** on their hand any more! Now with our photographers we find **cool** ways to **display our rings** and** create a moment** we'll never forget. For your **ring shot inspiration** read on to see these** 5 cool ring shots for every season**...

## 5. Holiday Celebration...

![Holiday Celebration...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/ring-shotchristmasornaments_403x265.jpg)

Photo: [NicheWhite](https://nichewhite.wordpress.com/2010/12/22/the-most-gorgeous-2/)

[Christmas](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-cozy-winter-weddings-to-heat-up-your-fire/)and the **holiday season** may have passed but there are newly engaged couples out there planning a** 2011 Christmas wedding**! Even if you're having a summer or fall wedding, this **clever** and** stunning ring shot** makes for a great[newlywed Christmas card](https://goo.gl/68gp5z)!

## 4. Spring Grapes...

![Spring Grapes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/graperingshot_408x272.jpg)

Photo: [MDavidMedia](https://web.archive.org/web/http://mdavidmedia.com/blog/?p=971)

Whether you're walking down the [vineyard aisle](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.zenithvineyard.com/), a [wine loving couple](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-places-to-travel-on-your-honeymoon-in-italy/), or planning a [purple wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-inspiring-ways-to-plan-a-purple-wedding/), this is an amazing** ring shot** for your** Spring Wedding. **I love the way the sunlight hits the **rings** and shines through the vineyard. Will there be **grapes** and **wine** on your wedding day?

## 3. Summertime Crabs...

![Summertime Crabs...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/crab_wedding_ring_shot_409x275.jpg)

Photo: [GabrielBoone](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.gabrielboone.com/blog/2010/08/18/jen-paul-married-lopez-island-wa/)

It's **summer time** and the **crabs** are cooking, or they are taking part in the **best ring shot ever**! Who would have thought to put a **crab** with their **wedding** **rings**? The [photographer](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.gabrielboone.com/blog/)did an **incredible** job and thought [outside the box](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-find-ideas-outside-the-wedding-box/)for this one! This is my** favorite shot** for a **Summer Wedding**!

## 2. Fall into Love...

![Fall into Love...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/leaf_405x610.jpg)

Photo:[EverOurs](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.ever-ours.com/2011/01/real-wedding-wildflower-tennessee.html)

**Simple**, Delicate, and [Rustic](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-rustic-wedding-centerpiece-ideas/). I love that this **ring shot** speaks for your **fall** **wedding** in just **one** **simple** **photo**. This is a great idea for a rustic, **outdoor** wedding as well. Play in the **leaves**, walk down the aisle in your[cowgirl boots](https://shoes.allwomenstalk.com/7-hot-western-boots/), and say 'I Do'. Are you planning a **rustic fall wedding**?
