# 7 Designer Bridal Gown Sketches for Kate Middleton ...

If you love **weddings** and **celebrities** then you may have heard the wonderful news about the recent **engagement** of **Prince William** and **Kate Middleton**. Millions, perhaps billions of people all over the world will find out in **April 2011** if Kate will be wearing one of these **7 Designer Bridal Gowns**, take a guess at the one you think she'll be wearing...

## 4. Gowns Made for a Princess...

![Gowns Made for a Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-pictures3_405x306.jpg)

Photo: [Brides.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/blogs/aisle-say/2010/11/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-ideas-royal-princess-gown.html)

Left: [Melissa Sweet](https://beta.davidsbridal.com/?search=melissa+sweet), Right: [Priscilla of Boston](https://beta.davidsbridal.com/?gid=1)

I love the endless **romance** that flows from these two stunning gowns. The **Melissa Sweet** ballgown reminds me of a traditional [princess gown](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-prettiest-disney-princess-wedding-gowns/)but has a magical glow to it that would perfectly match Kate's glowing smile! **Priscilla of Boston **designers know how to complement a woman's body with the lovely **lace** gown on the right. How beautifully it would show off the young Princess, but still make her look like a **Royal bride**.

## 3. One Day a Queen...

![One Day a Queen...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-pictures2_405x307.jpg)

Photo: [Brides.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/blogs/aisle-say/2010/11/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-ideas-royal-princess-gown.html)

Left: [Amsale](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.amsale.com/), Right: [Angel Sanchez](https://web.archive.org/web/http://angelsanchezusa.com/blb_1.php)

A [cathedral veil](https://goo.gl/YpzPNN) just speaks **Queen** and one day Kate shall be just that. So when **Amsale** paired their remarkable **cap-sleeved gown **with a **flowing veil** they knew they would be on[Queen Elizabeth'](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/queen-elizabeth/)s good side, but what about Kate's? On the other hand sometimes young **Royal** heirs like to be **daring**, and **Angel Sanchez** has given this bride-to-be a wonderful **creative** dress for that option! **Do you think Kate would be bold and daring? **

## 2. One Very Stylish Bride...

![One Very Stylish Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-st-pucchi_403x403.jpg)

Photo: [Brides.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/wedding-dresses-style/wedding-dresses/2010/09/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-top-designers-sketch-ideas)

Designer[Rani Totman](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.stpucchi.com/)wanted to give Kate two options with one **beautiful bodice**. Both skirts remind me of the very **stylish** young lady Kate is, she would love both and maybe she'll wear both! The first amazing skirt has a **silk satin inlay** with an overskirt that is covered with sparkling **Swarovski crystals**. For the second skirt the bride would dance in a fun **ruched silk organza** with a **long cathedral train, **one that **Princess Diana** would love. I'm sure she is very proud of her son and soon to be[daughter-in-law](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-find-a-relationship-with-your-future-mother-in-law/). **What dress would Diana choose for Kate?**** **

![One Very Happy Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/kate-middleton-wedding-dress-pictures1_405x296.jpg)
