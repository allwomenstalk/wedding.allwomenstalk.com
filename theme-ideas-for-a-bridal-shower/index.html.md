# 5 Theme Ideas for a Bridal Shower ...

Instead of planning a bachelorette party and a shower, why not put more emphasis into a **bridal shower** with themes that last all day long. Combine both celebrations into one when you spend the day at the spa, take a cooking class together, or go on a walk through a winery. Here are 5 **theme ideas** for your bridal shower to share with your family and friends throwing the celebration...

## 5. How Does Your Garden Grow...

![How Does Your Garden Grow...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/gardentheme_406x508.jpg)

Shower your DIY, **green thumb bride** with a day filled with potting tips, tulip bulbs, and seed packets when you throw her a **garden theme** bridal shower. Maybe the soon to be Mr. & Mrs. have moved into a new house and need fresh [flowers and plants](https://gardening.allwomenstalk.com/5-tips-for-designing-a-garden/). Surprise her with digging tools, gloves, and [little rose bushes](https://web.archive.org/web/http://johnsminiatureroses.com/cgi-bin/browse.cgi?page=searchresult&browseall=1)as you get down in the dirt to help beautify their new home.

## 4. Wine Tasting...

![Wine Tasting...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/winetheme_406x508.jpg)

This bridal shower theme should be a fun one that could last all night! Ask the ladies to bring a **bottle of wine** with them for the[wine loving couple](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-benefits-of-red-wine/)and spend all night guessing where it came from and how old it is. Or head out for a **road trip** to the nearest [vineyard](https://web.archive.org/web/https://napavalley.com/)and enjoy wine, cheese, and even a [grape stomping](https://web.archive.org/web/http://stonehillwinery.com/locations/hermann/events/GrapeStomp.aspx)!

## 3. Time for Tea...

![Time for Tea...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/TeaParty_406x508.jpg)

With the [Royal Wedding](https://allwomenstalk.com/mother-of-the-bride-dresses-for-the-royal-wedding/)on Friday everyone is sure to have **English tea** on their minds! Ask everyone to bring a beautiful **tea cup** with them, or give them out as [favors](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-inspiring-ideas-from-martha-stewart-weddings/)and sip from them as you enjoy your lemon cookies. You could also spend the day at a [tea house](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.savannahtearoom.com/teaservice.html)and let someone else do all the work for you!

## 2. Spa Day...

![Spa Day...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/SpaDay_406x508.jpg)

Why not **pamper your bride** and each other when you share a **day at the spa** laughing, [drinking mimosa](https://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?B=120126&U=389818&M=17025&affTrack=l5qur1vwxk0025vh05bii&urllink=www.minted.com%2Fcustomizer%2Fsku%2FMIN-Z30-BSI), and getting your nails done. What bridal shower guests wouldn't love this idea! Gift each girl and the bride with a personalized robe or towel for a day everyone will remember sharing.

## 1. Bring a Recipe...

![Bring a Recipe...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/cookingtheme_403x504.jpg)

If the bride and groom love to **cook together** ask everyone to bring a **new recipe** the couple can [whip up](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-best-cooking-websites/)with their new kitchen tools you bought them. To make a day out of it look for a [cooking class](https://www.long-island-caterer.com/cooking_classes_long_island/cooking-class-photo-albums/cooking-class-photos-bridal-shower-party.htm)everyone would enjoy; maybe you can learn how to make cupcakes for the wedding!
