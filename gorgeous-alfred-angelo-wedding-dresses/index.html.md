# 7 Gorgeous Alfred Angelo Wedding Dresses ...

Alfred Angelo wedding dresses are known for their affordable prices and wide range of styles, from the classically elegant to the chic and trendy. The line’s current collections are definitely no exception, with tons of gorgeous dresses available to satisfy every bride, whether she wants to channel her inner Disney princess or glam it up like a Hollywood starlet. But let’s stop talking about the dresses and get on with actually looking at them, okay? Here’s a quick glimpse at a few of my favorite Alfred Angelo wedding dresses.

## 1. Modern Classic

![Modern Classic](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/1_modern-classic_340x450.jpg)

From the off-shoulder sleeves to the modern combination of satin, taffeta and pleated organza, the first on my list of fabulous Alfred Angelo wedding dresses strikes me as being versatile enough for everything from a classic church wedding to a summertime vineyard celebration. The beaded detailing on the belt adds the perfect amount of sparkle, while the chapel train is also simple yet eye-catching. With a dress this pretty, plus a price tag of only around $1,149, how can you help but fall in love?

## 2. Cascading Romance

![Cascading Romance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/2_cascading-romance_340x450.jpg)

Thanks to a sleek bodice, plus cascading layers of organza tumbling the entire length of the skirt and train, this dreamy dress manages to be both sexy and romantic all at once. And let’s not overlook the strapless cut and sweetheart neckline, which also add timeless and classic touches. While this gown is slightly more expensive than the first, getting a dress you love for right around $1,499 is guaranteed to be well worth the money.

## 3. Blush Beauty

![Blush Beauty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/3_blush-beauty_540x750.jpg)

Part of Alfred Angelo’s popular Disney Fairy Tale Bridal collection, the elegant Sleeping Beauty gown allows you to look like a princess while also getting in on the blush trend – one of the hottest looks in bridal fashion today. Like many other Alfred Angelo gowns, this dress features beautiful detailing at the waist, along with a romantic chapel train. Need another reason to love it? This lovely gown has a suggested retail price of just $999.

## 4. Vintage Starlet

![Vintage Starlet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/4_vintage-starlet_340x450.jpg)

There are few times in life when you truly get the chance to be a star, and your wedding day is certainly one of them! This form-fitting lace and charmeuse dress from Alfred Angelo's Modern Vintage Bridal collection instantly evokes thoughts of Old Hollywood glamour, so pair it with a couple pieces of decadent jewelry, plus a vintage floral headpiece, and you’ll be more than ready to make the grandest of entrances. Want to make this dress your own? You can for no more than $1,799.

## 5. Black and White Flair

![Black and White Flair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/5_black-and-white-flair_340x450.jpg)

Black and white details are having a huge moment in the world of bridal fashion, and this uniquely designed dress is the perfect example of trendy meets romantic. More than any of the others we’ve seen so far, this gown just might be a perfect choice for the bride who’s planning a formal evening wedding but also wants to add a non-traditional twist to her look. Not into the trend, but still love the dress, which costs right around $1,199? It’s also sold in white or ivory.

## 6. Fairytale Elegance

![Fairytale Elegance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/6_fairytale-elegance_554x385.jpg)

If you were a little girl who always fantasized about becoming Cinderella, this is one dress that’s sure to impress! Aptly named the Cinderella Platinum, this show-stopping ball gown features a bejeweled bodice and semi-cathedral train. With a price tag of around $1,599, it’s definitely one of the most expensive on my list, but also one of the most stunning by far.

## 7. Short and Sweet

![Short and Sweet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/7_short-and-sweet_340x450.jpg)

Priced at right around $575, this tea-length dress is perfect for the bride who wants to show off her heels and also add a pop of color at the waist. Sash colors come in 50 fun shades, too, allowing you to perfectly match your bridesmaids. Check it out for a dress that will have you looking and feeling beautiful without breaking the bank.
