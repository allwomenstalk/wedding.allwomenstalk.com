# 7 Classic Tiffany Engagement Rings ...

There really is nothing quite **like a classic Tiffany engagement ring**. They’re timeless, beautiful, something that can be handed down for generations, heirlooms in the making. If you’re considering an engagement, and haven’t chosen a ring yet, here are 7 classic Tiffany engagement rings to consider.

## 1. Tiffany Legacy

_ Priced from $14,900 at tiffany.com_

Inspired by the romantic designs of the Edwardian period, this classic Tiffany engagement ring features a cushion-cut diamond set in a band with bead-set diamonds. It’s all sweetness and shine, with a center stone varying in size from 0.3 to 2.5 carats.

## 2. Tiffany Novo

_ Priced from $13,700 at tiffany.com_

This very contemporary yet classic Tiffany Engagement ring was inspired by the fire and brilliance of the legendary 128.54-carat Tiffany Diamond. The center stone is cushion-cut, and ranges in carat size from 0.3 to 2.5.

## 3. Tiffany Embrace

_ Priced from $13,300 at tiffany.com_

How pretty! This ring features a round-cut center stone, «embraced» by more than a dozen smaller stones. The center stone can be as small as a half-carat or as large as two and a half carats.

## 4. Tiffany Grace

_ Priced from $10,900 at tiffany.com_

There’s nothing as classic as a princess-cut diamond like the one in this engagement ring. It’s set in platinum, complimented by an array of round brilliant diamonds. Choose a center stone ranging in size from a 0.25 to 2.5 carats.

## 5. Tiffany Etoile

_ Priced from $ at tiffany.com_

**This classic Tiffany engagement ring** is very unique! It features a bezel-set round brilliant diamond (0.25 to 1 carat in size) in a platinum surround, set in a gold band. It’s also available in a platinum band, or with three stones rather than one.

## 6. Tiffany Soleste

_ Priced from $4,200 at tiffany.com_

So much sparkle and shine! This classic Tiffany engagement ring is all about the glamour, with a round or cushion-cut center stone and row after row (well, only two rows) of round brilliant diamonds. The total carat weight us just under a half-carat, but the shine makes it seem like so much more.

## 7. Tiffany Lucinda

_ Priced from $12,900 at tiffany.com_

Ranging in size from 0.3 to 2.5 carats, the center stone in this ring is a Tiffany exclusive, chosen for its brilliance and beauty. The band is very unique, split and curved. How gorgeous!

With so many gorgeous, **classic Tiffany engagement rings to choose** from, the difficulty will be in choosing just one! Which of these beautiful rings do you like best, and why? Or is there another classic Tiffany engagement ring you fancy more?
