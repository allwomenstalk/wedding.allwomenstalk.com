# 5 Unique Vases for Your Wedding Centerpieces ...

Let your wedding stand out and be unique. Forget the trends, be a non-traditional couple. Bring your personality and style to your details. Look through **thrift stores**, [etsy](https://goo.gl/WUjdv4), and even relatives homes to find that special item you've been looking for. Let these 5 **unique vases** inspire that spark and create a bold look for your **wedding centerpieces**...

## 5. Mosaic Art...

![Mosaic Art...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/vase5_410x678.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/PeacebyPieceCo](https://goo.gl/rqcS41)
Price: $16.95

How amazing would this **mosaic vase** look on your reception tables with [billy balls](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.fiftyflowers.com/product/Craspedia-Billy-Balls-Yellow-Flower.htm)popping out! This would be a lovely gift for your guests to take home as well. Perfect for a DIY centerpiece.

## 4. A Garden Theme...

![A Garden Theme...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/vase3_417x543.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/CommonStonePottery](https://goo.gl/sPhOxc)
Price:$15.00

This bright and cheerful **dragonfly vase** would be a wonderful piece for a **garden theme wedding** in the spring or summer. I love this shade of pinkish purple, the seller calls it "Raspberry"...love! How beautiful will it look **lining the aisle** as you walk down with butterflies in your hair!

## 3. Rustic Charm...

![Rustic Charm...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/rustic_419x558.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/Sierraclayart](https://goo.gl/Ga5HNT)
Price:$14.00

I love the **rustic** tones and feel of this vase. I can see it at the end of your aisle, one on each side, just as it is in the photo with beautiful **wild flowers**. Wouldn't it be wonderful for a **vineyard wedding** with gorgeous mountains and hills in the background?

## 2. Bubbly & Sweet...

![Bubbly & Sweet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/vase1_417x474.jpg)

Price:$25.00

Does this** bubbly little vase** not put a smile on your face! The shape is so creative and unique. The **colors**, **stripes**, and **little flowers** would bring some funk and fun to your reception tables!
