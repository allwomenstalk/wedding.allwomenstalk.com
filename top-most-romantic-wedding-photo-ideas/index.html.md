# Top 10 Most Romantic Wedding Photo Ideas ...

Pictures are a great way to remember your wedding, so posted below are some romantic wedding photo ideas for you to try. It is essential to capture the most romantic moments of your wedding and these shots do just that! These romantic wedding photo ideas will surely showcase the love between you and your future spouse!

## 1. I Can't Live without You

![I Can't Live without You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/1_i-cant-live-without-you_600x900.jpg)

This is one of the best romantic wedding photo ideas I have ever come across because it depicts the essence of true love. The way the man is clinging to her and holding her close to him indicate the fact that he doesn't want to let her go. He's holding her securely and lovingly. Try to capture a shot like this at your wedding. It's definitely not one that should be posed; it should be genuine and candid!

## 2. A Nontraditional Pose

![A Nontraditional Pose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/2_a-nontraditional-pose_400x546.jpg)

This time, this shot isn't candid. Do something different than a typical pose. Maybe he can give you an upside down kiss! Or jump into the arms of your spouse and give him a kiss. It's adorable, tender and oh so delicately romantic!

## 3. Your Own Secret Spot

![Your Own Secret Spot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/3_your-own-secret-spot_600x900.jpg)

The fact that the couple is hidden behind the bride's veil gives a subtly romantic and intimate feeling to the piece. The fact that they are all alone, just the two of them, in a space all their own is essential!

## 4. Into the Sun

![Into the Sun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/4_into-the-sun_533x800.jpg)

Tell your photographer to shoot into the sun. Not directly, but slightly. It'll create a nice silhouette and beautiful angle to your photo (like this one!).

## 5. Aww

![Aww](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/5_aww_250x376.jpg)

I love this photo! It's just so darn cute! A photo to make your guests (and you!) say "Aww!" is an absolute must! Whether your spouse is kissing your nose, holding your hand or doing some other cute act, make sure you capture the moment! I love that the husband in this photo draped his jacket over his wife's shoulders. It's so gentleman-like!

## 6. Oooh La La!

![Oooh La La!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/6_oooh-la-la_600x904.jpg)

Photo Source: [michaelsegalphoto.com](http://michaelsegalphotography.com/)

Can you say sexy? The black and white silhouette of this photo totally adds to the steaminess. I totally want a photo like this from my wedding! It's not too over the top or risque, but intimate and slightly mysterious!

## 7. I do!

![I do!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/7_i-do_467x700.jpg)

This shot depicts excitement, happiness and enthusiasm. It's like the husband is saying "Yes, finally!" Snap a photo of you two in mid laugh or expressing some type of excitement and enthusiasm for the future -- it is the start of a new life together!

## 8. The Kiss

![The Kiss](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/8_the-kiss_600x600.jpg)

How could you not have a photo of "the kiss"? It doesn't have to be the one at the end of the ceremony. Instead, try to have your photographer catch a genuine and intimate kiss between the two of you.

## 9. Sparks Fly

![Sparks Fly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/9_sparks-fly_497x600.jpg)

Like whoa! How cool is this photo? The point of this shot is to do something nontraditional. Use props if you want to. Just make it unique! Have fun!

## 10. The Water's Reflection

![The Water's Reflection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/04/10_the-waters-reflection_600x905.jpg)

This is such a creative photo! If you're having an outdoor ceremony or something similar, try to find a body of water so the reflection of you and your spouse will show. Go ahead and be creative with the pose!

Don't these photos make you dream of your wedding day? Which one is your favorite? Or do you have another one to share?
