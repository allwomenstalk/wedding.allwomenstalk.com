# 4 Sparkly Wedding Gowns That Will Keep You Shining ...

On a [Say Yes to the Dress](https://lifestyle.howstuffworks.com/weddings)episode I was watching yesterday there was a [glamorous bride](https://web.archive.org/web/http://tlc.howstuffworks.com/tv/say-yes-to-the-dress/say-yes-to-the-dress-season-6-wedding-dress-pictures39.htm)looking for a second wedding dress that out sparkled her first gown that cost $17,000! With her first gown's bodice being custom-made with **swarovski crystals**, the [Kleinfeld](https://www.kleinfeldbridal.com/)bridal team told her she probably needed another customized gown since she didn't like the ones she tried on with them. Whether your budget is unlimited like hers or more DIY like many brides here are 4 sparkly wedding gowns that will keep your style shining all night long...

## 4. Pnina Tornai...

![Pnina Tornai...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/pninatornai_436x652.jpg)

Photo: [Kleinfeld Bridal](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.kleinfeldbridal.com/index.cfm?pid=51&changed=1&title=weddinggown)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/732/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Pnina-Tornai)

If you've seen [Say Yes to the Dress](https://lifestyle.howstuffworks.com/weddings)than you know how fabulous [Pnina Tornai](https://www.pninatornai.com/)gowns are; you also know how extravagant these dresses are. The **crystals**, the sheer bodice, the hugging silhouettes, and head to toe details in one's opinion can either be amazing or tacky! If you're a **one of a kind bride** who likes to shine and sparkle, a **Pnina Tornai gown** like this $10,000 plus dress is a style you should try on.

## 3. Demetrios Bride...

![Demetrios Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/Demetrios_432x576.jpg)

Photo: [DemetriosBride](https://www.demetrios.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/861/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Demetrios-Sensualle)

Perhaps your a bride-2-be looking for a dress with a little less bling than the Pnina. You like **sparkles** but want something more chic and low-key. I'm not a big fan of a bodice full of beading either, and I think this beautiful [Demetrios](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.demetrios.com/us/index.php?page=collection_sensualle&styleID=3176)gown is made with just the perfect amount of **beading**. I love the way the beads go across the bodice; it's a unique design that will sure catch the eye of your groom and guests!

## 2. Ines Di Santo...

![Ines Di Santo...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/fw11-disanto-039_420x630.jpg)

Photo: [Ines Di Santo](https://goo.gl/w0a05F)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/707/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Ines-Di-Santo)

I'm not certain about the price tag of this **sparkling** [Ines Di Santo](https://goo.gl/w0a05F)wedding gown, but I would bet it's pretty high being that the entire gown is covered in stunning multicolored [Swarovski crystals](https://goo.gl/lWvJ3w). I wonder if this amazing dress would have suited the bride from[Say Yes to the Dress](https://web.archive.org/web/http://tlc.howstuffworks.com/tv/say-yes-to-the-dress/say-yes-to-the-dress-season-6-wedding-dress-pictures39.htm)!

## 1. Alfred Angelo...

![Alfred Angelo...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/alfredangelo_432x576.jpg)

Photo: [Alfred Angelo](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.alfredangelo.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/805/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Alfred-Angelo)

So you love crystals, beading, and **sparkles** that will make you shine all night long, but you aren't sure if a gown like that fits into your budget. Well say hello to this gorgeous [Alfred Angelo](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.alfredangelo.com/Collections/ProductDisplay.aspx?style=2163)wedding dress full of **rhinestones**, beading, crystals, and sequins that will make your groom's jaw drop...and not because of the price tag! This show stopping gown costs under $1400, now that's a dress I can love!

Will your dress sparkle and shine on your wedding day?
