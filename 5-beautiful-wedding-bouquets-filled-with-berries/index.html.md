# 5 Beautiful Wedding Bouquets Filled with Berries ...

Sometimes you don't know where you'll find **inspiration** for your wedding design, theme, or details. The same goes for all of us in the wedding industry; whether you're just a wedding blogger like me or you create wedding shoots, invitations, or help brides and grooms coordinate their entire wedding. Today I was looking for garden theme inspiration when I found a **beautiful berry bouquet** on [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), which could work for a garden wedding but inspired me to find more berry bouquets to share. Here are 5 beautiful wedding bouquets filled with **berries**, may they inspire your wedding and your first full day of Spring...

## 5. Pink Snowberries...

![Pink Snowberries...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/snowberries_406x508.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7874180/unique-bouquets)

This soft, **pink snowberry** **bouquet** is beautiful for a bride and her bridesmaids. Add a few more stems of **berries**, or some pink flowers to make a full bouquet for the bride. I love that this bouquet is made of just pink snowberries, it's unique and beautiful for any season.

## 4. Red Berries...

![Red Berries...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/redberries_405x609.jpg)

Photo: [TheFlowerLab](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theflowerlab.com/)via [WeddingAces.com](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas)

Roses, ranunculus, mini callas, tulips, and **hypericum berries **make up this red bouquet that would be lovely for a winter or Christmas time wedding. I love the different tones and shades of red, the hypericum fit in so nicely.

## 3. Peonies & Unripe Blackberries...

![Peonies & Unripe Blackberries...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/blackberry-bouquet_411x274.jpg)

Photo: [ViburnumDesigns](http://www.viburnumdesigns.com/)via [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/02/13/valentines-day-at-ashford-estate-kay-english-photography/)

The **green blackberries **and succulents bring a little bit of a rustic feel to this stunning peony bouquet. Brilliant textures of dusty miller, peacock feathers, sprigs of heather, gold aspidistra leaves, and uluhe fern shoots complete this wonderful bouquet from this [Kay English](https://tedxannecy.fr/)photo shoot.

## 2. Purple Callas & Green Berries...

![Purple Callas & Green Berries...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/purpleandgreen_409x273.jpg)

Photo: [JMFlora](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.jmflora.com/404./)via [TheBridesCafe.com](http://www.thebridescafe.com/?postID=496&bouquet-style-with-jm-flora-design)

Love, love, love the contrast of colors in this gorgeous purple miniature calla lily, dahlia, and **green** **hypericum berry** bouquet. After planning our blue and yellow wedding I saw so many beautiful green and purple palettes, it would have definitely been my second choice for our wedding colors!

## 1. Flowers, Grapes & Pepper Berries...

![Flowers, Grapes & Pepper Berries...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/berriesandgrapes_409x612.jpg)

Photo: [EventsbyShowStoppers](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.eventsbyshowstoppers.com/) via [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/03/18/sedona-wedding-photo-shoot-by-sedona-wedding-studio/)

This is the amazing **berry bouquet** that inspired my berry filled post! The colors, textures, non-traditional look and details are just so breathtaking. Immature green grapes, **pepper berries**, blown garden roses, echinacea, and pale blue scabiosa all come together in this** dream wedding bouquet**.

**Will your beautiful boquet be filled with berries?**
