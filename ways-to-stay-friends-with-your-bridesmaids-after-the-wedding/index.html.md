# 7 Ways to Stay Friends with Your Bridesmaids after the Wedding ...

Do you want to make sure that you know some ways to stay friends with your bridesmaids ? It isn’t a secret that sometimes a wedding will cause a bride and her bridesmaids to lose their friendship. Don’t let this happen to you. Make sure you follow these ways to stay friends with your bridesmaids so that there is no issue in your friendships.

## 1. Appreciate Them

The first key in the ways to stay friends with your bridesmaids is to appreciate them. Remember that they are filling this position for you. Yes, it is an honor that you chose them to be in your wedding but it is also an honor that they accepted. Don’t forget to appreciate them for that. This goes a long way in making sure there are no issues in your friendships.

## 2. Don’t Take Advantage of Them

Don’t take advantage of your bridesmaids. Yes, there are certain things they can help you with throughout your wedding. But acting entitled is not going to win you any friends and can lose you some of the ones you cherish the most, your bridesmaids. If you do ask for assistance, make sure that you are asking rather than ordering. And accept their answer graciously.

## 3. Speak Kindly

There can be a lot of stress that comes with planning a wedding. It is only natural that it can wear on you. But it is important to not allow that stress to cause you to be unkind to others. Make sure you make an effort to speak kindly to others, including your bridesmaids. You will always be glad you did.

## 4. Don’t Be a Bridezilla

I don’t know about you but I have never enjoyed seeing a bridezilla in action. A bridezilla is a bride that gets so wrapped up in her wedding she doesn’t care about anyone or anything but herself and her wedding day. This can include being unkind to her fiancé as well as all of her bridal attendants and others. It is not a good choice to make. Your wedding day is important but not more so than those you care about.

## 5. Be Their Friend Throughout the Process

One of the best ways to make sure that you are still friends with your bridesmaids after the wedding is to be their friend as you are planning and preparing for your wedding. Be there for them when they have a bad day. Ask them how they are doing in difficult situations they are facing. They will love you for still continuing to care about what is going on in their life when you have so much going on. This will make you even closer.

## 6. Show Your Gratitude

Don’t forget to show your gratitude to your bridesmaids. This is typically done through a gift of appreciation on the day of or after the wedding. You may also want to do something special, like treat all of your bridesmaids to a manicure before your wedding or some other little pleasure. These little gestures mean a lot to your bridesmaids. They show your appreciation for them.

## 7. Find Time to Hang out

Yes, you are crazy busy doing wedding stuff. But it is still important to find time to hang out with your bridesmaids if that has been something you have always done. It keeps your friendship intact. It also provides a distraction from stressful wedding stuff for you. It is a great way to find calm in the midst of chaos.

These are 7 ways to make sure that you stay friends with your bridesmaids after the wedding. What are your thoughts? How do you plan to do this?
