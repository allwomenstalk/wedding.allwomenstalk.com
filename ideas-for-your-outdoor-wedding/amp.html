7 S'mores Bar

Via Indian Wedding Trend Alert: S'mores ...

***

S'mores bars are a fun and unique way to add an interactive element to any outdoor wedding. They provide a great way to keep guests entertained while also giving them the opportunity to customize their own snacks. Guests can mix and match different ingredients to create their own unique s'mores.

A s'mores bar typically includes graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate bars. Guests can also add other ingredients such as nuts, dried fruit, or peanut butter to their s'mores. Once the ingredients are gathered, guests can roast the marshmallows over the fire and assemble their s'mores. It's a great way to encourage guests to mingle and socialize.

S'mores bars are a great way to add a unique and fun touch to any outdoor wedding. They are easy to set up and require minimal effort. All you need is a fire pit or a fire bowl, some graham crackers, marshmallows, and chocolate bars. You can also provide additional ingredients such as nuts, dried fruit, or peanut butter for guests to customize their s'mores.

8 Pomanders of Purple Roses, Scattered Petals and Purple Satin Ribbons

Via Purple Passion

***

Pomanders of purple roses, scattered petals and purple satin ribbons are a beautiful and romantic decoration for any outdoor wedding. They are a great way to add a touch of color and elegance to any ceremony or reception. Pomanders are typically made from fresh flowers, but they can also be made from silk flowers, paper, or even fabric. The petals and ribbons are often chosen to match the color of the roses, adding to the romantic feel of the decorations.

Pomanders can be hung from trees, placed on tables, or even used as centerpieces. They can also be used as part of the wedding procession, with the bride and groom each holding a pomander. The petals and ribbons can be scattered around the ceremony or reception, creating a romantic and whimsical atmosphere.

The purple roses in the pomanders are especially popular for outdoor weddings, as they symbolize love, admiration, and loyalty. The petals and ribbons also add a touch of femininity and grace to any wedding. The purple color is also associated with royalty, making it a great choice for any wedding.

36 Makes a Great Photo Op

Via Other Inspirations

***

Outdoor weddings are a great way to create a special atmosphere for your special day. They provide an opportunity to take advantage of the beauty of nature and create an unforgettable experience. One of the best ways to capture the memories of your outdoor wedding is with photos.

Having a great photo op is one of the best ways to ensure that your wedding photos are unique and special. You can use natural elements such as trees, flowers, and greenery to create a stunning backdrop for your photos. You can also use props such as vintage furniture, signs, and other decorations to create a unique look.

When it comes to creating the perfect photo op, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure that the area is well-lit. Natural light is best, but if you are planning an evening event, you may need to supplement with artificial lighting. Second, make sure that the area is large enough for your guests to gather and take photos. Third, choose a spot that is visually appealing. Consider the colors, textures, and other elements of the landscape.
