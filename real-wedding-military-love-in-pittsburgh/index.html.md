# Real Wedding: Military Love in Pittsburgh ...

**Matthew** and **Jena** were training for the **army,** ready to fight for their country. Little did they know that they would find **love **while standing tall and proud. Now both **Captains**, they are ready to say 'I Do' and celebrate amongst family, friends, and **Steelers fans** at their reception in **Pittsburgh**!

## 11. The Glamourous Bride...

![The Glamourous Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0198_411x308.jpg)

One of my **favorite wedding shots** is the **hanging bridal gown**. **Photographers** get so **creative** and always find the perfect lighting for the bride's dress to glow in the sunlight. While the [photographer](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.blog.rimacampbellphotography.com/)hung the stunning [Casablanca gown](https://www.casablancabridal.com/), the **bride** and her bridesmaids continued to get ready at the townhouse they rented right next to the [Priory Hotel](https://www.thepriory.com/)where they held their beautiful reception.

## 10. The Stunning Flowers...

![The Stunning Flowers...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0401_408x305.jpg)

I love the colors of the **bride's** and **bridesmaid's bouquets** and how lovely they match their **dresses**. This is such a beautiful way to capture their **flowers** and placing them side by side creates a great photo for their **wedding album**.

## 9. The Adorable Flower Girl...

![The Adorable Flower Girl...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0326_408x272.jpg)

For** winter weddings** you never know what the weather will be like, especially when your ceremony is in **Ohio** and your reception is in **Pittsburgh**. Jena's little **flower girl** was **adorable** and **warm** in her [David's Bridal](https://goo.gl/0Iue13)dress and her little white shaw. I love this photo and how only the flower girl is looking at the camera!

## 8. The Military Ceremony...

![The Military Ceremony...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0571_408x272.jpg)

Their beautiful **military ceremony** was held at the **Colonial Baptist Church** in [Wintersville, Ohio](http://www.wintersville.net/). Matthew's **groomsmen** were made up of members of his **troop** who saluted him and his bride in **traditional military fashion**.

## 7. The Awesome Bridal Party...

![The Awesome Bridal Party...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0733_408x272.jpg)

**A little note from the beautiful bride**:

"I had a blast at our wedding, but it went by so fast! At one point
one of my friends said, 'Jena would be having a really great
time if she was here. Look at her over there in that love bubble.'
It is such a memorable day with all your family and friends in one
place. Just sit back and enjoy!"

## 6. Their First Dance...

![Their First Dance...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0959_410x307.jpg)

"A wedding is just one day, but a marriage is forever. We wanted to
throw a great party for our family and friends, but we made sure from
the beginning that we would never forget to place emphasis on building
a solid marriage foundation first. And that is the most important
thing."

## 5. Their Gorgeous Cake & Cake Topper...

![Their Gorgeous Cake & Cake Topper...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0901_408x305.jpg)

Jena and Matthew were so happy to find a venue that helped ease their worries of finding everything on their own. Their **beautiful cake** was made by[Priory Fine Pastries](https://www.prantlsbakery.com/) who are also the owners of their **reception hall** at [Priory hotel](https://www.thepriory.com/). How much do you love their adorable **personalized cake topper**, it was made by the groom's sister!

## 4. Steelers Fans Rock...

![Steelers Fans Rock...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0075_408x305.jpg)

Matthew and Jena's [guests](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-celebrate-a-football-themed-wedding/)were big time [Steelers](https://www.steelers.com/)fans **waving around** their [terrible towels](https://goo.gl/OUKrDz)all night long! They even watched the [AFC playoff game](https://www.espn.com/nfl/recap?gameId=310115023)on a screen during the **reception** inside the [Grand Hall](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.thepriory.com/grand.asp)at the [Priory Hotel](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.thepriory.com/prioryNews.asp)located blocks away from [Heinz field](https://heinzfield.com/stadium/heinz-field-tours/).

## 3. The Beautiful Reception...

![The Beautiful Reception...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_1323_408x272.jpg)

"Some advice for brides: If you can, find a venue that will take care of
the details. Our venue took care of center pieces, the cake, favors,
lighting, chair covers, etc. It took a lot of stress off of our
shoulders to not worry about those details. Also, have the cake plated and
served as a course. That way everyone gets a piece."

## 2. The Groom's Grandfather...

![The Groom's Grandfather...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0067_408x272.jpg)

I have a sweet spot in my heart for wedding photos that include **grandparents**. Both sets of mine are no longer with us and it touches my heart when I see **grandfathers** and **grandmothers** dancing and having a wonderful time. The grandfather of the groom is pictured here dancing with the groom's sister [Mallorie](https://web.archive.org/web/http://theknottybride.com/17853), who recently had her own** Military wedding**!

## 1. A Shared Moment...

![A Shared Moment...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/HUTCHISON_0817_408x305.jpg)

Always remember to steal away a **shared moment together**. Take a moment to look at all the excitement and the loved ones who came together on this day just for you. I love this photo of Jena and Matthew and wonder what she was whispering in his ear! You can see and feel the **love** they share and the wonderful day they experienced. **Congratulations Jena & Matthew**!
