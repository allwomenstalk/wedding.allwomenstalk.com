# 6 Adorable Flower Girls and Their Beautiful Dresses

**Flower girls** are just the cutest little kids; they just steal my heart as they twirl around in their pretty dresses! Sometimes I think they steal the show from the bride and groom, everyone can't help to just smile when they see these little girls walking down the aisle. Whether they are wearing tutus like in [point 4](#4) or walking with friends like in [point 6](#6), flower girls bring a special joy to the occasion. **Put a smile on your face when you see these 6 adorable flower girls in their pretty dresses!**

Here are a few quick tips for choosing the perfect flower girl attire:

- Consider the season and the weather.

- Coordinate with the bridesmaids' dresses.

- Ensure the dress is comfortable for a child to wear.

## 2. Just Go with the Flow...

![Just Go with the Flow...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/flowergirlbunny_406x349.jpg)

Photo: [English-Wedding.com](https://english-wedding.com/)

Sometimes we don't always get what we want or get to do what we want and that makes for a [cranky day](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-ways-to-cheer-up-your-kids/)! Little kids need naps and play time. Give your **flower girl** a break during the day before she has to stand still to take pictures. Bring along her favorite toys, surprise her with a new doll, and **just go with the flow** if she gets upset. But remember, whatever happens it makes for **great photos** and memories! **Did your flower girl get upset?**
