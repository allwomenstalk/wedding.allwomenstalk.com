# 7 Fabulous Things You Can do Instead of a Bachelorette Party ...

There are lots of fun things you can do instead of a bachelorette party. While a bachelorette party can be a lot of fun, it simply isn’t the style of a lot of brides and that is okay. Celebrating your upcoming wedding should be something that you enjoy and help you to make wonderful memories. Try one of these 7 fabulous things you can do instead of a bachelorette party and enjoy yourself!

## 1. Spend the Day at the Spa

A day at the spa is a wonderful alternative to a bachelorette party. What is not to love about this choice? You get a day to be pampered and enjoy beauty treatments. The spa always leaves you feeling rested and refreshed. That is something every bride loves and needs to feel with an upcoming wedding. You can choose to go alone as a treat to yourself or go with your whole bridal party to share the joy.

## 2. Go Shopping Together

I truly believe shopping is therapy for the heart. It can just melt away stress and can be tons of fun at the same time. A day of shopping together can be a great thing to do instead of a bachelorette party. You can find tons of great deals while spending time with your best friends. It is a great way to connect before all of the official festivities get underway.

## 3. Do Lunch

Going to lunch with your bridal party is a great idea. You can enjoy a time of visiting together and having fun. You can also talk about what is really important, the wedding. It can be a time to brainstorm and get everything in line or just enjoy the excitement of this time period. It is a great way to spend time together.

## 4. A Girls Weekend Getaway

I don’t think it gets any better than a girls weekend getaway. There are just some things that only your girlfriends are going to enjoy with you. Why not spend a whole weekend doing them? Shop to your heart’s content. Indulge in beauty treatments galore. Talk and share the things that you know only they can understand.

## 5. Go for Manis and Pedis

Going for manis and pedis can accomplish two things. One, it gives you some great girl time together to have fun and bond. Two, it makes sure that everyone has lovely nails and toenails for the big day. This is a great gift to your wedding party if you don’t have another one in mind. Or you can let them treat you as their gift to you. Both ways are good.

## 6. Take Them Lingerie Shopping with You

You know, sometimes you want a little help when you go lingerie shopping. It can be helpful to have someone along to give you their opinion and tell you what they think would be a good choice for you. They can also help to bolster your confidence to be a bit more daring in the lingerie department if you need that. If nothing else, it can be a lot of fun and a lot of laughs. It is a fun activity to do in place of a bachelorette party.

## 7. Have a Sleepover

Do you think it sounds juvenile? Maybe to some, but it can really be fun. What is better than staying up half the night with your best friends laughing and eating tons of junk? It is a great opportunity for those heart-to-hearts you never seem to have time for anymore! Just be sure to do this activity at least a few days before the wedding so you aren’t tired on your big day.

These are 7 things you can do instead of having a bachelorette party. Did you pass on having a bachelorette party? What did you do instead?
