# 4 Inspiring Ways to Plan a Purple Wedding ...

I've seen this gorgeous wedding color and now I want more of it! **Purple** has become my favorite **wedding color** for every season. There are so many creative ways to incorporate purple into your **wedding details**. Find your love for this magical color with these 4 **inspiring** ways to **plan a purple wedding**...

## 4. Orange Bliss & Juicy Plum...

**Orange** and **Purple** have become so hot and **popular** lately and I couldn't love it more! The[wedding shoot](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault/gallery/pink-polka-wedding-design+2743)with the **invitation**, bridesmaid dresses, and **table** **setting** from [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/) is so **rich** and brilliant. Both **colors** together are fabulous for a [fall wedding.](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ideas-for-a-fall-wedding/)

## 3. Grey Tones and Purple Magic...

**Grey** is a wonderful** accent color** for a** purple wedding**. How adorable is the little [flower girl](https://goo.gl/wYv1N2)in her grey dress! I love how grey makes purple **pop** even **brighter**. This is a wonderful **palette** for all seasons. You can dress your girls in [purple dresses](https://goo.gl/N4YhgS)and your guys in [grey suits](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-hot-looks-for-every-groom-to-rock-in-style/)like this handsome groom! **Are you planning a purple wedding?**

## 2. Yellow Flowers & Purple Rain...

Spring and [5 Beach Wedding Ideas to Inspire Your Summer Wedding ... @Diana](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-beach-weddings-to-inspire-your-summer-wedding/) are on their way...hopefully! This means the **wedding plans** are already coming along while the **yellow flowers** are blooming in your garden. I can imagine [yellow calla lilies](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=flower-arrangement-advisor.com) **glowing** against a beautiful **purple table runner** or yellow cookies inside **purple** [favor boxes](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-inspiring-ideas-from-martha-stewart-weddings/). Will your **summer wedding** be filled with purple and yellow decor?

## 1. A Purple Shimmer & a Turquoise Glow...

Maybe you can't decide whether you would like a **purple** or a **turquoise** **wedding** and see that both colors are **strong**, **bold**, and stand wonderfully on their own. Don't be afraid to **combine** bold **colors** together or any[color palette](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-hot-color-palette-trends-for-your-2011-wedding/)together. Have fun with[turquoise heels](https://goo.gl/3IH39O)that glow under your **gown** while carrying a **remarkable** **purple bouquet** down the aisle.

**What accent color will you pair with your purple decor? **
