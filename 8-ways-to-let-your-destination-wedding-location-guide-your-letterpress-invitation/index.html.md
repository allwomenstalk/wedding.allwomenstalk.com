# 8 Ways to Incorporate Your Wedding Location into the Invitation ...

I've had the wonderful opportunity of working with [Ajalon Printing & Design](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/index.html)before and I am happy to share with you more of their beautiful work from **Guest Blogger** [Katie Nealon](https://web.archive.org/web/http://invitationsbyajalon.com/blog/katie-nealon-bio/)who blogs for **Ajalon. **

"Destination weddings are the perfect opportunity to plan a wedding invitation unlike anything you’ve ever seen before. Letting your location guide your invitation design will do wonders to get guests excited about your upcoming day--or week! Pick a motif related to your destination location, and work it into your wedding stationery..." Here are eight different destination locations with amazing motifs, just to get you started.

## 1. Cape Cod

![Cape Cod](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/capecod-01_475x400.jpg)

If you are planning a **seaside ceremony**, I can think of nothing better than a light house motif to guide your guests to your celebration. Nautical embellishments in the blind impression really make our [Cape Cod destination wedding invitation](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/capecod.html)a sight to see.

## 2. Key West

![Key West](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/keywest-02_475x400.jpg)

**Tropical wedding** ceremonies in places like Key West need a refreshing invitation design to accompany them! [Letterpress printed seashell and starfish](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/keywest.html)motifs will make it hard for guests to turn down your destination wedding invitation.

## 3. Sonoma County Vineyard

![Sonoma County Vineyard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/caballero-01_475x400.jpg)

Are you planning a rustic Wine Country wedding in 2011? [Whimsical grape vines](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/caballero.html)are perfect for radiating your growing love. What a clever destination, and a lovely invitation!

## 4. England

![England](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/cathryn-02_475x400.jpg)

If you’re traveling to **England** to have your own **Royal Wedding**, consider a more [formal design](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/cathryn.html). Monograms are fit for royalty, and can be customized to fit your name and taste.

## 5. Mexico

![Mexico](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/stcroix-03_475x400.jpg)

Tropical palms work wonders to get guests excited about destination wedding celebrations in places like Mexico. Not to mention, the tall trees look especially stunning on [tea length paper](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/stcroix.html)! Aren't these gorgeous?

## 6. South Beach

![South Beach](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/southbeach-04_475x400.jpg)

Custom fonts are a great way to have fun with your letterpress destination [wedding invitation design](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/southbeach.html). Destination locations with art deco architecture, like **South Beach**, look great with fun fonts and set the tone for your modern affair.

## 7. Japan

![Japan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/sakura-02_475x400.jpg)

If you are heading to Japan for your spring destination wedding, cherry blossoms are an excellent motif to utilize within your [invitation design](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/sakura.html). The beautiful nature of this native flora will attract guests from every continent. This would also work beautifully for a spring wedding in Washington D.C.

## 7. India

![India](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/avani-01_475x400.jpg)

Indian wedding celebrations are a big hit in 2011, and the [invitations](https://invitationsbyajalon.com/gallery/avani.html)that come with them are truly stunning. Think about using shiny gold foil within your Indian wedding card! It will really get guests excited about your upcoming destination celebration.

**Where are you heading for your 2011 destination wedding, and what motif will you use?**

Thank you Katie for giving us a special insight on these beautiful designs that can be inspired by a bride and groom's destination wedding.
