# 4 Ways to Be Inspired by the Royal Wedding ...

Almost every day we hear something new about the **Royal Wedding** of **Prince William** and **Kate Middleton**. This morning on [ABCNews](https://abcnews.go.com/International/Royal_Wedding/royal-wedding-kate-middletons-tiara/story?id=13035793&page=1)they were wondering which tiara the Queen would give Kate to wear for the big day. And now they have launched their [official wedding website](https://www.officialroyalwedding2011.org/)! Are you excited by the engagement, if so here are 4 ways to be **inspired** by **Royal Wedding** for your big day...

## 4. A Royal Invitation...

![A Royal Invitation...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/crownsuite2_414x289.jpg)

**Prince William** and **Kate **have already sent out their invitations to all their guests, including [Elton John](https://web.archive.org/web/http://web.eltonjohn.com/index.jsp)and [David Beckham](https://www.davidbeckham.com/). Maybe your guest list isn't as famous as theirs, but you can create a **royal inspired invitation** that your guest will love. Check out the paper goods and much more over at [Wedding Chicks](https://www.weddingchicks.com/modul/print-1970-royal-crown-free-printable-wedding-invitation-suite-l-15-l-17.html), where they want everyone to feel like a **princess**!

![Royal China...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/royal_wedding_aynsley_china_group_414x308.jpg)

Photos: [Dailymail.co.uk](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-1346099/Royal-wedding-Official-tea-towels-WILL-allowed-Palace-U-turn.html)& [TheUKgiftcompany.co.uk](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theukgiftcompany.co.uk/)

For some reason, even though you can purchase the [official royal china](https://www.royalcollectionshop.co.uk//), others have created **plates**, **cups**, and [bowls](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theukgiftcompany.co.uk/royal_wedding_commemoratives/aynsley_royal_engagement/10535_0c.html)with [Prince William](http://www.royal.gov.uk/ThecurrentRoyalFamily/PrinceWilliam/PrinceWilliam.aspx)and Kate's **faces** on them! Would this inspire you to make your own wedding china with your face or monogram on it?

## 2. The Royal Tiara....

![The Royal Tiara....](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/0225-the-spencer-tiara-kate-middleton-royal-wedding_we_412x289.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour](https://www.glamour.com/story/royal-wedding-which-of-these-t)& [PrincessBrideTiaras](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.princessbridetiaras.com/Silver_No_Pearls_2/N191A.php)

[Queen Elizabeth](http://www.royal.gov.uk/HMTheQueen/HMTheQueen.aspx)has given many Royal Princesses their **tiaras** as a wedding gift and now we are all wondering which tiara **Kate** will receive. There is even a tiara that [Princess Diana](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.biography.com/articles/Princess-Diana-9273782)used to wear that could be given to the new princess. It may not be worth as much as Kate's tiara, but gems like this one from [Princess Bride Tiaras](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.princessbridetiaras.com/)can give you that** royal feeling** as you walk down the aisle to marry your prince!

![A Ring for a Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/Prince-William-and-Miss-Catherine-Middleton-Royal-Engagement-Photo-2_400x600.jpg)

You would not believe how many **replicas** of Kate Middleton's **engagement ring** are available.
