# 6 Sweet Ideas for a Candy Themed Bridal Shower ...

There are so many **themes **you can choose from to shower your bride with, but knowing more about her will always help you decide which theme you should go with. The** bridal shower **doesn't have to be the same theme of the wedding, but it should have a few things that tie them both together. Always remember to start with what the bride and groom might like and what fits their personalities, like a **candy theme **for the[candy shop owner](https://goo.gl/BUqqQ2)...

## 6. Candy Ring Pop...

![Candy Ring Pop...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/06/ringpop_414x517.jpg)

If you've ever been to NYC you may have walked by or even stopped in [Dylan's Candy Bar](https://goo.gl/9G3xd2), she carries all sorts of goodies from chocolate and toffy to hard candy and gummies. So when **Darcy Miller **of Martha Stewart's["Darcy's Idea of the Day"](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.marthastewartweddings.com/idea-of-the-day) and[Darcy Miller Designs](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.darcymillerdesigns.com/shop.html) started planning [Dylan Lauren's](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.self.com/magazine/blogs/lucysblog/2011/05/dylan-laurens-secret-wedding-s.html)bridal shower, she knew candy of all sorts would be the perfect theme. These fun **candy ring pops **were a great **favor **to have for the guests as they do double duty for the **candy theme **and for any bride-to-be theme!

## 5. Candy Guestbook...

![Candy Guestbook...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/06/notecards_414x517.jpg)

How sweet are these **candy-shaped notecards **for the shower **guestbook**! Guests of Dylan's bridal shower were asked to write words of love on these delightful notecards and drop them into an oversized hurricane vase. If you're thinking of a candy-theme shower or would love to display these on your candy buffet go to [Martha Stewart Weddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/7887385/good-things-guest-books)for the template.

## 4. Bride & Shower Host...

![Bride & Shower Host...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/06/candyshower_414x517.jpg)

Aren't these ladies lovely! **Darcy Miller **was the perfect person to help celebrate and design Dylan's candy bridal shower. Behind them is a giant **candy heart **print made from **gum **and jars of assorted candies from[Dylan's Candy Bar](https://goo.gl/7n6OwY),[Vintage Confections](https://goo.gl/HZLmiT), and[Kidz Confections](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.kidzconfections.com/home.html). Dylan's **wedding dress **is being made by none other than her dad, [Ralph Lauren](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.bridefinds.com/2011/ralph-lauren-reveals-that-he-designed-his-daughters-wedding-dress-on-oprah/)!

## 3. Candy Bar Invitation...

![Candy Bar Invitation...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/06/invite_414x517.jpg)

Is this not the coolest **bridal shower invitation **ever! The cute bride and groom cartoon characters on the cover of the **candy bar **were designed by host [Darcy](https://www.darcymillerdesigns.com/)and the bar itself is from [Chocolate Editions](http://chocolate-editions.com/). See those little lollipop stamps, they're from [Zazzle](https://goo.gl/XoPfW5)! I love everything about this invitation!

## 2. Bubble Gum Cocktail...

![Bubble Gum Cocktail...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/06/martini_414x517.jpg)

What's a bridal shower without a **cocktail **for the ladies and what's better than a** bubble-gum martini **for the candy themed shower! To make this pink drink for your shower or wedding mix together vanilla vodka and banana and melon liqueurs.

## 1. Adorable Cupcakes...

![Adorable Cupcakes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/06/cupcake1_414x517.jpg)

To help pull together the theme Darcy used the same sketch of the charming bride and groom throughout the shower. I love these **mini cupcakes **from [Baked by Melissa](https://www.bakedbymelissa.com/)and the little toppers from [American Chocolate Designs](https://americanchocolatedesigns.com/). Maybe these little guys will show up at Dylan's wedding too!
