# 5 Wedding Dresses to Inspire Your Inner Princess ...

Up until the very day of **Kate Middleton** and Prince William's[royal wedding](https://www.officialroyalwedding2011.org/)there will be many speculations of who her **wedding dress** designer will be. Lately it has been [rumored](https://web.archive.org/web/https://premiuminquiry.com/sk-domsale.php?dom=popeater.com&eds=dWx0cmFwcmVtZG9tYWluQHlhaG9vLmNvbQ%3D%3D&frsmnv=500&_isk_=8266)to be [Alexander McQueen](https://goo.gl/W9GeyG)creative director, Sarah Burton, but all rumors have been denied. Maybe you're not of high status like Miss Middleton, but who says your wedding dress can't inspire that **inner princess** waiting to walk down the aisle in a pretty gown and princess crown like Kate's!

## 5. A Ruffled Princess...

![A Ruffled Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/weddingdress1_422x562.jpg)

Photo: [Mori Lee](https://www.morilee.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/809/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Blu-by-Mori-Lee)

This p**rincess bride** is ready to have fun and twirl on the dance floor in her beautiful organza, **ruffled** **ballgown** by [Blu from Mori Lee](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridals/blu). She'll walk down the aisle at the[Roosevelt Hotel](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.rooseveltweddings.com/)in NYC and feel like the Manhattan princess she always knew she was!

## 4. Sophisticated Princess...

![Sophisticated Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/dress4_426x568.jpg)

Photo: [Sassi Holford](https://sassiholford.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/970/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Sassi-Holford)

A princess bride never kisses and tells! I love the **sophisticated** and **modest** look of this elegant [Sassi Holford](https://sassiholford.com/)gown. You don't have to wear a ballgown on your wedding day to feel like a princess. This **A-line** silk and satin detail organza dress will keep your prince smiling with happiness.

## 3. Modern Princess...

![Modern Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/dress2_422x562.jpg)

Photo: [Mori Lee](https://www.morilee.com/)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/809/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/Blu-by-Mori-Lee)

I just love the full skirt on this** Mori Lee** **ballgown**, another gorgeous dress from their [Blu collection](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.morilee.com/bridals/blu/4808). Could you imagine yourself walking down the aisle in a **princess ballgown** like this one?

## 2. Fashionista Princess...

![Fashionista Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/dress31_420x560.jpg)

Photo: [James Clifford](https://www.enchantingbymoncheri.com/collections/bridal)via [FashionableBride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/762/fashiontrends/Wedding-Dresses/James-Clifford-Collection)

As a princess bride she deserves only the best because she loves fashion and big names like [Christian Louboutin](https://us.christianlouboutin.com/), [Prada](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.prada.com/), and [Armani](https://goo.gl/p4qykX). ## 1. A Fairytale Princess...

![A Fairytale Princess...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/Ecosse0012_421x567.jpg)

Photo: [Cymbeline](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.cymbelineusa.com/index.htm)via [EternityBridalWear](https://web.archive.org/web/http://eternitybridalwear.co.uk/wedding-dresses/?app=ecom&ns=catshow&ref=cymbeline)
