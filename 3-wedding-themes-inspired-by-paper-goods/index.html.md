# 3 Wedding Themes Inspired by Paper Goods ...

There are many ways **engaged couples** find **inspiration** to start planning their dream wedding. Many brides and grooms first think of **wedding colors** for their bridal party attire and go from there for the rest of the **decor** and paper goods. But don't be afraid to find your wedding colors in whatever catches your eye, perhaps these **3 wedding themes** inspired by **paper goods** will light the creative fire inside you...

## 3. Rustic Chic....

Photos: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)
Invitation: [Griffin-Vites.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.griffin-vites.com/)

**Rustic Chic** is a beautiful **wedding** **trend** to follow if you find an invitation that **inspires** you like this **sophisticated rooster invitation** from [Griffin-Vites](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.griffin-vites.com/wedding.html). Believe it or not the **barn invitation** was created for an [inspiration to reality shoot](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2009/12/15/from-inspiration-to-reality-the-design-iv-2/) for [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)! There are many different elements you can pull together for a **rustic chic** **theme**; [mason jar centerpieces](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-rustic-wedding-centerpiece-ideas/), **wild flower bouquets**, wooden chairs, and so much more. **Where did you find your [wedding theme](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/country-rustic-wedding-theme-ideas/)?**

## 2. Proud as a Peacock...

Photos: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)
Invitation: [AMileStonePaperCo.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.amilestonepaperco.com/)

**Peacock feathers** can easily turn into your** gorgeous wedding theme** when you find **colorful paper goods** like this **blue**, **green**, and **yellow** invitation from[A Milestone Paper Company](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.amilestonepaperco.com/wedding-invitations.html). But perhaps you are just delighted with the stunning colors and would like to include just a few peacock feathers in your centerpieces. Don't let a theme scare you, run with your **inspiration** in your own way. It can be a **theme** wedding or it can just be your wedding with [peacock details](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-boutonnieres-for-your-groom-to-proudly-wear/)!

## 1. Our Summer Garden...

Photos: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)
Invitation: [OneFourThreeEvents.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://onefourthreeevents.com/%7BHello%7D.html)

How could you not find inspiration in a fun, **bright yellow wedding invitation**! I love the **sweet couple** on the lovely **wedding invitation** from [OneFourThreeEvents](https://web.archive.org/web/http://onefourthreeevents.com/%7Bmeet_the_girls%7D.html). The **talented bride **works for the company and together with her groom they created their one of a kind **garden theme invitation** and wedding. Like them, you can grow [succulents](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.simplysucculents.com/) in your [garden](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ways-to-prep-your-garden-for-winter/), keep things simple, and let a **summer garden theme** flow through your big day!
