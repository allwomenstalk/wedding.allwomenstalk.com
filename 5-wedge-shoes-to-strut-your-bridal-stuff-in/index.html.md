# 5 Wedge Shoes to Strut Your Bridal Stuff in!

I was trying to decide what type of **bridal shoe** to feature today and [Zappos.com's](https://goo.gl/7DSHr9)front page gave me all the inspiration I needed! It's all about the **wedge** ladies. Whether they are **cork**, **raffia-wrapped**, **leather**, or **satin**, you'll be sure to find the wedge that fits your style for your big day. Would any of these 5 sexy shoes be the one you **walk down the aisle** in?

## 5. A Wedge with a Little Sparkle...

![A Wedge with a Little Sparkle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/wedge5_404x303.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/38bDhR)
Price: $100.00

So how high do you wear your **wedge heels**? I love the look of this **glamorous bridal wedge **from [Pour La Victoire](http://redirect.viglink.com/?key=a7330d1bba7bc7d258ecc1463a1b25b1&u=http://www.pourlavictoire.com/index.html); it's soft and satin with a touch of glitz and **sparkle**. This is a bride who wants to shine without going over the top.

## 4. Retro Style...

![Retro Style...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/wedge4_404x303.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/qsFojD)
Price: $54.95

Picture the **offbeat**, **retro bride** who would wear these lovely[Rocket Dog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://store.rocketdog.com/page/home)**floral wedge** shoes! Can't you just see her stunning birdcage veil, red lip, the wavy curl in her hair, her short wedding dress, and these fun **red bow wedge** sling-backs. **Retro** or not, these would be a great Spring or Summer shoe for any woman.

## 3. Rustic Ruffle...

![Rustic Ruffle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/wedge2_404x303.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/MLfggd)
Price: $49.95

It's a wedge with a **ruffle**! I love that this **wedge** is not your normal, adorable, bridal ruffle shoe. To me this [Madden Girl](https://goo.gl/Ri09K6)canvas wedge has a **rustic feel** to it and would be a great shoe for a country or **barn reception bride**.

## 2. Mellow Yellow...

![Mellow Yellow...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/wedge3_404x303.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/pKqei6)
Price: $85.00

Who doesn't need a [Nine West](https://goo.gl/QErhwf)**yellow wedge** shoe to brighten up their day! What's great about the tone of this yellow is how **mellow** and calm it is without being too bright, but it is still very cheerful. It's a cute wedge with a pop of floral detail on the toe.

## 1. Sleek & Classy...

![Sleek & Classy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/wedge_404x303.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/EYQkO8)
Price: $89.00

**Neutral**, **classic**, and perfect for any event, especially a **wedding**! I love the **stacked wedge** and the sleek look of this [Franco Sarto](https://goo.gl/KWnL4U)shoe. You can wear it right from your engagement party, to your bridal shower, and all the way down the aisle! This is one wedge that will never go out of style.
