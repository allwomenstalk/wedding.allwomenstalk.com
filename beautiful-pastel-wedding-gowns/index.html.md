# 7 Beautiful Pastel Wedding Gowns ...

Pastel nail polish, pastel shoes…why not pastel wedding gowns? From mint to blush, pastel shades are incredibly popular this spring and have been a hot trend in bridal fashion throughout the past couple of years. Want to break away from the ordinary and walk down the aisle in a dress that’s just as unique as it is eye-catching? Here are seven beautiful pastel wedding gowns I just can’t help but love.

## 1. Mint

![Mint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/1_mint_320x500.jpg)

As one of the year’s hottest wedding colors, mint is a popular shade for everything from bridesmaid dresses and invitations to cakes and décor. Why not carry the color scheme even further by stepping out in a romantic mint gown like this one by Romona Keveza? I don’t know about you, but I think it’s by far one of the most gorgeous pastel wedding gowns I’ve ever seen.

## 2. Blush

![Blush](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/2_blush_540x750.jpg)

Blush gowns are another ongoing trend in bridal fashion, and this Sleeping Beauty dress by Alfred Angelo is certainly one of my all-time favorites. From the beautiful embellishments at the waist to the soft flow of pink fabric, this gown evokes a romantic, feminine vibe that’s simple, not fussy. Budget-savvy brides will also surely love the fact that this dreamy dress retails for less than $1,000, allowing you to channel your inner princess without suffering from sticker shock.

## 3. Blue

![Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/3_blue_360x450.jpg)

Another gorgeous Romona Keveza showstopper is this lovely silk-taffeta gown in light blue. While the calm, relaxing shade alone is enough to make me fall in love, the back of the dress is also entirely stunning. With a dress like this, there’s no doubt your groom and guests will be thoroughly wowed all throughout the night.

## 4. Floral

![Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/4_floral_550x766.jpg)

Planning a springtime garden wedding? While gowns in solid pastel shades are definitely beautiful, I also love this soft and breezy floral dress by David’s Bridal. The subtle pattern reminds me of a watercolor painting, and the gentle flow of the fabric helps make it a perfect choice for an outdoor celebration.

## 5. Yellow

![Yellow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/5_yellow_550x827.jpg)

Also just right for a spring or summer wedding, this Rivini gown in sunny yellow adds an extra hint of cheer to your day. I especially love the way this dress evokes a vibe that is both soft and glamorous thanks to its combination of a beautiful pastel shade, figure-flattering cut and beaded details.

## 6. Green

![Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/6_green_550x825.jpg)

Like green, but want to avoid a bluish mint shade? Other hues can also be beautiful, as seen in this unique green ball gown by Terry Fox. Along with the pretty color, I also love the corset-style top and pretty flower detail at the bride’s waist.

## 7. Purple

![Purple](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/05/7_purple_493x744.jpg)

Looking to give your wedding an especially regal vibe? Float down the aisle in a pretty purple gown like this one by Romona Keveza. While the color is surely unique, the strapless cut and beading also give the dress a simultaneously classic look. I only wish more dresses like this had been around back when I was planning my own wedding!

Like the pastel trend, but didn’t see anything you liked here on this list? Keep searching, because there are a ton of other gorgeous dresses out there, especially in particularly trendy shades like blush and blue. What do you think of pastel wedding gowns? Would you consider wearing one? Which shade is your favorite?
