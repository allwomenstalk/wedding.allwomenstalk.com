27 Draped Arbor

Via Gallery & Inspiration | Picture ...

***

The Draped Arbor is a popular choice for wedding ceremonies, as it creates a romantic and whimsical atmosphere. This type of arbor is typically made of a wooden structure covered in flowing fabric, creating a soft and elegant backdrop for the couple to exchange their vows. The draped fabric can be customized to match the wedding theme, with options such as sheer white for a classic look or bold colors for a more modern twist. The addition of flowers, greenery, or other decorations can also enhance the beauty of the arbor and tie in with the overall wedding decor. The Draped Arbor is a versatile choice that can be used both indoors and outdoors, making it a great option for any type of wedding.

35 Book Arch

Via Wild colors, fauxhawks, and dreaded ...

***

Creating an archway from your favorite literature can add a personal touch and whimsy to your special day. Stacked vintage books, with their spines facing outwards, build a unique and intimate atmosphere, highlighting your love for stories and each other. Use hardbacks to vary in height and color, for a visually captivating structure that will be the perfect backdrop for saying your vows. Consider intertwining some greenery or string lights between the tomes to bring your literary fantasy to life. This bibliophilic design is sure to have your guests talking and be a memorable feature of your ceremony.
