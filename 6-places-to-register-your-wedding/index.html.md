# 6 Places to Register Your Wedding ...

There are many items and places you can **register** for your **wedding**. If buying a new home compare what you both already own and what you might need or would like to receive. Always remember proper wedding etiquette and allow your maid of honor or those throwing your **bridal shower** to announce where you have registered for gifts. There are also **charities** you can donate to in lieu of gifts.

## 6. Bed & Bath...

![Bed & Bath...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/Towels_410x410.jpg)

![Bed & Bath...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/bedding_410x410.jpg)

Photos: [BedBathandBeyond](http://www.bedbathandbeyond.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

For **bedding** and **bath** essentials the best place I like to shop and also registered for our wedding is [BedBathandBeyond](https://goo.gl/IFZjV6). You can find beautiful **comforter sets**, **monogrammed bath towels** for your new last name, and matching [bathroom accessories](https://goo.gl/OmM3uV). Return any unwanted gift and buy items from your registry up to 90 days after your wedding for a **10% discount** during a one-time purchase.

## 5. Time for Dinner...

When combining **two households** you might find many mix matched **dinning sets** within your collections. For colorful **dinnerware**, fancy **flatware**, unique glassware and that **gravy boat** you always needed check out [Crate&Barrel](https://www.crateandbarrel.com/). They have a great **range of prices** that will suit everyone on your **guest list**.

## 4. Cutting the Cake...

There are many items you may need for your kitchen, especially now that you'll be hosting more parties and **holiday events**! For all your **bakeware**, cake pans and plates, and **pastry tools** register at [Williams-Sonoma](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.williams-sonoma.com/?cm_type=gnav)and you'll be able to add new items for a (one time only) 10% discount up to six months after you're married!

## 3. A Cup of Joe

There's nothing like **sharing** the **perfect cup of coffee** or tea together as **newlyweds** the morning after your wedding. You may also find that you need more than just a new coffee brewer. Check out [Target](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.target.com/b/ref=in_br_display-ladders?ie=UTF8&node=1038580) to **register** for all of your favorite **kitchen gadgets** and **appliances** so your new husband can cook his new wife **breakfast in bed**!

## 2. Home Decor...

![Home Decor...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/vases2_413x371.jpg)

Photo: [PotteryBarn](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.potterybarn.com/products/voluminous-camilla-canisters/?pkey=cvases-urns)

Picking out kitchen and bath essentials is one thing, but when it comes to **home** **decor** there may be a difference in style. To sort out all your likes and dislikes turn to[Pottery Barn](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.potterybarn.com/?cm_type=gnav)and you just might find a way to **tie** your **home decor styles together** just like you tied the knot!

## 1. Charity...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/ido_407x306.bmp)

Photo: [VintageIndie](https://vintageindie.typepad.com/)

In lieu of [favors](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.joyceandjimlavene.com/guide), included with your [wedding registry](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.joyceandjimlavene.com/guide), or [instead of a gift registry](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.joyceandjimlavene.com/guide), are the many ways you can **donate to charity** through your big day celebration. **Register your wedding** with the [I DO Foundation](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.joyceandjimlavene.com/)and set up a wedding website to share your donation details with your guests. You can choose the [charity of your choice](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.joyceandjimlavene.com/champix-how-to-take-medicine)from the many options provided.
