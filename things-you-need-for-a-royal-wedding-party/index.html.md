# 5 Things You Need for a Royal Wedding Party ...

Tomorrow is the big day and what better way to celebrate the **Royal Wedding** than hosting your own viewing party! Maybe you won't be up at 4am to watch [Kate Middleton](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/7-designer-bridal-gown-sketches-for-kate-middleton/)and [Prince William's](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-ways-to-be-inspired-by-the-royal-wedding/)big day, but you can DVR it and invite your friends over for a party even the Queen would love....

## 5. Royal Food...

![Royal Food...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/finger-sandwhiches_403x302.jpg)

Photo: [YumSugar](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.yumsugar.com/Wich-Week-Benedictine-Cheese-Tea-Sandwiches-1610066)

For your **Royal Wedding Party** serve little **finger sandwiches **that will go great with your English tea. These tasty sandwiches are filled with cheese and a creamy cucumber-and-scallion spread. Check out the link for an easy to make [recipe](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.yumsugar.com/Wich-Week-Benedictine-Cheese-Tea-Sandwiches-1610066).

## 4. Don't Be Late for Tea...

![Don't Be Late for Tea...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/hearttea_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/6417/weddings)

We all know the [British](https://www.tea.co.uk/)love tea, there's even an official[tea cup](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.royalweddinggift.com/index.php?main_page=product_info&cPath=20&products_id=56)for the Royal Wedding! So make sure your guests are on time for high noon tea, which is 2pm. Decorate your tea bags with this adorable heart cut-out idea from [Martha Stewart](https://www.marthastewart.com/7933119/bridal-shower-tea-party-ideas)and write Kate loves William on each tag!

## 3. British Decorations...

![British Decorations...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/royal-wedding-366x600_366x600.jpg)

Photo: [DesignEditor](https://web.archive.org/web/https://designeditor.typepad.com/design_editor/2011/04/free-royal-wedding-party-printables.html)

What's a **Royal Wedding party**without British flags, a Royal invitation, and crowns fit for a Prince and Princess! Head on over to[Design Editor](https://web.archive.org/web/https://designeditor.typepad.com/design_editor/2011/04/free-royal-wedding-party-printables.html)for free royal wedding printables to make your party the one everyone won't want to miss.

## 2. Jam and Scones...

![Jam and Scones...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/scones_400x261.jpg)

Photo: [YumSugar](https://www.popsugar.com/food/How-Host-Royal-Wedding-Viewing-Party-16080615)

Along with tea and finger sandwiches, you need to have a **Royal Wedding dessert** for your guests. You can [make your own](https://www.joyofbaking.com/CranberryOatScones.html) or call your local bakery for fresh **jam and baked scones**, but be certain to serve them at your party because they are very popular in the UK and will be popular with your guests.

## 1. Fancy Feathers...

![Fancy Feathers...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/feathers_399x399.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/itsthelittlethingsut](https://goo.gl/8lmcey)

Follow the trends of [Kate Middleton](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-bridal-hair-accessories-for-the-royal-princess/)at your Royal Wedding party as you invite your guests to don **fancy** **feather hair accessories**. Many parties might put on their fancy hats, but I think the **feathers** would be more suited for a party honoring Kate and her new Prince!
