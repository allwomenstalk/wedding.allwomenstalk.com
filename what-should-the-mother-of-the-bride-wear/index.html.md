# What Should the Mother of the Bride Wear?

It's a fascinating question to think about when it comes to a wedding day that's dear to a mother's heart. Seeing your child in a beautiful wedding gown is a delightful experience that will live with you forever. However, what are you going to be wearing on the big day?

This is something mothers have to think about while preparing for the [wedding day](https://www.brides.com/story/tips-for-staying-present-on-wedding-day).

You are going to be a major part of the festivities and should be dressed accordingly. Keeping this in mind, it's time to pinpoint what you are going to be wearing and the message it's going to exude.

## 1. Sync with the Bridesmaids

The best starting point would be to look at what the bridesmaids will wear. If they are going with something pink, it doesn't hurt to wear a shade that's relatively close to that specific color. The idea is to stay in line with what others associated with the bride are wearing while still standing out a little bit.

You don't want to wear the same color as the bridesmaids. Remember, you are still the bride's mother, which means you are going to have a different role than the bridesmaids.

It's more about looking like you are from the same group but still have your own personality. If you sync with the bridesmaids, you are not going to stick out like a sore thumb and will still look personable. This is a fine balance to find, but this is a rule of thumb that's going to help out along the way.

Don't be afraid to plan these details out well in advance by keeping up to speed with [what the bridesmaids are wearing](https://www.southernliving.com/weddings/dresses/trending-bridesmaid-dresses-short-long).

## 2. Follow the Bride's Lead

The bride's lead is something that is going to matter the most. You have to make sure the bride's lead is taken into account because she is going to be the center of attention.

You want to focus on what she is wearing and make sure not to overdo that specific look. She has to be the one that is in the middle of everything.

However, you can also use her formality as a way to choose your dress. For example, if she is wearing a casual look, you will want to stick to the same type of dress too. [Going too formal](https://www.marthastewartweddings.com/231259/formal-wedding-ideas) in that situation will make you stand out for the wrong reasons.

## 3. Don't Stand out

This is a simple rule anyone can follow and it needs to be in the back of your mind. There is nothing worse than a mother that overrules her bride when it comes to their look.

You want to be a mother that is seamlessly fitting into the wedding day while the bride takes center stage. Anything other than this is going to put you in a bad light and will look out of place.

Always tone it down one or two notches when looking at what the bride is wearing.

## 4. Figure out Your Makeup Choices

What type of look are you going for when it comes to makeup? Are you going to be wearing specific colors to look good?

Each mother is going to be different in this regard and there's nothing wrong with that. It's important to finalize these choices in advance including your hairstyle. Having this information is going to make it ten times easier to pinpoint which dress is best suited for your overall look.

Otherwise, you are going to be guessing along the way and that can be challenging. Stay smart and make sure you figure out your makeup choices properly.

## 5. Know the Venue

What is the venue going to be like? For example, you are not going to wear the same dress inside a wedding hall as you would outside during a beach wedding. These are venues that are going to require different types of dresses and that has to be taken into account.

If you are going to be sticking to a traditional ballroom wedding, it might be okay to stick to a more fanciful dress.

However, if you are going to be spending time outdoors under the sun, it might be time to wear something more breezy and easy-going. This is going to end up depending on the venue.

## 6. Final Thoughts

This is what the mother of the bride should wear.

If you take the time to plan everything, the look will come out the way you want it to be. This is going to depend on the wedding's theme, location, and what others are going to be wearing around you.

Each detail matters and it's important to focus on something that's going to exude a bit of personality along with style. This is when you are going to find a dress that gets the job done.
