# 5 Ways to Celebrate Your New Year's Eve Wedding ...

New Year's Eve is one of the best times of the year for a party so what better day to celebrate your wedding! Ring in the New Year with these 5 ideas for your amazing New Year's Eve Wedding!

## 5. Champagne Toast....

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/invitation_405x405.jpg)

Photo: [MagnetStreet.com](https://www.trulyengaging.com/product/champagne-toast-half-fold-wedding-invitations?productId=30185&null)

Kissing at Midnight, Party Hats, and Champagne Toast are all a big part of [New Year's Eve](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-new-years-dresses-under-20/). Let your guests know how you will be ringing in the new year with this bubbly [champagne toast wedding invitation.](https://www.trulyengaging.com/product/champagne-toast-half-fold-wedding-invitations?productId=30185&null) Surprise them as [sparkly confetti](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.partycity.com/404) falls out of the envelope!** How will you be celebrating New Year's Eve 2011?**

## 4. Time is Ticking....

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/New-Years-Eve-Tabletop1_402x271.jpg)

Photo: [Elizabethannedesigns.com](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/categories/new-years-eve/)

Some New Year's Eve parties can be pretty crazy and wild, but party in style and class for your NYE wedding with a beautiful table setting and centerpiece like this one. I love the metallic colors; perfect for winter and new years weddings. The vintage clock [centerpiece](https://goo.gl/Aw2UZz)is such a [creative idea](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-find-ideas-outside-the-wedding-box/)that I have yet to see. Your guests will always remember how they had the** time of their lives** at your wedding! **Do your centerpieces match your wedding theme? **

## 3. Guestbook That Captures Time...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/timecapsule_400x300.jpg)

Photo: [Elizabethannedesigns.com](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/categories/new-years-eve/)

Remember the year and the moments you've shared with your friends, family and** each other** with this amazing [time capsule guest book](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/2009/03/03/time-capsule-guest-book/). For her 10 year wedding anniversary Ami Elizabeth at [ElizabethAnneDesigns](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/)celebrated with a vow renewal and came up with this unique time capsule guest book idea. Ask your guests to bring a small item or token of who they were ****this year. It could be a charm, an earring, a button, a beer bottle cap, or anything they can think of. Then in a book or on a DIY notecard have your guests write about the item they put in the time capsule, what their new year's resolution is, their favorite memory of the [lovely couple](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-wedding-details-to-get-your-groom-involved-with/), and their prediction for the next 5 years or so. What have your consider for your unique guestbook?

## 2. Making an Exit...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/nyessparklers_408x272.jpg)

Photo: [SweetTeaPhotographybylisamarie.blogspot.com](http://sweetteaphotographybylisamarie.blogspot.com/)

You may not know how to set up a fireworks display for your New Year's Eve wedding, but you can set up your own show as your guests light these fun [sparklers](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.daisy-days.com/wedding-sparklers.html)for your grand exit! What would a New Year's Eve celebration be without a sparkling show! Bring out the [noisemakers](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.bulkpartysupplies.com/store/c/4820-New-Years-Eve-Noisemakers-and-Blowouts.html), party horns, and [confetti wedding poppers](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.americanbridal.com/wedding-poppers-dska28046wt.html?affProgram=linkshare&siteID=_kqJoBgx.1Q-wywxzWBEx6E3HVxulm_UzQ) to make it a true end of the year wedding! **Are your guests doing anything special for your grand exit? **
