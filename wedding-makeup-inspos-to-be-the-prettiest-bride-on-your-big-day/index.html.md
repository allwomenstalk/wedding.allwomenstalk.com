# Wedding Makeup Inspos to Be the Prettiest Bride on Your Big Day ...

Your wedding is the biggest day of your life and you want to look good. After having years of experience wearing makeup, you know what looks best on you, but your wedding is a day when you should wear something more special and to truly make you feel like a beautiful bride. With one of these makeup tutorials, you will be the most beautiful bride.

## 1. Classic Glam for the Modern Girl

This tutorial is really fun to watch and has a beautiful look at the end. The personal aspect of it makes it fun to watch. Patrick is great at explaining what the products are and what they'll do for you. This is the perfect look for a modern girl who wants a classic look.

## 2. Romantic Look for Spring or Summer Wedding

This look includes a soft, glam smokey eye, flawless skin, and a nude lip. The tutorial also includes a hair tutorial of an updo with flowers, which complements the romantic look. This video shows all the products clearly, making it easy to recreate.

## 3. Elegant and Simple for the Natural Beauty

This is the perfect look for the girl who doesn't usually wear a lot of makeup. It is simple to recreate and shows your natural beauty. This is the perfect elegant and simple look for a natural beauty.

## 4. Soft Bridal Makeup by a Pro

This video tutorial is done by a real makeup artist, so the look is fabulous and soft. She directs you how to apply with each step, creating a soft natural look that looks beautiful on anyone. This is perfect for brides who want a little more direction with their makeup tutorial.

## 5. No Touch Ups Needed Makeup

If you're looking for a makeup look that will stay fresh from beginning to end, this is the look for you. The artist emphasizes the use of primer in this video for makeup that stays all night long.

## 6. Pretty in Pink Classic Look for the Girly Girl

This is the perfect look for girls who love pink. This look creates a sun kissed glow that transforms you into a beautiful goddess on your big day.

## 7. Enhance Your Features

This look uses the combination of strong brows, contoured cheekbones, and doe-like eyes to create a beautiful look that shows off the beautiful you. This is a perfect look for brides who still want to look like themselves but add a little oomph.
