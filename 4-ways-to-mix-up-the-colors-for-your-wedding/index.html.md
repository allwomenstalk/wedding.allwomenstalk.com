# 4 Ways to Mix up the Colors for Your Wedding ...

There are so many** gorgeous wedding trends** and **themes** out there for a bride and groom to choose from that it can be difficult to want to plan just one. What if you wanted to** mix trends**, **themes**, and** colors together** into one **big** **celebration**? Would it be so wrong! Have fun and **mix it up** with these 4 ways to plan a** colorful wedding**...

## 4. Keeping It Fresh...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [EmilyonaSunday](https://www.flickr.com/photos/emilyandthemagic/sets/), [Etsy](https://goo.gl/3g9lBm)

I have found a new **love** for [colorful weddings](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/06/09/garden-wedding-in-georgia/)and not just two or three **colors**, but **four or five colors** mixed together for one big day! Let the[colors pop](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/06/09/garden-wedding-in-georgia/)in your beautiful [bouquet](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-sweet-simple-wedding-bouquets/) like this** yellow and pink** show stopper and **decorate** your tables with an amazing** array of** **colors**. Forget the rules, just **have fun** and keep it **fresh**!

## 3. A Colorful Day...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

It's **ice cream** **on a warm day**, a phone call from your best friend, or the **kiss** he sneaks when you're not looking. **Bright**, colorful **weddings** have a good, **sunshine feeling** that will make your guests' smile last through the whole day. Show off your bubbly side with[lollipop favors](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.top10weddingsites.com/wedding-favors.htmlprod/OT5754.htm), **rainbow** [umbrellas](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-umbrella-ideas-for-your-wedding-day/), and **bridesmaids** dressed in [fun colors or prints](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-dresses-for-every-spring-2011-bridesmaid/). Do your** wedding colors** give you **good vibrations**?

## 2. Buttons, Balloons, & a Beautiful Bouquet...

Photos: [StylemePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [EmilyonaSunday](https://www.flickr.com/photos/emilyandthemagic/sets/)

**Mix up** your wedding theme with **colorful decor details** like[button and spool centerpieces](https://web.archive.org/web/https://greenweddingshoes.com/rustic-vintage-wedding-ideas/), **balloons** for your [bridesmaids](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-unique-things-for-your-bridesmaids-to-carry/), and **fun props** for your reception. You don't need to stick to one idea; for** our wedding** we had a[beach theme cake](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-reasons-why-our-wedding-rocked/)along with lemon and butterfly **centerpieces**. Do things your way!

## 1. A Rainbow Explosion...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Etsy](https://goo.gl/97Wcwl), [EmilyonaSunday](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.flickr.com/photos/emilyandthemagic/sets/72157624248377126/)

**Happiness **is what this inspiration board is all about, but it's also what your wedding should be about. Don't get lost in the details, planning, guest list, or seating arrangement. If you want to wear[red rain boots](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/08/10/canadian-rockies-wedding-by-orange-girl-photographs/), have [yarn centerpieces](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-find-ideas-outside-the-wedding-box/), and have your **bridesmaids** carry adorable [pinwheels](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)than go for it! Remember your **true colors** and the love that will show when you stick to that one rule!

**How will you mix things up for your wedding day? **
