# 4 Bridal Hair Accessories for the Royal Princess ...

If you have seen the many looks of [Kate Middleton](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.yahoo.com/lifestyle/tagged/health/channel/beauty/hat-s-off-kate-middleton-s-most-stylish-hair-accessories-2447914)than you know she is a big fan of funky, offbeat hair accessories. She loves hats, feathers, and even hats with feathers! On her **royal wedding day** she may be wearing a crown from the queen for the ceremony, but maybe one of these **bridal hair accessories** will make an appearance during the reception...

## 4. Vintage Lace...

This **vintage lace flower** is big and beautiful, what's Kate not to love about wearing it for her wedding day look. I haven't seen a photo of Kate with any **floral hair accessories** yet so this would be a lovely piece to add to her collection.

## 3. Glitz & Glamour...

![Glitz & Glamour...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/rhinestoneheadband_428x321.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/WeeGardens](https://goo.gl/7VUC2z)

Since she'll be wearing a gorgeous, eye-catching crown for her ceremony, maybe she can carry that look through the reception with this glamorous** rhinestone headband**. I love the **satin ribbon** that ties in the back, I wonder if this would be the perfect match to Kate's wedding gown!

## 2. Funky & Unique...
