# 8 Great Sightseeing Honeymoon Destinations ...

Choosing a **honeymoon destination** can be just as difficult as choosing your wedding ceremony and reception location. Start out by knowing what you want to do and see; do you want to relax or explore? Today we have guest blogger Nisha from [Holiday365](http://www.holiday365.co.uk/) here to share with us her thoughts on **7 Great Sightseeing Honeymoon Destinations**...
Explore and Sightsee on Your Honeymoon...
Sightseeing and enjoying the picturesque sceneries of some of the most beautiful vacation spots in the world are perfect for honeymoon vacations. Most sight lovers enjoy being in the midst of scenic environments and photographing distinctive sceneries and landscapes, rather than a local family holiday park. There are a variety of honeymoon vacation destinations around the world for the romantic enjoyment of sight lovers.
Caribbean Destinations...
Planning a Caribbean honeymoon is a wonderful romantic getaway for sightseers. Some of the popular Caribbean islands are Bahamas, Puerto Rico, Jamaica and the Grenadines. Because the Caribbean islands are very popular for honeymooners, there are many all-inclusive Caribbean honeymoon packages. View the beautiful blue skies and pristine ocean waves at a romantic resort in the Caribbean islands. Some of the most popular Caribbean resorts are the Las Casitas Resort in Puerto Rico, 7 Sandals Resort in Jamaica, Palm Island Resort in the Grenadines and the Almond Beach Village in Barbados.

## 1. Puerto Rico...

It is home to many natural attractions, including the breath-taking sunset and beaches of Porta del Sol and Cabo Rojo.

## 2. Jamaica...

Throughout the resort locations in Jamaica, you will find Jamaican sightseeing tours and excursions that include scuba diving, deep sea fishing and more.

## 3. Grenada...

It has beautiful blossoming mountains and crystal clear beach waters. There are a variety of tours and sightseeing opportunities for you to enjoy dolphin watching, deep sea fishing, kayaking, and hiking in the rain forest and more.

## 4. Barbados...

If you are planning your honeymoon vacation in Barbados, you will surely discover its distinctive scenic lookouts and style of life. There are Barbados boat cruises, scuba diving excursions, powerboat tours, submarine tours and more.

## 5. Nassau, Bahamas...

Plan your next honeymoon destination at one of the Nassau beach hotels and enjoy the beautiful clear waters of the beaches. As the palm trees sit perfectly along the waters, you will certainly be able to take pleasure in seeing the sights of the tropical destination of the islands of the Bahamas. Many resort hotels for honeymooners are located near Paradise Island and Cable Beach. Also, you may find lovely resort hotels near downtown Nassau. Moreover, there are dinner cruises, such as the Nassau Majestic Lady Dinner Cruise and the Rose Island Snorkel and Day Cruise.
European Destinations...
Planning a European honeymoon vacation is another great choice for sight lovers. The top destinations for European honeymoons are Rome, Paris and Barcelona. Many sightseers love the historical nature and luxurious properties of these European locations.

## 6. Rome...

It has been called the iconic city of love. Enjoy a memorable getaway at one of the romantic honeymoon hotels in Rome, and don’t miss out on the sightseeing tours throughout Rome’s historical architectural landmarks.

## 7. Paris...

If you choose to plan your honeymoon in Paris, France, you will get a taste of a rich culture as you view the sophistication of the world’s most romantic destination. In Paris, the Hotel Duquesne Eiffel overlooks the Eiffel Tower, and it’s is an excellent location for sight lovers. Also, in Paris, the Seine River Cruise is an excellent sightseeing tour.

## 8. Barcelona, Spain...

It is the perfect destination for wine lovers, and it is home to many great honeymoon vacation hotels. There are daily sightseeing tours in Barcelona, and Barcelona has several popular attractions, such as the Sagrada Familia, La Seu Cathedral and the Casa Batllo.
