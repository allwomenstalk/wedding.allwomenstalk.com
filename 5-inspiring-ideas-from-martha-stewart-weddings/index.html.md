# 5 Inspiring Ideas from Martha Stewart Weddings ...

[Martha Stewart](https://www.marthastewart.com/) inspires weddings every day, from the ideas on her [wedding website](https://www.marthastewart.com/6417/weddings) to the inspiration on her [television show](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.marthastewart.com/tv). There are DIY details, fabulous real weddings, and everything in between. Be **inspired** for your wedding by these **5 wonderful ideas from Martha Stewart Weddings...**

## 5. More Wine Please...

![More Wine Please...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/winebottlenumbers_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7940835/paper-wedding-decoration-projects)

Everything about **Martha Stewart's** wedding **ideas** seem **so** **simple yet so classy**. These [wine bottle table numbers](https://www.marthastewart.com/7940835/paper-wedding-decoration-projects)are a great idea for a [wine loving couple](https://goo.gl/jS7Q8n), an[Italy themed wedding](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-wedding-themes/), or a beautiful wedding reception located at a [vineyard](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.weddingcompass.com/LocationDetails.cfm?id=168). **Do you have a unique idea for your table numbers? **

## 4. Delightful Favors...

![Delightful Favors...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/marthafavors_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7950518/chris-and-tim-bedford-new-york)

I've seen many [tea wedding favors](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-unique-wedding-favors/), but none with **adorable cookies** like this one from a fabulous real wedding. The **charming favor box** came from Martha's own nephew's wedding that took place right at [Stewart's horse barn](https://www.marthastewart.com/7950518/chris-and-tim-bedford-new-york). I love the **personalized box**, T&C, for Tim and Chris. **What favors do you have in mind for your wedding?**

## 3. Two Ideas in One...

![Two Ideas in One...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/banner_402x503.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7916370/templates-wedding-decorations)

This [fun banner](https://www.marthastewart.com/7909070/scrapbook-paper-ideas-how-tos) is another simple idea from **Martha Stewart**. It can be **hung for decor** at your laid back summer time wedding or it can be used as a [creative seating chart](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-unique-place-card-ideas-for-your-wedding/) with the names of your **guest** and **table numbers **on each flag. **Are you using banners for your wedding decor? **

## 2. Drink Me...

![Drink Me...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/drinklabels_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7940835/paper-wedding-decoration-projects)

**Drinks**, [dessert tables](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-delicious-cupcakes-for-your-wedding/), bars, candy jars, and **food tables** can be just that. But why not make them more when you **dress them up** with **charming little signs** like this DIY project from [Stewart's wedding site](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.marthastewartweddings.com/224264/marital-bliss-cocktail-recipe-and-drink-label-how). Then they become part of your **wedding decor **and **details** guests will remember. **Are you creating signs and labels for your wedding items?**

## 1. Simple Centerpieces...

![Simple Centerpieces...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/centerpiece_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7950518/chris-and-tim-bedford-new-york)

Based on the ideas you have for your **wedding details**, **theme**, and **decor**, [wedding centerpieces](https://goo.gl/UtHDxO) can range from **simple chic** to [city elegance](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-celebrate-your-new-years-eve-wedding/). **Beautiful centerpieces like this one from [Martha Stewart's nephew's wedding](https://www.marthastewart.com/7950518/chris-and-tim-bedford-new-york) help create the perfect atmosphere you want your** guests to experience. I love the **simplicity** of the rope tied around the bottles; who would have thought a **simple rustic idea** could develop so much **ambiance**.
