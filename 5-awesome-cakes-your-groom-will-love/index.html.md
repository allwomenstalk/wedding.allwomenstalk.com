# 5 Awesome Cakes Your Groom Will Love ...

We've been talking about the bride so much lately that I've been starting to feel a little guilty about leaving the **groom** out of all the fun! This is a post your guys will love so tell them to look this way and listen up! We have our pink flowers and our pretty bird cake toppers so what about him? Here are 5 cakes I know your groom is going to love, especially because one of them was my** grooms' cake! **

## 5. The Music Lover...

![The Music Lover...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/guitar1_408x306.jpg)

Photo:[CupaDeeCakes](http://cupadeecakes.blogspot.com/2010/04/acoustic-guitar-grooms-cake.html)

There are so many great options when it comes to surprising your handsome **groom** with a **cake** in his honor. Show him how much you appreciate him helping you with all those crazy DIY projects, going with the pink and green palette instead of the blue and white, and for just being him. This **awesome guitar** was made for a groom that wanted a replica of a [Martin Acoustic](https://www.martinguitar.com/), and Jennifer White at[Cup A Dee Cakes](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.cupadeecakes.com/)did just that! Whether he is in a **band**, likes to **play guitar** at home, or just really **loves music**, your guy will love the **musical grooms cake **you suprise him with.

## 4. The Computer Geek...

![The Computer Geek...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/laptopcake_406x406.jpg)

Photo:[Cake Girls](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.wetv.com/amazing-wedding-cakes/bios/cakegirls)via [Brides](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-cakes/editorial-pick/2008/06/grooms-cake-guide)

Does your guy know how to make **computer** graphics, fix computers, or maybe he's just a savvy business man who always carries around his laptop? Perhaps a geeky **computer grooms cake** is the surprise that will knock him off his feet! I love the monogram on the computer screen of this cool** laptop cake** from[Cake Girls](https://thecakegirls.com/). The details are right on spot.

## 3. The Sports Guy...

![The Sports Guy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/IMG_2196_403x303.jpg)

Photo: [Conroy Catering](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.conroycatering.com/) via Personal Photo

I am so excited to share this amazing grooms cake with you...because it's the **Steelers grooms cake** I surprised my husband with! I knew I had to surprise him with a [Steelers football](https://www.steelers.com/)cake, but what would the **cake** look like? Well mostly everyone, fan or not, knows about the **Terrible Towel** and we have several of them around the house! So I emailed Bridget at[Conroy Catering](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.conroycatering.com/docs/glenfoerd.html)with a few photos and on the day of our wedding we were both so happy and surprised with how **perfect** it turned out!

## 2. We Heart Nerds...

![We Heart Nerds...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/superman_402x605.jpg)

Photo: [OffbeatBride](https://offbeatbride.com/cool-grooms-cake/)

Sometimes less is more! I love how simple yet cool this **Superman grooms cake** looks. The [bride](https://www.flickr.com/photos/jenluvsmark/4118345670/)actually had a local mom make the cake for only $80! With the **colors**, the S symbol, and the **cool cake topper **you know it's a Superman cake, but it's not over the top or outrageous and that's why I love it!

## 1. Favorite Food...

![Favorite Food...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/Burger_groom_cake_400x316.jpg)

Photo: [Minette Rushing](https://web.archive.org/web/http://savannahcustomcakes.com/)via [PetalSweet](http://petalsweet.blogspot.com/2009_10_01_archive.html)

How much do you just want to bite into this cake! I am always amazed when I see **grooms cakes **that look like the real item they represent! This **burger cake** looks better than a real burger! The details are incredible. Do you think your groom would love a **cake** that looks like his **favorite food**?

What kind of cake will you surprise your groom with?
