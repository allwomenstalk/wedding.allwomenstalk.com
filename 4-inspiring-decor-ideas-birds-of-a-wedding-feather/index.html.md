# 4 Inspiring Decor Ideas: Birds of a Wedding Feather ...

Flock together! From **theme** and **color**, to style and attire, **feathers** can either play a little part in your** decor ideas** or they can be centerstage in your **wedding day** spotlight. Find the **details** that suit your big day with these 4 inspiring decor ideas: **Birds of a Wedding Feather**...

## 4. Classic Chic...

Photos:[StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

**Feathers** for your [wedding decor](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-wedding-themes-inspired-by-paper-goods/)can be fun and bright or they can be **classic** and **chic** like this **gorgeous** inspiration board. I love the soft palette of pinks and the **classic look** of black and white. Stroll to your **reception** in **style** as you step out of a [Rolls Royce](https://www.rolls-roycemotorcars.com/en_US/home.html), parade around with your [feather bouquet](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), and dance the night away as your **bridesmaids** rock their amazing [feather dresses](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/fashion/dresses/gallery/wedding_dresses/editorspick/designer/angelsanchez/detail/177204)!

## 3. Red Delicious...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

This **feather wedding decor** look is[old hollywood glam](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/wedding-dresses-style/wedding-hair/editorial-pick/2007/06/old-hollywood-glamour)meets[jazz nightclub](https://www.100layercake.com/blog/2010/07/26/real-wedding-joanna-brian/). Pull together glamourous details of **black feathers**, red roses, and[deep purple calla lilies](http://www.flowers.pacificcallas.com/darkpurplecallalilyflowers.htm)for your bouquets and centerpieces to set a **dramatic** ambiance. How much do you love the** checkered dance floor**, who wouldn't want to create a **jazz age theme** when you have a [reception room](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2009/04/27/dramatic-hollywood-glam-from-viva-bella-events-ii/)like that!

## 2. Perfectly Pink...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

Whether your groom likes it or not you want a** perfectly pink** wedding from head to toe! There will be [feminine pink feathers](https://goo.gl/x5SSSY)in your **centerpieces**, [sparkling pink heels](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2008/11/10/real-wedding-by-james-abel-4/)for you to dance in, [pink raspberry macarons](https://www.foodandwine.com/recipes/raspberry-macarons) for your **guests** to nibble on, and a [pretty pink peonie bouquet](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-hot-color-palette-trends-for-your-2011-wedding/)for you to carry. Maybe there will be a **surprise grooms cake** for him since you turned **pink** on him!

## 1. Purple Goodness...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

Create a** purple feathered** wedding for your **outdoor ceremony** with **rustic** touches for **decor details** everyone including your groom will **love**! Fill your [mason jars](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-rustic-wedding-centerpiece-ideas/)with purple hydrangeas and **peacock feathers**, gift your bridesmaids with[delicate feather headbands](https://goo.gl/VnMJr2), and wow your **hubby-to-be** in an [amazing feather detail gown](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.couturecandy.com/sue-wong/antique-beaded-empire-feather-gown-/product.html?fninfo=dD1jJmNoPTEwNDQmY209MTUmbnI9MQ__).
