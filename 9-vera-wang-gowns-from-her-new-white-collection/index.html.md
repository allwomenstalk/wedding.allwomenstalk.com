# 9 Vera Wang Gowns from Her New White Collection ...

Only one more day until **brides** everywhere will be lined up outside [David's Bridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)for these **stunning gowns**. Unless you have been living under a rock or just don't care about **bridal fashion** than you know that [Vera Wang's White Collection](https://www.davidsbridal.com/Content_StyleandFashionGuide_WeddingDressDesigners)will be now sold at [David's Bridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Bride's on a budget get your running shoes on and step right up to these beautiful gowns...

## 6. Layers of Fabric...

![Layers of Fabric...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/White_by_Vera_Wang_for_Davids_Bridal_7_420x306.jpg)

Price: {Left} $1,200 {Right} $1,200

[Vera Wang](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.verawang.com/wedding/bridal-collections)knows how to make **layers**, upon layers look **sophisticated** and **romantic**! I love the **one shoulder gown** and the fit and flare style of it; something I haven't seen from Vera before. Wouldn't you just love to be daring and walk down the aisle in the** gorgeous pink gown**, another with a "flange" skirt; as they are named on [David's Bridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com). Are you going to wear a [wedding dress](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-real-brides-that-will-catch-your-eye/)from the **White Collection**?

## 5. Romantic & Delicate...

![Romantic & Delicate...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/White_by_Vera_Wang_for_Davids_Bridal_10_415x285.jpg)

Price: {Left} $700 {Right} $800

Could you have ever imagined that [Vera Wang's](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.verawang.com/wedding/bridal-collections)wedding gowns would have been this inexpensive and available to [brides on a budget](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/diy-wedding-ideas-on-a-budget-guest-blogger-poppies-sunshine/)? I love the **soft** details on both of these gowns, very **dreamy** and **delicate**. The first gown has illusion straps and a net gown draped over the bodice, while the second dress has a beautiful lace bodice over a net skirt.

## 4. She Has Accessories Too!

![She Has Accessories Too!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/White_by_Vera_Wang_for_Davids_Bridal_2_420x288.jpg)

Price: {Left} $1,300 {Right} $700

**David's Bridal** not only has dresses by **Vera**, but they have **accessories** too! The unique **floral black sash** on the second gown just speaks of the **one of a kind style** that everyone knows from **Vera Wang** and cost $78. How amazing does the bride in the first picture look with the stunning **crystal haircomb**; I just love the way they styled her hair for the shoot! It will cost you $128 for that piece.

## 3. Simple & Classy...

![Simple & Classy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/White_by_Vera_Wang_for_Davids_Bridal_5_402x424.jpg)

Price: $600 Sash: $148

Are you a bride looking for something** simple yet classy** because you can find that in [Vera Wang's gowns](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)as well. I love that not all of her gowns are so runway and bold. She knows how to make a gown for **every bride **and now every bride can buy her gowns with the new **White Collection**. How classic is this simple one shoulder draped **chiffon gown**, lovely for a **beach wedding**.

## 2. Very Vera...

![Very Vera...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/White_by_Vera_Wang_for_Davids_Bridal_13_402x424.jpg)

Price: $1,400 Headband: $98
Speaking of runway and **bold**! This **tulle** and **organza** ball gown shouts [Vera Wang](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.verawang.com/wedding/spring-2011/)and all of your guests will know it! I love the **swirls** in the skirt; very different and **offbeat**. This is a dress for a bride who wants to show off her [unique personality](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-brides-who-know-how-to-rock-a-non-traditional-wedding-dress/)and creative style. Is this the gown for you?

## 1. A Princess in Tulle...

![A Princess in Tulle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/White_by_Vera_Wang_for_Davids_Bridal_8_402x424.jpg)

You will not only feel bridal in this gown, but you will feel like a Princess too. I love the amazing overflowing** tulle ball gown**; have you ever seen so much tulle on a dress!
