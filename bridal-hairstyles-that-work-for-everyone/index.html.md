# 4 Bridal Hairstyles That Work for Everyone ...

I saw some lovely **bridal hairstyles **on [Martha Stewart Weddings](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.marthastewartweddings.com/fashion-and-beauty) and thought, "What classic bridal looks would work for every bride that wouldn't go out of style?" I found four beautiful looks that are great for **short hair**, **long hair**, **curly**, and **ethnic**, which one works best for you....

## 1. Bridal Braids...

![Bridal Braids...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/braids_407x509.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/6417/weddings)

I love** brides with braids **and really wanted a braid in the front of my hair, but it wasn't long or thick enough to look as good as this bridal model. So I inspired my stepdaughter (bridesmaid) to get a side braid like the one above and we all loved it! Braids add a whimsical look and can be a great **wedding **day **hairstyle **for brides with curly and frizzy hair.

## 2. Short & Retro...

![Short & Retro...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/short_406x508.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/6417/weddings)

Brides with **short hair **might believe they don't have many options to go with. But there are lovely looks out there for everyone. If you're planning a retro theme **wedding **why not go glam with **wavy locks **and a red lip. This is a gorgeous style that works well with short hair and will stay classic for years to come.

## 3. Side Pony...

![Side Pony...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/side-ponytail-flower-hairstyle_404x608.jpg)

Photo: [Hair Romance](https://www.hairromance.com/)

Another **hairstyle **that I love is the **side ponytail**, especially when brides add a romantic **flower. **A birdcage veil and a short wedding dress could complete this bridal look. Will you have flowers in your hair on your big day?

## 4. Half up & Half down...

![Half up & Half down...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/kate-middleton-wedding-day-makeup2_408x473.jpg)

Photo: [WeddingBellsBlog](https://www.martelnyc.com/)

By now everyone has seen photos from the[Royal Wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/the-royal-wedding-live/), if you haven't then you must live under a rock! But since you have then you'll agree how stunning and classic **Kate Middleton's hair looked under her beautiful veil and crown. She usually wears her hair down, so going with a** half up, **half down hairstyle **was very suiting for a bride who doesn't like to wear her hair in an updo. She may have stayed with a traditional look, but she blew me away with her wedding day beauty and that will never go out of style!
