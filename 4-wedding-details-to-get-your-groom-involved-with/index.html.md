# 4 Wedding Details to Get Your Groom Involved with ...

When it comes to weddings some **grooms** believe the brides want to do all of the **planning** with her best group of gals. There are **some** **grooms** that really **get involved** and we love them for it, but if you're having trouble getting your **fiance** interested in any of your wedding planning ideas here are **4 wedding details your groom may want to be involved with**...

## 4. Handle the Money Honey...

![Handle the Money Honey...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/groom1_408x605.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault/image/76257)

The **Budget** is the first thing, after celebrating your wonderful engagement, that should be set. You should sit down together to go over what you would like to spend. Then when you have your limit set the **groom** can get involved with **paying the vendors on time**, making a **budget chart** of the amount everyone gets paid, and if there is any room in the budget for **extras**. He may not be cutting out [little hearts](https://goo.gl/1GWym8)for your [wedding banner](https://www.marthastewart.com/7916370/templates-wedding-decorations), but helping with the [budget](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-stick-to-your-wedding-budget/) is one of the most important details of your wedding. **Have you set your wedding budget?**

## 3. Pimp My Ride...

![Pimp My Ride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/groomride_407x271.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

**Men love cars**...enough said! So what better wedding detail to have your **groom** involved with. First follow number four and set your **transportation budget.** This way he'll know whether he can pick out the **glamourous **[Rolls Royce](https://www.nlslimo.com/gallery/antique-cars/silver-cloud-rolls-royce.html) or join with the rest of the[bridal party](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-ways-to-have-fun-on-your-wedding-day/) on the ever so fun [school bus](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.busbank.com/school_bus.aspx) or [trolley](http://cescaphetrolley.com/?gclid=CJnL2NjP06UCFcTb4Aod6l0Sjw)! Will you be **riding in style** on your wedding day?

## 2. Entertain the Guests...

![Entertain the Guests...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/fireworks_405x608.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault/image/27572)

Is your **groom** always the life of the **party**? Booking the **entertainment**; [DJ](http://www.800dj.com/), [band](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.gigmasters.com/liveband/), [fireworks](https://www.mypartyplanner.com/), or [dancers](http://www.gigmasters.com/BellyDancer/Belly-Dancer.htm), can be something **fun for your groom** to be involved with. He may even surprise you with an awesome [choreographed dance](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-awesome-wedding-dance-videos/) from him and his **groomsmen**! **What kind of entertainment are you look for on the big day? **

## 1. Groomin' the Groomsmen...

![Groomin' the Groomsmen...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/groomsmen_405x270.jpg)

Photo: [StyleMePretty.com](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault/image/30625)

After you decide the **tone of your wedding**; black tie event, beach wedding, casual, or semi-formal, your **groom** can take his groomsmen for a night out trying different [suits](https://goo.gl/6Qanu4), [tuxedos](https://goo.gl/ttz1ao), and [ties](https://goo.gl/TIMjcR) on. They can even take some pictures so you can choose your [favorite ensemble](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-hot-looks-for-every-groom-to-rock-in-style/) together. But if you're a brave bride, give your **groom** full freedom in choosing their looks and see how well they did on your big day!** What kind of tuxedo will your groom be wearing?**

**In what ways will your Groom be involved with wedding planning? **
