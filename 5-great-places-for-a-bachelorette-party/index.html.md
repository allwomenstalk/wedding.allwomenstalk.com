# 5 Great Places for a Bachelorette Party!

There are always great places in your own town to hit for a **bachelorette party**; the local bar, clubs, the spa, or even your maid of honor's hot tub! But if you're looking for a great place to make** lasting memories**, **see some sites**, **go wild**, or stay tame, then check out these 5 great places for your bachelorette party!

## 5. The Big Apple...

![The Big Apple...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/newyorkcity_407x272.jpg)

Photo:[PopArtUK.com](http://www.popartuk.com/photography/new-york/colourful-night-life-in-times-square-ph0237-poster.asp)

Everyone loves **New York** and you will too for your **"out on the town" ****bachelorette party**! [Shop](http://www.nyctourist.com/shopping_fifthave.htm), take in a show on [Broadway](https://www.broadway.com:443/shows/chicago/), have fun learning to [striptease](http://madamexnyc.comhttp//madamexnyc.com/m3/options.html), [dance with the drag show](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.nycdragshow.com/), and even tour the city ["Sex and the City style"](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.screentours.com/tour.php/satc/) to see all the **hot spots** where the [fabulous ladies](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-80s-pictures-from-sex-and-the-city-2/)made a name for themselves! Looks like **NYC** is the place to be for your last night as a** single lady**! What's your favorite **place to party** in [New York City](https://www.nycgo.com/) ?

## 4. HollyWood...

![HollyWood...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/hollywood_400x300.jpg)

Photo: [Hollywood-blog.net](http://www.hollywood-blog.net/category/news/)

If you're a bride-to-be who has always wanted to see the **West Coast **and [party like the stars](https://bilbr.com/10-best-paid-hollywood-actors-and-actresses/) then [Hollywood](https://latourist.com/index.php?page=hollywood) is the best place for your **"red carpet" bachelorette party**! Stroll down the [Hollywood Walk of Fame](https://hollywoodchamber.net/), Stand at the famous [Hollywood sign](https://latourist.com/index.php?page=hollywood-sign), and check out a **movie premiere** with all the stars at [Grauman's Chinese Theatre](https://manntheatres.com/404.cfm). Don't forget to dip your toes in the sands of the **California beaches **and take a little drive to nearby **Malibu** to [learn how to surf](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.surflessonswithkatie.com/surf-lessons.php)in the beautiful **Pacific ocean**. At night you and your best girls can hit all the [dance clubs](https://web.archive.org/web/http://hollywood.drais.net/html/)where you might see people like [Paris Hilton](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-facts-on-paris-hilton/), [Kelly Osbourne](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/osbourne/), [Fergie](https://allwomenstalk.com/17-celebrities-wearing-headbands/), [Justin Timberlake](https://allwomenstalk.com/12-celebrity-basketball-fans-couples-edition/), or even [Christina Aguilera](https://celebs.allwomenstalk.com/tag/christina-aguilera/)!

## 3. The Good Old South...

![The Good Old South...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/charleston_415x332.jpg)

Photo: [Destination360.com](http://www.destination360.com/north-america/us/south-carolina/charleston)

Not such a wild bride and looking for something a bit classier than hitting the strip club? Gather your ladies, pack your best hat and high heels and head to [Charleston, South Carolina](https://www.charlestoncvb.com/). [Relax](https://www.charlestoncvb.com/plan-your-trip/), enjoy the **southern charm**, chow down on some of the [best seafood](https://www.charlestoncvb.com/plan-your-trip/)you'll ever eat, explore the [Charleston Tea Plantation](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.charlestonteaplantation.com/), and [set sail at sunset](http://www.osasailing.com/12daysails/skipperedcharter.html) for a night on the water you'll always remember with your maid of honor and [bridesmaids](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-great-gift-ideas-for-your-bridesmaids/)!

## 2. Stay Hot in South Beach...

![Stay Hot in South Beach...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/miami-beach_410x279.jpg)

Photo: [Miamism.com](https://miamism.com/)

Tell your ladies that you love **fun in the sun** and they'll be sure to take you to [South Beach, Miami](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=mysobe.com) for your **"Hot Beach" bachelorette party**! In the afternoon tan on the [hottest beaches in Florida](https://nikkibeach.com/miami/events.php)and at night party until 5 am at one of the [hottest clubs](https://www.howstuffworks.com/?parent=tc-beaches-miami-beach-nightlife.htm+&%3Burl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.bedmiami.com) in South Beach that was inspired by the ladies of [Sex and the City](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0159206/)! Is there a club with your name on it in South Beach, Florida?

## 1. What Happens in Vegas...

![What Happens in Vegas...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/las_vegas_boulevard_410x277.jpg)

Photo: [TheEatenPath.com](http://theeatenpath.com/2008/04/16/las-vegas-day-one-the-lotus-and-the-lever/)

In [Vegas](https://allwomenstalk.com/top-10-las-vegas-wedding-ideas/) the whole world is right at your finger tips. [Play](https://www.visitlasvegas.com/) and [Win](https://www.visitlasvegas.com/) at the **world famous casinos**, sing along to your favorite [Beatles](https://www.thebeatles.com:443/) tunes as you watch the amazing **acrobatic performers** in [Cirque du Soleil's "Love"](https://mirage.mgmresorts.com/en/entertainment/the-beatles-love.html), see [Elvis](http://ww25.lvhilton.com/Play/Entertainment/Shimmer-Cabaret-Calendar/Trent-Carlini?subid1=20220717-2116-1493-b616-8170d16313bc) rock n roll forever, dance on top of the bar at [Coyote Ugly](https://newyorknewyork.mgmresorts.com/en/entertainment.html), and walk on air over the [Grand Canyon](http://www.grandcanyonskywalk.com/). Just don't run into any wild tigers or get locked out on the roof like the guys in [The Hangover](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1119646/)! Where do you like to gamble in Vegas?

Share your great place for a bachelorette party with us, we know there are more hot spots for a bride to get wild!
