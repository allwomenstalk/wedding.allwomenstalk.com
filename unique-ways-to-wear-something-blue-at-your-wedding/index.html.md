# 7 Unique Ways to Wear Something Blue at Your Wedding ...

There are many different ways to wear something blue at your wedding. You can be very creative with this old saying. There are a lot of ways to have fun with wearing something blue. Here are some different ideas on ways to wear something blue at your wedding.

## 1. Consider Blue Nails or Toenails

![Consider Blue Nails or Toenails](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/pv/glcfamyo_600x920.jpg)

One of the unique ways to wear something blue at your wedding is to sport a blue mani or pedi. If a blue manicure seems a bit to bold for you, stick with something classic, such as a simple French manicure, and go for the pedicure option. You could have full blue toenails or just blue tips. There are also a lot of pretty designs that you can have added to a pedicure. Ask your stylist what options there are.

## 2. Choose a Dress with Blue Detail

This is an option I chose to go with. It isn’t something that will be for everyone and that is okay. I wore a white dress with tiny blue flowers embroidered into it. It was a little something different that set the dress apart and made me fall in love with it. It is another option for wearing blue that you can consider.

## 3. Wear a Blue Garter

This is a very popular way to wear blue at your wedding. A blue garter is a way to wear blue but no one will be the wiser unless you allow your groom to take it off and toss it to his groomsmen. It is a way to wear blue that doesn’t interfere with the bridal look you have pictured, if that is what you wish. There are many options in garters. You can have a very basic garter or one that is elaborate and very frilly.

## 4. Wear Blue Panties

![Wear Blue Panties](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/z1/c14c12d3_600x600.jpg)

Wearing blue panties is a racy way to wear blue at your wedding. If anyone asks you if you are wearing blue, you can tell them yes but it is your little secret as to where. Again, this is a way to wear blue that won’t interfere with the all white bridal look many brides want. There is one little hint you should follow here. Be sure to try them on with your dress before the big day to make sure they will not show through.

## 5. Consider Blue Eye Makeup

![Consider Blue Eye Makeup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xr/kb/u96qp2t0_600x901.jpg)

Consider wearing blue eye makeup for your wedding. You don’t have to look like you stepped out of the '60s. There are many tasteful ways to wear blue eye makeup. You can go for a very pale icy blue eye shadow or even a navy eyeliner. Blue eye makeup worn in those two ways is actually very stylish.

## 6. A Piece of Blue Jewelry

Blue jewelry is an option. Some brides wear something blue in their jewelry, such as a bracelet or necklace. If you want it to be less obvious, you could choose to go with an ankle bracelet. If you don’t want something obvious such as bright blue, you can go with a lighter shade. Very pale blue is actually a color that is being worn a lot this year.

## 7. A Blue Hair Accessory

![A Blue Hair Accessory](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ei/ue/qo1pc2mf_600x744.jpg)

A blue hair accessory is another way to wear blue at your wedding. You could go for something very delicate, such as tiny blue jewels to be pinned in, if you don’t want something that calls a lot of attention to itself. If you are feeling braver, you may want to go with something like a blue flower or a veil with blue in it. There are many options you can choose from. You just have to find the one that feels right for you.

These are 7 ways you can wear blue at your wedding but there are certainly more. How do you plan to wear blue at your wedding? Please share your ideas!
