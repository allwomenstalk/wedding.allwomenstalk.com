# 6 Ways to Tickle Your Wedding Pink ...

Last week I talked about beautiful [pink peonies](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/peony-bouquets-to-brighten-up-your-wedding-day/)and not ever really being one of those blushing pink brides. Little girls love pink and dream of** pink details **and decor at their fairytale wedding, but I was never that girl. So for all those girly girls out there hoping your grooms don't crush your dream of a totally **pink wedding **here are 4 ways to tickle your wedding in your **favorite color**...

## 6. Pink Pinwheels...

![Pink Pinwheels...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/pinkpinwheels_456x684.jpg)

Price: $12.00 for 12 @ [Etsy.com/shop/ElizabethSt](https://goo.gl/4ep4ge)

I've been in love with the **pinwheel **trend ever since I saw them on [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/08/10/canadian-rockies-wedding-by-orange-girl-photographs/)and saw how whimsical yet classy they could be! With these adorable pink and black pinwheels you can be have playful bridesmaids carry them down the aisle or fun and bright centerpieces that guests can take home with them.

## 5. Pink Cherry Blossoms...

![Pink Cherry Blossoms...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/cherryblossom_456x545.jpg)

Price: $31.00 set of 15 @ [Etsy.com/shop/PoppySeedStation](https://goo.gl/NLSwsL)

If you're a **pink bride but don't want to cause a pink explosion at your wedding, decorate with subtle hints of pink like these beautiful **cherry blossom votive candle holders**. Sometimes the **little details **of your wedding make a much bigger statement than when you over power the look and theme. Let pink glow with** candle centerpieces, little flowers in your hair, or with a lovely pink sash around your waist and you'll impress your guests with your chic style.

## 4. A Pink Breeze...

![A Pink Breeze...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/fans_446x670.jpg)

Price: $18.00 set of 10 @ [BHLDN](https://goo.gl/oQYk9p)

Could you just imagine all of your guests fanning these **bright pink fans at your wedding as they keep themselves cool, your entire wedding would be** tickled pink! These brilliant fans are also a great way for your guests to have fun during the photo booth shots and be part of your pink celebration.

## 3. The Ring Please...

![The Ring Please...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/ring_456x342.jpg)

Price: $120.99 @ [Etsy.com/shop/Titaniumknights](https://goo.gl/dhhkhe)

Maybe your groom won't wear a pink tie, but perhaps you can convince him how handsome this** Titanium wedding band **is with the rose gold pinstripe! With this little hint on his band he'll remember that pink isn't so bad after all and you'll always remember how wonderful he was for helping you rock your **pink wedding **dreams!

## 2. Tickled Pink...

![Tickled Pink...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/poms_446x670.jpg)

Price: $32.00 set of 5 @ [BHLDN](https://goo.gl/DBzxVy)

Pink is soft, romantic, and fluffy like these gorgeous **paper poms**! I have seen these all over the place, especially handmade ones, and keep thinking how cool blue and yellow poms would have looked for our wedding decor. Now you can get them at [BHLDN](https://goo.gl/gMjI09), my favorite one stop [wedding shop](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/new-bridal-gowns-from-bhldn/)! I love the mix of tones and sizes, great for the cake table, hanging through out your reception, or tying them on ceremony chairs.

## 1. Pink Brides...

![Pink Brides...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/pinkheadband_427x640.jpg)

Price: $31.77 @ [Etsy.com/shop/SaraLaurence](https://goo.gl/kxQwrW)

Okay I have said this before, but is she not the most stunning pink bride! I just love when brides wearing their headbands like this, reminds me of the 1920's flapper style. This** pink beaded leaf headband **is just what every pretty in pink bride needs to complete her theme. It's classy, chic, and pink...so what's not to adore!
