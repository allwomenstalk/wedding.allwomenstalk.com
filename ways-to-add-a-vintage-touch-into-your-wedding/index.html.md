# 7 Ways to Add a Vintage Touch into Your Wedding ...

There are many ways you can add a vintage touch to your wedding. Don't worry if you are not sure how to go about doing that just yet. The truth is that there are many different ways you can do this. There is no right or wrong way to add a vintage touch to your wedding. Follow your own style and unique taste and it will be something you treasure about your wedding.

## 1. Choose a Vintage Style Dress

![Choose a Vintage Style Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hg/bd/yqlt0zs1_600x787.jpg)

One way to add a vintage touch to your wedding is to choose a dress that has vintage style. There is nothing as lovely as the feminine styles of days gone by. I could just lose myself in looking at vintage style wedding dresses. To wear one would simply be a dream come true. If you feel that way too then this may be the perfect way to add a vintage touch to your wedding.

## 2. Go for Vintage Makeup and Hair

![Go for Vintage Makeup and Hair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/fq/v2ya1ukj_594x828.jpg)

I also adore the styles of makeup and hair from times gone by. This look has a definite romanticism about it. My favorite time period for hair and makeup is the 1940s. Yours may be different. Whatever time period that captivates you could be a perfect way to add a vintage touch to your wedding.

## 3. Put on Some of Your Favorite Oldies

![Put on Some of Your Favorite Oldies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jb/cp/y495zmzn_600x792.jpg)

Don’t you just love oldies music? I have a playlist full of them. There are a lot of ways that oldies can be used in your wedding. You could choose a ballad to have your first dance to or a more upbeat tune that lends an air of fun. The only problem is that there are so many great oldies songs that it would be hard to choose.

## 4. Opt for Black and White Wedding Photos

![Opt for Black and White Wedding Photos](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/k2/m4u5amez_600x900.jpg)

Black and white photos capture vintage style perfectly. They give a vintage feel to your wedding photos instantly. This is a very easy way to pull vintage style into your wedding. Be sure to purchase a few pictures in black and white. You will not regret this decision.

## 5. Vintage Style Invitations

![Vintage Style Invitations](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j2/jc/ei9x3ajj_400x560.jpg)

You can also choose to kick off your wedding with vintage style. Send out vintage style invitations. This is a beautiful way to incorporate this style. It is fun and playful. Your guests will enjoy receiving them too.

## 6. Bring in Some Vintage Decor

![Bring in Some Vintage Decor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rk/y5/0a69gzya_600x901.jpg)

If you want to make a big impact, you can bring in some antique furniture for your wedding and reception. Additionally, you can choose decorations with a vintage feel to them. If you need inspiration, you can always Google vintage style wedding themes. Pinterest can help you out with this too. You can come across ideas you would have never thought of on your own.

## 7. Ride off in a Classic Ride

![Ride off in a Classic Ride](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/eh/u45i91nt_600x900.jpg)

A classic ride can be the finishing touch to a beautiful wedding. This is a surprise way you can add a bit of vintage style to your wedding. It is a small way to bring in vintage without going over the top. It is like the finishing touch. It also makes for some beautiful photo opportunities.

There are numerous ways that you can add a bit of vintage style to your wedding. You can use one idea or more than one. What do you plan to do to accomplish this?
