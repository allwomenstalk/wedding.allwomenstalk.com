# Real Wedding: Get Your Party on in New Jersey!

Sometimes you can tell when the **bride** put together the entire wedding and thought of every last detail including the colors, flowers, and venue. But this **fun**, **exciting**, **wild**, **gorgeous** **wedding** from [NylaGray Photography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/)makes me think the **groom** may have had some say in the **planning process**!

## 10. The Stunning Bride...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/bride_410x273.bmp)

Photo: [NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

Christina met her soon-to-be husband when they were both studying at [Rutgers University](https://www.rutgers.edu/)in** New Jersey**. My's best man, Frank, introduced them freshman year in 1999. But it wouldn't be until 2005 that they would start dating, and then five years later Christina** walked down the aisle** in a gorgeous [Rembo Styling](https://web.archive.org/web/https://rembo-styling.com/collection.htm)gown from [Kleinfeld Bridal](https://www.kleinfeldbridal.com/).

## 9. The Touching Ceremony...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/ceremony_412x138.bmp)

![The Touching Ceremony...](https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/ceremony2.jpg_410x137.bmp)

Photos:[NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

I love the photo of the guests crying and wiping their eyes. There is never a dry eye during a **wedding ceremony**. Christina and My look so sweet and romantic during their ceremony holding hands and exchanging rings. I just love these shots.

## 8. The Cool Dudes...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/cigars_410x274.bmp)

Photo: [NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

The **groom** and his **groomsmen** look like they are ready to start something...a wedding reception perhaps! They just look **too cool** with their purple ties, dapper tuxedos, and mob like **cigars**! This is a** great casual photo** of them strutting along, but also captures them all together without being too stuffy.

## 7. The Glamorous Ladies...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/ladies_412x275.bmp)

Photo: [NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

Christina's beautiful **MOH** and **bridesmaids** all look so fabulous in their beautiful **purple gowns**. I love that her MOH, Cristine, is wearing a darker purple than the other ladies, Gail, Pia, Loan, and Lori, who look lovely in their **lavender gowns**. How **glamorous** is this shot of them each linked into each other's arms!

## 6. The Party Props...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/guys_405x270.bmp)

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/radio_408x272.bmp)

**Party on dude!!** The **groomsmen** are ready to get the ceremony done so they can start the **reception** and pose for pictures in front of the **DIY photobooth!** Don't you just love when your bridal party helps out, even when it's blowing up your **party props**! How awesome is the** boombox radio** balloon prop!

## 5. Amazing Details...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/details_410x273.bmp)

Photo: [NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

Their **ceremony** and **reception** were held at the beautiful [Addison Park](http://theaddisonpark.com/)in Aberdeen, NJ. I love the beautiful **purple flowers** and **purple lighting** that looks remarkable above the **glowing chandelier. **The [Addison ballroom](http://theaddisonpark.com/)was the perfect venue for Christina and My's **modern glam celebration**!

## 4. Dance with Me...

![Dance with Me...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/addisonpark_17_410x273.jpg)

Photo: [NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

For their **reception** and **first dance** Christina changed into her beautiful [BCBG](https://goo.gl/d7wURt)dress. I love that both of her **wedding dresses** were** one shoulder**, she looks amazing in both of them.

## 3. Kiss Me...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/kiss_418x279.bmp)

Photo: [NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

There are always those special **kissing** moments during your **wedding** that you'll **remember forever.** I love how the **photographer** captured the **moment** right **before the kiss** in this beautiful photo of My and Christina. Their eyes are closed, they are both smiling, and they are ready to share that **newlywed kiss** and share a moment in the middle of all the fun and excitement!

## 2. Time to Party...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/party_418x556.bmp)

Photo:[NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

It's finally **time to party**! The ceremony is over, the first dance has been played, the cake is cut and now it's time to **rock n' roll**! I love how all of their **guests** got down on the **dance floor** and really had an amazing time. Who thought of the** glow sticks**? Genius! Check out the photos on [NylaGray's blog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/), there is a **glow stick limbo** going on! How funny is Christina's **bridesmaid** at the bottom right!

## 1. Smile for the Camera...

[img.allw.mn](https://web.archive.org/web/https://img.allw.mn/content/wedding/2011/02/funny_405x270.bmp)

Photo:[NylaGrayPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.nylagray.com/blog/2010/09/christina-my-wedding/)

She knows all the planning is finally done and she can now **relax** and **soak up all the glory**!
