# 8 Weddings and One Bride: a Tribute to Elizabeth Taylor ...

**Elizabeth Taylor**, as we all know, is a true beauty icon and will be missed by so many. Her life in Hollywood was always in front of the camera and that was no different when it came to her **wedding** day, her many wedding days. This is a tribute to Liz; her beauty, her life, and her many beautiful wedding gowns. May she live on forever.

## 8. Elizabeth & Larry...

![Elizabeth & Larry...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/elizabeth-taylor-and-larry-fortensky-25-million_420x315.jpg)

Photo: [BusinessInsider.com](https://www.businessinsider.com/most-expensive-weddings-2010-7?op=1)

In 1991 [Elizabeth Taylor](https://goo.gl/Tcbxsk)took her last husband, [Larry Fortensky](https://people.com/archive/cover-story-he-does-she-does-they-do-vol-36-no-15/). They were married on the Neverland ranch of her long time friend [Michael Jackson](https://www.michaeljackson.com/). Michael walked the beautiful bride down the aisle that fall day so she could wed her construction worker groom and live happily ever after....for five years at least.

## 7. Liz & John...

![Liz & John...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/lizandjohnwarner_400x393.jpg)

Photo: [IfitsHipitsHere.blogspot.com](https://ifitshipitshere.blogspot.com/2011/03/her-men-her-movies-her-diamonds-her.html)

With all of the many men Liz married you knew there had to be a **politician** in there some where. In 1976 **Elizabeth** married[John Warner](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Warner), a republican from Virginia who became Senator two years after their wedding. Their marriage lasted six years.
6 & 5. Elizabeth and Richard...

![Liz & John...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/0323-5-richard-burton-liz-taylor-wedding_we_422x439.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/story/the-many-wedding-dresses-of-el)

Not only did Elizabeth Taylor marry actor [Richard Burton](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000009/bio)once in 1964 for 10 years, but they married again after their first divorce, but sadly divorced again a year later in 1975. They costarred in many films together, including the expensive production of [Cleopatra](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0056937/), [Who's Afraid of Virginia Woolf](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0061184/)?, and [Taming of the Shrew](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0061407/).

## 4. Elizabeth & Eddie...

![Elizabeth & Eddie...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/0323-4-eddie-fisher-liz-taylor-wedding_we_422x471.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/story/the-many-wedding-dresses-of-el)

All of the men in the world were'nt good enough for Liz after her husband [Michael Todd](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0865239/)died in a plane crash. She went to his best friend, actor[Eddie Fisher](https://goo.gl/VbmOZj), for support and later love! At the time Eddie was married to Taylor's best friend, actress [Debbie Reynolds](https://goo.gl/LjbCZc), but that didn't seem to bother Liz. The two were married in Vegas after she converted to Judaism, but the marriage ended in divorce number three.

## 3. Liz & Michael Todd...

![Liz & Michael Todd...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/0323-3a-michael-todd-liz-taylor-wedding_we_427x343.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/story/the-many-wedding-dresses-of-el)

Ironically with her third husband, film producer [Michael Todd](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0865239/), Taylor had her third child. Todd and Taylor were married in 1957 with two ceremonies, one in Spain and another in Mexico. Her best friend, Debbie Reynolds and her soon to be forth husband (at the time Debbie's husband), Eddie Fisher, served in the wedding party!

## 2. Elizabeth & Michael Wilding...

![Elizabeth & Michael Wilding...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/0323-2-michael-wilding-liz-taylor-wedding_we_422x457.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/story/the-many-wedding-dresses-of-el)

Although her marriage to british actor [Michael Wilding](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0928697/)only lasted five years, he would give Taylor two very special loves of her life, that is her first two children. They were married in 1952 and divorced in 1957, only two years after their second son, Christopher, was born.

## 1. Her First Husband, Conrad Hilton...

![Her First Husband, Conrad Hilton...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/0323-1-liz-taylor-conrad-hilton_we_424x594.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/story/the-many-wedding-dresses-of-el)

For her very first marriage Taylor wed [Conrad Hilton Jr](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1260744/bio)., son of [Hilton Hotel](http://www1.hilton.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)founder [Conrad Hilton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conrad_Hilton). That means that at one time, before she was even born, [Paris Hilton's](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0385296/)great-aunt was Elizabeth Taylor! This marriage only showed signs of things to come as they divorced a little less than a year later in 1951.

She was a strong, beautiful woman who did many great things in her life, which included being the founder of the [Elizabeth Taylor AIDS Foundation](https://elizabethtayloraidsfoundation.org/). She will truly be missed.
