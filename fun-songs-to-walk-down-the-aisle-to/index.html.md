# 4 Fun Songs to Walk down the Aisle to ...

**Wedding songs **for the walk down the aisle have come a long way from the traditional slow tune our parents and grandparents played at their weddings. Many couples are now looking for fun pop and rock songs to **walk **and **dance down the aisle **to. The party now gets started right from the beginning! Here are a few of my favorite songs for weddings this year....is yours on this list?

## 1. Marry Me...

You can feel the words through your soul as you listen to the love in this sweet, romantic song. As you walk down the aisle to [Train's 'Marry Me'](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ghZt2cILcCU)your eyes will connect and your love will lift you high above the clouds. Wouldn't the words of this song be perfect to add into your vows! Every time I hear this song I think of our wedding day and smile.

## 2. I do...

There isn't a song of **Colbie Caillat's **that I haven't fallen in love with since first hearing her pop hit, "Bubbly", a few years ago. So when I saw the commerical on [TLC](https://www.tlc.com/)featuring her new song I wanted to download it right away so I could keep singing the fun lyrics! Dance down the aisle to her hit,["I Do"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E0oyglKjbFQ) that she sang in [Times Square](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ICFgFakU3Pw)for the [Royal Wedding](https://allwomenstalk.com/royal-wedding-the-kiss-and-more/)and your new man will say 'I Do' too!

## 3. Rhythm of Love...

For couples looking for a song that is less mushy and romantic, you'll find that [Plain White T's 'Rhythm of Love'](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JWiwuiT58Yc&amp%3Bfeature=relmfu) is a tune that everyone can rock to! It has a great beat, fun guitar chords, and lyrics that even the guys will approve of.

## 4. Marry You...

Get your bridesmaids to join in on the fun walk down the aisle as you and your ladies skip to the church bells in [Bruno Mars 'Marry You'](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DTL3cAt75Yk&amp%3Bfeature=related). This is a great song for any type of wedding, traditional or offbeat. I can see couples in Vegas getting hitched to this one!

**Will you walk down the aisle to something fun and upbeat?**
