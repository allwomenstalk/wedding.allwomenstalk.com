# 8 Gorgeous Glitter Wedding Dresses ...

What little girl doesn't love sparkly, sequined dresses and dream that one day they will walk down the aisle in a glitter wedding gown. I'm not talking about just beaded dresses, I'm talking about dresses that shine like a diamond! Check out these 8 Gorgeous Glitter Wedding Dresses to find your favorite...

## 1. Elie Saab Pink Glitter Wedding Dress...

_ Photo Credit: Capture Brides_

I love every dress from the Elie Saab 2012 Haute Couture collection, they are all filled with intricate feminine details and they sparkle! What girl wouldn't want a glitter wedding dress that is also pink! I love the way it shines and looks modest and sexy at the same time.

## 2. Elie Saab Short Glitter Wedding Dress...

![Elie Saab Short Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/eliesaabshortgown_554x827.jpg)

Photo Credit: [One Fab Day](https://onefabday.com/elie-saab-2013/)

What's better than a pink glitter Elie Saab gown, a short and sliver one! I can see a bride wearing this glitter wedding dress to her reception after wearing the longer version to the ceremony. It's fun, flirty, and great for dancing!

## 3. Pam Chorley Glitter Wedding Dress...

![Pam Chorley Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/glittergown_600x902.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Wedding Chicks](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/backless-sparkly-sequin-wedding-dress-l-6296-l-43.html)

Have you ever seen such a sparkly, glitter wedding dress before! I love brides who dare to be different and think outside the box for their wedding gown and details. You don't need to plan a big, city wedding to wear a dress like this either, this gorgeous bride had a small church wedding.

## 4. BHLDN Glitter Wedding Dress...

![BHLDN Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/BHLDN_554x832.jpg)

Photo Credit: [BHLDN](http://www.bhldn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Who knew that a glitter wedding dress could be so retro and stunning. This is one of my favorite gowns featured in the post, I love every detail and how vintage, 1920's chic it looks. The sequins are in all the right spots and they don't overwhelm the soft silhouette.

## 5. Sarah Seven Short Glitter Wedding Dress...

![Sarah Seven Short Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/sarahseven_553x830.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Sarah Seven](https://sarahseven.bigcartel.com/)

Another adorable short glitter wedding dress that I just had to include on my list. It's so sweet and girly. How cute would it look on your bridesmaids for a glitter wedding theme or on you for an elopement. If you are looking for a dress to change into for your wedding after party this could be the one!

## 6. Sarah Seven Glitter Wedding Dress...

![Sarah Seven Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/sarahsevenlonggown_481x724.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Sarah Seven](https://sarahseven.bigcartel.com/)

Now that you have a short glitter wedding dress for your reception or after party from Sarah Seven, you'll need one of her longer glitter gowns to wear to your ceremony! What's fun about all of these glitter dresses is that you can wear them on your big day and after to a New Year's Eve party!

## 7. Monique Lhuillier Glitter Wedding Dress...

![Monique Lhuillier Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/MoniqueLhuillierglittergown_550x942.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Wedding Chicks](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/designer-wedding-and-bridesmaid-dresses-from-wedding-shoppe-l-8561-l-36.html)

I love how this glitter wedding dress looks like it has confetti sewn right into the gown. If you are really looking to sparkle on your wedding day, this Monique Lhuillier gown is your girl.

## 8. A Bride's Design Glitter Wedding Dress...

![A Bride's Design Glitter Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/Abridesdesign_554x779.jpg)

Photo Credit: [100 Layer Cake](https://www.100layercake.com/blog/2012/11/13/ohio-lake-wedding-beth-tyler/)

You will certainly shine in this glitter wedding dress that has details that look like glass beads. I love the design the beads form on the bodice and how they pop up again on the top of the skirt line. Another retro beauty that brings me back to the days of the flapper girls!

I love that glitter wedding dresses come in so many colors, shapes, and silhouettes, making it easy for any bride to have her choice of glitter gowns. Whether you want to look like a princess, an offbeat bride, or a flapper bride from the 1920's, there is a glitter wedding dress for you! Would you wear one of these glitter wedding dresses, which is your favorite?
