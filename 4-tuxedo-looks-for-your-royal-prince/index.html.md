# 4 Tuxedo Looks for Your Royal Prince ...

Now that we've covered **Kate Middleton** and those of you looking for that princess ballgown, let's not forget about **Prince William** and what your royal prince might wear. William will most likely wear his [military uniform](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/femail/article-1352245/Like-father-like-son-Prince-William-wear-military-uniform-Royal-wedding.html) like many royal princes have worn in the past. However, some princes and royal fiances have worn very dapper tuxedos like [Princess Victoria's husband did](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-celebrities-who-tied-the-knot-in-2010/). Maybe your **groom** will look like a **royal prince** in one of these fine looking tuxedos...

## 4. A Royal White Tuxedo...

![A Royal White Tuxedo...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/tux3_418x596.jpg)

Photo: [MyTuxedoCatalog](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.mytuxedocatalog.com/tuxedos/C916-Ivory-Parisian-Tuxedo)

I can just imagine a handsome **prince groom** in this crisp, white [Jean Yves](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.jeanyvescatalog.com/)tuxedo walking down the aisle with his new princess wife. The sun is shining, the flowers are in bloom, and they both live **happily ever after**! Where would your groom wear this clean, formal look; in a **castle**, on the beach, or maybe in a **ballroom** at the [Plaza](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.theplaza.com/events/the-grand-ballroom/)hotel?

## 3. Very Formal & Fancy...

![Very Formal & Fancy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/tux5_424x605.jpg)

Photo: [MyTuxedoCatalog](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.mytuxedocatalog.com/tuxedos/C495-Black-Somerset-Cutaway-Tuxedo)

"Nice ascot"...can you tell me what movie that's from! [Ascot's](https://goo.gl/g6vy0p)are very popular in the UK, so why not make them popular at your **royal inspired wedding** when your groom dons this lovely **ascot tie** and [Ralph Lauren](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.ralphlauren.com/home/index.jsp?direct)tuxedo. I love the grey and black together and the way the stripes on the ascot match his tuxedo pants; a very sharp look your **prince charming** will love!

## 2. White & Black All over...

![White & Black All over...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/tux2_424x605.jpg)

Photo: [MyTuxedoCatalog](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.mytuxedocatalog.com/tuxedos/C943-White-Savoy-Tuxedo/)

Is this not a handsome, red carpet look! Perhaps you can combine your **royal wedding theme** with an **old** **Hollywood theme**, think [Casablanca](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0034583/)meets Prince William and Kate Middleton! It's a trendy look from [Jean Yves](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.jeanyvescatalog.com/) that will keep you and your Hollywood prince in the spotlight.

## 1. A Bow Tie for Your Guy...

![A Bow Tie for Your Guy...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/tux1_420x644.jpg)

Photo: [Shop.Nordstrom](https://goo.gl/J5m2GZ)

**Bow ties** have become a very big wedding trend for **grooms** of all styles and personalities. This rich looking **tuxedo** by [Hugo Boss](https://www.hugoboss.com/us/)is perfect for that **cool**, calm, and ready to party **prince**. He has a drink in one hand and a rose for his new princess in the other!
