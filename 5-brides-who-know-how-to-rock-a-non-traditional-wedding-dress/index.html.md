# 5 Brides Who Know How to Rock a Non\-Traditional Wedding Dress ...

Little girls dream of fluffy, long, big white ball gowns for that one **special day** when they walk down the aisle to say** 'I Do' **and live happily ever after. But sometimes when those little girls grow up their dreams change and so does the color, length, and idea of their wedding gown. See if you're wedding dreams change when you're inspired by these 5** **brides who know how to **rock a non-traditional wedding dress...**

## 5. Retro Inspired...

![Retro Inspired...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/vegas-wedding-photos023_405x608.jpg)

Photo: [ChrisSchmittBlog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://chrisschmittblog.com/2010/07/vegas-wedding-pictures/) via [RocknRollBride](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/amy-daylens-las-vegas-elvis-romance/)Dress:[Anthropologie](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.anthropologie.com/)

When you're married in [Vegas](https://www.visitlasvegas.com/)and tribute your wedding theme to the **King of** **Rock n Roll** you can't just wear any type of wedding dress! It has to be **retro**, hip, and cool like [Elvis](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.elvis.com/)himself; besides he will be your **officiant** as well! How romantic is this [sweet couple](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/amy-daylens-las-vegas-elvis-romance/)! They were married at the[Graceland Chapel](https://gracelandchapel.com/)in Las Vegas and held their reception at the [MGM Skyloft Suite](https://mgmgrand.mgmresorts.com/en/hotel/skylofts-mgm-grand.html). I love couples who aren't afraid to create **unique wedding memories! **

## 4. Mellow Yellow...

![Mellow Yellow...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/yellowbride_408x540.jpg)

Photo: [EclecticImagesBlog](https://eclecticimagesblog.com/)
Dress: Vintage

Being a [bride](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-amazing-offbeat-weddings/)doesn't mean you have to wear a white dress any more. When you're an** artistic couple** it shows in your wedding **details** and **theme**. Sarah is a [fine art photographer](https://www.sarahsudhoff.com/)and her new hubby, Gary, is a metal artist. Their love for **art and vintage** **decor** showed in her beautiful [yellow dress](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/01/24/san-antonio-wedding-at-southwest-school-of-art-craft-eclectic-images/), their[vintage cameras](https://eclecticimagesblog.com/)posted at the guestbook table, and their** homemade centerpieces** made by the groom himself!

## 3. The Colorful Bride...

![The Colorful Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/shortgown_400x600.jpg)

Photo: [RocknRollBride.com](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/carroll-dans-detail-heavy-day-of-the-dead-plantation-wedding/)
Dress: [Dress Forms Design Studio](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/carroll-dans-detail-heavy-day-of-the-dead-plantation-wedding/)

Carroll and her husband Dan wanted their day to truly be about who they were and about their loved ones living and passed. The bride was born in [Mexico](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.visitmexico.com/wb2/Visitmexico/Visi_Home)where they celebrate [Day of the Dead](https://www.inside-mexico.com/jose-guadalupe-posada-creator-of-la-catrina/), which is a **spiritual** and **colorful** way of remembering your family members, including Carroll's father. With [colorful decor](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/carroll-dans-detail-heavy-day-of-the-dead-plantation-wedding/), **skulls**, [DIY paper flowers](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-unique-wedding-bouquets-that-will-last-forever/), and **temporary tattoos** the bride, groom, and guests shared a wedding day they'll never forget.

## 2. Blue Moon...

![Blue Moon...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/bluedress_400x267.jpg)

Photo: [OffbeatBride](https://offbeatbride.com/monday-montage-blue-wedding/)
Dress: Vintage

When you're a [crafty bride](https://goo.gl/liI6kl)you know you're going to [celebrate](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-ways-to-mix-up-the-colors-for-your-wedding/)a **unique wedding** and wear a **one of a kind wedding dress**. For their [library reception](https://www.grandmaraislibrary.org/), Jessica wore a beautiful** blue polka-dot vintage dress** and changed into another blue vintage dress for their reception at the [Tarnish & Gold](https://tarnishandgold.wordpress.com/) art gallery. I love her **yellow crinoline**, which was made by [ReSashay](https://offbeatbride.com/), it looks perfect with her **blue dress**.

## 1. The Brides Wore Pink...

![The Brides Wore Pink...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/twobrides_407x271.jpg)

Photo: [TinyWaterBlog](https://web.archive.org/web/http://ww16.tinywaterblog.com/category/wedding/?sub1=20220718-0001-155f-8189-025ccdcc9951) via [RocknRollBride](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/kelly-danis-same-sex-diy-fire-dancing-wedding/)Dresses: [Siri](https://www.siriinc.com/)

I just love how these two beautiful brides **coordinated their **pink** and white **dresses** and **shoes**! Their feminine personality, love for the outdoors, and DIY skills were shared throughout their[special day](http://ww25.tinywaterblog.com/wedding/backyard-wedding-photograph/?subid1=20220718-0001-2385-accd-5746ca78a097)with family, friends, and [their dog](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-reasons-to-have-your-dog-in-your-wedding/)! Kelly's sister not only officiated the ceremony, but she also performed a[fire dance](https://www.rocknrollbride.com/2011/01/kelly-danis-same-sex-diy-fire-dancing-wedding/)for their one of a kind** reception at the [Highland Ranch](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.highlandranch.com/index.html)in California.
