# 7 Unique Ways to Announce Your Engagement ...

Are you searching for some unique ways to announce your engagement? These are some creative ideas that can help you out with that. Everyone wants the fun of announcing their engagement in their own personal way and that is understandable. Try one of these 7 unique ways to announce your engagement with fun and flair.

## 1. Have a Party with a Surprise Announcement

You can choose to have a party and make a surprise announcement. This is one of the ways to announce your engagement that allows you to be present when you do. You get the pleasure of seeing the look on everyone’s faces and seeing their surprise and happiness for you. You get to enjoy all of their happy congratulations, too. It is for this reason that many people choose this option.

## 2. With a Photo

Many people choose to announce their engagement with a photo. They may take a simple photo and mail it out to all of their friends and family. This may be an option that appeals to you if you don’t live near most of your friends and family. It is also a choice you might prefer if you don’t have time to do a big announcement or don’t want the hassle of it. It is also a fun and economical choice for announcing your engagement.

## 3. A Timeline of Your Relationship

A timeline of your relationship can be fun and very individualized. It is a way to share a peek into your relationship if you wish to do so. This is a good thing because it gets people excited about your upcoming wedding and the fact that they are going to get to be a part of it. It can be as simple or as fancy as you want to make it. You can simply print it on cardstock and mail it out after you decorate it as you like.

## 4. Announce It on Social Media

This one is simple and fast. If you want to get the word out but don’t want to spend time on any of these other options, post it on social media. People will know very quickly, most of them within hours. Social media can be both good and bad but one thing that is good about it is the ability to spread the word about something very quickly. You can still make a cute announcement on social media, too.

## 5. Create a Wedding Website and Send out Invites

Wedding websites are a fun way to keep people in the loop about your wedding. It allows them to feel included and have the scoop. They can enjoy your wedding website at their convenience and check back for new information whenever they wish to do so. After you create your wedding website, you can send out invitations to join. It is a definite way to send the message that a wedding is happening.

## 6. Person to Person

If you want to do so, you can announce your engagement from person to person. You don’t have to do one great big announcement to everyone you know all at the same time. You can simply choose to share your happy news when you see people you know and the time is right. This has the advantage that you get to have lots of little moments with all of those that you are close to. You get to enjoy taking in their congratulations one at a time and you can savor them.

## 7. A Quiz

If you really want to mess with people, send out an engagement announcement in the form of a quiz. It can feature questions about the two of you individually and then about the two of you as a couple. At the very end of the quiz, you can ask a question pertaining to who might be getting married soon or making them guess why a certain date is very important to both of you. This is a lot of fun for both you and them. People love receiving fun announcements like this in the mail.

These are 7 different and unique ways that you can choose to announce your engagement. How are you planning to announce yours? I would love to hear from you!
