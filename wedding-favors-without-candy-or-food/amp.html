Frequently Asked Questions

What are some unique non-food wedding favor ideas?
Oh, you're going to love this! Think along the lines of mini succulents, personalized candles, or even custom coasters. Your guests will have a sweet reminder of your special day, minus the calories.
Why should I choose non-edible wedding favors?
Great question! Non-edible favors tend to last longer and often become cherished keepsakes. Plus, they can add a personal touch that food sometimes can't match.
Are non-food wedding favors expensive?
They don’t have to be! You can find affordable options like DIY bookmarks, custom keychains, or even small photo frames. It's all about being creative and thoughtful.
Can I mix non-edible and edible favors?
Absolutely! A combo of both can be fantastic. Maybe pair a lovely scented candle with a small jar of local honey? Best of both worlds!
Any tips for making sure guests appreciate the non-food favors?
Yes! Think about what reflects your personalities as a couple. Personalized touches like initials or wedding date can make them extra special and meaningful to your guests.
