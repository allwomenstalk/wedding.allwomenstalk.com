# 8 Amazing Ideas for Wedding Invitations ...

A wedding is one of the most special occasions in everybody’s life. You can make your wedding even more special by adding your own touch to every aspect of it, including your wedding invitations. Here are some amazing ideas for wedding invitations that will make them truly special and memorable.

## 1. Draw Your Family Trees

![Draw Your Family Trees](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wi/lv/svu57y3954c8f5f4be326560110142_500x313.jpg)

Image source: [mlcassid](https://www.flickr.com/photos/48901172@N00/2720132628/sizes/m/in/photostream/)

A wedding is not only a union of two individuals but also of two families. Draw family tress for both yourself and your fiancé and show the two of you connecting. This is one of those amazing ideas for wedding invitations that will touch family members on both sides and have them beaming.

## 2. Write a Short Story

Everybody likes a story with a happy ending. Write a very short and witty story of how you and your fiancé met, fell in love and decided to tie the knot. This will have all invitees chuckling as well as feeling warm and mushy.

## 3. Make a Photo Collage

In these days of digital photography, it is hardly difficult to trace a story through photographs. Collect all photos of you and your fiancé and tell your love story with the help of pictures. This is one of the amazing ideas for wedding invitations where you can include your family and friends that have been instrumental in cementing your relationship.

## 4. Video Wedding Invitation

Image source: [vkontakte.ru](https://web.archive.org/web/https://m.vk.com/id51770104)

Create a video invitation for your family and friends and choose your own storyline. You can playact your love story and end with formally inviting them to your wedding. This invitation is as personal as it can get.

## 5. Write a Poem

This is one of the classic as well as amazing ideas for wedding invitations. Write a romantic and touching poem about your love story and end with inviting your family and friends to your wedding. Normally poems are exchanged between lovers and you can now extend the romance to all your loved ones by writing a poem for your wedding invitation.

## 6. Seasonal Wedding Invitations

Allude to the season you are getting married in and its romantic overtones in your wedding. Personalize it by writing about how getting married in that particular season makes you feel.

## 7. Destination Wedding Invitation

![Destination Wedding Invitation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/destination-wedding-invite-031_500x339.jpg)

Image source: [originalspaperart.com](https://www.originalspaperart.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/destination-wedding-invite-03.jpg)

If you are planning a destination wedding, add a touch of the exotic location in your wedding invitation. For instance, if you are planning a beach wedding put some glue on your invitation and sprinkle some sand on it.

## 8. Fairy Tale Wedding Invitation

Create a fairy tale wedding theme by adding a couple of wings to your wedding invitation and using the right words. Also, consider taking a picture of your favorite fairy tale where the prince marries the princess and use it as a background for your wedding invitation.

While these are amazing ideas for wedding invitations, I encourage you to let your imagination run wild. Get as creative as you can. The more personalized you can make your wedding invitation, the more appealing and special it will be to your guests.
