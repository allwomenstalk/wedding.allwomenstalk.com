# 5 Wedding Inspiration Ideas: a Colorful Valentines Day ...

**Valentine's Day** is about love, **passion**, and spending the time with someone who truly adores you on those other 364 days of the year! Whether you're celebrating a **wedding**, marriage, family, children, or just a wonderful day, remember to smile knowing that someone loves you. Be inspired by today and these 5 **wedding** **inspiration** ideas for a **colorful Valentine's Day**!

## 5. Love for Blue & Red...

![Love for Blue & Red...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/bk/nvghz2vu54c8f25a68b6f742850723_600x800.jpg)

This striking **bright red** and **blue** wedding cake inspired this lovely board that reminds me of a Sunday afternoon **country wedding**. [Mason jars](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-rustic-wedding-centerpiece-ideas/), old milk bottles, apples, and groomsmen in the woods, it can't get more country than that! Don't forget the **apple pie dessert bar** for your red and blue wedding.

## 4. Purple & Red Passion...

![Purple & Red Passion...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/it/y6/osysn40g54c8f25bde9dd212758904_600x800.jpg)

**Purple** and **red** wedding colors just fill my heart with **passion**. They are gorgeous colors on their own, but together they just grab your attention and make you think of **love**, **romance**, and **sunsets**. I just love the [groom's](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-awesome-cufflinks-for-your-groom/)red and purple socks; a man who wears purple is confident and **sexy**!

## 3. Red and Yellow Summer...

![Red and Yellow Summer...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/by/iuot38hm54c8f25d7a48f387425404_600x800.jpg)

**Summer time** is filled with fun colors like this **red** and **yellow** inspiration board. Give your **red wedding** some pop with yellow flowers, and [yellow sandals](http://www.zappos.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)under those beautiful[red bridesmaid dresses](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-colorful-bridesmaid-dresses-for-spring-summer/). If you're inspired by **Valentine's Day** but will be hosting your wedding in the summer include little **heart details** in your banners, favors, or even on your[red and yellow invitation](https://goo.gl/k6FGwp)!

## 2. Red & White All over...

![Red & White All over...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cb/sc/fucfofw554c8f25ee83ee194934635_600x800.jpg)

**Simple and Chic**. Little hints of **red** in your **white wedding** can make a stunning but simple statement. How amazing will your bridesmaids look as they wear beige colored dresses while carrying [red ranunculus bouquets](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.fiftyflowers.com/product/Red-Ranunculus-Flower_256.htm). I love those little ring bearers, how adorable are they in their red ties! Are you planning a white wedding with** red details**?

## 1. Green & Red Apples...

![Green & Red Apples...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/k9/af79o6l554c8f26072dcc954142474_600x800.jpg)

**Fall** is a great season for red weddings, especially when the palette is mixed with shades of green. I just love the thought of **red** and **green** apple centerpieces and [green moss decor](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-handmade-wedding-items-to-buy-on-etsy/). Start the fire, roast some marshmellows, and celebrate your **reception** at your grandpa's **barn **for the best fall wedding ever!
