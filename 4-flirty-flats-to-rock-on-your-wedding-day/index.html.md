# 4 Flirty Flats to Rock on Your Wedding Day ...

Most **brides** I've talked to love sexy high heels for their wedding day, but what about the brides who want to look **fun and flirty in flats**? I'm on your side when it comes to wearing flats on your wedding day, **comfort** is more my style! Thanks to Shawna at[Poppies & Sunshine](http://poppiesandsunshine.blogspot.com/2011/02/ruche-spring.html)I have found the adorable online bouqtiue [Ruche](http://www.shopruche.com/maintenance.html). Check out these 4 Flirty Flats to Rock on Your Wedding Day from their online store that will keep your feet sexy and comfortable!
