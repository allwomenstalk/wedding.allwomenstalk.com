# Nontraditional Dresses to Make You a One\-of\-a\-Kind Bride ...

There’s nothing wrong with being a nontraditional bride, even though there are plenty of TV shows and magazines that will tell you otherwise! If you just can’t see yourself fitting into that long, white dress mold and see yourself going elsewhere when it comes to the dress you wear on your big day, check out this list. I’ve compiled some of the craziest and coolest dresses so that you can have the best (and most stylish!) day ever!

![Short Beaded Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kg/qs/r2h8amjj563a0d7a87620400802039_400x600.jpg)

![Zac Posen Black Lace Wedding Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dw/xi/l2wfn6id563a0d7ae0165685931674_400x600.jpg)

## 5. White by Vera Wang Chiffon Dress

![White by Vera Wang Chiffon Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wv/k8/z39uaftq563a0d7b19265323826957_400x600.jpg)

This dress is absolutely beautiful no matter what color the dress is!

![Melissa Sweet 3D Cap Sleeve Wedding Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fk/i7/l161wsl2563a0d7b5f98f235810621_400x600.jpg)

## 7. White by Vera Wang Sequin Organza Dress

![White by Vera Wang Sequin Organza Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/ny/k8ohps0a563a0d7bbd40e849204696_400x568.jpg)
