# Engagement Shoot: Forever Friends ...

Sometimes when you see a **couple **enjoying a night out you have to guess at what their story may be and you make things up about how long they've been together and how well they really know each other. Not with this couple, not with this **love story **captured by[Frey Fotos](http://freyfotos85.blogspot.com/). When you look at Erin and Scott you can see their story in the way they look at each other; you can feel the friendship and the connection they share. A bond like theirs is one that will last forever....

This bridge here that the sweet couple are standing in front of is called["The Kissing Bridge"](https://www.woolwich.ca/Common/error.aspx?aspxerrorpath=/Common/pagenotfound.aspx)and is the last covered bridge that remains in Ontario, Canada. People from all over the world come to enjoy this historic site that was built in 1881. And now Erin and Scott will always have a piece of it to remember from their beautiful engagement shoot.
