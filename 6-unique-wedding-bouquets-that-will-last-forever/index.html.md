# 6 Unique Wedding Bouquets That Will Last Forever ...

Floral bouquets may become a thing of the past for brides who love to **make a statement or want to show off their quirky personalities. With a fun **button** and **brooch** **bouquet or a **sparkling pinecone** bouquet perfect for your walk down winter wonderland, all the single ladies will be sure to line up for the bouquet toss! To walk down the aisle in style check out these **6**** amazing wedding bouquets that will last forever**...

## 6. A Simple and Chic DIY Bouquet...

![A Simple and Chic DIY Bouquet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/11/pinecone_418x276.jpg)

** Photo**: [OnceWed.com](https://www.oncewed.com/diy/diy-pinecone-wedding-bouquet/)

The snow is falling, the lights are twinkling, and**[winter weddings](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-very-cool-winter-wedding-ideas/)** are rocking. If you're a bride still worrying about your **winter wedding** details this simple yet elegant **pinecone bouquet** is a stress free DIY project you can tackle. I love the **soft** and **romantic** feel the beautiful [lace handkerchief](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.thelaceshop.com/bridandgroom.html)adds to the **rustic** look of the **pinecones**. Are you walking down the aisle with an amazing DIY bouquet like this one?

## 1. Vintage Memories...

![Vintage Memories...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/11/broochbouquet_412x330.jpg)

** Photo**: [fancypantsweddings.com](http://www.fancypantsweddings.com/how-to-design-a-brooch-bouquet/)

**Traditions and memories** are a big part of many weddings. Memories of our grandparents or loved ones we may have lost and traditions that have been passed down for generations. Keep those [traditions](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-cultural-wedding-traditions-for-any-bride-and-groom/) and memories alive with [brooches](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-pretty-brooches-and-pins/) you may have collected from your grandma through the years in a **wonderful** **vintage** **brooch bouquet**. Thanks to Judy over at [Fancypantsweddings](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.fancypantsweddings.com/how-to-design-a-brooch-bouquet/) you won't have to spend a ton of money on a brooch bouquet, you can **make your own**! For my own wedding I pinned a few of my grandmother's brooches to the stem of my bouquet, but now I wonder if I could have made a beautiful bouquet like this one! **Do you have any vintage brooches that will be a part of your special day? **
