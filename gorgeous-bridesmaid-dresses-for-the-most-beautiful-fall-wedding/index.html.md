# 7 Gorgeous Bridesmaid Dresses for the Most Beautiful Fall Wedding ...

The best bridesmaid dresses for fall complement the season and your personal style. Jewel tones, thicker fabrics, and longer hems are all great choices for fall bridesmaid gowns, however anything goes. Here are my picks for gorgeous bridesmaid dresses for a beautiful fall wedding.

## 1. Alfred Sung in Style D535

![Alfred Sung in Style D535](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/08/1_alfred-sung-in-style-d535_599x789.jpg)

Alfred Sung has many gorgeous bridesmaid dresses for fall, but I'm particularly loving Style D535. The high neck with bow adds a level of sophistication which is perfect if your wedding is a black tie affair. The wide belt at the waist is slimming and adds great dimension to the dress. The full shirred skirt has pockets so your bridesmaids can keep extra powder and lipgloss handy for discreet touch ups before the ceremony and reception. This dress comes in over 30 colors so you can get it to match the colors you've picked out for the ceremony. You can also order this dress in cocktail length. You can purchase one at [bestbridalprices.com](https://www.lcoffers.com/index.htm?atid=content_image_039902000007004808&q=Dresses++Gowns) for under $200.

This satin and matte crepe dress comes with a satin sash as well as the peplum detailing. It's a great value at just $178.

## 4. Calvin Klein in Cap Sleeve Fit & Flare Dress

![Calvin Klein in Cap Sleeve Fit & Flare Dress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/08/4_calvin-klein-in-cap-sleeve-fit-flare-dress_350x537.jpg)

The V-neck bodice and A-line skirt make for a fun and slightly sexy gown. The fit of this dress looks great on nearly everyone! What's most fantastic about this dress is that you can order it on [nordstrom.com](http://www.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) where it is has been on sale for just $74!

## 5. J. Crew Bridal in Carson Dress in Silk Dupioni

![J. Crew Bridal in Carson Dress in Silk Dupioni](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/08/5_j-crew-bridal-in-carson-dress-in-silk-dupioni_418x418.jpg)

This silk dress by the J. Crew bridal line is truly special. The dress is made from the finest dupioni which is known for its unique lustre and textured finish that's caused by the silk fibers on the surface of the fabric. This irregular finish showcases the natural beauty and the exceptional quality of the silk. The sculpted side peplum design on the dress creates architectural interest. This dress would look great for any season but I think it looks especially great for fall. The gown comes in 6 different colors and you can preorder it now on [jcrew.com](https://goo.gl/Et7FH2).

## 6. BCBGMAXAZRIA in Barbara One-Shoulder Gown

![BCBGMAXAZRIA in Barbara One-Shoulder Gown](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/08/6_bcbgmaxazria-in-barbara-one-shoulder-gown_347x683.jpg)

This romantic dress is a gorgeous option for your bridesmaids if you are having a fall wedding. The one shoulder strap is a pretty pleated-bow that cascades into a ruffle. The dress has a front slit and comes in several different colors. The eye-catching design on this gown is perfect for making a statement at your ceremony. Your wedding party is guaranteed to look amazing in this dress. You can order them at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) for just under $300.

## 7. Ann Taylor in Silk Georgette Flower One Shoulder

![Ann Taylor in Silk Georgette Flower One Shoulder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/08/7_ann-taylor-in-silk-georgette-flower-one-shoulder_600x738.jpg)

Your bridesmaids will channel modern romance in this silk dress from Ann Taylor. The one shoulder has a gorgeous flower detail to compliment the smooth design of the rest of the dress.
