# 5 Peony Bouquets to Brighten up Your Wedding Day ...

There are a few lovely bushes in my yard filled with beautiful red buds that just seem to be taking their time blooming. I really wasn't sure until today what they were and was excited to find out they are peony bushes! I can't wait to bring them in and fill the house with their beauty. In honor of my excitement here are 5 wonderful** peony bouquets **to brighten up your **wedding day**...

## 5. Poppies & Peonies...

![Poppies & Peonies...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/poppiesandpeonies_403x504.jpg)

I never saw myself as a blushing [pink bride](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-hot-color-palette-trends-for-your-2011-wedding/)with pink flowers, pink bridesmaids, or pink decor. But if this is what a pink wedding looks like; a gorgeous **peony**, poppy, rose, ranunculus, and sweet pea bouquet then sign me up! This is what a **wedding bouquet **should look like.

## 4. Tropical Paradise...

![Tropical Paradise...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/tropicalpeonies_403x504.jpg)

Brides who want **color **don't hide from peonies because you only think of the pale pink, yellow, and white tones. Look how amazing this **bright red peony**, philodendron, euphorbias, passionflower vine, and croton leaf bouquet shines. Whether you're getting married in [Hawaii](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/a-honeymoon-cruise-to-hawaii-guest-blogger-bicoastal-bride/)or just want that **tropical feeling**, let peonies transform your big day.

## 3. A Rustic Bouquet...

![A Rustic Bouquet...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/treepeonies_403x504.jpg)

**Peony bouquets **can fit into any [wedding theme](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-rustic-wedding-centerpiece-ideas/)with just a little touch of leaves, accessories and a few other elements. These bold, burgundy Japanese maple leaves turn these romantic pink peonies into a **rustic bouquet **in just one look, imagine what you could do with some [succulents](https://goo.gl/yf2UUE)and burlap!

## 2. On the Beach...

![On the Beach...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/beachpeony_403x504.jpg)

From the rustic barn weddings to the sweet sands of Mexico, peonies are a wonderful flower for any setting. I love the pale, soft colors of this **beach inspired **peony, cybister amaryllis, and astilbe bouquet. With [sandals](https://allwomenstalk.com/beautiful-sandals-for-the-beach-bride/)on your feet, seashells scattered down the aisle, and earth tone **peonies **filling your bouquet you'll be one beautiful **beach bride**.

## 1. Sweet & Simple...

![Sweet & Simple...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/pinkpeonies_403x504.jpg)

Yes **peonies **can be tropical, rustic, and beachy, but they can also be** sweet and simple **as shown in this lovely pink and peach bouquet from [Martha Stewart](https://www.marthastewart.com/6417/weddings). I love the mixture of closed and open peony blooms, which do you perfer?

**Have you found the right peony bouquet to brighten up your wedding day?**
