# 4 Amazing Wedding Shots You Need to See!

We browse **wedding blogs and** magazines hoping to find the **inspiration** we need for own wedding day. Sometimes it's the little details like the flower in her hair, or the rustic tin can centerpiece that sparks our imagination. Other times it's the **big picture** that grabs our attention, like these** 4 amazing wedding shots** from [JPG Photography](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.jpgphotovideo.com/) that just took my breath away...

## 4. There's a Shark in the Water...

OK so they're not in the water with the **sharks**, but you have to admit this **wedding shot** is pretty **amazing**! The [JPG photographer](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.jpgphotovideo.com/)captured this breathtaking shot at the[NJ Adventure Aquarium](https://www.adventureaquarium.com/) at the perfect moment. The bride and groom will always remember their [unique venue](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-tips-for-the-not-so-typical-bride/) and the wonderful memories they shared. Not everyone can say they **kissed** in front of four **sharks** on their **wedding day**!

When I saw this **amazing photo** it took me back to days of **imagination and** wonder. **Does it not remind you of the beautiful movie and book,["The Secret Garden"](https://goo.gl/U3jUbf)? This is a [secret garden](https://www.bbg.org/) with a **secret moment** shared between the bride and groom **on one of the [happiest days](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/4-ways-to-be-inspired-by-wedding-balloons/) of their lives.
