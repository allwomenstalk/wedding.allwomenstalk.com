# 8 Wedding Dress Ideas for Jennifer Aniston ...

I am so excited for Jennifer Aniston and her boyfriend Justin Theroux, the two recently became engaged on his 41st birthday on August 10th. We all know too well how much Aniston has been through and I just wish her a lifetime of love and happiness with someone who will give her the love she deserves. Okay, so enough about that on to 10 Wedding Dress Ideas for Jennifer Aniston, whose style I couldn't love more....

## 1. Nicole Miller V-Neck Wedding Dress...

![Nicole Miller V-Neck Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/09/7231781_480x736.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Nordstrom](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

Jennifer Aniston's style is sexy chic yet relaxing and effortless. She doesn't seem to be a bride who would wear ruffles, lots of sparkle, or a full tulle ballgown. When you look at her red carpet gowns they are simple and not too over the top, but they are also fitted to show off her amazing body and curves. I love this Nicole Miller V-neck wedding dress for her because of it's criss-cross bodice and how it just drapes beautifully to the floor. She could wear this with her signature style of always wearing her hair down or pull it back into a romantic, low do full of soft curls.

## 2. Wtoo Floral Lace Wedding Dress...

_ Photo Credit: Watters_

This gorgeous Wtoo floral lace and tulle gown is a wedding dress I would love, love, love to see Jennifer Aniston walk down the aisle in. It has a stunning sweetheart neckline and a fitted bodice that would hug Aniston's curves in all the right places. It's a dress everyone would be talking about and one brides would be inspired by.

## 3. J.Crew Grecian Inspired Wedding Dress...

_ Photo Credit: [Jcrew](https://goo.gl/EzuyQY)

Aniston actually wore a similar dress_like this J.crew Grecian inspired gown to the London premiere of Marley and Me in 2009 and looked chic as always. I can picture her walking down the aisle on the beach in this flowy, silk chiffon wedding dress with her hair down and matching Grecian inspired sandals. But I wonder if her fiance Justin Theroux would rock beach wedding attire as well!

## 4. BHLDN Sheer Neckline Wedding Dress...

![BHLDN Sheer Neckline Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/09/bhldngown_465x350.jpg)

Photo Credit: [BHLDN](http://www.bhldn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

The sweet little details are sometimes the memories guests remember and enjoy the most about a big, crazy wedding day. This is why I love the little details of the sheer neckline on this breathtaking BHLDN wedding dress, it's not too dramatic but you take notice. I could see Jennifer Aniston wearing this as she walks down the aisle in a chic city loft that has big windows overlooking NYC.

## 5. BHLDN Draped Goddess Wedding Dress...

![BHLDN Draped Goddess Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/09/bhldn_465x350.jpg)

Photo Credit: [BHLDN](http://www.bhldn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

The draping and ruching on this goddess style wedding dress is incredible. I could see Jennifer Aniston wearing this on her wedding day with a side braid in her hair as she carries a bouquet of baby's breath. This gown is sophisticated yet simple like most of Aniston's red carpet looks.

## 6. Little White Wedding Dress...

![Little White Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/09/7003613_456x700.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Nordstrom](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

I don't know about you, but I just might love this little white dress by JS Collection more than any of these other gowns for Jennifer Aniston. I can see her rocking this and saying 'I Do' at city hall or even for a little backyard wedding. Aniston seems to be low key and doesn't really make a scene too much and I see her big day being the same way; small and low key.

## 7. J.Crew Lace Fit & Flare Wedding Dress...

![J.Crew Lace Fit & Flare Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/09/jcrewlace_438x438.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Jcrew](https://goo.gl/3HmnCd)

This J.crew lace fit & flare wedding dress is effortless beauty at its best. Most of the time Jennifer Aniston's red carpet looks seem to be as easy as rolling out of bed, but if anyone can make that look sexy it would be her. That's why this dress might be the one for her; she could just slip this romantic gown on, throw on a long pearl necklace, put on a pair of dancing shoes, and she's all set to walk down the aisle.

## 8. Nicole Miller Trumpet Wedding Dress...

![Nicole Miller Trumpet Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/09/7026190_480x736.jpg)

Photo Credit: [Nordstrom](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

If you are a bride who is looking for a simple gown to play up with accessories, this is the perfect wedding dress for you. I can see Aniston being this bride as well with a plain yet elegant gown like this one and dressing it up with a sparkly or floral belt. You don't need many details when you have an amazing body and personality like Jen does.

Jennifer Aniston may just surprise us all and walk down the aisle in a white pant suit, but whatever she decides to wear she will no doubt look amazing. I wonder if she is searching on the internet for wedding dress ideas that would be perfect for her big day? If she is I hope she likes the ones I have chosen for her and I hope you have been inspired by these wedding dress ideas for your own day as well. Do you think any of these wedding dresses will be the style we may see on Jennifer Aniston for her wedding day?
