# Holiday Weddings That'll Inspire You ...

Weddings are my absolute favorite! I'm that guest sitting in the aisle, bawling my eyes out! I love going on #pinterest and seeing all the awesome wedding themes! Even though I'm already married, I still pin a ton of wedding ideas. Here are some awesome holiday weddings to inspire the newly engaged!

## 1. NATURE/ELEGANCE/WINTER

This holiday atmosphere is simply divine! I LOVE the natural ambience of the plants and the chandelier!

## 2. CRANBERRY RED HOLIDAY WEDDING

This subtle color gives a nice pop, but it's timid enough so that everything flows beautifully!

## 3. HOLIDAY ACCENT PIECES

This holiday accent piece is super cute! I think that this screams holiday!

## 4. WINTER WONDERLAND

This Winter Wonderland is just beautiful, and although I'm not a fan of the cold, this would just be gorgeous to have a setting in!

## 5. HALLOWEEN INSPIRED

This Halloween-inspired wedding isn't scary, but it screams fall and it's dainty and elegant!

## 6. HALLOW'S EVE

The pumpkins and the floral arrangements are all perfectly tied into the fall Halloween theme!

## 7. CHRISTMAS WEDDING DECOR

This color scheme and the gold accents tie in the Christmas colors smoothly! I think it is super festive!

## 8. NAVY and GOLD

This navy and gold color scheme is also perfect for the holidays. The colors suit a variety of different celebrations!

## 9. BLACK, GOLD, WHITE

These colors scream fancy but, they're perfectly in unison for the holidays as well!

## 10. GOLD LEAF

The gold leaf is perfect for the holidays and ties in a rustic holiday feel!
