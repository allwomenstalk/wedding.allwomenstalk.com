# 5 Fantastic Potted Plant Wedding Ideas ...

Ever since I saw those rustic and simple [potted plants](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-creative-styles-for-your-wedding-aisle/)lining an outdoor wedding aisle I knew I had to find more wedding ideas that featured them. As I found out they don't always have to be rustic, but they are mostly simple! Potted plants can be romantic, fun, chic, and beautiful. Maybe one of these 5 Fantastic Potted Plant Wedding Ideas is the right decor for your big day...

## 5. Romantic Silver & Pink...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/plantedhyacinths_402x402.jpg)

Photo: [Brides](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.brides.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-flowers/editorial-pick/2006/01/potted-beauty)

I love the colors and tones of this big silver tin with the potted pink hyacinths. These lovely plants always remind me of Spring when they used to pop up in my parents' garden around Easter. The silver and pink palette are very sweet and romantic for a daytime [garden theme wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-rustic-wedding-centerpiece-ideas/)or perhaps for a bridal shower.

## 4. DIY Tags...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/chinesepot_410x326.jpg)

Photo: [ProjectWedding](https://www.weddingwire.com/projectwedding-is-now-weddingwire)

When I first saw these adorable potted zinnias I thought they were in cute [Chinese take-out boxes](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.save-on-crafts.com/chinesetakeout.html)! That wouldn't be a bad idea either, but these colorful plants are just wrapped in [Kraft paper](https://goo.gl/zOjqRg), and tied with some string. The idea I really love is the** personalized tag** with your guests' name and table number. A great idea as an escort card for guests to take home as favors.

## 3. Mix It up...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/plantedgeraniumsherbs_414x517.jpg)

Photo:[MarthaStewartWeddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/7890910/laura-and-ben-brooklyn-new-york)

What I really love about** potted plant centerpieces** is when they are home **grown just for the big day. It's more than just a centerpiece, a flower, or plant, it's the **love and care that the couple put into their potted plants just for the wedding. Here[bride and groom](https://wrenhandmade.typepad.com/wren_handmade/2009/06/the-wait-is-finally-over.html)Laura and Ben, and family members grew the herbs, geraniums, and other plants and hand-painted the pots with love! The lovely bride handmade many of their wedding items and has her own [Etsy](https://goo.gl/OJZTdw)store as well.

## 2. Potted Favors...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/pottedfavors_415x415.jpg)

Photo: [Beau-coup](https://goo.gl/81fOqW)

Say** "Thank You"** with these wonderful little pots from [Beau-coup](https://goo.gl/9QOQj7). I love the sweet little saying on each and the painted flowers around the rim. Plant whatever you like from tulip bulbs to succulents. No matter what's inside these are sure to put a smile on your guests' faces as a favor that can be enjoyed in their gardens as they remember your beautiful wedding day.

## 1. Vintage Tins...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/planttins_411x273.jpg)

Photo: [Lemiga Events](http://lemiga.blogspot.com/2009/10/courtney-doug-wedding-recap.html)

Based on your venue, whether it's rustic, simple, elegant and formal, or chic and modern, find pots, tins, or bowls that match well with the setting and decor. These vintage tins and lovely colors of the plants suit the modern details of the [Studio 887](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.boldamerican.com/studio887/)event space. I love the way the tins and potted plants stand out on their own as unique and creative centerpieces but don't overtake the space.

Have you found a love for potted plants for your wedding decor?
