# 9 Fabulous & Inspiring Wedding Songs of 2011 ...

Wedding Songs for 2011 are here! If you are looking for your perfect wedding song but haven't been able to find it yet, don't worry! I've got the top 9 wedding songs of 2011 that will blow your mind a bit. I know that when I get married – there are a few wedding songs of 2011 that will be played at my wedding – no matter when it is!

## 1. Marry Me by Train

I never liked Train much until I heard 'Hey Soul Sister' – this song though … it's awesome! Out of every list that I took a look at for this post, this was always number 1 on the wedding songs for 2011 chart toppers. After listening to it, I have to say that this might be my wedding song! It's so touching, the lyrics are awesome and … it's beautiful.

## 2. Marry You by Bruno Mars

Bruno Mars is the guy to have at your wedding this year! I have to admit though, I didn't hear this song until it was covered on Glee. Now though? I love Mr. Mars and I love this song! It's totally playful, the perfect song to have at a reception and to dance to!

## 3. Rhythm of Love by Plain White T's

Picking out a wedding song is hard, especially if you have no clue what type of song you are looking for! This song for me was a no brainer because I love the Plain White T's. It's sweet, it's cute and I love the tune it has. It's the perfect song for a quirky and fun couple!

## 4. Love Story by Taylor Swift

I think that Taylor is absolutely beautiful. She's so young, has an incredible voice and her lyrics are awesome! Love Story is one of the first wedding songs of 2011 that I heard and instantly, I loved it. It's fresh, young, has a possession to it and it's got Romeo & Juliet in it. What more could you ask for out of a wedding song?

## 5. Mine by Taylor Swift

Taylor has done it again! Mine is off of her new album and it's definitely at the top of my wedding songs for 2011 list! It could be a reception song or a song to walk down the aisle to. Personally, I love the meaning to it – two kids taking on the world together and in the video, you get to watch their lives unravel. It's beautiful, sweet and makes me cry every time I listen to it!

## 6. You & Me by Dave Matthews Band

The BH actually loved Dave Matthews and while I've never been the hugest fan, after hearing this song – I may be converted. It's fresh, his voice is beautiful and it gave me chills! This is one of the many wedding songs of 2011 that I can definitely relate to in so many different ways.

## 7. Everything by Michael Buble

Michael Buble has always been one of the few artists that knows all of the right lyrics and knows how to play a girl just the right way. 'Everything' is definitely one of the wedding songs of 2011 that I'll have at my wedding! What do you ladies think? Would you like to have Michael Buble's songs at your wedding?

## 8. Just the Way You Are by Bruno Mars

Most women are just looking for that man that wants them, just the way that they are. Bruno Mars gave it up in a song – he loves his woman just the way she is. On your wedding day, your man is going to love you no matter what you look like, no matter what happens – when he sees your face, there isn't going to be a thing he would change!

## 9. I'm Yours by the Script

Not all wedding songs of 2011 have to be extremely poppy and fun, there are songs out there that really dig deep and express exactly how you feel about your new spouse. That is what this song does for me. I've always been a fan of The Script, but this song gets me every. single. time. It's beautiful. No other words can describe it.

So there you have it brides! Those are my top 9 inspiring wedding songs of 2011, that should definitely show up in any reception. For me, The Script, Taylor Swift and Bruno Mars steal the cake every time. What about you? What wedding songs of 2011 are your favorite?
