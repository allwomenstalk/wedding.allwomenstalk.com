# 10 Gorgeous Hairstyles for Your Summer Wedding ...

The Summer months can be hot and sticky depending on where you live and if planning a wedding isn't stressful enough, you don't want to have to worry about your hair on top of everything else. **Hairstyles for Summer Weddings** should be easy, loose, flowing, or pulled back into a simple but chic updo. Summer has that beachy, carefree feel and so should your hairstyle. Check out these** 10 Gorgeous Hairstyles for Your Summer Wedding** and choose the perfect one for you...

## 1. High Bun Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

The look of a high bun **hairstyle for your summer wedding** can be chic with a vintage feel like this beautiful bride's effortless loose bun or it can have a classy look to it if you pull your hair together more tightly. I love the loose, high bun hairstyle for summer weddings because it has that easy, beachy feel to it.

## 2. Front Braid Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

Braids are everywhere this year; on the red carpet, magazine covers, runway shows, and are lovely **hairstyles for summer weddings** with their versatile look. With a gorgeous front braid you still have plenty of hairstyle options for the rest of your hair as well as having beautiful details in the front to complete your look.

## 3. Boho Chic Summer Wedding Hairstlye...

Short, sweet, sassy, and simple; this boho chic **hairstyle for summer weddings** couldn't get any cuter or easier. Short hair is the perfect hairstyle for a summer wedding, when it's hot and sticky out you won't have to worry about your hair being a problem. All you need is an adorable headband and you're set to walk down the aisle!

## 4. Messy Updo Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

Messy, carefree hairstyles are trending more than ever and I can see why when they look as pretty as this messy updo does. With a few bobby pins, a headband, and a few loose strands of hair, your **summer wedding hairstyle** will be the best DIY of the day! Now you won't have to worry about going over your budget to pay for a hairstylist!

## 5. Loose Braid Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

Another way to do a lovely braided **hairstyle for your summer wedding** is to have it loose and over to the side for a sweet, feminine look. Add romantic florals to your wedding hairstyle to complete the look of your summer garden theme, or just to bring that bright, cheerful summer feel that you love so much to your big day.

## 6. Wavy Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

I love that this bride's hair is wavy and sun kissed, it's the perfect beachy **hairstyle for summer weddings**. While you're on the beach, the ocean breeze will flow through your wavy hair and help to create magical photos as you walk together in the sand with a beautiful sunset in the background.

## 7. Butterfly Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

Float down the aisle this summer with butterflies in your hair for a unique and one of a kind hairstyle that will put a smile on your groom and guests' faces. If you are looking for a gorgeous, no veil **hairstyle for your summer wedding**, hair pieces like these butterflies as well as big flowers, feathers, and pretty bows, are a great alternative . I love how hair accessories like these can help add a sassy detail to a simple look.

## 8. Braided Bun Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

You love the high bun look but want a braid in your hair too, so why not have both like this stunning bride did with her **summer wedding hairstyle**! I love how the braid is styled around the bun to look like a crown for an elegant hairstyle. I can picture delicate wildflowers with this hairstyle too for a more boho look.

## 9. Rockstar Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

All brides are rockstars, but why not go the extra mile to really shine with a rockstar **hairstyle for your summer wedding**! Instead of the party being in the back, with this kick ass coif there is a party in the front complete with smokey eyes and red lip. Your makeup should always compliment your wedding hairstyle; whether it's a rockstar look like this or a much softer, beachy look, the tones and color of your makeup should work with and not against your hair.

## 10. Ribbon Summer Wedding Hairstyle...

Summer is a time to be playful and let loose, which is why **hairstyles for summer weddings** are inspired by the season itself like this nautical ribbon accessory that is twisted through a loose braid. It's a playful look yet chic and sophisticated with soft bangs and blush colored makeup that help pull the look together.

Whether you have short and sassy hair, long and wavy hair, or naturally curly hair, there is a simple yet chic **hairstyle for your summer wedding** that will keep you feeling carefree on that hot and sticky day. Let your **hairstyle for your summer wedding** be one of the easy details of the day while you pull on the spanx, step into the sparkly high heels, and try not to make those ugly crying faces! **Hairstyles for summer weddings** are no longer about big curls, tons of hairspray, or tight updos, they are all about loose braids, soft waves, crazy coifs, and messy updos that keep you looking and feeling like the worry free bride that you are!
