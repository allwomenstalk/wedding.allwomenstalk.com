# 5 Creative Ways to save the Date ...

You just got **engaged** and now you're ready to **set the big date **and start **planning**. But how do you let your guests know so they'll remember when your big day is? Maybe one of these **5 creative ways to save the date** will remind your **guests** to take off from work!

## 5. Put the Pieces Together...

![Put the Pieces Together...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/blocksstd_408x308.jpg)

Photo: [TheLoveliestDay.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://ww16.theloveliestday.com/2011/01/save-date-puzzle-by-luna-and-chloe.html?sub1=20220717-2209-4973-a2fb-1a9b22b8a1ba)

**Fun**, **Creative**, and **Adorable**! I love this **puzzle block save the date** made by Kristi at [Luna and Chloe Weddings](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.lunaandchloeweddings.com/). You can either **tie these together** so your guests will see the [save the date](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-retro-ways-to-have-a-mad-men-inspired-wedding/) wording or leave them apart to let your guests put the **puzzle** pieces together and **make a game of it**! Check out her guest post on [TheLoveliestDay](https://web.archive.org/web/http://ww38.theloveliestday.com/2011/01/save-date-puzzle-by-luna-and-chloe.html) for more info on her** creative DIY idea**.

## 4. Post a Note...

![Post a Note...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/notepadSTD_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7948023/creative-save-dates)

Everyone loves [post-it notes](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.post-it.com/wps/portal/3M/en_US/Post_It/Global/)for their desk or office so why not give your guests a **big reminder** with this **cool save the date note block!** Download the clipart from [MarthaStewartWeddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/7948023/creative-save-dates)and send it over to [ArtPromos.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.artpromos.com/detail.asp?id=603&deptid=108) to order your **personalized** note block. Don't forget to write a little message on the top for your guests to read!

## 3. Open Your Heart...

![Open Your Heart...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/heartSTD_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings.com](https://www.marthastewart.com/7948023/creative-save-dates)

This **little heart** is more than just a postcard or an invitation. This little heart is a creative way for your **guests** to open up a **sweet little note** about your wedding details for when they should **save the date**. How wonderful is this idea for a [Valentine's Wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-romantic-ideas-for-a-valentines-day-wedding/) or a ["Two Hearts as One"](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.thefavourshoppe.com/heart-inspired-favours) themed wedding! See [MarthaStewartWeddings](https://www.marthastewart.com/7911895/folded-heart-save-date-how-and-clip-art) for the DIY instructions.

## 2. Message in a Bottle...

![Message in a Bottle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/savethedatebottle_402x533.jpg)

Photo: [Etsy.com/shop/seasideinvitations](https://goo.gl/gv6blu)

Your guests will never leave the **beach** when they receive this spectacular **message in a bottle **save the date. Perfect for an [island destination wedding](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.islandbrides.com/), [a beach theme](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-beach-weddings-to-inspire-your-summer-wedding/), or for **couples who live on the coast**. All your guests will be sure to remember your wedding date as they smell the s**ummer breeze **fresh from the bottle!

## 1. Spell It out...

![Spell It out...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/crosswordsavethedate_404x270.jpg)

Photo: [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/story/a-super-creative-save-the-date)

Could you imagine getting this clever** crossword save the date** in the mail? I would love to be **invited** to this **wedding** just to have fun figuring out who's [wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-real-brides-that-will-catch-your-eye/) it actually is! [Engagement Chick](https://www.glamour.com/contributor/engagement-chick) over at the wedding section of [Glamour.com](https://www.glamour.com/?us_site=y)had these made by her friend, web designer [Kelsey Rahn](http://www.kelseyrahn.com/index.html/), for her own wedding!
