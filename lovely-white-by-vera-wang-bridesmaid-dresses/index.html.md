# 7 Lovely White by Vera Wang Bridesmaid Dresses ...

[Vera Wang](https://www.verawang.com/)has always been a top name in the **bridal industry**, especially when it comes to wedding gowns. As most of us wedding crazed people know, earlier this year Vera introduced her new bridal gown line at [David's Bridal](https://goo.gl/fWyXp6)for the brides who were on a budget but still wanted something couture. Well now the time has come for the **bridesmaids** to step into the couture spotlight with the new [White by Vera Wang Bridesmaid line](https://goo.gl/k1DoDK)...here are the **7 lovely bridesmaids dresses**, which one is your favorite?

## 7. Short Draped Satin Dress...

![Short Draped Satin Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/07/verabm5_459x612.jpg)

Price: $158.00 @ [DavidsBridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

It's short, sweet, and sassy! This lovely **draped satin dress** by [Vera Wang](https://www.verawang.com/)comes in eight different colors at [David's Bridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), I believe this one is shown in the very pretty blush shade. The dress does come with a sash but not the horsehair one shown, which can be purchased for $58.00 at[David's Bridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)as well. Have you checked out her shoes yet? Those are new to the[White collection](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)by **Vera Wang** too!

## 6. Tiered Crinkle Chiffon Dress...

I've always loved the look of tiered dresses, but this is the first time I am seeing them for a **bridesmaid and I love it! This** tiered crinkle chiffon dress is perfect for so many themes, from classy and elegant to simple and relaxed. [Vera Wang](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/9-vera-wang-gowns-from-her-new-white-collection/)knows fashion, her designs are like works of art so why not make your [ladies](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-great-gift-ideas-for-your-bridesmaids/)feel special as they walk down the aisle on your wedding day! For prices under $200 and colors like this beautiful Amethyst, what's not to adore.

## 5. V-Neck Chiffon Column Dress...

![V-Neck Chiffon Column Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/07/verabm7_459x612.jpg)

Price: $168.00 @ [DavidsBridal](https://goo.gl/Q2BWz1)

Speaking of classy and elegant, this gorgeous [v-neck sleeveless chiffon column dress](https://goo.gl/pGCuIJ) is picture perfect for a black-tie affair. I have seen many [bridesmaid dresses](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-royal-wedding-looks-for-your-bridesmaids/)with **sheer overlays** and I'm usually not a big fan, but Vera has turned my opinion around with this one! The back of this gown is just as remarkable and the pretty [crystal orchid clip](https://goo.gl/YCCH2N)adds just the right amount of sparkle to catch the eye.

## 3. One Shoulder Satin Dress...

Vera Wang's [bridesmaids gowns](https://goo.gl/1XCHLa)are more than just simple dresses; if they didn't have some **funk** and **style** to them they wouldn't be a true Vera dress! How cool is the** "flange"** of fabric that wraps around the the body of the skirt to create a stunning silhouette. ## 2. Organza Dress with Bubble Skirt...

![Organza Dress with Bubble Skirt...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/07/verabm3_459x612.jpg)

Price: $158.00 @ [DavidsBridal](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

What is great about the[White collection by Vera Wang](http://www.davidsbridal.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)is the **diversity of her designs**. She has gowns for **every woman** of every age, every shape, and every size. If you have many different ages and sizes in your wedding you want to make sure each woman is comfortable in what she is wearing. This pretty[bubble skirt dress](https://goo.gl/BUJIY6)is adorable for someone who is in her teens and twenties, but perhaps a [bridesmaid](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-beautiful-rings-for-the-bride-and-her-bridesmaids/)in her forties might not feel the same way. Have your younger girls wear this** cute dress** and the older ladies wear something like the** v-neck column dress** for a look that pleases everyone.
