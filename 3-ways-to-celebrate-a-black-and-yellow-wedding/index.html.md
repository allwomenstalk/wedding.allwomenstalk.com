# 3 Ways to Celebrate a Black and Yellow Wedding!

That's right I'm still on a **Steelers** kick here! Yes the [Pittsburgh Steelers](https://www.steelers.com/)colors are Black and Gold, but the gold is more of a yellow and I just love the way **yellow** pops against **black** wedding **details**. So here to help you kick off your favorite football colors are 3 inspiring ways to celebrate a** black and yellow** **wedding**...

## 3. Happy & Bright...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Etsy](https://goo.gl/cQyi0F)

**Yellow wedding details** always make me **smile**, they can fill any room with a **pop of happiness**. I love how beautiful the yellow calla lilies stand out against the black bridesmaid dress...so **bright** and smart! **Black** and **Yellow** are also[great colors](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-wedding-themes-inspired-by-paper-goods/)that work well for **every wedding season**.

## 2. City Chic...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Etsy](https://goo.gl/cQyi0F), [AisleDash](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.aisledash.com/2011/02/03/creative-escort-cards/?utm_source=twitterfeed&utm_medium=twitter)

Black wedding details are **classy** and **elegant** like this** city chic inspiration board**. I love the **taxi escort cars** from [AisleDash](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.aisledash.com/2011/02/03/creative-escort-cards/?utm_source=twitterfeed&utm_medium=twitter), how perfect would they be for a [New York City wedding](https://nymag.com/weddings/)! There are so many wonderful **black** and **yellow** details that warm my [Steelers](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-celebrate-a-football-themed-wedding/)fan heart! This color palette is **sophisticated** and sharp like the amazing **black** and **white** **bridesmaid** dress accessorized with a yellow beaded necklace and a [yellow spider chrysanthemum](https://www.wholeblossoms.com/mums-and-spider-mum/spider-mums/yellow-spider-mum-anastasia-flower.html) bouquet. Are you **celebrating** your team colors in **style**?

## 1. Sweet & Romantic...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [Etsy](https://goo.gl/cQyi0F)

**Black and Yellow** wedding colors can also be **sweet** and **romantic** with soft details. Go [vintage](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/3-vintage-inspired-wedding-looks/)with adorable **yellow bridesmaid dresses**, or gift your ladies with romantic **yellow peony necklaces** from [Etsy](https://goo.gl/IcLJ3J). Bring in soft details like this stunning wedding dress with the **lovely black bow** that I would love to have!
