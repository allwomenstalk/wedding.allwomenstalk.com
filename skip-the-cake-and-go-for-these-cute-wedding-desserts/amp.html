Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I skip the traditional wedding cake?
I totally get it, wedding cakes are classic! But let's be honest, having a variety of cute little desserts can be so much more fun and memorable. Plus, everyone loves options, right?
What are some alternative dessert ideas for my wedding?
Oh, so many yummy ideas! How about a cookie bar? You can have different flavors and shapes. Or maybe mini cupcakes, macarons, or even ice cream sandwiches. The sky's the limit!
How can I make a cookie bar look impressive at my wedding?
Presentation is everything, my friend. Use tiered stands, pretty trays or even cute jars. Decorate with flowers or little signs to label each type. It'll look adorable, trust me!
Are these dessert options more cost-effective than a big wedding cake?
Usually, yes! Going for smaller, simpler desserts often means saving some cash. Plus, you can play around with quantities and keep it budget-friendly.
What if some guests still want a traditional cake experience?
Great point! You could keep a small cake for the traditional cutting moment and let your guests indulge in the dessert bar. Everyone wins!
