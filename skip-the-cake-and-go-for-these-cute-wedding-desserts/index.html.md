# Skip the Cake and Go for These Cute Wedding Desserts ...

These days are all about nontraditional weddings, and a lot of the time, that starts with the cake! If you're not obsessed with the idea of having a cake, go for one of these unique options instead. No matter what you choose, it's sure to be the talk of the wedding!

## 1. Fruit Salad

_ Source: Top 40 Traditional Wedding Cake_
Fruit salad is such a fun and fresh dessert for your wedding, and serving it in a champagne glass adds a chic, little touch!

![Tux Oreos](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fm/vw/zd8w1equ563929c7c4886666776475_600x600.jpg)

![Cake Pops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/lk/ai0wbtiu563929c83c3bc059140484_534x800.jpg)
