Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the perfect wedding dress for my body type?
Look for styles that highlight your favorite features and create balance, like A-line for most shapes or ball gown for pear shapes.
When should I start shopping for my wedding dress?
Begin shopping at least 8-12 months before your wedding to allow time for ordering and alterations.
What are the most popular wedding dress silhouettes?
Some favorites include A-line, ball gown, mermaid, sheath, and trumpet shapes.
How much should I budget for a wedding dress?
Typically, brides spend between 10-15% of their overall wedding budget on their dress.
What fabrics are best for wedding dresses in hot weather?
Lightweight fabrics like chiffon, organza, and tulle keep you cool and comfortable.
Should I wear a veil with my dress?
Veils add tradition and elegance, but it’s optional and should complement your dress style.
How do I care for my wedding dress before the big day?
Store it in a breathable garment bag, hang it carefully, and keep it away from direct sunlight.
Can I customize my wedding dress?
Yes, many designers offer customization options like sleeve length, neckline, and embellishments.
What shoes should I wear with my wedding dress?
Choose comfortable shoes that match your dress style and consider bringing a backup pair.
How do I prepare for my wedding dress fitting?
Wear the right undergarments and shoes, and bring any accessories to ensure the best fit.
