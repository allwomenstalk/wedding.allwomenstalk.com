# 4 Lovely Bridal Gowns under $800.00 ...

With gas prices rising, many people unemployed, and looking for ways to save, big budget weddings are no longer the trend. DIY, back-yard, under $10,000 **weddings** have become the norm and many wedding gown designers are following the **low-budget trend**. Here are 4 lovely **bridal gowns** under **$900.00** for the brides who want to wow their guests, not their bank account.

## 4. Sweet Rosettes...

![Sweet Rosettes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/rosette_420x644.jpg)

Price: $798.00

**Budget brides** can still look amazing as they walk down the aisle, especially in a gown like this one by [Tadashi Shoji](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.tadashicollection.com/#). You don't have to go cheap on style because you don't have $5,000 set aside just for your dress.

## 3. Beach Bride...

![Beach Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/BCBG_420x644.jpg)

Price: $360.00

The simple look of this chic gown from [BCBG](http://www.bcbg.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) makes it perfect for the **beach** or even a back-yard wedding in the Spring. I love the flow of the skirt and the **sash** around the waist and who wouldn't love it for that price!

## 2. Elegant Lace...

![Elegant Lace...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/lace_420x644.jpg)

Price: $588.00

There are so many brides looking for **lovely lace gowns** who just haven't found the right one for the budget price they were hoping for. My lace gown was around $1,000, so I couldn't believe it when I saw the price of this **elegant** lace wedding dress by [Sue Wong](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.suewong.com/public/intro.htm).

## 1. Feather-like Organza...
