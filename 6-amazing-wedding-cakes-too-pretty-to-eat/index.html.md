# 6 Amazing Wedding Cakes Too Pretty to Eat!

I loved our wedding **cake** so much that I wanted to save it forever! **Wedding cakes **can be anything we imagine. Some are so stunning they should be showcased at the [The Museum of Modern Art](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.moma.org/)! Here are **6 Amazing Wedding Cakes that just look too Pretty to Eat...**

## 6. Perfect for a Winter Wedding...

**
**

**

![Perfect for a Winter Wedding...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/prettycake_401x535.jpg)

**

**Photo: [Fashionablebride.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/planninginspiration/Wedding-Cakes)**

**Cake: [Bobbette & Belle](https://web.archive.org/web/https://bobbetteandbelle.com/index.html)**

**Classy**, **simple**, and **pretty**...and I'm sure it's **tasty** too! Sometimes it's the simple things in life that take our breath away. For your [winter wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-unique-wedding-bouquets-that-will-last-forever/) impress your guests with this fabulous white and blue cake. Add a personal touch with a [cute snowflake cake topper](https://goo.gl/M0QfkI)or [kissing couple](https://goo.gl/UKTc7Z). **H****ave you found your cake design yet?**

## 5. Pretty in Pink and Teal...

**

![Pretty in Pink and Teal...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/stunningcake_402x479.jpg)

**

**Photo: [BobbetteandBelle.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://bobbetteandbelle.com/wedding_cakes_gallery.html)**

**Cake: [Bobbette & Belle](https://web.archive.org/web/https://bobbetteandbelle.com/index.html)**

**Too Pretty** to eat or touch! I love the colors and the **damask pattern** on each layer. This is a wonderful cake if you're looking to match your [damask wedding invitations](https://goo.gl/mOFhrg)or [wedding colors](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-wedding-color-ideas/). This cake can be created in other colors as well, just contact [Bobbette & Belle](https://web.archive.org/web/https://bobbetteandbelle.com/contact.html)for all your cake questions! **Does your cake match any of your other wedding details?**

## 4. Tropical Paradise...

**

![Tropical Paradise...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/hawaiilove_403x538.jpg)

**

**Photo: [Fashionablebride.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/240/planninginspiration/Wedding-Cakes/Cakelava)**

**Cake: [Cakelava](https://web.archive.org/web/https://cakelava.com/welcome.html)**

**Take your guests on a trip to [Hawaii](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-top-us-honeymoon-locations/) with this tropical** beauty. Made with [fondant](https://whatscookingamerica.net/pegw/fondant.htm)to make things a little easier for the cake designers at [CakeLava](https://web.archive.org/web/https://cakelava.com/welcome.html), which just happens to be located on the beautiful island of Oahu. Use this **pretty cake**** inspiration** for your baker and bring your [summer luau wedding](https://www.topweddingsites.com/wedding-blog/uncategorized/pull-off-hawaiian-luau-wedding-reception-anywhere-ease) to life. **Are you planning a themed wedding? **

## 3. Lovely Little Details...

**

![Lovely Little Details...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/weddingcake_402x364.jpg)

**

**Photo: [LoveStreetCakes.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.lovestreetcakes.com/gallery_wedding.html)**

**Cake: [Love Street Cakes](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.lovestreetcakes.com/chef.html)**

**I love sweet**, **delicate** **details** that are **thoughtful** and planned out from the [baker](https://web.archive.org/web/http://lovestreetcakes.com/index.html) to make a special cake for the **bride and groom**. Remarkable details like the one on this cake make me think of the [bride's gown](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-wedding-gown-trends-for-2010/)and how amazing cake bakers can match little pieces of **lace** or **flowers** from her gown onto the cake. Works of **art in every little bite**! Will your wedding cake match your beautiful gown?

## 2. Roses Are Red...

**

![Roses Are Red...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/rosecake_403x537.jpg)

**

**Photo: [Fashionablebride.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/266/planninginspiration/Wedding-Cakes/Sylvia-Weinstock-Cakes-Ltd)**

**Cake: [Sylvia Weinstock Cakes](https://web.archive.org/web/http://sylviaweinstockcakes.com/index_fl4.html)**

**Flowers** can be very important details for some brides and grooms and whether you have real flowers on your cake or your baker can create **edible flowers** they make for a stunning result. This pretty cake has gorgeous **hand painted and sugar flowers**, making the entire cake very edible! [Sylvia Weinstock,](https://web.archive.org/web/http://blog.sylviaweinstock.com/) who created this cake, has made fabulous cakes for [celebrities](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-most-insanely-insured-celebrity-body-parts/) like **P.Diddy** and **J.Lo**. Will you have a celebrity baker make a cake for you?

## 1. Hand-painted Beauty...

**

![Hand-painted Beauty...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/handpaintedcake_402x537.jpg)

**

**Photo: [Fashionablebride.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.fashionablebride.com/gallery/256/planninginspiration/Wedding-Cakes/Little-Venice-Cake-Company)**

**Cake: [Little Venice Cake Company](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.lvcc.co.uk/index.asp)**

**This is a true work of art** and a cake too pretty to cut into! There are no words to say how **brilliant** the **hand-painted birds** are. The bakers at [Little Venice Cake Company](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.lvcc.co.uk/iced-wedding-cakes-detail.asp?cakeid=47)were inspired by the designs of [Alexander McQueen](https://goo.gl/W9GeyG) and **Matthew Williams**. Birds are a very popular **wedding theme** including [bird cake toppers](https://allwomenstalk.com/7-awesome-wedding-cake-toppers/), [bird invitations](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-gorgeous-wedding-invitations/), and [bird cage centerpieces](http://www.stumpsparty.com/cgi-sys/suspendedpage.cgi). Your wedding will be complete when you top your bird theme off with this amazing **too pretty to eat cake**!
