# 15 Creative Wedding Ring Designs That Will Make You Gasp ...

Looking for creative wedding ring designs that go beyond the typical silver or gold band? Whether you’re looking for something just a little bit different or entirely unusual, a unique ring is guaranteed to add a twist to your day and become a treasured part of your jewelry collection for years to come. Ready to find the one that’s right for you? Here are nine creative wedding ring designs you’re sure to love and that will make everyone gasp!

## 1. ‘Love’ Script

![‘Love’ Script](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164923_2841_600x588.jpg)

What better choice for your wedding than an unconventional script ring that spells out the word “love”? While wire rings are a simple and understated choice, similar creative wedding ring designs are also available in silver and gold for those who prefer a look that’s just a bit more traditional.

## 2. Vintage Flower

![Vintage Flower](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07221854_5790_600x418.jpg)

Vintage rings are all the rage right now, and with pretty designs like this, it’s easy to see why. While I absolutely adore this floral look, there are also plenty of simpler but just as eye-catching vintage designs that look somewhat more traditional, so be sure to do some research and Pinterest stalking before choosing the one you love best.

## 3. Monogrammed

![Monogrammed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/07221854_9781_600x514.jpg)

Celebrate the beginning of a new chapter in your life with a wedding ring engraved to showcase your personalized monogram. Like the “love” ring mentioned previously, a monogrammed ring can also be crafted in a script design for yet another type of innovative look.

## 4. Rose Gold

![Rose Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164923_2648_600x600.jpg)

When you want a ring that’s different yet still evokes a somewhat classic vibe, rose gold is a beautiful and increasingly popular choice. Whether you go simple or bold, as seen here, rose gold is simply gorgeous to look at.

## 5. Twisted

![Twisted](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164922_8824_600x600.jpg)

This natural white gold ring’s design reminds me of a ladder twisted around to form a circle. What do you think of when you look at it? Would you wear an offbeat ring like this?

## 6. Organic Freeform

![Organic Freeform](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164922_8055_600x450.jpg)

Seeking a ring that’s truly a one-of-a-kind work of art? An organic freeform design offers a look that’s entirely unique and incredibly unexpected.

## 7. Sparkling Sapphire

![Sparkling Sapphire](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164922_1421_600x385.jpg)

Kate Middleton made sapphire rings a top choice for brides everywhere, and this unique design offers a look that’s breathtakingly regal. Do you love this refined ring just as much as I do?

## 8. Infinity Band

![Infinity Band](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164922_3235_600x454.jpg)

Also accented with sparkling jewels, a gorgeous infinity band like this one will serve as a constant reminder of the promises you and your partner have made. While I’ve seen other infinity bands in just plain silver or gold, the unique addition of glittering jewels makes this one all the more special.

## 9. Thumbprint

![Thumbprint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24164923_8185_600x400.jpg)

Personalize your ring with both your birthstone and a custom thumbprint. Of course, this ring design isn’t necessarily specific to a wedding and can also make a great gift and keepsake when given for a birthday, anniversary or any other special occasion.

## 10. Rose

![Rose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24174906_4307_590x471.jpg)

What could be more romantic than a rose-shaped wedding ring? This bold and beautiful design is a definite showstopper. This particular design is 2.60 carats but of course you could get something a little more understated but just as lovely.

## 11. Nature Gem Ring

![Nature Gem Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24174906_7124_600x600.jpg)

How about a delicate band with a stone that is reminiscent of nature and all her beauty? While this ring might not be a classic design, it's perfect for the bride who wants to start her own traditions.

## 12. Pale Pink and Plum Cushion Rose Cut Ring

![Pale Pink and Plum Cushion Rose Cut Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24174906_1050_569x448.jpg)

Soft, beautiful and precious, just like the bride-to-be. Either of these dazzling gems would make an incredible engagement ring that any woman would be proud to wear. Also great for stacking, this vintage-inspired design would make a great anniversary gift as well.

## 13. Rounded Square Ring

![Rounded Square Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24174906_0462_554x454.jpg)

This wedding ring is fitting for the girl who likes to stand out from the crowd. The rounded square design with five Swarovski crystals is breathtaking and so very distinct from your average ring.

## 14. "I do" Waveform Ring

!["I do" Waveform Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24174906_1504_400x267.jpg)

Designed by Japanese artist Sakurako Shimizu, these extraordinary rings have an engraved sound wave of you and your partner saying "I do" or any word you wish. Capture the most important day of your lives on your ring!

## 15. Holly Ring

![Holly Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/24174907_0251_449x220.jpg)

Who says you have to have a white diamond on your finger? Buck tradition and opt for an onyx gem atop a 14K rose gold band. This ring is classy, super sleek and sure to turn heads wherever you go!

In the end, no matter what type of ring you choose, as long as it’s something you know you’ll love slipping on your finger each and every day, it’s sure to be the perfect choice. What do you think of these creative wedding ring designs? Do you have a favorite, or is there another type of ring not already listed here that you especially love?
