# 5 Christmas Songs to Play at Your Winter Wedding ...

Get everyone in the **holiday spirit** at your **winter wedding** when you play these **5 favorite Christmas songs** to sing and dance along to. Maybe **Santa** will even make a special appearance and **dance with the bride **on her big day!

## 5. Jingle Bell Rock...

![Jingle Bell Rock...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/jinglebellrock_400x400.jpg)

Photo: [MusicStack.com](https://www.musicstack.com/item/153659724)

Song: [YouTube.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=itcMLwMEeMQ)

How cute would this **song** be as you play it for your **bridal party entrance** into the reception! Give them each some **jingle bells** and your guests will be ready for **Christmas** with a smile in their heart. I love this **old holiday favorite**; it gets me in the spirit to start [decorating](https://allwomenstalk.com/15-lovely-ornaments-id-love-to-see-on-my-christmas-tree/) every year! **Are you planning a Christmas wedding?**

## 4. Rockin' around the Christmas Tree...

**

![Rockin' around the Christmas Tree...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/Brenda+Lee+-+Rockin+Around+the+Christmas+Tree+Front_400x400.jpg)

**

**Photo: **[Christmas-Oldies.blogspot.com](http://christmas-oldies.blogspot.com/2010/08/brenda-lee-rockin-around-christmas-tree.html)

Song: [YouTube.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_6xNuUEnh2g)

[Decorate your wedding](https://goo.gl/en3tA2)with **Christmas trees **as you **dance** along to this song with the [flower girl](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-reasons-to-have-kids-in-your-wedding/) and ring bearer. All the **kids** at your wedding will love hearing fun **Christmas songs **and will be glad their parents brought them to your [holiday wedding](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.americanbridal.com/ho.html)! **What song brings back your favorite Christmas memories? **

## 3. Merry Christmas Baby...

![Merry Christmas Baby...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/elvis_400x398.jpg)

Photo: [Music-online-store.com](http://www.music-online-store.com/node/christian-and-gospel/list_359203.html)

Song: [YouTube.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJMGaEj9LAo)

Make a new **Christmas** memory as you play **Elvis**' sweet, romantic **"Merry Christmas Baby" when your groom slides that [garter belt](https://goo.gl/KElttq) off your leg! If you aren't doing the garter toss, play this song for your **first dance** under the **mistletoe** wearing a santa hat! **Do you love **[Elvis](https://allwomenstalk.com/top-10-las-vegas-wedding-ideas/)** as much as I do?

## 2. Santa Baby...

![Santa Baby...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/madonna-celebration_400x400.jpg)

Photo: [imusicdaily.com](https://www.hugedomains.com/domain_profile.cfm?d=imusicdaily.com)

Song: [YouTube.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TuPitU2nJD8)

Madonna's version of **"Santa Baby"** is one of my favorite **Christmas** songs. This is another great song for the** garter or **[bouquet](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/6-unique-wedding-bouquets-that-will-last-forever/)** **toss. Have your **groom** dress up as **Santa** and **dance** with him before he heads out into the cold winter's night! **Are you hoping for snow for your winter wedding?**

## 1. Santa Claus is Coming to Town...

![Santa Claus is Coming to Town...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2010/12/brucespringsteen_407x538.jpg)

Photo: [StyleCaster.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://stylecaster.com/news/5262/bruce-springsteens-2009-birthday-bash)

Song: [YouTube.com](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iSgEDKjmT5o&amp%3Bfeature=related)

He is the Boss! This is a fun song to end your **wedding night**. Send your guests off into the **holiday** with a **childhood Christmas song** everyone loves! **Make that list and check it twice** to make sure this song is on your **wedding** **playlist**. Rock out as you wave goodnight in a [reindeer sleigh](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.laplandfinland.com/In_English/Experiences_in_Lapland/Four_Seasons/Winter/Reindeer_sleigh_rides.iw3) on your way to your [honeymoon](https://allwomenstalk.com/top-10-places-for-a-romantic-honeymoon/)!

**Will you dance to Christmas songs at your winter wedding?**
