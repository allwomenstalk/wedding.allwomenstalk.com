# 8 Fab Wedding Gown Trends for Summer 2012 ...

Spring is here and Summer is right around the corner and that only means one thing, it's Wedding Season! So what **wedding gown trends** are we going to see this season; will there be big and bolder dresses full of color and beading or will they be more sophisticated with lace and sleeves like Kate Middleton's gown? Perhaps one of these **8 Fab Wedding Gown Trends for Summer 2012** will catch your eye for that walk down the aisle...

## 1. Two Tiered Wedding Gown Trend...

_ [Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/badgley-mischka-bridal-spring-2012-l-5964-l-46.html)

This unique, two tiered **wedding dress trend** may have stepped on the runway before the Oscars, but when [Michelle Williams](https://www.latimes.com/archives/blogs/ministry-of-gossip/story/2012-02-26/oscars-2012-michelle-williams-now-a-better-dressed-misfit)walked on the red carpet in her two tiered Louis Vuitton red gown, brides every where started to notice just how fabulous it really was! For brides who want the mermaid look but want to cover up their hips the two tiered gown, like this Badgley Mischka_named Dierdre, is perfect.

## 2. Off the Shoulder Wedding Gown Trend...

_ [Photo Credit](https://www.onewed.com/blog/inspired-wedding-dresses-romona-keveza-2012-25625/)

I think off the shoulder **wedding gowns** are sweet and romantic for all seasons but look lovely in the summer for brides who like strapless dresses but want to be a bit more covered up. This beautiful gown by Romona Keveza_is dramatic yet soft and would be a wonderful dress for a garden wedding.

## 3. Illusion Neckline Wedding Gown Trend...

![Illusion Neckline Wedding Gown Trend...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/04/Monique-Lhuillier-2012_553x830.jpg)

[Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/monique-lhuillier-fall-2012-bridal-l-5838-l-46.html)

If I was getting married this year the illusion neckline would be the **wedding gown trend** I would rock down the aisle. It may even be more romantic than the off the shoulder look, especially this [Monique Lhuillier](https://goo.gl/dU5Gv4)gown, Keepsake, with it's stunning embellished neckline, matching belt, and veil.

## 4. Bold Colors Wedding Gown Trend...

_ [Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/elie-saab-spring-couture-2012-l-6384-l-46.html)

Do you dream in color? The colorful **wedding gown trend** is back for Spring and Summer 2012 and it's bigger than ever, I've even seen [black wedding gowns](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/vera-wang-bridal-fall-2012-l-5825-l-46.html)trending for fall! This Elie Saab_sea green dress is a magical dream for a Summer wedding on the beach filled with margarita green details and centerpieces of sea glass. It's fresh and bold for the bride who wants to be daring and unique.

## 5. Beaded Bodice Wedding Gown Trend...

_ [Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/badgley-mischka-bridal-spring-2012-l-5964-l-46.html)

The beaded **wedding gown trend** has seen it's ups and downs. When done right a beaded bodice can look classy and sophisticated. However, a beaded wedding gown can also go the opposite way if there is too much going on and turn off brides who are looking for something more modern. Badgley Mischka_certainly knows how to do modern and a beaded bodice as you can see in this gorgeous Ava gown. It's one of my favorites!

## 6. Sexy Slit Wedding Gown Trend...

_ [Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/monique-lhuillier-fall-2012-bridal-l-5838-l-46.html)

The summer is a great excuse to show some skin and be super sexy so why not do the same on your wedding day in a sexy slit gown! Just like the color trend, the sexy slit **wedding gown trend** may be reserved for daring brides who want to surprise their guests with something bold and fun like this stunning Monique Lhuillier_dress named Monroe. This sexy style is perfect for a destination wedding.

## 7. Lace Sleeves Wedding Gown Trend...

_ [Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/check-out-the-elizabeth-fillmore-nyc-bridal-fall-2017-collection-l-13970-l-38.html)

Are you seeing Bella Swan or Pippa Middleton when you look at this Elizabeth Filmore_lace wedding gown? Ever since the Royal Wedding and the Twilight Wedding the lace **wedding gown trend** and sleeve trend is becoming more and more popular. Brides who want to look more sophisticated and conservative will love this gown that looks just as remarkable in the back.

## 8. Short Wedding Gown Trend...

![Short Wedding Gown Trend...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2012/04/Jenny_Packham_2012_Bridal27_550x886.jpg)

[Photo Credit](https://www.weddingchicks.com/blog/jenny-packham-2012-bridals-l-5999-l-46.html)

How adorable is this short wedding dress by [Jenny Packham](https://jennypackham.com/)! It's feminine, chic, and has a touch of retro with a bit of a modern look to it. Along with the short **wedding gown trend** it also has the illusion neckline, which makes the dress feel complete. It's a lovely little dress that is sweet for the ceremony or just for the reception as a 2nd dress.

Whether you love to follow fashion trends straight from the runway or just love the bold look of the 2012 **wedding gown trends**, remember to find the look that suits you and fits your personality. Don't go for a look just because it's trendy or because it's what Michelle Williams wore to the Oscars. Also, don't feel disappointed if your favorite **wedding gown trend** doesn't look the way you wanted it to, try on as many dresses as you want until you know it's the one. You should love your gown because it's what feels good and not because someone said it's the latest in **wedding gown trends**, but I do hope you love these gowns as much as I do!
