# 6 Wedding Decorating Accessories I Love from BHLDN!

[BHLDN](https://goo.gl/bxp4GH)is become quite the one stop **wedding shop**! From [bridal gowns](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-lovely-bridal-gowns-from-bhldn/)and bridesmaid dresses to their new collection of **decorating accessories**; you'll be able to find everything for your big day all bundled together on one creative site. Here are a few of my favorites, come back to share yours....

## 6. For Your Cake...

![For Your Cake...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/cakestand_446x670.jpg)

Price: $148.00

I saw this sweet **tree trunk cake stand **and fell in love. I love the mixture of the woodsy look with the **crisp white color**. This handmade Earthenware treat stand is lovely for so many themes; beach weddings, backyard weddings, and even fall weddings.

## 5. Storybook Cake Topper...

![Storybook Cake Topper...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/caketopper_446x670.jpg)

Price: $48.00

Your wedding is a **fairytale** come true and what better cake topper to display than one inspired by a storybook. The details and colors on this creative **cake topper **are amazing. Handmade and painted by artist [Nathalie Lete](https://goo.gl/2uw8nd) and [Mara Mi](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.mara-mi.com/)for **BHLDN**.

## 4. Time to Eat...

![Time to Eat...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/mrandmrs_445x334.jpg)

Price $24.00

Now that you have your cake stand and cake topper you'll need something to help you eat your delicious cake! Why not feed each other in style with these **personalized** bride and groom or Mr. & Mrs. **cake forks **by [WoodenHive](https://web.archive.org/web/http://woodenhive.com/)! Purchase the bride and groom set for the wedding and the Mr. & Mrs. for your anniversary.

## 3. Rustic Lighting...

![Rustic Lighting...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/woodcandle_446x670.jpg)

Price: $14.00-$22.00

Romantic, rustic, and beachy; summer is here in the most wonderful way! These** adrift candle baskets **are the coolest candle holders I've seen and I wish we had them for our centerpieces! The thing I love about [BHLDN](https://goo.gl/gMjI09) is that most of their products are **handmade** like this beach lovers set. Can you smell the ocean air yet?

## 2. Heart to Heart...

![Heart to Heart...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/hearts_446x670.jpg)

Price: $58.00 for a set of 8

How adorable are these **slate chalkboard hearts**! I can think of a million ways to use them in your wedding plans, what about you? You could make **placecards** out of them, send them home with guests as **favors**, or **gift **your bridesmaids with little love notes...for just a few ideas!

## 1. Vintage Love...

![Vintage Love...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/05/vintagehankie_446x670.jpg)

Price: $28.00

Don't just call up your favorite ladies and ask them to be part of your big day. This is a **special moment for you both and you'll want her to remember how she felt when you ask her to be your** maid of honor. Send her a lovely **vintage handkerchief **by [Bird and Banner](http://birdandbanner.com/)and she'll always have something to hold on to that moment with.
