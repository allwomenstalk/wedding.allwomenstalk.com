# A Spunk Filled Engagement Shoot in NYC ...

When you have ice cream, food trucks, **New York City**, and spunky personalities like these two wonderful souls, there isn't anything else you need but fantastic **photographers** like Kristin and Steven from [Photomuse](https://www.photomuse.com/). As soon as I saw these fun, creative **engagement photos** I knew I had to share them and wait until you see their wedding...coming up later today!

## 8. A Colorful Setting...

![A Colorful Setting...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0136_407x271.jpg)

Not every couple could look this adorable infront of a wall full of **graffti**, and I doubt my husband would think this was a great location for an **engagement shoot**! But I love it and I love them. Their **spirit** and fun personalities just jump off the photo and pull you in for a night full of excitement.

## 7. Laughter in the Air...

![Laughter in the Air...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0226_400x602.jpg)

Can you imagine what they were saying and laughing about in this **cute photo**! I just want to be friends with these two. I'm guessing he was trying to give her a lift, but this photo full of **laughter** works out better than if the back ride would have actually been successful!

## 6. Smile...

![Smile...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0172_411x619.jpg)

Did I not tell you how this couple pulls you into their photo. Her **smile** is so beautiful and his little smirk is charming and sweet. When you see their wedding day you will really see how much in **love** these two are!

Continue for more Spunk!

## 5. Food Truck Heaven...

![Food Truck Heaven...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0419_403x268.jpg)

Okay, I'm jealous and want some tasty food from a food truck! If you're in New York or LA you know you have to take a picture with a food truck...it's all the rave, so I hear on the foodie blogs!

## 4. We All Scream...

![We All Scream...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0467_404x606.jpg)

## 3. New York City...

![New York City...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0721_408x614.jpg)

Another must on your photo list of **NYC** is taking a shoot with a **yellow cab**, duh! I know I said the last photo was my favorite but this is another one. They are just standing there in the middle of the street with all the crazy drivers in New York, they really do have spunk!

## 2. Kiss the Girl...

![Kiss the Girl...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/FEH10016_0726_411x619.jpg)
