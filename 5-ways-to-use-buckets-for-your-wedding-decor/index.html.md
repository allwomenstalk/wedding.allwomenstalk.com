# 5 Ways to Use Buckets for Your Wedding Decor ...

I saw this idea and thought it was such a lovely way to easily decorate your wedding with so many different items. You can fill **buckets and pails with **flowers**, fruit, wine, **champagne**, and even sand and seashells for a beach theme. Whatever your colors, theme, or centerpiece items are, create unique details when you add one of these 5 **bucket** **decor ideas to your wedding...

## 5. For the Wine Lover...

![For the Wine Lover...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/Bucket-Wedding-Centerpiece-250x375_405x307.jpg)

Photo: [ElizabethAnneDesigns](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/2010/08/24/real-weddings-melanie-scott/)

Whether you're planning an [Italian theme wedding](https://allwomenstalk.com/5-hot-wedding-themes/), love to drink **wine**, or would like to give wine as favors, wine and flowers for your **bucket centerpiece** is a **romantic** idea. If you have as many bridesmaids as tables let their **bouquets** do double duty and place them in the buckets after the ceremony.

## 4. A Bucket of Fruit...

![A Bucket of Fruit...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/Classic-Savannah-Place-Setting-250x376_405x304.jpg)

Photo: [ElizabethAnneDesigns](https://elizabethannedesigns.com/2010/10/18/classic-savannah-wedding-georgia-peach-theme/)

Finding the[perfect gown](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/9-vera-wang-gowns-from-her-new-white-collection/), photographer, and venue can put enough stress on you when planning your big day. Go with** easy decor ideas** like these wonderful** buckets of fruit** to give yourself some worry-free **wedding details**. I love the thought of going with a **fruit theme**; peaches for a Georgia wedding, oranges in Florida, and apples for NY!

## 3. Candy Favors...

![Candy Favors...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/favorbucket_400x400.jpg)

Photo: [Shop.GetMarried.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://shop.getmarried.com/Wedding-Reception/Reception-Decorations/2909-GMBWJ54-Grey--White-Bucket.pro)

Who doesn't love candy! I also love this adorable **monogrammed bucket**, great for your **candy buffet** or even as centerpieces for your laid back beach wedding. Don't forget the[little bags](https://thingsfestive.weddingstar.com:443/product/merchandise-bags)for your guests to take some **candy** home as [favors](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-unique-wedding-favors/)!

## 2. Walk down the Aisle...

![Walk down the Aisle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/bucketpews_403x504.jpg)

Photo: [MarthaStewartWeddings](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.marthastewartweddings.com/photogallery/good-things-decorations)

These lovely **church pews** have been given just a simple wedding makeover with a tin bucket and some purple flowers! If you like the look of a pomander but are more of a [non-DIY bride](https://web.archive.org/web/https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-etsy-wedding-finds-for-the-non-diy-bride/), try this easy **wedding aisle decor** idea from [Martha Stewart.](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.marthastewartweddings.com/photogallery/good-things-decorations)

## 1. Sweet Peonies...

![Sweet Peonies...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/03/peoniesbucket_405x271.jpg)

Photo: [OnceWed](https://www.oncewed.com/wedding-ideas-blog/diy-wedding-peony-centerpieces/)

With this great idea from [Chelsea Fuss](https://elseachelsea.typepad.com/)you can use buckets to create your centerpieces and** table numbers**. Check out her beautiful **DIY tutorial** over at [OnceWed](https://www.oncewed.com/). I love the soft look of the colorful **peonies** paired with the **rustic** **buckets**.
