# 7 Beautiful Alfred Angelo Bridesmaid Dresses ...

With tons of flattering and affordable styles to choose from, Alfred Angelo bridesmaid dresses are guaranteed to put a smile on the face of every girl in your wedding party. Seeking the perfect romantic dress for a fairytale wedding? How about a short and sassy vintage look? Whether you’re going for classic elegance, beach-ready flair, or anything else in between, these gorgeous Alfred Angelo bridesmaid dresses will have your maids looking and feeling like a million bucks for less than $250!

## 1. Floral Elegance

![Floral Elegance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/1_floral-elegance_340x450.jpg)

Part of Alfred Angelo’s Disney Maidens collection, this beautiful satin and organza dress evokes a classic vibe, while the flower at the waist also adds a modern touch of romance. The elegant fabric and floor-length cut make it the perfect choice for an affair that demands a formal look but certainly not a hefty price tag. After all, what girl wouldn’t love snagging a dress this lovely for less than $200? Like many other Alfred Angelo bridesmaid dresses, it also comes in 50 unique shades.

## 2. Ruffled Romance

![Ruffled Romance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/2_ruffled-romance_340x450.jpg)

Looking for a short dress that’s still regal enough for a fairytale wedding? Look no further than this one-shouldered Disney Maidens gown, which features a modern, figure-flattering silhouette, along with a romantic cascade of ruffles down the side. Even though it costs less than $150, this dress also comes in 50 different colors, allowing your maids to achieve the perfect look and then put the savings toward throwing you a stellar bachelorette party!

## 3. Springtime Beauty

![Springtime Beauty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/3_springtime-beauty_340x450.jpg)

Perfect for a springtime garden wedding, this Disney Maidens dress features a flowing fabric that looks beautiful in a floor-length cut but is still lightweight enough for an outdoor affair. From the eye-catching floral detail to the touches of crystal beading and sequins, this is one dress that will definitely make your maids feel as if they’re floating on air. The other awesome part? Like all the others we’ve seen so far, this style is sold for less than – that’s right, you guessed it – $200. Choose from 15 pretty shades, and get ready to fall in love!

## 4. Modern Vintage Flair

![Modern Vintage Flair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/4_modern-vintage-flair_340x450.jpg)

Think the Disney Maidens collection is just a bit too girly for your taste, but still prefer a lightweight, flattering dress? Check out Alfred Angelo’s Modern Vintage Bridesmaids line, where you’ll find stylish and sexy options like this halter-top dress, which features an open back and 15 colors to choose from. Although this style is slightly more expensive than some of the others we've seen, at less than $250, it’s still an absolute steal.

## 5. Short and Sassy Glamour

![Short and Sassy Glamour](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/5_short-and-sassy-glamour_340x450.jpg)

Also from the Modern Vintage Bridesmaids collection, this sassy cocktail-length dress inspires visions of the glamorous flapper era, while also offering a modern one-shouldered cut and illusion neckline. You and your maids will love the 15 color choices available – from Morning Fog to Vivid Pink, along with the less than $200 price tag. Pair this flouncy frock with your favorite 1920s-inspired jewelry, plus some fabulous heels, and get ready to dance the night away!

## 6. Mix-and-Match Color

![Mix-and-Match Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/01/6_mix-and-match-color_340x451.jpg)

Planning a summertime wedding at the beach? This breezy two-toned dress may be just the right colorful choice to keep your maids feeling cool while looking hot. The dress and trim colors come in 50 stunning shades, giving you tons of mix-and-match options for no more than $179. Love the dress but not the two-toned look? Just match the sash color to the rest of the dress, and you’ll be good to go!

## 7. Stylish Maternity

When you have a bridesmaid who’s expecting, finding a comfortable and flattering dress she loves is a definite must. This halter-style dress comes in 50 different shades, allowing your pregnant maid to match the rest of the girls while also looking beyond beautiful herself. And if you prefer a shorter dress, Alfred Angelo also sells cocktail-length maternity dresses that, like this one, cost no more than $179. Head on over to the company’s website and start browsing!
