# 1 Guy  1 Girl  1 Dog  a Fun Loving Engagement Shoot in Florida ...

Two good looking people and their adorable dog, it doesn't get better than that for a fun loving **engagement shoot**. Plus, when you have an amazing photographer who is as sweet, kind, and beautiful as Casie from [Rebel Belle Photography](https://goo.gl/igbf1S), you're in a win-win situation!
Hugs & Kisses....

Rob, Jackie, and their beautiful dog Layla look so sweet in this photo; they look so happy like a family should! Their love story begins after Jackie graduated college, but there is a surprising twist you have to hear about...
Dog on the Run...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_48-1_copy_438x292.jpg)

I think Layla was finished with taking engagement photos and just wanted to go for a run! "Come on mom and dad, aren't we done yet!"
She Said Yes...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_219_copy_432x326.jpg)

Amazing ring shot...a few words from Jackie...

"Robbie and I met after I graduated FSU in 2004 and moved back home to Orlando. He was at UCF at the time and we had a mutual friend Dan who we both hung out with. During the course of about a year to year and a half we basically became best friends. We did everything together..."
On the Right Track...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_84-1_copy_436x329.jpg)

How gorgeous is Jackie in her high heels on the train tracks! Can you walk in high heels on tracks? I love how they are such a good looking couple, but they seem so sweet and kind too! No snobs here!
I Want to Hold Your Hand...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_96-1_copy_438x292.jpg)

Back to their love story....

"Then after that we just started to like each other more and started dating. 5 years later here we are engaged! I do want to note that we are both shocked that we had not met sooner since we went to the same high school for 3 years and also knew the same people. Still think that’s kind of funny."
Never Let Go...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_114_copy_438x328.jpg)

Can you believe these two went to the same high school and never knew each other! It was meant to be that they found each other even after Jackie went away to college and came back to her hometown...what a romantic story.
Sit With Me for a While...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_133-1_copy_438x292.jpg)

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_327_copy_438x292.jpg)

"We are getting Married Feb 18, 2012 at the First United Methodist Church in Winter Park and having our reception at the Winter Park Civic Center. Lots of Scottish elements are going to be incorporated; groom and groomsmen in kilts, also Robbie’s dad. We will have bagpipers also."
Love is Magical...

![](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/2011-02-12_Jackie_and_Rob__Engagement_158_copy_267x400.jpg)

Their love story and their fun engagement photos are all so magical and I'm sure their wedding day will be just as amazing. I can't wait to see Jackie in her beautiful wedding gown and Rob in his kilt! I wonder if Layla will be walking down the aisle as ring bearer!
