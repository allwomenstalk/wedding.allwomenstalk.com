# 7 Fun Bridal Shower Games ...

It's always a good idea to come up with fun bridal shower games. There is more to a bridal shower than just sitting around watching the bride open all of her new gifts. Liven up the party with these fun bridal shower games and help the bride-to-be get excited for her big day...

## 1. Bridal Bingo...

This is a fun bridal shower game to play while the bride is opening up her gifts. Instead of writing down numbers, fill in the blank spaces with items from the wedding registry. You get to mark off the items as she opens them. One easy space you will most certainly cross off...the item you bought!

## 2. The Newlywed Game...

I love this bridal shower game, it's a great way to get the groom involved as well. Before the shower, interview the husband-to-be and ask him questions about the bride and their relationship. Then at the shower ask the bride the same questions. To make this game even more exciting have the bride stick marshmallows or gum in her mouth for every incorrect question!

## 3. He Said, She Said...

Turn the newlywed bridal shower game around on your guests and have them guess who said what! Again, ask the bride and groom relationship questions beforehand so you can make up game sheets for the shower. Your guests can circle on the sheets who they believe said things like, "I can't stand the way he/she makes coffee!" Make sure the answers aren't too obvious to either the bride or groom.

## 4. Two Truths and a Lie...

Is your bride a fun gal who loves to go out and have a good time for Ladies' Night? Well this bridal shower game might just expose what kind of good time she likes to have! Each guest gets to tell two truths and a lie while the others try to think which of these could possible be the lie. The crazier the truths, the more fun you'll have!

## 5. Word Scramble...

Word games are a great way to pass the time while the bride is opening up her gifts or even while you eat. Scramble around words that are connected to planning the big day and see who can unscramble all 20 words first. Use fun words like bachelorette party, honeymoon, and lingerie to give your guests a good laugh when they figure them out!

## 6. Secret Word of the Day...

This bridal shower game is one of my favorites: secret word of the day, also known as the clothespin game! Give each guest a clothespin and share with them the secret word of the day, whether it's bride, groom, or wedding. After it is shared with everyone, they are no longer allowed to use this word in conversation. If they are caught saying the secret word, the person who hears it can take their clothespin and add it to theirs. The gal with the most clothespins at the end of the shower wins a special prize!

## 7. Toilet Paper Dress...

This fun bridal shower game will certainly get some laughs and make some memorable moments. Have your guests gather into groups and appoint one of them to be the "Bride." Give each group two rolls of toilet and tell them to get creative and make a wedding gown for their "bride-2-be"! Have the real bride of the shower vote on her favorite dress to determine who wins a prize.
