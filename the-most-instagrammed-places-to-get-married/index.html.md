# The Most Instagrammed Places to Tie the Knot for Girls Going out of the Norm ...

The world is full of beautiful, meaningful, and picturesque places to get married. Some of them are small, quaint, charming local spots and others … others are larger, more well known, and simply mesmerizing. Do you need some venue inspo? If you're eager to plan a fairytale wedding, all you have to do is pick your fairytale … and let yourself become inspired by some of the most Instagrammed hotspots for a happily ever after.

## 1. New York City

NYC is practically a given because it's such a vivid, versatile, varied city. You can find so many amazing places, from the library to the Cloisters and beyond.

## 2. Paris, France

Paris is the most romantic city in the world, so it stands to reason that couples all over the world dream of getting married there. I'd love to renew my vows with Heather in Paris some day.

## 3. Disney Theme Park

Now, if you really want a fairy tale, Disney is the place to celebrate your nuptials. The best part is that you can head to the park for your honeymoon!

## 4. Umaid Bhawan Palace

The Umaid Bhawan Palace at Jodhpur is incredibly popular on the 'gram and in the hearts of newlyweds from all over the globe. It's easy to see why, don't you think?

## 5. San Francisco

San Francisco would be such a fun, vibrant place to get married! The city is so open and welcoming, plus there are definitely opportunities for amazing photos.

## 6. Kauai, Hawaii

Hawaii is a popular destination in and of itself, but couples especially love getting hitched in Kauai. It's definitely the most epic spot for a beach wedding.

## 7. Caribbean: Anguilla

If you're going to have a destination wedding, you might as well take it to the Caribbean, right?

## 8. Caribbean: Turks and Caicos

In fact, the Caribbean is so popular that it makes the list twice! Turks and Caicos is enormously popular, for obvious reasons – that's gorgeous!

## 9. Amalfi Coast, Italy

Italy is another beloved location for weddings, beginning with the stunning Amalfi coast. I just want to jump into this picture and join the celebration.

## 10. Rome, Italy

Rome is an amazing place for a casually glamorous, stunningly gorgeous wedding. The entire city makes the perfect backdrop.

## 11. Tuscany, Italy

If you want something more rustic and richly beautiful, however, head to Tuscany. Maybe you can get married in a villa!

## 12. Bali

Want to keep it low-key and a little bit Boho? Take yourself and your honey to Bali and get married on one of the gorgeous beaches.

## 13. Valladolid, Spain

I want to get married here and I'm already married. I don't even care, though.

## 14. Mexico: Riviera Maya

How stunning is this? It's the perfect mix of casual and subtle glam.

## 15. Bora Bora

Bora Bora is one of the best spots for a beach wedding, especially if you want added flavor and rich cultural touches.

## 16. Fiji

Fiji is the same way. This is where you go when it's all about the two of you.

## 17. The Bahamas

And you should head to the Bahamas when you want everyone, from the wedding party to the guests, to have the time of their lives.

Where would you love to get married if you could pick anywhere in the world?
