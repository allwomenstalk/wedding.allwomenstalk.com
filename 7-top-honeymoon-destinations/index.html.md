# 7 Top Honeymoon Destinations ...

So, you’re about to engage in one of the sweetest travels of your life. Congratulations! Do you have any special honeymoon destinations on your mind or your selection depends on the location and finances? Well, I’m about to give you 7 top honeymoon destinations to think about and decide which one is just perfect for you.

## 1. Hawaii

Who wouldn’t like to spend a honeymoon chilling under a palm tree or sipping a tropical cocktail next to the pool of some high end honeymoon resort? Mmmm, I’m getting goose bumps as we speak! A hot honeymoon is always a good way to start a hot marriage, isn’t it? Definitely check out this destination, especially if you live in the US because I bet you could find some very interesting offers.

## 2. Paris

The city of love is the ideal honeymoon destination for a new couple, no doubt about it. Take a walk down the Champs Elysees, see the Eiffel Tower, kiss and hug everywhere and don’t forget to take plenty of pictures. Paris just spells “romance” and if you choose this wonderful city for your honeymoon, you can bet your new life together as a husband and wife will have a sweet, Hollywood-like start. So don’t be afraid to take a huge bite of it and “bon appétit”!

## 3. Venice

Relaxing and oh-so-romantic gondola ride with your husband is definitely something you’d want to try out some time in your life, so why not now, when you’re still in the lovey-dovey mood. Venice is sinking, you know that, so you’d better plan your trip now, before having some mean diving skills becomes a must. Okay, that wasn’t very romantic but, don’t worry, Venice is still on the surface, just as incredible and breathtaking as it always was. I’ve been there and I must admit that, in Venice, vita e bella always!

## 4. Bahamas

I don’t really need to tell you anything about this popular destination – Don’t I? Kind folks, rich culture and folklore, amazing beaches and many, many gorgeous resorts to chose from. If you live in or close to Florida, these gorgeous islands are practically your next door neighbors so picking one of them as your honeymoon paradise won’t only be fun but incredibly easy too. Well, hope you’ll have fun because, from this part of the globe, Bahamas look just a little bit closer than the Moon so I guess I won’t have the chance to see them any time soon.

## 5. Santorini

Blue skies, blue sea and those cute white houses with blue doors and windows look best on this amazing island! Don’t deny yourself that gorgeous caldera view and make sure you get one of those hotels very high on the cliff so you could see all those amazing stuff every day when you wake up and go out on the balcony. All that kissing and hugging in the morning and swimming during the day will make you tired so definitely check out some of the local taverns and let tasty Greek cuisine take care of your energy levels.

## 6. Florence

If you’re in the mood for a classic renaissance love story, you better head off to Florence where you won’t need any transportation other than the one Mother Nature has already given you- your legs. Walk and see all the wonders Florence has to offer and then rest and refill your energy with something traditional and tasty. Don’t forget the vine either and be sure to exchange those special looks, reserved only for the newlyweds and the ones in love.

## 7. Las Vegas

Las Vegas is not only the best place to go to have your bachelor or bachelorette party but also a great place to continue having fun after the wedding is over. Who knows, you might even win the jackpot and return home with enough cash to organize 5 weddings and honeymoons more! Yup, fun factor is definitely something you’d want to consider when picking you honeymoon destination and Vegas will give you that, plus a chance to get married again, just to be sure. LOL!

Are these top honeymoon destinations exactly what you were looking for or you prefer different scenery and landscape? I know, I know, most of my dream honeymoon destinations involve beaches and tropical or Mediterranean paradise but that’s just me, I love the sea. Mountain resorts are also lovely but, since I’m not really a fan of dry land, I’ll leave it to you to tell me which mountain resorts are worth checking out.
