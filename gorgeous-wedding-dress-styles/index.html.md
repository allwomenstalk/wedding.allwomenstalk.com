# 16 Gorgeous Wedding Dress Styles ...

I'm a firm believer that no two wedding dresses are the same whether that be due to embellishment, tulle, lace, form-fitting shape or a ribbon around the waist! Each style is so beautiful and luxurious in its own way. Get inspired with these styles!

## 1. Mermaid

![Mermaid](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mo/r7/jq52wg8256f49413f228b330132234_600x910.jpg)

A mermaid dress will typically be tight and fitted around the bodice and waist area before flaring out toward the bottom, similar to that of a mermaid's tail.

## 2. Traditional

![Traditional](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mb/ie/prj2xse956f49420ae891890729460_526x957.jpg)

A traditional dress is typically kept quite simple with a fitted bodice and flowing bottom half from the waist downwards. Generally speaking, embellishment and other details are excluded to reflect traditional style.

## 3. Strapless

![Strapless](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e1/h1/tmhjc7ql56f49453962be836498409_600x957.jpg)

A strapless dress, as the name suggests, does not have straps - although this is the only specification for the dress itself. This means that it can have other elements such as embellishment, lace, tulle and many more.

## 4. Lace

![Lace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/k5/ckiajy4i56f496783fa62485825050_600x818.jpg)

A lace dress will either be made partially or fully of lace, which can be anywhere on the dress itself. Additionally, it may also have a lace 'overlay' (external layer of a dress) with lining underneath.

## 5. Embellished

![Embellished](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/ya/oybb01dz56f49a5d252d9661172800_600x800.jpg)

An embellished dress will have some sort of embellishment, which could be anything from sequin to lace and everything in between. Embellishment gives the dress a more complex look due to the added detail.

## 6. Sweetheart

![Sweetheart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rp/ep/o2achnmd56f4969f13a1f951685454_600x764.jpg)

A sweetheart neckline dress means it's similar to the shape of the top of a love heart, which typically curves outwards and downwards. Due to this, it accentuates the bodice in a shapely way.

## 7. Fitted

![Fitted](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/gr/juyf8xg756f49a8bcebeb538862574_600x818.jpg)

A fitted dress is typically very tight and form-fitting, which is incredibly flattering on many body shapes. It will have minimal to no flare or 'fan' effect, instead being quite straight up and down in shape.

## 8. Princess

![Princess](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a1/jo/iyi7dlgy56f496553775d925632130_450x613.jpg)

A princess dress is also commonly known as a 'ball gown' due to the many similarities. One of which being a fitted bodice and a dramatic, full flared bottom half which is often made of tulle.

## 9. Fit & Flair

![Fit & Flair](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e7/u8/keeb0gbx56f49536927f0344897498_600x957.jpg)

A fit and flare dress is tight and fitted around the top and mid section before giving a subtle or obvious flare outwards towards the bottom. The style gives the dress plenty of shape and interest.

## 10. Decorative

![Decorative](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ag/oq/j9u5tjd956f4954606133709144260_500x681.jpg)

A decorative dress will often have an added accessory designed to 'decorate' the dress. This could be anything from ribbons to a belt and anything in between the two.

## 11. Tulle

![Tulle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oo/wf/vcu1qmsx56f4982199949744455981_600x800.jpg)

A tulle dress describes a fabric which is commonly used for wedding gowns. It's a very lightweight, thin netting which acts as an overlay for the main fabric used.

## 12. Textured

![Textured](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bv/uu/p6zc9vr256f4980153d17026850586_460x680.jpg)

A textured dress includes any element designed to give it a certain texture, look or feel. An example of this could be ruching on the bodice or bottom half of the dress, which instantly adds complexity.

## 13. Train

![Train](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/m9/t7fg4sl156f498692aac6471453075_600x900.jpg)

A train dress is one that has a large, back portion of fabric which hangs or 'drags' behind the dress for an effect such as making it more dramatic and eye-catching.

## 14. Ball

![Ball](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/me/lz/fr0p5kva56f498b5e9c97917249327_600x900.jpg)

A ball dress dramatically fans outwards from the bodice down and is often made of lots of fabric to create an extravagant, luxurious feel.

## 15. Trumpet

![Trumpet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/ek/x5jv4rvi56f49925d51ba732002007_600x878.jpg)

A trumpet dress is form fitted around the top half and gradually flares outwards from the waist down. This is usually done in a subtle way for an added softness.

## 16. Overlay

![Overlay](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ic/wy/sqa8vows56f4995a7b30e964443154_600x800.jpg)

An overlay dress describes a thin piece of fabric over the top of the main fabric or lining, used to create a two-tone effect. This can typically be lace or tulle for wedding gowns.
