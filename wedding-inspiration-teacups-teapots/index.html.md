# 4 Wedding Inspiration Ideas: Teacups & Teapots ...

I love seeing mason jars, bottles, and vintage vases for centerpieces and easy DIY wedding ideas. But something else I've caught my eye on and would love to see more of is **teacup** and **teapot** **centerpieces**, **favors**, and **DIY **details. There are so many inspiring ideas that are wonderful for any wedding decor, theme or no theme. Maybe **teacups** are just the inspiration you needed for your wedding planning...

## 4. Alice Inspired...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [KitchenofCarla&Elaine](http://justfordesserts.blogspot.com/2010/01/tea-cup-cake.html)

Everyone's favorite** tea time** includes a **mad hatter**, a **white rabbit** running late, and a little girl named [Alice](https://movies.disney.com/). Your wedding details don't have to be a crazy [Alice in Wonderland](https://goo.gl/gPYTJ8)**theme** just because you want to include beautiful **teacups** and **teapots**. Let your [imagination](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/diy-wedding-ideas-on-a-budget-guest-blogger-poppies-sunshine/)run wild like the little girl who fell down the rabbit hole, and let Alice **inspire** you and your big day like this amazing **teacup wedding cake** from [Carla and Elaine](http://justfordesserts.blogspot.com/)!

## 3. Tea for Two...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [SweetsbyNatalieKay](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.sweetsbynataliekay.com/tag/jane-eyre/)

**Teacups** can be fun, they can be **elegant**, vintage, or even rustic. Find the perfect teacup to match your **decor** at [thirft shops](https://www.careandshareshoppes.org/), [online](https://goo.gl/44IRgc), or by **borrowing** them from your relatives. I love the details and soft colors on these teacups. I can just imagine a sweet newlywed couple enjoying a nice **cup of tea** on their [beach honeymoon](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/romantic-honeymoon-in-puerto-vallarta-guest-blogger-savy-deets-bridal/)looking out from their balcony.

## 2. Tea & Succulents...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/), [WeddingObession](https://weddingobsession.com/2010/11/25/vibrant-sunshine-coast-wedding/)

I love how versatile teacups and teapots are; they can be paired with any wedding color or decor. How stunning are these** teapot centerpieces** with orange, purple, and green flowers and plants inside them. Can you just envision an array of [succulents](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/5-ways-to-display-succulents-at-your-wedding/)and moss surrounding a **white** and **orange** **teapot**. What a great decor idea for a **fall wedding**.

## 1. Romantic & Rustic...

Photos: [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/)

Did you ever think of **teacups** for your **barn wedding decor**? I just love the potted aloe plant inside the delicate teacup, what a creative idea and great idea for a **wedding favor**! When you think of teacups you usually don't have **rustic**, barn, dirt, or plants on your mind as well. But the soft, **romantic**, feminine touches mix so well with the rough, hard, outdoor decor.

**Have you started planning your teacup inspired wedding? **
