# 7 Playful Ruffle Wedding Dresses ...

To me, most brides who are considering ruffle wedding dresses are looking for a dramatic, show stopping gown. These brides are fashionistas who know what style they love and will go to far lengths to find the wedding dress they have in mind. So sit back and enjoy 7 Playful Ruffle Wedding Dresses that will stop everyone in their tracks...

## 1. BHLDN Ruffled Bodice Wedding Dress...

![BHLDN Ruffled Bodice Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/1_bhldn-ruffled-bodice-wedding-dress_550x825.jpg)

I'm not sure that I could pull off a ruffle wedding dress like this one from [bhldn.com](http://www.bhldn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) with a dramatic bodice and high collar. However, I can picture a sassy, [fashionista](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-on-how-to-become-a-fashionista/) bride strutting her stuff down the aisle and owning her moment in this gown. You really don't need too many accessories with a wedding dress like this one; perhaps a sparkling bracelet and matching earrings, and you are set.

## 2. Lazaro Chapel Train Ruffle Skirt...

![Lazaro Chapel Train Ruffle Skirt...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/2_lazaro-chapel-train-ruffle-skirt_350x514.jpg)

This ruffle wedding dress chapel train goes on for days! Ruffles are so sweet and playful, and moms who dress their little girls in them now know that one day they will be walking down the aisle in them! This stunning Lazaro might look like a city chic gown, but I bet a country chic bride in cowgirl boots would have a ball in it on the dance floor!

## 3. Sophisticated Allure Ruffle Wedding Dress...

![Sophisticated Allure Ruffle Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/3_sophisticated-allure-ruffle-wedding-dress_480x640.jpg)

I love the simplicity of this sleeved, organza Allure Ruffle Wedding Dress. It's a sophisticated ball gown that isn't over the top, which is perfect for the fashionista bride who wants a toned down ruffled look. Wear a sparkly sash around the waist and matching glittered heels to show off your personality and give this dress some sparkle.

## 4. Short & Sweet Ruffle Wedding Dress...

![Short & Sweet Ruffle Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/4_short-sweet-ruffle-wedding-dress_300x450.jpg)

How adorable is this short ruffle wedding dress! This Dennis Basso little number would be a great second dress to change into for your reception. It would also be really sweet and sassy for a courthouse wedding. I love how they styled her hair in a ballerina bun, would you consider this look for your big day?

## 5. Mori Lee Beaded Lace Ruffle Wedding Dress...

![Mori Lee Beaded Lace Ruffle Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/5_mori-lee-beaded-lace-ruffle-wedding-dress_600x850.jpg)

If you think the front of this ruffled wedding dress is gorgeous, wait until you see the back! I love the fit and flair style of this Mori Lee organza gown, the ruffles give it a Spanish vibe while the drop waist shows off the bride's beautiful curves. The beaded lace is stunning and gives this otherwise simple dress a few romantic details.

## 6. Mori Lee Ruffled Ball Gown...

![Mori Lee Ruffled Ball Gown...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/6_mori-lee-ruffled-ball-gown_600x850.jpg)

Many of the ruffle wedding dresses I featured have drop waists and low skirts, but this Mori Lee is a beautiful full organza ball gown. This is a ruffled gown I could see myself in, it's not over dramatic; its ruffles are soft and subtle. What do you think about the flower on the bodice, would you keep or remove it?

## 7. Peach Colored Ruffle Wedding Dress...

![Peach Colored Ruffle Wedding Dress...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2013/02/7_peach-colored-ruffle-wedding-dress_450x600.jpg)

I love colored wedding dresses, this peach ruffle wedding dress by Moonlight is so enchanting. It reminds me of a fairy tale princess, a gown I could see The Little Mermaid wearing! I love the floral details on the shoulder and the top of the skirt. Are you wearing a colored gown?
