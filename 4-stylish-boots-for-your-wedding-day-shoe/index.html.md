# 4 Stylish Boots for Your Wedding Day Shoe ...

I was inspired to write about wedding day booties, you know the cute low boots with a heel., when I saw the coolest pair on [Zappos](https://goo.gl/ZNDn56).com But then I figured why not write about different kind of **bridal boots** you can wear on your big day! From cowboy boots to winter boots, one of these might be the perfect style for your wedding day.

## 4. Rainy Day Wedding...

![Rainy Day Wedding...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/blueboots_411x308.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://www.zappos.com/diesel-la-boot-11?oosRedirected=true)

Why let the rain and cloudy day bring down your celebration on your wedding day. Slip on these adorable **rain** **boots** from [Diesel](https://shop.diesel.com/en/)for your **something blue **and all of your guests will start to cheer up! I saw a similar pair posted by Jacin on her [Lovely Little Details](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser)facebook page a few weeks ago, she sure knows style when she sees it!

## 3. Winter Wedding Day...

![Winter Wedding Day...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/uggboots_415x312.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/owskhC)

**Snow boots** are another great shoe to have for your **wedding day** when the weather isn't too friendly. Warm those toes up in style with these cozy [Bailey Button Uggs](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.uggaustralia.com/search.aspx?searchfor=bailey%20button)and share the love with your bridesmaid to pose for a cute wedding day photo.

## 2. Sparkly Booties...

![Sparkly Booties...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/sparklybootie_413x310.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/jq66J6)

Let the sun and your gorgeous **ankle boots** shine on your wedding day when you strut down the aisle in these hot **sparkling booties** from [Pelle Moda](https://goo.gl/Oh9DwE). I love everything about this little bootie, the open toe, the sparkly studs, the suede; what a fun shoe!

## 1. Country Bride...

![Country Bride...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/04/cowboyboots_413x310.jpg)

Photo: [Zappos](https://goo.gl/awS1ud)

Calling all **country brides**! These aren't just any **cowgirl boots**, check out those beautiful roses all along the boot! Book that barn for your venue, get the sweet tea ready for your mason jars, and turn up the Carrie Underwood music as you dance down the aisle in these **country wedding boots** from [Lucchese](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.lucchese.com/).

**What style of boots will you rock on your wedding day?**
