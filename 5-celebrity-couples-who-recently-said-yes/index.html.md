# 5 Celebrity Couples Who Recently Said Yes!

**Celebrity engagements**, weddings, and sadly divorces happen all the time. Sometimes we just shrug our shoulders, but other times we take a double look or listen and can't believe what we just heard! See if any of these **5 celebrity couples who recently said yes** make you take a double look...

## 5. The Sweet Girl Next Door...

Though I loved seeing her and [Jake Gyllenhaal](https://people.com/celebrity/source-reese-witherspoon-and-jake-gyllenhaal-split/) together, I love that [Reese Witherspoon](https://allwomenstalk.com/10-reese-witherspoon-street-style-inspirations/) is truly happy. How adorable does she look with her **fiance**, celebrity agent [Jim Toth](https://web.archive.org/web/http://thecelebritycafe.com/feature/witherspoon-debuts-new-ring-lakers-game-01-06-2011). We've watched **Witherspoon** grow up and go through two pregnancies, a divorce, and dating many famous actors. We hope she has found **true love** and can't wait to see what her **wedding gown** looks like!

## 4. A Beautiful Swan...

![A Beautiful Swan...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/BENJAMIN-MILLEPIED_410x335.jpg)

Photo: [Huffingtonpost.com](https://www.huffpost.com/entry/benjamin-millepied-natali_1_n_802080)

Strong, Sexy, and Smart. Did you know that [Natalie Portman](https://allwomenstalk.com/20-amazing-and-stylish-natalie-portman-looks/), other than being an amazing actress, graduated from [Harvard University](https://www.harvard.edu/)? I've always been inspired by her. Recently she showed us her dark side in the new movie [Black Swan](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0947798/), which has blossomed a **new love** for Portman. Her **ballet choreographer**, [Benjamin Millepied](https://usatoday30.usatoday.com/life/people/2010-12-28-millepied27_ST_N.htm), popped the question along with becoming her baby's daddy! She'll be an amazing **bride** and **mother. **

Do you know [Tony Romo](http://www.tonyromo9.com/) from being the **star quarterback** from the [Dallas Cowboys](https://goo.gl/RPaJvs)or do you know him from dating all the [leading ladies](https://allwomenstalk.com/8-actresses-in-fancy-shorts-can-you-pull-these-off/)in **Hollywood**? Hopefully he has finally found the right blonde, [Candice Crawford](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.candice-crawford.com/), since he has asked her to be his **wife**. ## 1. First a Divorce, Now an Engagement...

![First a Divorce, Now an Engagement...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/01/leann-rimes-eddie-cibrian_400x600.jpg)

Photo: [Celebrityphotos.sheknows.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://celebrityphotos.sheknows.com/)

Fiance [Eddie Cibrian](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.starpulse.com/Actors/Cibrian,_Eddie/) met Rimes while they were filming the [Lifetime](https://www.mylifetime.com/) movie [Northern Lights](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1363127/) together. Shortly after they rocked both Nashville and **Hollywood** when their **love affair** was the [hot gossip](https://allwomenstalk.com/is-he-a-cheater-10-obvious-signs-of-cheating/) topic of the week since they were **both married at the time**. Hopefully it works out for them.
