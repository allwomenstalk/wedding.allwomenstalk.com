# 6 Creative Styles for Your Wedding Aisle ...

Whether your **wedding ceremony** is being held outdoors on a beautiful landscape or **inside** a rustic barn, there will always be an **aisle** that needs **decorating**. You can go simple and chic, bright and bold, or even wild and offbeat! See these 6 **creative** **styles** to find the one that is right for your **wedding aisle**.

## 6. The White Party

![The White Party](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/whiteaisle_408x612.jpg)

Photo: [KristinVining](http://www.kristinviningphotoblog.com/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/11/17/modern-wedding-by-kristin-vining/)

**Classy**, **Simple**, and **Chic**. If you're a** DIY bride** who would like to keep things easy on yourself and those helping out with your wedding day projects, choose something** simple but chic** like this beautiful **white aisle**. I love that everything from the chairs and petals to the floating candles and runner are white, very **elegant** but not over the top.

## 5. All about the Tulle...

![All about the Tulle...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/tulleaisle_411x274.jpg)

Photo: [RowellPhoto StyleMePretty](https://www.rowellphoto.com/)

**Tulle** is another simple wedding item that can be purchased at many craft stores to turn your aisle into something fabulous. It's **soft**, dreamy, and can be used in many ways. I love the pairing of the yellow flowers and petals with the tulle in this photo, it gives a great **pop of color** to this deep brown **wedding aisle**. Transform any blank aisle into something wonderful with the smallest touch of fabric and flowers.

## 4. A Rustic & Organic Atmosphere...

![A Rustic & Organic Atmosphere...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/pottedplantsaisle_402x603.jpg)

Photo: [MantasPhotography](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.mantasphotography.com/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2010/12/01/brooklyn-factory-wedding-by-dm-events/)

Even on the deck of a hip [Brooklyn restaurant](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.mymoonnyc.com/)you can find a beautiful wedding aisle. I love how they kept the decor** unique and rustic** to match the amazing wood chairs, wood planks, and warehouse feel of the background. If you have never thought of **potted plants** lining your aisle, think again! This may just start a cool trend.

## 3. Art and Succulents...

![Art and Succulents...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/candlesandsucculentaisle_408x306.jpg)

Photo: [Betwixtstudio](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.betwixtstudio.com/#/special/splash/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/01/27/san-diego-wedding-by-betwixt-studio/)

When you have an** amazing venue** like the [Museum of Contemporary Art](https://mcasd.org/index.php)in San Diego you don't want to go overboard with your **decor**, especially for your **wedding aisle**. The bride and groom couldn't have picked a better plant than the **succulent** to style their cool, **artistic aisle** with. It stands out on it's own like art and is more than just a simple flower.

## 2. The Love of Mason Jars...

![The Love of Mason Jars...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/masonjaraisle_405x270.jpg)

Photo: [Bluephoto](https://www.bluephoto.biz/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/vault)

How amazing is this mason jar wedding altar! I just love** homemade rustic altars** that look like walls or doors. There are so many great **details** in this wedding photo that can inspire DIY brides, **rustic or vintage brides**, and **outdoor brides**. What does your outdoor wedding altar look like?

## 1. Amazingly Creative...

![Amazingly Creative...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wedding/2011/02/petalsaisle_405x270.jpg)

Photo: [Sengbush](http://www.sengbush.com/)and [DamionHamilton](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.damionhamilton.com/index2.php#/home/)via [StyleMePretty](https://www.stylemepretty.com/2011/01/03/california-wedding-by-jenne-hohn-events/)

With the **great outdoors** as your gorgeous background in [California](https://aubergeresorts.com/solage/)you may think that any extra decor wouldn't be needed. Never have I seen** flower petals **laid in such a **creative design** as this. This stunning **artwork** was created by [Asiel Design](https://www.asieldesign.com/), who love to work with more than just flowers!
